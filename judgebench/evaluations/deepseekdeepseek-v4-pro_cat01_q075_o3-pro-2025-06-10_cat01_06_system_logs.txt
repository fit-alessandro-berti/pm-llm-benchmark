The transformed event log is largely sound, with reasonable case identification (per artifact) and meaningful activity abstractions. However, it is not flawless. The most notable issue is the omission of “switch away” activities in the source cases (e.g., DOC1 when the user switches to email, or XLS1 when returning to Word). Capturing these transitions would better reflect the interruptions and provide a more complete trace of each case’s lifecycle. This absence slightly weakens the narrative and process mining potential, even though the chosen mapping is still usable. Other minor points could be debated (e.g., naming the first email event “Open Email Inbox” rather than “Access Inbox”), but the main gap is the handling of switch events. Therefore, while very good, it does not reach near-flawless perfection.
