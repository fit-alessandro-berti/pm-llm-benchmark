While the prompt permits hypothesizing, the tone sometimes implies stronger evidentiary weight than the log actually supplies. This is a subtle but real logical overreach.

- **Query imperfections (even if minor)**:

It works for the given data but implicitly assumes every required activity is present exactly once; missing activities (Cases 1003/1004) produce incomplete deviation sets without explicit warning.
