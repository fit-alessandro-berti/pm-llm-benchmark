### Key Activities:

2. **Send Fine**: This could involve notifying the offender about the fine.
3. **Insert Fine Notification**: Recording the notification of the fine in the system.
4. **Add Penalty**: Applying additional penalties to the fine for late payment or other reasons.
5. **Payment**: Making a payment to settle the fine.
6. **Send for Credit Collection**: This could involve further actions to recover the fine through credit collection agencies.
7. **Appeal to Judge**: Appealing the fine to a higher authority (e.g., a judge) for review.
8. **Send Appeal to Prefecture**: Sending an appeal to a local administrative unit (e.g., a prefecture).
9. **Receive Result Appeal from Prefecture**: Getting a response or decision from the prefecture regarding the appeal.
10. **Notify Result Appeal to Offender**: Informing the offender about the outcome of the appeal.
11. **Insert Date Appeal to Prefecture**: Registering the date and details of the appeal in the system.
