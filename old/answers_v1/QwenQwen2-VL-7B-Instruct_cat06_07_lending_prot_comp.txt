One of the main differences between the protected and unprotected groups is the presence of the "skipped_examination" process variant in the unprotected group. This process variant is not present in the protected group. Additionally, the unprotected group has a much higher frequency of the "Application Rejected" process variant compared to the protected group. The protected group has a much higher frequency of the "Make Visit to Assess Collateral" process variant compared to the unprotected group.
