 Based on the provided data, it appears that the process involves creating fines for offenders. The process variants can be broken down into several steps:

1. **Create Fine**: This is the initial step where a fine is created for an offender.

2. **Send Fine**: After the fine is created, it is sent to the offender.

3. **Insert Fine Notification**: A notification related to the fine is inserted into the system.

4. **Add penalty**: A penalty can be added to the fine.

5. **Payment**: The offender pays the fine.

6. **Send for Credit Collection**: If the payment is not made, the fine may be sent for credit collection.

7. **Insert Date Appeal to Prefecture**: The offender can appeal the fine to the prefecture, and the date of this appeal can be inserted into the system.

8. **Send Appeal to Prefecture**: The appeal is sent to the prefecture for consideration.

9. **Receive Result Appeal from Prefecture**: The result of the appeal (e.g., approved, denied) is received from the prefecture.

10. **Notify Result Appeal to Offender**: The offender is notified of the result of their appeal.

11. **Appeal to Judge**: If the offender is not satisfied with the decision from the prefecture, they can appeal to a judge.
