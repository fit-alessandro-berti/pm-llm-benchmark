### Major problems

1. **Incorrect interpretation of several DECLARE templates**
   - It treats **response** as ōthe next step must be ...ö, but `response(A,B)` means **B must eventually occur after A**, not necessarily immediately next.
   - It treats **coexistence** as a direct transition, but `coexistence(C,F)` means **if one occurs, the other must also occur**; it does **not** mean C is followed by F.
   - It treats **responded existence** as if it were an ordering relation, but it only requires the target to exist somewhere in the same trace if the source occurs.
   - It blurs **succession**, **alternate**, and **chain** constraints, often converting them into simplistic ōnext stepö statements.

2. **A very important logical implication is completely missed**
   - `Receive_Application` is mandatory (`existence`) and initial (`init`).
   - `Transfer_Funds` is in **noncoexistence** with `Receive_Application`.
   - Therefore, **`Transfer_Funds` can never occur in any valid trace**.

4. **Overstates determinism**
