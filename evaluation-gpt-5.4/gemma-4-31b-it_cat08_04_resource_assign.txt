### 2. Several metrics are oversimplified or imprecise
Some proposed metrics are useful, but others are too simplistic or not robust enough.

Examples:
- **FCR definition is shaky.** Measuring FCR as "`Work L1 End` immediately followed by `Ticket Closed`" is too narrow and may be wrong depending on logging behavior. - **Lowest WIP routing** is simplistic. Lowest active-ticket count is not the same as shortest expected completion time or true capacity.
