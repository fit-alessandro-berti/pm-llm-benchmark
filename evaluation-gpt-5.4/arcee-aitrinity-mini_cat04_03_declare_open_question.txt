- It ignores or mishandles important constraints:
  - `altresponse(G, H)`
  - `altprecedence(H, G)`
  - `altsuccession(C, E)`
  - `chainresponse(D, G)`
  - `chainprecedence(F, B)`
  - `chainsuccession(E, D)`
  - `noncoexistence(G, A)`
  - `nonsuccession(H, B)`
