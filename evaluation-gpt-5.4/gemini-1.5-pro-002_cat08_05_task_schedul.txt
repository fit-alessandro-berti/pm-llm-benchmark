### 2. Technical inaccuracy on setup-time analysis
This is one of the bigger flaws.

The answer says:

> “Sequence-Dependent Setup Times: Analyze the time difference between ‘Task End’ of a job and ‘Setup Start’ of the next job on the same machine.”

That is **not the setup time**.  
That gap may include:
- machine idle time,
- operator unavailability,
- waiting for material,
- breakdowns,
- scheduling delay.

The actual setup duration is typically:
- **Setup End - Setup Start**

And the **sequence dependence** should be modeled by conditioning that duration on:
- previous job/product family,
- next job/product family,
- machine,
- operator,
- tooling/configuration,

This is a meaningful analytical error, and under a strict rubric it should significantly reduce the score.
