- **`LIST_AGG` is not the correct DuckDB aggregate for building a list sequence** in this form. In DuckDB, the usual aggregate is `list(activity ORDER BY timestamp)` (or `array_agg(...)`), while `listagg` refers to string concatenation, not a list-valued sequence.
