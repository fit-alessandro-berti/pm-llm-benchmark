   That is wrong: **response** means *eventually after*, not immediately after.

   The answer says it prevents “money moves without a proper originating application on record” or prevents funds transfer “before” application receipt.  
   That is not what **noncoexistence** means. It means **A and G cannot both occur in the same trace at all**. Since `Receive_Application` is mandatory (`existence` + `init`), this effectively means **`Transfer_Funds` can never happen in any valid trace**.
