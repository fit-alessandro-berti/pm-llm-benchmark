This is **too simplistic** for real event logs with:

- parallel activities
- overlaps
- missing timestamps

- optional branches

A proper approach should mention **reconstructing activity instances from START/COMPLETE pairs first**, then identifying the **actual predecessor-successor relation in the process model**, not just “the next timestamp.” As written, the method risks pairing the wrong activities and mismeasuring waits.
