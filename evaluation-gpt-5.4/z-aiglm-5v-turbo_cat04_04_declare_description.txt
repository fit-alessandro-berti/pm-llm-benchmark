- It repeatedly misstates DECLARE semantics.

  - `nonsuccession` is reduced to ōnot immediately after,ö which is also false; it forbids the second activity from occurring later after the first at all.
  - `altsuccession` is described too loosely and partly as if it were just a normal sequential flow.

- It invents a clean linear ōhappy pathö that the model does not actually support.
  - The answer presents the process as basically linear from application to transfer to notification.
  - But the model contains serious tensions or outright inconsistencies, especially around:
    - `precedence(Quality_Assurance_Review, Authorize_Contract_Terms)`
    - `chainprecedence(Authorize_Contract_Terms, Preliminary_Credit_Check)`
    - `succession(Preliminary_Credit_Check, Gather_Additional_Documents)`
