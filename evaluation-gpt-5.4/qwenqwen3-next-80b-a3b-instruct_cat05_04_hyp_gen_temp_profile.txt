- Then the outer query joins again to any `P` and any `N` for that claim, not necessarily the exact pair used in the duration calculation.
- That makes the reported `approve_resource` and `notify_resource` unreliable.

This is a serious flaw, not a minor one.

The temporal profile explicitly says activity pairs are “not necessarily directly, but eventually” related.

That is conceptually wrong. In the intended process, `A` and `E` should often happen between `R` and `P`. The query therefore filters out many normal cases and tests the wrong thing.
