Why it deserves a high score:
- The main query is correct:
  ```sql
  SELECT el.case_id, el.activity, el.timestamp
  FROM event_log el
  WHERE el.case_id IN (
      SELECT case_id
      FROM event_log
      GROUP BY case_id
      HAVING COUNT(*) <= 6
  );
  ```
  This correctly filters out `case_id`s with more than 6 events and returns events for the remaining cases.
- The `QUALIFY` alternative is also correct in DuckDB:
  ```sql
  SELECT case_id, activity, timestamp
  FROM event_log
  QUALIFY COUNT(*) OVER (PARTITION BY case_id) <= 6;
  ```
