- **Key semantic mistake:** the temporal profile pairs are stated as **eventual, not necessarily direct**. The answer repeatedly interprets short `E -> N` or `A -> C` intervals as implying skipped intermediate steps. That is **not justified** from the profile alone.
- **SQL pairing issue:** most queries join activity tables by `claim_id` and `timestamp < timestamp` without selecting the **correct matching occurrence**. If a claim has multiple `P`, `N`, etc., the queries can create many false pairs.
- **Thresholds are arbitrary:** several cutoffs (`>10 days`, `<4 hours`, `<30 minutes`) are not cleanly derived from the given averages/STDEVs or any explicit zeta rule.
