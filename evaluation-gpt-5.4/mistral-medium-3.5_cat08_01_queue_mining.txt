That is a **simplification**, not a robust queue-mining definition. In real process mining, especially in healthcare, the “waiting time” for an activity should ideally be measured from the activity’s **enablement time**, not merely the immediately preceding recorded activity. This matters because outpatient processes can involve:

- parallel branches,
