- It misses that the model is likely inconsistent / unsatisfiable.
  - `init(Receive_Application)` requires the trace to start with `Receive_Application`.
  - `noncoexistence(Transfer_Funds, Receive_Application)` means `Transfer_Funds` and `Receive_Application` cannot both occur in the same trace.

  - `response` does not mean “immediately following.”
