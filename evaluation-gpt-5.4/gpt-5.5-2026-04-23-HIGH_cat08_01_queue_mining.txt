as if that were always a queue. In reality, that gap may include:
- clinically necessary delay,

- patient-caused delay,
- planned waiting,
- preparation/setup time,

- observation time,
- deliberate scheduling gaps.

A stricter answer would have distinguished:
- **true waiting-for-capacity**
- **waiting-for-information/results**

- duplicated events,
- inconsistent resource IDs,
- timestamp granularity issues,

- incomplete/open cases,
