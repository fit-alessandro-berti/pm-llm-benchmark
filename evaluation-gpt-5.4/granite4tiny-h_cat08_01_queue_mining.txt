### 1. Major conceptual error in the definition of waiting time
This is the most serious issue.

The answer defines waiting time as:

> “the time a patient spends from the moment they start an activity … until the next activity begins”

That is generally incorrect in this context.  
With start and complete timestamps, the usual queue/waiting time between two consecutive activities is:

**start time of next activity  completion time of previous activity**

not **start of previous activity  start of next activity**.

The answer’s definition mixes **service time + waiting time**, which would systematically overstate queueing delay and distort all downstream analysis. Since the question explicitly emphasizes using **start and completion timestamps**, this is a substantial error.
