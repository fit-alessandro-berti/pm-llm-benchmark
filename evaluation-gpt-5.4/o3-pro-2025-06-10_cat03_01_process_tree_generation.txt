Main problems:

- **The loop is modeled incorrectly.**  
  In `* ( A, B )`, the first branch `A` is executed **at least once**.

- **Operator notation is used inconsistently with the prompt.**  
  The prompt defines operators as binary:
  - `-> (A, B)`
  - `X (A, B)`
  - `+ (A, B)`
  - `* (A, B)`
