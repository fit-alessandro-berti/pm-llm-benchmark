**Why the score is only moderate**
1. **Inconsistent mapping of FOCUS/SWITCH events**

     - `09:07:15 FOCUS ... Quarterly_Report.docx` becomes **“Switch to document”**

2. **Over-inference from raw events**

4. **Weak standardization**
   - Activity naming mixes object-specific and generic styles:
     - “Open document”
     - “Open spreadsheet”
     - “Open PDF”
     - “Open e-mail client”
     - “Switch to document”
   - For process mining, stronger standardization would help, e.g.:
     - `Open Object`
     - `Edit Content`
     - `Save Object`

     - `Send Reply`
