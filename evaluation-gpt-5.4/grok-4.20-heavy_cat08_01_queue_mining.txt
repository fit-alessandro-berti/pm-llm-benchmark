True waiting for a resource would ideally be measured from the moment the next activity becomes **enabled/ready/requested** to the moment it starts. In healthcare, those are not always identical:
- clinical review may be pending
- patient transport/movement time may be embedded

The answer briefly mentions parallel activities, but it does **not fully resolve the readiness/enabling issue**. For a process mining answer focused specifically on **queue mining**, that is a meaningful conceptual limitation.
