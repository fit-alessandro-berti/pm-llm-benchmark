- some gaps may be **planned/clinical delays**, not queueing,
- some gaps may be **patient-caused** rather than system-caused,
- some cases may have **parallel branches** or multiple prerequisites,
- the relevant queue start should often be the **enablement time** of the next activity, not merely ōprevious activity complete.ö
