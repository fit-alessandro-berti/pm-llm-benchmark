Main issues:
- **Not all raw events were transformed.** At least these are omitted:
  - `2024-12-11T09:01:45Z` `SWITCH` to email
  - `2024-12-11T09:06:00Z` `SWITCH` back to `Document1.docx`

- **Inconsistent treatment of `FOCUS`/`SWITCH`.**
  - `09:04:00 SWITCH` to PDF is kept as “Open PDF.”
  - `09:07:15 FOCUS` to Quarterly Report is kept as “Resume Document.”
  - But `09:06:00 SWITCH` back to Word is dropped entirely.
  That inconsistency weakens the modeling logic.
- **“Open Document” is often too strong an interpretation.** A `FOCUS` event does not necessarily mean the file was opened; it may only mean it became active. This affects:
  - `08:59:50` Quarterly_Report.docx
  - `09:00:00` Document1.docx
- **Case logic is slightly oversimplified.**
  - The answer claims cases are keyed by `Window` title, but the email case is actually inferred from a click action, not the window title (`Email - Inbox` is generic).
  - That is a reasonable choice, but the explanation should be more precise.
