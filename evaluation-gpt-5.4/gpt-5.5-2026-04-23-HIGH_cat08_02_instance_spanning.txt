That is **stricter than the stated regulation**.

The scenario says:

That means the limit applies while an order is **actively in Packing or actively in QC**, not necessarily during any idle gap between the two. Holding a token continuously from pre-Packing until post-QC would artificially reduce capacity if a hazardous order finishes Packing and then waits before QC.
