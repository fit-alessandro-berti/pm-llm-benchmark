  In DuckDB, `date_diff('day', ...)` is not the same as checking whether the actual interval exceeds exactly 5 days. This can misclassify borderline cases. A more correct condition would be something like:
  ```sql
  n3.timestamp > n2.timestamp + INTERVAL '5 days'
  ```
