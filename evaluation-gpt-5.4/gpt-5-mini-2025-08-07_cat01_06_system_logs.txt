     - `Quarterly_Report.docx` is “opened” at 08:59:50 and again at 09:07:15 without an intervening close.
   - For process mining, this is not a minor wording issue; it changes the inferred process behavior.

   - The answer changes the original window value to `Email - Inbox (Annual Meeting)`, which is not present in the log.
