### Main reason for the low score
The proposed quality loop `*(AS, IQC)` is not a correct fit for the log.

With the stated POWL loop semantics, `*(A, B)` means:
- execute `A`
- then either exit
- or execute `B` and then `A` again

So `*(AS, IQC)` generates patterns like:
- `AS`
- `AS, IQC, AS`
- `AS, IQC, AS, IQC, AS`
- etc.

But the log contains behavior that this does **not** capture correctly, especially **Case 3**, which includes consecutive `IQC` events and also ends the quality-related segment with `IQC` before moving to `TST`. The proposed loop cannot express that. This is a major flaw because one of the key nontrivial parts of the process is modeled incorrectly.
