#### 1. Major technical flaw in the dispatching rule
The biggest issue is the proposed **ATCS-like formula** in Strategy 1.

Canonical ATC/ATCS logic should penalize:
- **larger slack** less urgently than smaller slack
- **larger setup times** rather than reward them

But the formula as written appears to use:
- a **positive** exponent on the tardiness/slack term
- a **positive** exponent on setup time

If taken literally, that would prioritize jobs with **more slack** and **larger setup penalties**, which is the opposite of what ATCS is supposed to do. That is a serious technical error in the most important optimization proposal.

Even if this is partly a formatting issue, under strict grading it is a significant flaw.
