**Main problems:**

     - PDF highlight is not represented as a distinct meaningful event

3. **Activity naming is often too generic**
   - `PROCESS_EMAIL_TASK` is not a good activity name for process mining.

     - `Draft Email Reply`
     - `Send Email`
     - `Review PDF`
     - `Highlight Key Finding`
