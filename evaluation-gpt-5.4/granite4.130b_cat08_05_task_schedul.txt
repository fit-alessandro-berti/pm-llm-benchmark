That is not robust enough. In reality:

- variability can be endogenous to scheduling logic, setups, breakdowns, or mix changes

A stronger answer would have proposed:
- counterfactual simulation
- decomposition of delay into queueing vs processing vs disruption effects
- regression/survival models with controls
- comparison of observed performance against finite-capacity feasible benchmarks
