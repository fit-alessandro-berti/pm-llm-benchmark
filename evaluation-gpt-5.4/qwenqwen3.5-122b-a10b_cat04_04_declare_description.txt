Main issues:

- It presents a mostly coherent “happy path” loan process, but the model itself is not coherently supportive of that path. A very important flaw is that the answer does not clearly conclude that the constraints make the process effectively inconsistent or infeasible under standard DECLARE semantics.

  - `altresponse(Transfer_Funds, Notify_Customer)` plus `altprecedence(Notify_Customer, Transfer_Funds)` is not just “tight coupling”; under standard readings it implies `Notify_Customer` must occur before any `Transfer_Funds`, and also after each `Transfer_Funds`, which is highly problematic.

- It overclaims that the model ensures a compliant, logically ordered process, when in fact the constraint set appears internally contradictory.

- It at least notices that some constraints are problematic.
