Main problems:

- **Incorrect branching semantics:**  
  The biggest flaw is that `DV` and `FC` each have **two outgoing arcs from the same transition**:
  - `t_dv -> p_fc` and `t_dv -> p_rds`
  - `t_fc -> p_ca` and `t_fc -> p_ii`

- **Closure is modeled incorrectly:**  
  The scenario says the process reaches the final marking **after closure**. But here:
  - `p_cl` is used as the final marking
  - yet also `p_cl -> t_cl` is added  
  This means the final marking is reached **before** `CL`, so closure is not actually required. Worse, `t_cl` has **no output place**, making it a dead-end transition.

  Several places are named as “after X” but are actually **before X**:

  - same issue for `p_ca`, `p_ap`, `p_p`, `p_n`  
  This makes the model confusing and conceptually sloppy.
