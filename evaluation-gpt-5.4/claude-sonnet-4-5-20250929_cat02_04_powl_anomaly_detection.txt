For a 2-child loop in pm4py / process-tree style semantics, `LOOP(A, I)` means:
- `A` must happen once,
- then optionally `I`,
- then `A` again,
- and so on.

So possible traces are like:
- `A`
- `A, I, A`
- `A, I, A, I, A`
