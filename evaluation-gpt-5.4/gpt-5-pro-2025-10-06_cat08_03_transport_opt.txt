### 2. Some KPI definitions are weak or partially incorrect
This is a meaningful deduction.

- **On-Time Delivery Rate**
  - Defined as “deliveries within customer time window / total deliveries attempted.”
  - That is ambiguous. A **failed attempt** is not really an on-time delivery, even if it occurred within the window.

  - The answer offers an estimated model if fuel telemetry is absent, which is reasonable in principle.
