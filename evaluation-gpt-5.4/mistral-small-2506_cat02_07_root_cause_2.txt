#### 2. It makes causal claims that are not actually supported by the data
This is the biggest issue.

Examples:
- “**Adjuster_Mike ... suggesting inefficiency**”
- “**Adjuster_Lisa ... had the longest duration**”

A strong answer should have said these are **possible hypotheses**, not evidence-backed conclusions.
