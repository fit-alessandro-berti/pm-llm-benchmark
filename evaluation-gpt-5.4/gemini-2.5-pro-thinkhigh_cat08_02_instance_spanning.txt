Problems:
- With ordinary START/COMPLETE logs, that is often **not enough to infer true preemption**.
- If the same resource is simultaneously attached to both orders, that may indicate:
  - logging noise,
  - multitasking,
  - resource misassignment,
  - missing suspend/resume events,
  - coarse timestamps,

- True preemption generally requires explicit **pause/resume** semantics or a much more careful reconstruction assumption.
