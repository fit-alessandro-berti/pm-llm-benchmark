Attributes that are sensitive for fairness are those that can be used to discriminate against certain groups of individuals. In this case, the following attributes are sensitive for fairness:

  * gender
  * private_insurance
  * underlying_condition
  * citizen
  * resource

These attributes are sensitive because they can be used to discriminate against certain groups of individuals. For example, the gender attribute can be used to discriminate against men or women, the private_insurance attribute can be used to discriminate against individuals with private insurance or those without, the underlying_condition attribute can be used to discriminate against individuals with underlying conditions or those without, the citizen attribute can be used to discriminate against citizens or non-citizens, and the resource attribute can be used to discriminate against different types of resources.
