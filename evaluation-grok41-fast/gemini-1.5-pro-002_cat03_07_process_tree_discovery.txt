Fitness = 0 for all traces; precision critically low due to overgeneration.
- **Major flaw in inner loop (RMI not optional, wrong repetitions)**: Inner `* ( ->(AE, RMI), VF )` mandates RMI after every AE (as part of "A"), then optionally VFrepeat.

  - Case 3 (RAVFAERMIRMIVF...): Impossible; cannot produce consecutive RMI without mandatory VFAERMI in between. Only allows RMI after AE, VF only as loop "B".
