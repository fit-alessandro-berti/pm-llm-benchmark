### Evaluation Rationale (Hypercritical Breakdown)
This grading is based on utmost strictness, deducting heavily for **any** inaccuracies (factual errors in timestamps, calculations, or rule interpretations), unclarities (vague phrasing), logical flaws (missed violations, incorrect assumptions), incompleteness (omitting key violations or desiderata), and deviations from the task (e.g., adding unrequested "recommendations" while underdelivering on "interpretations/justifications"). Perfect score requires **zero** issues; even one minor flaw demands major deduction. Here, **multiple major flaws** (e.g., missed normative violation, factual errors) dominate, with partial strengths insufficient to offset.

#### **Strengths (Minor Credits, ~+3.2 base)**
- **Structure**: Clear per-case breakdown, summary, and distinction between normative/desiderata. Matches task format well.
- **Case 1**: Nearly flawless—correctly identifies full normative compliance, appropriate desiderata notes (prompt timings, minor follow-up skip).
