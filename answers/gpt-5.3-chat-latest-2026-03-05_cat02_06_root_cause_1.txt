2. Potential root causes of performance issues

Escalations to Level2 support
- Case 102 and 105 both include "Escalate to Level2 Agent".
- These cases are among the slowest.
- Escalation introduces additional waiting before investigation or resolution.

Delayed start of investigation

Overnight waiting periods
- Many long delays occur overnight before the resolution step.
- This suggests tickets are left unresolved until the next working day.

3. How these factors increase cycle time

Escalations introduce queueing delays  
Level2 teams likely handle fewer agents and a larger backlog. Once escalated, tickets wait in another queue before investigation resumes, increasing total cycle time.

Idle time between activities  
Long gaps (several hours) between assignment, investigation, and resolution indicate tickets waiting in queues rather than being actively worked.

Operational cutoffs / shift boundaries  
The frequent pattern of “investigate in afternoon  resolve next morning” suggests work stops at end-of-day, causing overnight delays.

4. Insights and recommendations

Improve Level2 escalation handling
- Add capacity or dedicated escalation queues.
- Introduce SLAs for starting investigation after escalation.

Start investigation sooner after assignment
- Reduce queue times for Level1 agents.
- Use workload balancing or automatic ticket routing.

Resolve tickets the same day when investigation is completed
- Encourage immediate resolution if investigation finishes before end of shift.

Prequalification during triage
- Better triage could route complex tickets directly to Level2, avoiding wasted Level1 handling and later escalation.

Monitor waiting-time KPIs
- Track:
  - Assign  Investigate delay
  - Escalate  Investigate delay
  - Investigate  Resolve delay

Key pattern summary:
The primary drivers of long cycle times are Level2 escalations and long idle waiting periods (often overnight) between investigation and resolution steps. Reducing queue delays and improving escalation handling would likely shorten overall ticket resolution times.
