### **Key Constraint Justifications**  
1. **`absence` for `Proceed_Without_Compliance`**:  
   - **Ethical Rationale**: Prohibits bypassing compliance steps, avoiding regulatory violations or unfair practices.  

2. **`exactly_one` for `Preliminary_Preliminary_PCredit_Check`**:  
   - **Ethical Rationale**: Prevents unnecessary multiple checks, reducing customer friction and preventing bias in repeated assessments.  

3. **`coexistence` (Gather & Authorize)**:  
   - **Ethical Rationale**: Ensures all docs and terms are validated before final steps, preventing rushed approvals.  

4. **`noncoexistence` (Transfer & Receive_Application)**:  
   - **Ethical Rationale**: Prevents premature disbursement, protecting customer funds.

### **Consequences of Violating Constraints**  
- **Skipping `Quality_Assurance_Review`**:  
  - **Risk**: Non-compliant loan terms could mislead customers or violate regulations.  

- **Omitting `Preliminary_Preliminary_PCredit_Check`**:  
  - **Risk**: Approving high-risk loans, leading to defaults and financial instability for the lender.  

- **Reversing `Authorize` and `Transfer_Funds`**:  
  - **Risk**: Funds could be sent without final approval, causing operational and legal issues.  

- **Bypassing `Notify_Customer`**:  
  - **Risk**: Lack of transparency erodes trust and could be grounds for legal action.
