**`-> ( RA, VF, AE, * ( tau, -> ( RMI, VF ) ), AD, SC, AC )`**

*   **Fitness:** The model allows for the linear path (Case 2) while accommodating the nested cycles of `RMI` and `VF` (Cases 1 and 3).
