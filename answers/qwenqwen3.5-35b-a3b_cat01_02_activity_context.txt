    *   **Clue:** The context in the second instance explicitly mentions "re-checks" (C001) or "Re-verify" (C003). Renaming these distinguishes the **standard gate** from the **exception handling loop**.

*   **"Clarification Call" vs. "Confirmation Call"**:

    *   **Clue:** The context keywords "clarify" (investigation) versus "confirm" (validation) dictate different process intents. Differentiating these helps identify whether the call is a **problem-solving step** or a **quality assurance step**.

*   **Consistency**:
