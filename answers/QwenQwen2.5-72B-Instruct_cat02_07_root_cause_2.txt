   - **High-complexity cases** often require multiple requests for additional documents, which can significantly extend the process.
   - **Medium-complexity cases** can also lead to extended durations, especially if they involve multiple requests for additional documents.

#### Mitigation Strategies
1. **Resource Management:**
   - **Redistribute workload:** Ensure that high-complexity cases are evenly distributed among resources to avoid overloading any single CSR or Adjuster.
   - **Training and support:** Provide additional training for resources handling complex cases to improve their efficiency.

2. **Region-Specific Improvements:**
   - **Hire additional staff:** Increase the number of resources in Region B to handle the higher volume of complex cases.
   - **Process audits:** Conduct regular audits of the claims process in Region B to identify and address bottlenecks.

3. **Complexity Management:**
   - **Streamline document requests:** Implement a more efficient system for requesting and tracking additional documents to reduce the number of requests and the time they take.
   - **Pre-approval checks:** Introduce pre-approval checks to ensure that all necessary information is provided during the initial submission, reducing the need for additional document requests.

By addressing these root causes, the insurance company can significantly reduce the lead times for claims processing and improve overall customer satisfaction.
