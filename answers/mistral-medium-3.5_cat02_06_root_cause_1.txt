#### **3. Recommendations to Reduce Cycle Times**
| **Issue** | **Root Cause** | **Recommended Action** |
|-----------|----------------|------------------------|
| **Escalation Delays** | Slow hand-off to Level-2 | - **Automate escalation triggers** (e.g., if investigation exceeds 2h, auto-escalate). <br> - **Prioritize Level-2 tickets** with SLAs (e.g., must start within 1h of escalation). |
| **Long Investigation Times** | Complex issues, external dependencies | - **Implement time limits** (e.g., if investigation >4h, require supervisor review). <br> - **Provide better documentation** to reduce troubleshooting time. <br> - **Pre-validate customer info** to avoid back-and-forth. |
| **Overnight Stalls** | No after-hours support | - **Introduce shift rotations** for critical tickets. <br> - **Use chatbots/self-service** for simple issues outside business hours. <br> - **Set expectations** (e.g., "Non-urgent tickets may take 24h"). |
| **Unnecessary Delays Before Escalation** | Level-1 agents spending too long | - **Train Level-1 agents** to recognize when to escalate early. <br> - **Use AI triage** to auto-route complex tickets to Level-2. |
