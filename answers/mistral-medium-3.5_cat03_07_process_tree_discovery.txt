### Why This Works:
- The loop captures the optional repetition of `RMI  VF` (observed in Cases 1 and 3).
- The silent step (`tau`) allows the loop to exit when no more repetitions are needed (as in Case 2).
- The rest of the sequence (`AD  SC  AC`) is fixed and follows the loop.

This model balances **fitness** (allows all observed traces), **precision** (does not allow unrealistic traces like `RA  AE`), and **simplicity** (avoids overcomplicating the loop).
