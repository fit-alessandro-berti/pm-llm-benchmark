### Explanation of Constraints:
1. **Equivalence**: No activities require equivalent occurrence counts, so this is an empty set.
2. **Always Before**: Ensures prerequisite activities are executed before dependent ones.
   - `('CA', 'RO')`: Availability check must follow order receipt.
   - `('RI', 'CA')`: Item reservation requires prior availability check.
   - `('PI', 'RI')`: Picking items requires prior reservation.
   - `('QC', 'PI')`: Quality check requires prior picking.
   - `('PO', 'QC')`: Packing requires prior quality check.
   - `('GSL', 'PO')`: Shipping label generation requires prior packing.
   - `('D', 'GSL')`: Dispatch requires prior label generation.
   - `('IC', 'PO')` and `('IC', 'D')`: Invoicing requires prior packing and dispatch.
   - `('RP', 'IC')`: Payment recording requires prior invoicing.
3. **Always After**: Ensures subsequent activities occur after prerequisites.
   - Core sequence: Order receipt  Availability check  Reservation  Picking  Quality check  Packing  Label generation  Dispatch.
   - Post-dispatch: Dispatch  Invoicing  Payment recording.
4. **Never Together**: No mutually exclusive activities identified, so this is empty.
5. **Activity Occurrences**: Each activity occurs at most once per case (0 or 1 times), as the process is linear without repetitions.
6. **Directly Follows**: Enforces immediate succession in the core fulfillment sequence:
   - Order receipt  Availability check  Reservation  Picking  Quality check  Packing  Label generation  Dispatch.

This Log Skeleton models the order fulfillment process as a linear sequence with mandatory prerequisites and post-dispatch activities (invoicing/payment) occurring after dispatch.
