## **Step 5: Recommendations to Reduce Delays**

### **A. Improve Escalation Workflow**
- Ensure escalated tickets are **routed to available specialists** with clear SLAs.
- Implement **escalation notifications** and track response times.

### **B. Reduce Idle Time Between Steps**
- Automate notifications when a ticket is assigned or escalated.
- Introduce **SLA tracking** for each activity to flag delays in real time.

### **C. Investigate Recurring Long Investigation Periods**
- Analyze if certain types of issues require longer investigation.
- Provide **knowledge base access** or **decision support tools** to agents during investigation.

### **D. Resource Allocation**
- Level-1 agents may be overloaded if they frequently escalate.
- Consider **load balancing** or **temporary staffing** during peak times.

### **E. Monitor and Benchmark**
- Track metrics like:
  - Time to escalate
  - Time from escalation to next activity
  - Investigation-to-resolution time
- Use this to identify systemic issues and drive process improvements.
