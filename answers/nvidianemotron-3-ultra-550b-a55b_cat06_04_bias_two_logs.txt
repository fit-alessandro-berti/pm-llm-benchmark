### 5. Conclusion: Type of Bias
This is **Institutional/Algorithmic Bias** resulting in **Disparate Impact**.

*   **Protected Attribute:** `LocalResident = FALSE` (defining Group A).
*   **Mechanism:** A hard-coded rule in the `Scoring Engine`: `IF LocalResident AND CommunityGroup == "Highland Civic Darts Club" THEN Score += 10`.
*   **Outcome:** **Systematic score inflation for the Unprotected Group**, causing **crossover approvals** (lower-scoring Unprotected applicants approved over higher-scoring Protected applicants).
*   **Fairness Violation:** **Equalized Odds / Calibration** violated. The score means different things for different groups (A score of 705 means "Approved" for Group B but is unattainable/means "Rejected" for Group A). **Individual Fairness** violated (P002 vs U003).

### Recommendation for Remediation
1.  **Remove `CommunityGroup` from Scoring Logic:** Social club membership is not a legitimate creditworthiness factor.
2.  **Decouple `LocalResident` from Adjustments:** If a "Local Resident Boost" exists, it must apply to *all* residents equally (but Group A is defined as `LocalResident=FALSE`, suggesting they *cannot* be residents).
3.  **Audit Thresholds:** Validate the 705–710 decision boundary. If 705 is approvable, P002 (710) should never have been rejected.
