Using the most reasonable reading of the description, the intended process has **two valid paths** after checking inventory:

1. **Fulfillment path**  
   **Receive order  Check inventory  Authorize payment  Package items  Ship order  Close order**

2. **Cancellation path**  
   **Receive order  Check inventory  Cancel order  Close order**

Key rules implied by the text:
- **Receive order** starts the case.
- **Check inventory** must happen before payment or fulfillment actions.
- **Authorize payment** must occur **after Check inventory** and **before Package items**.
- **Package items** must occur **before Ship order**.
- **Ship order** must **never happen after Cancel order**.
- **Close order** must be the final step.
