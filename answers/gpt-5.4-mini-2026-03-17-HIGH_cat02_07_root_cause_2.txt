## 3) Why these attributes cause delays

### Complexity
High-complexity claims usually require:
- more validation
- more missing information checks
- more approval scrutiny
- more back-and-forth with the customer

### Additional-document loops
Each request creates:
- customer waiting time
- internal idle time
- rework after documents are received

### Resource / region

- workload imbalance
- different handling styles
- queue delays
- regional process variations

## 4) Suggested mitigation actions

### For high-complexity claims
- Route them to specialized adjusters early
- Add a **fast-track triage** step to identify high-risk claims upfront
- Use stricter initial intake checks to reduce missing documents

### For document-request delays
- Implement a **submission checklist**
- Use automated validation for missing fields/docs
- Send proactive reminders to claimants
- Add SLA monitoring for overdue document responses

### For resource/region management
- Balance workload across adjusters and managers
- Compare processing times by team/region over a larger sample
- Standardize procedures across regions
- Investigate whether certain teams have more approval backlog
