Justification of naming choices:

- Verify Documents  Initial Document Verification / Document ReVerification  
  Context clue: the first occurrence happens immediately after request submission (initial check), while later occurrences happen after new information or corrections (re-check). In C003 the second verification explicitly follows an account correction.

- Call Customer  Contextspecific call purposes  
  The call activity serves different intents:
  - Request Missing Documents (C001) when verification fails.
  - Clarify Discrepancy (C002) during investigation.
  - Confirm Resolution (C002) as a follow-up after internal adjustment.
  - Request Return to Branch (C003) because the customer temporarily left.
  Each name reflects the business reason for the contact.

- Initiate Request  Submission channel specific  
  Renamed to Submit Request (Online) and Submit Request (Branch WalkIn) to distinguish digital selfservice initiation from inperson initiation.

- Adjust Account  Contextual internal change  
  Renamed based on the trigger:
  - PostInquiry Adjustment (after discrepancy clarification).
  - Data Correction (fixing missing or incorrect information).

- Approve Request  Final Approval  
  Clarifies that this step represents the final decision in the workflow rather than a preliminary approval.

These renamings make the event log semantically clearer and improve downstream process mining by preventing different contextual actions from being conflated under the same activity label.
