### Why these additions reduce bias
- **Sensitive checks must trigger a fairness control** (`response` from `CheckApplicantRace/Gender/Age` to `BiasMitigationCheck`).
- **Decisions require prior bias control** (`precedence` from `BiasMitigationCheck` to decision events), so decisions are not made before fairness validation.
- **No immediate decision right after sensitive-attribute inspection** (`nonchainsuccession`), reducing snap judgments linked to protected attributes.
- **Sensitive-group handling requires human oversight** (`coexistence`/`responded_existence` with `SensitiveApplicantFlag` and `ManualReview`, plus `precedence` for `Approve_Minority`/`Reject_Minority`).
- **Manual reviews cannot be skipped to closure** (`response` from `ManualReview` to `FinalDecision`).
