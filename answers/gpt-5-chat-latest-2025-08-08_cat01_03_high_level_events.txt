## **Step 2 – High-Level Grouping Rules**

The grouping criteria I used:

1. **Process Objective** – Events that together achieve the same production goal get grouped together.
2. **Temporal Proximity** – Events occur in quick succession without unrelated tasks.
3. **Resource & Role Alignment** – Events are often performed by similar resources (Operator(s) vs. machine(s)) at that stage.
4. **Manufacturing Domain Knowledge** – I map the sequence to standard process phases in metal fabrication.
