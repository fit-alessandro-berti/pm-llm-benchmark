A correct process tree for your scenario is:

\[
\rightarrow(
'A',
'RG',
'IA',
'TI',
'TC',
X('CE','FBP'),
*(\tau,\ \rightarrow('SC',X('TM','SPT'),'RDT',X('FCE','FBA'))),

### Notes
- `X('CE','FBP')`: exclusive initial diagnostic choice.
- `*(tau, ->(...))`: models **zero or more** abnormal-result cycles after initial diagnosis.
- Inside the loop: `SC -> (TM xor SPT) -> RDT -> (FCE xor FBA)`.
- `+('AI','NC')`: imaging and nutrition in true concurrency.
- `+('BL','ICP','MRF')`: final financial/admin tasks in true concurrency.
