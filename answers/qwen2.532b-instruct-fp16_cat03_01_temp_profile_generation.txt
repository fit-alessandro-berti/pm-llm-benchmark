A hypothetical Purchase-to-Pay (P2P) process typically involves activities like initiating a purchase request, approving it, ordering goods or services, receiving them, and then making the payment. Let's outline some activities and create a temporal profile based on typical time intervals between these actions.

- `A`: Initiating Purchase Request
- `B`: Approval of Purchase Request
- `C`: Order Placement
- `D`: Receipt Confirmation
- `E`: Invoice Verification
- `F`: Payment Processing
