12. What key performance indicators (KPIs) are used to measure the efficiency of the entire end-to-end process, and how are they balanced against resilience metrics (like supply chain risk exposure)?
13. What is the estimated carbon footprint of this specific logistics network, and how might redesigning the parallel sourcing or distribution paths contribute to sustainability goals?
14. How does the process incorporate feedback from end-customers (e.g., return rates, repair data) back into the R&D and prototype design phase to drive iterative improvement?
15. What is the strategy for managing intellectual property and secure data transfer across the multiple international partners and jurisdictions involved in this chain?
16. If a critical component from a single source (e.g., a specific sensor) becomes unavailable, what is the predefined protocol? Does the process include a parallel task for qualifying alternative suppliers?
17. How is the “24/7 Operation” at the assembly plant scheduled and staffed to maintain quality and worker safety, and what metrics monitor potential burnout or quality drift over time?
18. What visibility do downstream stakeholders (like regional distributors) have into upstream activities (like component sourcing delays), and how does this shared view impact their local planning?
19. Considering the rapid pace of smartphone technology, how frequently is this entire end-to-end process map reviewed and revised, and who owns that governance?
