**Justification of Naming Choices**

*   **Initial vs. Re-verify Documents**: The prefix "Initial" denotes the first compliance check, while "Re-verify" explicitly signals a subsequent check triggered by new information (additional documents in **C001**, account adjustments in **C003**). The parenthetical clarifiers *(Post-Submission)* and *(Post-Correction)* capture the specific triggering event, distinguishing between customer-driven resubmission and internal system corrections.
*   **Clarify vs. Confirm (Call Customer)**: In **C002**, the first call is labeled **Clarify Discrepancy** to indicate it is an information-gathering step that precedes and triggers the *Adjust Account* activity. The second call is labeled **Confirm Adjustments** to indicate it is a validation step occurring after the internal work is complete. This captures the causal sequence (pre-adjustment vs. post-adjustment) and the communicative intent (investigation vs. confirmation).
