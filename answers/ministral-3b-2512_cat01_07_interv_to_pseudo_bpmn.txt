### **Invoice Approval Process Model**
*(Textual BPMN Representation)*

#### **Roles & Participants**
1. **Accounts Payable (AP) Clerk (Mary)** – Receives invoices, validates PO, logs entries, forwards to Purchasing.
2. **Purchasing Officer (Dan)** – Validates invoice against PO, resolves discrepancies (e.g., missing PO, pricing issues).
3. **Department Manager (Carlos)** – Final approval authority; reviews for budget/suspicious items.
4. **Accounts Payable Supervisor (Rita)** – Escalates unresolved exceptions (e.g., no PO, informal contracts).
5. **Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen)** – Mediates supplier disputes (e.g., missing PO, pricing errors).

---

#### **Process Flow (Main Path)**
```
Start  [1] AP Clerk (Mary) receives invoice (email)
     [1a] Check for valid PO:
        * If **no PO**: Send to supplier for details (or escalate to Rita if informal).
        * If **PO exists**: Proceed to [1b].
     [1b] Log invoice in system  Forward to Purchasing (Dan) for receipt confirmation.
     [2] Purchasing (Dan) validates invoice against PO:
        * If **discrepancies** (e.g., missing PO, pricing errors):
            - If **resolvable by supplier**: Karen may intervene; Dan sends corrected invoice back.
            - If **unresolvable**: Escalate to Rita for action (e.g., reject, create retrospective PO).
        * If **matches PO**: Confirm to AP Clerk  [3] AP Clerk matches invoice line items to PO.
     [3] AP Clerk matches invoice to PO:
        * If **no discrepancies**: Forward to Manager (Carlos) for approval.
        * If **discrepancies**: Escalate to Rita (if unresolved).
     [4] Manager (Carlos) approves/rejects:
        * If **approves**: Invoice moves to AP for payment scheduling.
        * If **rejects**: Escalate to Rita for further review.
     [5] AP Clerk schedules payment  [End].
```

---

#### **Gateways/Decision Points**
1. **PO Validation (1a)**
   - **Gateway Type**: Exclusive (XOR) – Only one path (with PO or no PO).
   - *Note*: Rita handles informal contracts (no PO).

2. **Purchasing Validation (2)**
   - **Gateway Type**: Parallel (AND) – Multiple checks (PO reference, quantities, pricing).
   - *Trigger*: Automatic email to Dan after AP logs invoice.

3. **Discrepancy Resolution (2/3)**
   - **Gateway Type**: Exclusive – Resolve via supplier (Karen) or escalate to Rita.

4. **Manager Approval (4)**
   - **Gateway Type**: Exclusive – Approve/reject based on budget/suspicion.

---

#### **Exceptions & Variations**
1. **No PO Handling**
   - *Rita* coordinates with department heads to create a retrospective PO or reject the invoice.
   - *Karen* may contact suppliers for corrected invoices.

2. **Unresolvable Discrepancies**
   - Escalated to Rita; may require supplier corrections or rejection.

3. **Budget/Suspicious Items**
   - Manager (Carlos) may reject for further investigation.

4. **Informal Contracts**
   - Rita determines if invoice should be rejected or matched to a PO retrospectively.

---
#### **Artifacts/Inputs/Outputs**
- **Inputs**:
  - Invoice (email), Purchase Order (PO), Supplier contact details.
- **Outputs**:
  - System logs (AP Clerk), confirmation emails (Purchasing), approval notifications (Manager), payment scheduling (AP Clerk).
- **Data Objects**:
  - Invoice line items, PO details, budget comparisons.

This process is **mostly linear** with **3 key divergence points**:
1. PO validation (with Rita’s informal contract handling).
2. Discrepancy resolution (Karen or Rita).
