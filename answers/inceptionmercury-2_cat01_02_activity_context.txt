**1.  Activities that repeat inside a single case**

| Activity (original) | How many times per case? | What changes? (position / context) |
|---------------------|--------------------------|------------------------------------|
| **Verify Documents** | 2 (C001, C002, C003) | The first occurrence is the *initial* verification. The second occurrence happens **after** something else has been supplied (extra ID, an account correction, etc.) and therefore is a *reverification* or *postadjustment check*. |
| **Call Customer**   | 2 (C001, C002, C003) | The first call is made for **different reasons** (missing documents, clarifying a discrepancy, or getting a walkin customer back). The second call (when present) is a **followup / confirmation** after the earlier action. |

All other activities (Initiate Request, Submit Additional, Adjust Account, Approve Request) appear only once per case, so their meaning stays stable and they do not need disambiguation.

**4.  Justification of the new names**

| New activity name | Contextual clue(s) used for renaming |
|-------------------|--------------------------------------|
| **Verify Documents ñ Initial Identity Check** | First occurrence; no prior documents have been supplied. |
| **Verify Documents ñ Reverify after Additional Docs** | Second occurrence follows the ìSubmit Additionalî step where the customer uploads missing ID. |
| **Verify Documents ñ Identity Verification** | Single verification in C002; the generic ìidentity verificationî wording matches the log note. |
| **Verify Documents ñ Physical ID Check** | Performed by a BranchOfficer on a walkin customer, indicating a physical, onsite check. |
| **Verify Documents ñ Reverify after Account Adjustment** | Second verification after an internal ìAdjust Accountî step, implying a recheck due to changed account data. |
| **Call Customer ñ Request Missing Documents** | First call in C001 is triggered because the initial document check was incomplete. |
| **Call Customer ñ Clarify Account Discrepancy** | First call in C002 is explicitly described as ìto clarify account discrepancyî. |
| **Call Customer ñ Retrieve Customer from Branch** | First call in C003 occurs when the walkin customer steps out; the officer calls to get them back. |
| **Call Customer ñ Followup Confirmation** | Second call in C002 occurs after the account adjustment, serving as a confirmation/followup. |

These renamed activities now carry the *why* and *when* that were previously hidden inside the ìAdditional Contextî column, making the process model far easier to interpret, analyze, and later use for conformance checking or predictive analytics.
