### **Recommendations for Improvement**
1. **Enforce Strict Approval Workflows:**
   - **Purchase requisitions must be approved before PO issuance.**
   - Implement **mandatory approval gates** in the system.

2. **Three-Way Match Enforcement:**
   - **Never release payments** until:
     - Goods are received.
     - Invoice is matched to the PO.
     - All discrepancies are resolved.

3. **Receipt Controls:**
   - **Block goods receipt** until a PO is issued (or at least a requisition is approved).
   - Use **barcode/RFID tracking** to ensure receipts align with POs.

4. **Audit Trails & Separation of Duties:**
   - Ensure **different roles** handle:
     - Requisition approval.
     - PO issuance.
     - Goods receipt.
     - Invoice processing.
     - Payment release.
   - Implement **automated alerts** for deviations (e.g., payment before receipt).

5. **Training & Compliance:**
   - Train staff on **P2P best practices** to prevent manual errors.
   - Conduct **regular audits** to catch anomalies early.
