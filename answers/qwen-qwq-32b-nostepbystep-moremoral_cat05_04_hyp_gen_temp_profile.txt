### Verification Queries  
**1. Claims with Abnormal R  P Durations (Low Variability):**  
```sql  
SELECT claim_id, (EXTRACT(EPOCH FROM (p.timestamp - r.timestamp)) AS duration_seconds  
FROM claim_events r  
JOIN claim_events p ON r.claim_id = p.claim_id  
WHERE r.activity = 'R' AND p.activity = 'P'  
HAVING ABS(duration_seconds - 90000) < 3600; -- Checking for durations within 1 hour of the mean  
```  
**Ethical Note:** This identifies overly rigid processes, ensuring no claims are excluded due to systemic bias.  

**2. P  N Delays Exceeding 9 Days:**  
```sql  
SELECT claim_id, (EXTRACT(EPOCH FROM (n.timestamp - p.timestamp)) AS delay_seconds  
FROM claim_events p  
JOIN claim_events n ON p.claim_id = n.claim_id  
WHERE p.activity = 'P' AND n.activity = 'N'  
AND (n.timestamp - p.timestamp) > (7*24*60*60 + 2*(2*24*60*60)); -- 7 days + 2*STDEV  
```  
**Ethical Note:** Focuses on systemic delays rather than blaming individuals.
