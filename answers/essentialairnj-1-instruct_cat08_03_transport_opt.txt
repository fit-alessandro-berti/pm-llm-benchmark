   - Vehicle Utilization Rate
   - Frequency/Delay of Unplanned Stops due to Vehicle Problems.
   - Rate of Failed Deliveries / Delay analysis

   **Technique to Identify Bottlenecks:**  
   - **Process Mining Tools:** Use Simulators, Process Fragment Analysis, Blinking Bottleneck Analysis to identify inefficiencies.
