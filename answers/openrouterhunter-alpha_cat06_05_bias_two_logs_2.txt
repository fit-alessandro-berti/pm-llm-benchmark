1.  **Undermining Meritocracy:** The stated goal of a multi-stage hiring process (skill tests, assessments, interviews) is to select the best candidate based on measurable qualifications. The association boost corrupts this goal by inserting a non-meritocratic factor that has nothing to do with job performance. It privileges social or professional networks over demonstrable ability.
