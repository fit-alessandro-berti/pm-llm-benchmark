This DECLARE model captures:

1. **Precedence/Succession**: Sequential flow from idea generation to final launch
2. **Exactly One**: Only one activity from each stage can be active at a time
3. **Coexistence**: Activities that can happen in parallel (e.g., lab testing can occur while marketing plans are being developed)
4. **Response**: Causal dependencies between activities
5. **Chain Response/Precedence/Succession**: Multi-step causal relationships
6. **Alternative Response/Precedence/Succession**: Either/or relationships between activities
7. **Absence/Non-Coexistence**: Activities that cannot occur together or after certain other activities
8. **Init**: Only Idea Generation can be the starting point

The model reflects the logical sequential flow of the product development process while allowing for some parallel activities and alternative decision paths.
