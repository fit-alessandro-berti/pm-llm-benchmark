#### 3. **Explanations and Mitigation Suggestions**

**Why high complexity causes delays:**  
- Complex claims require more evidence (medical reports, police reports, detailed invoices).  
- Multiple rounds of document requests are needed if submissions are incomplete.  
- Managers may take longer to review and approve high-value or high-risk claims.

**Why region might indirectly affect performance:**  
- Different regions may have varying levels of staffing, expertise, or claim volumes.  
- Regional regulations might require different documentation.

**Proposed Improvements:**  
1. **Streamline documentation for high-complexity claims:**  
   - Use checklists upfront to reduce back-and-forth requests.  
   - Allow secure portal uploads to speed up submission.  

2. **Reduce waiting times for additional documents:**  
   - Set clear deadlines for claimants to submit documents (e.g., 24-hour SLA).  
   - Automate reminders via SMS/email.  

3. **Balance resource workload:**  
   - Ensure adjusters like Mike and Lisa are not overloaded with multiple high-complexity cases simultaneously.  
   - Cross-train adjusters to handle complex cases efficiently.  

4. **Expedite approval for complex cases:**  
   - Implement a priority queue for cases with multiple document requests.  
   - Provide managers with dashboards highlighting delayed approvals.  

5. **Regional performance reviews:**  
   - Monitor lead times by region to identify systemic issues (e.g., training gaps, staffing shortages).
