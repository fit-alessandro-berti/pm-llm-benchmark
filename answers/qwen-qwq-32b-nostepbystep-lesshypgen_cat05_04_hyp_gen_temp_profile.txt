### Verification SQL Queries  

#### 1. **R  P Anomaly**  
**Query:**  
```sql  
SELECT  
    c.claim_id,  
    EXTRACT(EPOCH FROM (p.timestamp - r.timestamp) AS duration_seconds,  
    a1.specialization AS assigner_specialization,  
    a2.region AS approver_region  
FROM claim_events r  
JOIN claim_events p ON r.claim_id = p.claim_id  
JOIN claim_events a ON r.claim_id = a.claim_id AND a.activity = 'A'  
JOIN adjusters a1 ON a.resource = a1.name -- Assuming 'resource' holds adjuster names  
JOIN adjusters a2 ON p.resource = a2.name -- approver's region  
WHERE r.activity = 'R' AND p.activity = 'P'  
AND p.timestamp > r.timestamp;  
```  
**Purpose:** Identify claims with durations tightly clustered around 25 hours and correlate with adjuster specialization or region.  

---

#### 2. **P  N Anomaly**  
**Query:**  
```sql  
SELECT  
    c.claim_id,  
    EXTRACT(EPOCH FROM (n.timestamp - p.timestamp) AS duration_days,  
    a.specialization,  
    a.region  
FROM claim_events p  
JOIN claim_events n ON p.claim_id = n.claim_id  
JOIN adjusters a ON n.resource = a.name -- notifier's role  
WHERE p.activity = 'P' AND n.activity = 'N'  
AND n.timestamp > p.timestamp  
HAVING duration_days > (604800 + 2*172800) OR duration_days < (604800 - 2*172800);  
```  
**Purpose:** Flag extreme durations and link to adjuster specialization or region.
