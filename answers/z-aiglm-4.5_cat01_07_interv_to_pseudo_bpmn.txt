#### **Start Event:**  
[Start] Invoice Received (by Accounts Payable via email)

---

#### **Main Flow:**
1. **Task:** Check for Valid PO Number  
   - **Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary)  
   - **Artifact:** Invoice  
   - **Sequence Flow:** Followed immediately after start event.  
   - **Decision Gateway:** XOR (PO Valid?)  
     - **No:**  
       - **Task:** Send Back to Supplier (request PO/details)  
         - **Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk  
         - **Artifact:** Invoice  
         - **Output:** Supplier
