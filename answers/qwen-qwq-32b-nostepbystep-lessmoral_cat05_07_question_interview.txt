### **Documentation & Initial Verification**

---

### **Property Manager Assignment**  
3. How do you weigh the factors (workload, geographic expertise, experience, and landlord preferences) when assigning a property manager? Are there prioritization rules if these criteria conflict?  
4. What happens if the most qualified property manager is unavailable? Is there a backup process, or does the timeline get adjusted?  

---

### **Rental Pricing & Market Analysis**  
5. How is the initial rental price range determined? Are there specific benchmarks or tools used to assess market trends, and how often are these updated?  
6. How do you handle situations where a landlord insists on a price higher than market rates?  

---

### **Inspection & Repairs**  
7. What criteria determine whether an inspection issue is critical enough to halt the onboarding process until resolved? How is urgency assessed?  
8. How do you manage cases where a landlord refuses to address inspection findings? Is there a formal escalation process?  

---

### **Tenant Selection & Lease Negotiation**  
9. When multiple qualified applicants apply, what non-financial criteria (e.g., pet policies, lease duration) are most influential in tenant selection, and how are trade-offs handled?  
10. How do you navigate conflicts between a landlord’s preferences (e.g., strict no-pets policy) and tenant demand for flexibility?  

---

### **Compliance & Exceptions**  
11. How do you handle properties requiring specialized compliance (e.g., historic preservation rules or unique zoning restrictions) that fall outside standard protocols?  
12. When a landlord proposes a custom lease clause, what steps are taken to ensure it complies with fair housing laws and company policies?  

---

### **Timeline & Bottlenecks**  
13. Are there predefined timelines for each step (e.g., document review, inspection turnaround), and how do delays in one step affect the overall onboarding timeline?  
14. What are the most common causes of delays, and how does the team mitigate them?  

---

### **Communication & Stakeholder Coordination**  
15. How is communication tracked and escalated between the regional office, landlords, and managers? Are there tools that consolidate updates for all stakeholders?  
16. How do you ensure consistency in communication when multiple teams (e.g., compliance, finance, marketing) are involved in final approvals?  

---

### **Quality Checks & Post-Onboarding**  
17. What specific compliance areas does the compliance officer review in lease agreements, and what are the consequences of non-compliance findings?  
18. After a property is activated, how do you audit whether the onboarding process met internal standards (e.g., time-to-market, compliance, tenant satisfaction)?  

---

### **Decision-Making & Trade-offs**  
19. When there’s a disagreement between the landlord and property manager (e.g., on marketing budget or tenant selection), what steps are taken to resolve it?

---

### **Process Adaptability**  
21. How does the team adapt the process for unique property types (e.g., high-rise condos vs. rural rentals) that don’t fit standard workflows?  
22. What triggers a re-evaluation of the onboarding process itself? For example, after a property faces repeated tenant complaints post-onboarding?  

---

### **Role Clarity & Accountability**  
23. Who owns the final decision to proceed to the next step (e.g., moving forward despite incomplete documents), and what authority do coordinators vs. managers have in approvals?  
24. What defines a property’s “activation” in the rental portal—is it purely technical (e.g., data entry) or does it require sign-offs from multiple teams?  

---

### **Risk Management**  
25. How do you handle properties in areas with volatile rental markets, where pricing models might rapidly shift during onboarding?  
26. What safeguards are in place if a property manager misses a critical step (e.g., failing to vet an applicant’s income thoroughly)?
