The temporal profile is constructed by simulating the typical delays across various stages of the supply chain. The time estimates are expressed in seconds, using realistic scales (e.g., a day is 86400 seconds, an hour is 3600 seconds).

We will categorize the estimated delays into three types of time spans to showcase the complexity:
1. **Short-Term Operational Delays (Hours):** Steps that happen quickly once materials are available.
2. **Medium-Term Logistical Delays (Days):** Waiting times, inspections, and assembly periods.
3. **Long-Term Strategic Delays (Weeks/Months):** Procurement, cross-continental shipping, and market cycles.
