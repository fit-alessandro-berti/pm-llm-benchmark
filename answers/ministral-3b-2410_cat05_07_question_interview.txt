1. **Documentation Review:**
   - Can you describe the specific criteria used to determine if the documentation provided by the landlord is sufficient and up-to-date?
   - What happens if the property coordinator identifies discrepancies or missing documents during the initial review?

2. **Property Manager Assignment:**
   - How is the workload of the property managers considered when assigning a new property?
   - What specific factors are used to match a property manager with a particular property type or landlord requirements?
   - How does the company ensure that the assigned property manager has the necessary experience and expertise for the property?

3. **Initial Meeting:**
   - What are the key points typically discussed during the introductory call or meeting between the property manager and the landlord?
   - How does the company handle landlords who have unique requirements or special conditions?

4. **Property Inspection and Photography:**
   - What are the specific safety and habitability standards that the certified inspector checks for during the property visit?
   - How does the company ensure that the photographs taken by the professional photographer accurately represent the property?

5. **Marketing and Pricing:**
   - What role does the digital marketing team play in creating online listings and preparing brochures?
   - How are the internal pricing models updated based on current market trends?

6. **Tenant Screening:**
   - What are the main criteria used during the tenant screening process?
   - How does the company handle multiple tenant applications and negotiate lease terms?

7. **Lease Agreement and Move-In:**
   - What steps are involved in finalizing the lease agreement and collecting the security deposit?
   - How is the move-in date scheduled and communicated to the tenant?

8. **Quality Checks and Audits:**
   - What specific checks are performed by the compliance officer to ensure adherence to fair housing regulations?
   - How does the financial analyst verify that the agreed rental price matches the company’s pricing strategy and profitability targets?

9. **Exception Handling:**
   - How does the company handle properties that require special insurance or landlords who insist on custom lease clauses?
   - What are the standard procedures for addressing any issues or exceptions that arise during the onboarding process?

10. **Communication and Tracking:**
    - How is communication tracked between regional offices, landlords, property managers, and support teams?
    - What tools are used to manage and track the various stages of the onboarding process?

11. **Decision Points and Sequencing:**
    - Can you provide an example of a decision point where the process could diverge or require additional steps?
    - How is the sequencing of tasks and responsibilities managed to ensure a smooth onboarding process?

12. **Training and Support:**
    - What kind of training is provided to property managers and other stakeholders involved in the onboarding process?
    - How does the company support property managers and landlords throughout the onboarding process?

These questions should help clarify the various aspects of the property onboarding process, including roles, responsibilities, decision criteria, and exception handling.
