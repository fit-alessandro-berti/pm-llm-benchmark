**BPMN-like Textual Process Model: Invoice Approval Process**

---

**1. Start Event**
- **Description:** Invoice received (typically by email).
- **Trigger:** External event (supplier sends invoice).

---

**2. Activity: Check for Purchase Order (PO)**
- **Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary).
- **Input:** Invoice.
- **Output:** Decision on PO presence.
- **Gateways:**
  - **If PO present:** Proceed to "Log Invoice."
  - **If PO missing:** Proceed to "Request PO from Supplier."

---

**3. Activity: Log Invoice**
- **Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary).
- **Input:** Invoice with PO.
- **Output:** Invoice logged in accounting system.

---

**4. Activity: Forward to Purchasing for Receipt Confirmation**
- **Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary).
- **Input:** Logged invoice.
- **Output:** Invoice forwarded to Purchasing Officer (Dan).

---

**5. Activity: Confirm Receipt of Goods/Services**
- **Role:** Purchasing Officer (Dan).
- **Input:** Invoice, PO.
- **Output:** Confirmation (or request for clarification).
- **Gateways:**
  - **If confirmed:** Proceed to "Match Invoice to PO."
  - **If discrepancy:** Proceed to "Clarify Discrepancy."

---

**6. Activity: Match Invoice to PO**
- **Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary).
- **Input:** Logged invoice, confirmation from Purchasing.
- **Output:** Match result.
- **Gateways:**
  - **If matched:** Proceed to "Request Manager Approval."
  - **If mismatch:** Proceed to "Clarify Discrepancy."

---

**7. Activity: Request Manager Approval**
- **Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary).
- **Input:** Matched invoice.
- **Output:** System notification to Department Manager (Carlos).
- **Trigger:** Automatic system email.

---

**8. Activity: Manager Approval**
- **Role:** Department Manager (Carlos).
- **Input:** Invoice details, PO, Purchasing confirmation.
- **Output:** Approval or rejection.
- **Gateways:**
  - **If approved:** Proceed to "Schedule for Payment."
  - **If rejected:** Proceed to "Resolve Issue."

---

**9. Activity: Schedule for Payment**
- **Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary).
- **Input:** Approved invoice.
- **Output:** Invoice scheduled in payment cycle.

---

**10. End Event**
- **Description:** Invoice paid.

---

**Alternate Paths / Exception Handling**

**A. PO Missing**
- **Path:** "Request PO from Supplier."
- **Roles Involved:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary), Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen).
- **Outcome:** Supplier provides PO or revised invoice; process returns to "Log Invoice."

**B. Discrepancy in Invoice**
- **Path:** "Clarify Discrepancy."
- **Roles Involved:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary), Purchasing Officer (Dan), Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen).
- **Actions:** Contact supplier, check contracts, request corrected invoice.
- **If unresolved:** Escalate to "Supervisor Intervention."

**C. Supervisor Intervention**
- **Role:** Accounts Payable Supervisor (Rita).
- **Actions:** Contact suppliers, coordinate between departments, create retrospective PO, or decide on rejection.
- **Outcome:** Exception resolved; process returns to "Confirm Receipt of Goods/Services" or "Match Invoice to PO."

**D. Manager Rejects Invoice**
- **Path:** "Resolve Issue."
- **Actions:** Return to Accounts Payable for investigation, involve Purchasing or Supplier Relationship Manager as needed.
- **Outcome:** Issue resolved; process returns to "Request Manager Approval."

---

**Artifacts / Data Objects**
- Invoice (email attachment).
- Purchase Order (PO).
- Confirmation email (from Purchasing).
- System notification (to Manager).
- Payment schedule record.

---

**Roles / Participants**
- Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary): Initial check, logging, matching, forwarding, payment scheduling.
- Purchasing Officer (Dan): Receipt confirmation, PO matching, discrepancy resolution.
- Department Manager (Carlos): Final approval.
- Accounts Payable Supervisor (Rita): Exception handling and escalation.
- Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen): Supplier communication for missing POs or pricing issues.

---

**Gateways Summary**
- **PO Check:** Diverges based on presence/absence of PO.
- **Receipt Confirmation:** Diverges based on confirmation or discrepancy.
- **Invoice Matching:** Diverges based on match or mismatch.
- **Manager Approval:** Diverges based on approval or rejection.

---

This textual BPMN model represents the consolidated understanding of the invoice approval process as described by the interviewees, with clear delineation of activities, roles, decision points, and exception handling. Ambiguities are minimized by aligning descriptions from multiple sources, and exceptions are captured as explicit alternate paths.
