**Justification for Naming Choices**

1. **Document Verification (Initial)** and **Identity Verification (Initial)**:
   - These names clarify that these are first-time verifications of documents or identities, distinguishing them from subsequent re-verifications.

2. **Resolution Call**, **Discrepancy Call**, and **Follow-up Call**:
   - These terms emphasize the purpose of each call—resolving issues, addressing discrepancies, or confirming actions—instead of just being calls related to document verification.

3. **Internal Adjust**:
   - This highlights that adjustments are made internally due to new information discovered during the process, differentiating them from routine document checks.

4. **Account Correction**:
   - Indicates a specific action taken after discovering missing information, emphasizing correction rather than general adjustments.

These renamed activities provide clarity on the sequence and purpose of actions within each case flow, aiding in understanding the context-dependent nature of these repetitive steps.
