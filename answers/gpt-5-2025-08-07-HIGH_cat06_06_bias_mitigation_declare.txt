Rationale for added constraints:
- responded_existence(CheckApplicantRace/Gender/Age, ManualReview): If any sensitive attribute is checked, a ManualReview must occur somewhere in the trace, adding human oversight when bias risk is present.
- chainresponse(CheckApplicantRace/Gender/Age, BiasMitigationCheck): Immediately after any sensitive attribute check, perform a BiasMitigationCheck, preventing a jump straight from sensitive information to consequential actions.
- nonchainsuccession(CheckApplicantRace/Gender/Age, FinalDecision): FinalDecision cannot occur immediately after a sensitive attribute check, eliminating “kneejerk” decisions based solely on sensitive data.
- precedence(BiasMitigationCheck, FinalDecision): Every FinalDecision must be preceded by a BiasMitigationCheck, ensuring fairness controls are applied before outcomes.
- coexistence(FinalDecision, ManualReview) and precedence(ManualReview, FinalDecision): Guarantees that when a decision is made, a ManualReview is present and occurs before it, promoting consistent human oversight on decisions.
