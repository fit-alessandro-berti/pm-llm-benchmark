3. **Precedence (BiasMitigationCheck before Decisions):**
   - `BiasMitigationCheck` must precede any decision activity. This prevents decisions from being made without explicit fairness validation.

4. **Non-chain Succession (Sensitive Check ⇸ Immediate Decision):**
   - Blocks decisions like `Approve`/`Reject` from **immediately** following a sensitive attribute check (e.g., `CheckApplicantRace`). This forces intermediate steps (e.g., `BiasMitigationCheck`) to break potential bias chains.
