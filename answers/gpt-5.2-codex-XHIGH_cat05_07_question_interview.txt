Below is a set of targeted, openended questions that would help clarify and deepen understanding of the property onboarding process:

---

### **Process Start & Documentation**
1. **How does the process officially begin once a landlord reaches out—what information is captured in that initial contact?**  
2. **What specific documents are required from landlords, and how do you determine whether each document is acceptable or needs updating?**  
3. **Who is responsible for validating compliance documents, and is there a formal checklist or approval step involved?**  
4. **If documentation is incomplete or delayed, how do you track that and prevent the case from stalling indefinitely?**

---

### **Property Data Entry**
5. **What property details are mandatory to record in the central system, and how is data accuracy ensured?**  
6. **Who enters the data, and is there a review/approval step before the property progresses to the next stage?**

---

### **Property Manager Assignment**
7. **How do you balance workload, geography, and expertise when assigning a property manager—are there defined criteria or a scoring method?**  
8. **What happens if no manager is a clear match or if the landlord requests a specific person?**

---

### **Landlord Introduction & Expectation Setting**
9. **What key topics are always covered in the introductory meeting, and how are agreements or special conditions documented?**  
10. **Is there a standard output from that meeting (e.g., notes, forms, approvals) that drives later steps?**

---

### **Inspection & Photography**
11. **Who selects and schedules the photographer and inspector—are they internal, external vendors, or both?**  
12. **What standards does the inspector use to pass/fail a property, and how are those standards communicated to landlords?**  
13. **If the property fails inspection, what is the typical remediation workflow and timeline?**  
14. **Who is responsible for coordinating repairs and confirming readiness for reinspection?**

---

### **Marketing & Pricing**
15. **How is the listing price determined, and who has final authority to approve it?**  
16. **How do you reconcile market pricing models with a landlord’s expectations if they differ?**  
17. **What marketing channels are used, and how do you decide which ones are appropriate for each property?**

---

### **Activation & Tenant Screening**
18. **What conditions must be met before a property is “activated” in the portal—are there formal signoffs?**  
19. **Who conducts tenant screening, and what criteria are considered nonnegotiable versus flexible?**  
20. **How do you handle multiple qualified applicants—what factors influence final selection?**

---

### **Lease Finalization & MoveIn**
21. **Who negotiates lease terms, and what authority do they have to offer discounts or custom clauses?**  
22. **How are security deposits collected and tracked, and are different payment methods allowed?**  
23. **What steps take place between lease signing and movein to ensure a smooth handoff?**

---

### **Quality Checks, Compliance & Exceptions**
24. **At what stages do compliance, finance, or IT audits occur, and how are issues resolved if found?**  
25. **How are exceptions like special insurance requirements or custom lease clauses handled and approved?**  
26. **Is there an escalation process when disagreements arise between internal teams or landlords?**

---

### **Timing, Tracking & Performance**
27. **What is the typical timeline from first contact to property activation, and where do delays most often occur?**  
28. **How is the full onboarding process tracked across teams—do you use a single system or multiple tools?**  
29. **What metrics or KPIs do you use to evaluate success or identify bottlenecks in onboarding?**
