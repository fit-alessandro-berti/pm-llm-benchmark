```text
->(
  'Receive Application (RA)',
  *(
    ->(
      'Validate Form (VF)',
      'Assess Eligibility (AE)'
    ),
    'Request Missing Info (RMI)'
  ),
  'Approval Decision (AD)',
  'Send Confirmation (SC)',
  'Archive Case (AC)'
)
```

This represents:

1. The case starts with **Receive Application (RA)**.
2. Then the process performs **Validate Form (VF)** followed by **Assess Eligibility (AE)**.
3. After assessment, the process may either:
   - continue directly to approval, or
   - execute **Request Missing Info (RMI)** and then repeat the validation/assessment part.
4. Finally, the case proceeds through:
   - **Approval Decision (AD)**
   - **Send Confirmation (SC)**
   - **Archive Case (AC)**.
