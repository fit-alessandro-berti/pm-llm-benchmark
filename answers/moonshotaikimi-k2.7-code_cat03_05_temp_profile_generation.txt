### Notes on the estimation

- **Sequential assumption**: The activities are assumed to follow the general order  
  `SS  OP  RC  QI  CA  PT  PK  WS  DT  AS`.
- **Multi-step averages**: For non-adjacent pairs, the average is roughly the sum of the expected durations of the stages in between.
- **Multi-step standard deviations**: Estimated by summing variances of the intervening stages and taking the square root, under the simplifying assumption that stage durations are approximately independent.
