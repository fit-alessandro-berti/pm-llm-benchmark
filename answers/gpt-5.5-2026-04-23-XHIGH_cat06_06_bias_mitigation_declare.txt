### Rationale for the added constraints

- **Sensitive-attribute checks trigger mitigation:**  
  `CheckApplicantRace`, `CheckApplicantGender`, and `CheckApplicantAge` now require a `BiasMitigationCheck`.

- **Immediate mitigation after sensitive data access:**  
  The `chainresponse` constraints force `BiasMitigationCheck` to occur immediately after checking race, gender, or age, preventing an unreviewed decision from being made right after sensitive information is seen.

- **Bias mitigation and manual review are coupled:**  
  `BiasMitigationCheck` coexists with `ManualReview`, and the chain response `BiasMitigationCheck -> ManualReview` ensures that mitigation is followed by a review step.

- **Decisions require prior safeguards:**  
  `Approve`, `Reject`, `RequestAdditionalInfo`, and `FinalDecision` must be preceded by both `BiasMitigationCheck` and `ManualReview`.

- **Protected-group decisions require review:**  
  Outcome-specific sensitive-group activities such as `Reject_SensitiveGroup` require `ManualReview`.

- **No direct sensitive-attribute-to-decision transition:**  
  The `nonchainsuccession` constraints prevent direct transitions like `CheckApplicantRace -> Reject`, reducing the risk of immediate biased outcomes.
