The diagram is a Business Process Model and Notation (BPMN) illustrating the process of shipping goods for an online computer hardware shop. It is divided into three lanes representing different roles: Logistics, Secretary, and Warehouse. The process begins with the shipping of goods, which leads to clarifying the shipment method. Depending on whether special handling is required, the Secretary checks if insurance is needed. If insurance is necessary, the parcel is insured. The Secretary then writes the package label, gets offers from logistic companies, and selects a logistic company to place the order. In parallel, the Warehouse packages the goods and notifies the customer if there is a delay. Once the package is ready, it is moved to the pickup area, where the shipment paperwork is filled out, marking the shipment as prepared. The diagram captures the interactions between the roles and the flow of activities, highlighting decision points and parallel tasks involved in the shipping process.
