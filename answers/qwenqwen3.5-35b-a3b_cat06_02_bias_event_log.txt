### 3. Implications for Fairness and Equity

**A. Disadvantage to Non-Affiliated Individuals**
Individuals who lack specific community affiliations (like the "Highland Civic Darts Club") face a significantly higher barrier to entry. To achieve the same outcome as a club member, a non-member must demonstrate a raw score 20-30 points higher than the club member.
