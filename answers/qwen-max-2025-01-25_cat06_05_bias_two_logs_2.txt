1. **Eliminate Preferential Adjustments**:

2. **Standardize Evaluation Metrics**:
   - Ensure that all candidates, regardless of group, are assessed using consistent and transparent metrics. This includes skill tests, personality assessments, and cultural fit evaluations.

3. **Audit Hiring Processes Regularly**:
   - Conduct regular audits of hiring practices to identify and address potential biases. Use data analytics to compare outcomes across different groups and ensure equitable treatment.

4. **Promote Diversity and Inclusion**:
   - Encourage diversity by actively recruiting candidates from underrepresented backgrounds and providing equal opportunities for professional development.

5. **Train Hiring Managers**:
