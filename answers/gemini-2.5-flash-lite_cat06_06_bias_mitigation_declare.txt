1.  **`coexistence`: `CheckApplicantRace` <-> `ManualReview`** (and similarly for `CheckApplicantAge`, `CheckApplicantGender`)

This ensures that any decision-making process involving sensitive attributes is subject to additional human scrutiny, acting as a safeguard against decisions being made solely on potentially biased data.

2.  **`response`: `CheckApplicantRace` -> `BiasMitigationCheck`** (and similarly for `CheckApplicantAge`, `CheckApplicantGender`)
    *   **Rationale:** This constraint ensures that if sensitive attributes are checked, an explicit `BiasMitigationCheck` activity is triggered afterwards. 3.  **`precedence`: `BiasMitigationCheck` -> `FinalDecision`**
    *   **Rationale:** This constraint mandates that any `FinalDecision` activity can only occur *after* a `BiasMitigationCheck` has taken place. This prevents immediate decisions from being made following the assessment of sensitive attributes and ensures that the fairness checks are completed before a final outcome is determined.

4.  **`precedence`: `ManualReview` -> `FinalDecision`**
    *   **Rationale:** This constraint requires that all `FinalDecision` activities must be preceded by a `ManualReview`. This establishes a strong procedural fairness rule, ensuring that critical decisions are always subject to human oversight, which is particularly important in contexts where sensitive attributes might influence outcomes.
