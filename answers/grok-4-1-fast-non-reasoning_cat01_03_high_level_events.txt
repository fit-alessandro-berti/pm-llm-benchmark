Groupings are based on:
- **Temporal proximity**: Events within ~10-45 seconds form a phase, with natural pauses (e.g., preheating to welding).
- **Logical sequence**: Events that achieve a single objective (e.g., all preparation actions enable welding).
- **Resource patterns**: Operator actions for handling/setup, machines/robots for automated steps, sensors for checks.
- **Semantic coherence**: Activities sharing a theme (e.g., all welding-related).
