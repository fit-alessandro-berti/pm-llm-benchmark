### Explanation of Constraints:

1. **Always Before/After**:
   - These enforce a strict sequential order for most activities (e.g., you can't check availability before receiving the order).

2. **Never Together**:
   - `Reserve Items` and `Pick Items` cannot co-occur (some orders might skip reservation).
   - `Invoice Customer` and `Record Payment` cannot co-occur (payment must come after invoicing).

3. **Activity Occurrences**:
   - Most activities must occur exactly once (e.g., `Receive Order`).
   - `Reserve Items` is optional (`{0, 1}`).

4. **Directly Follows**:
   - Enforces that activities must occur immediately after their predecessor (e.g., `Check Availability` must directly precede `Reserve Items`).
