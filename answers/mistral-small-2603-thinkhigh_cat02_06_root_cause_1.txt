## **3. Insights, Recommendations, and Proposed Solutions**

### ** Key Insights:**
1. **Escalations are the biggest source of delay**—**28-hour wait in Case 105** is unacceptable.
2. **Investigation-to-resolution gaps are consistent (~19 hours)** across slow cases, indicating a **process inefficiency**.
3. **Level-2 team capacity is a critical bottleneck**—they are either overloaded or not staffed properly.

### ** Recommended Improvements:**

#### **1. Reduce Escalation Delays (Highest Impact)**
- **Implement an SLA for escalations:**
  - **Level-2 assignment must occur within 30 minutes** of escalation.
  - **Automated alerts** when a ticket is escalated but not yet assigned.
- **Dedicate a "Level-2 Fast Track" queue:**
  - Reserve **20% of Level-2 capacity** for escalated tickets to prevent backlog.
- **Pre-emptive escalation rules:**
  - If a Level-1 agent predicts a ticket will need escalation, **initiate the process early** to avoid last-minute delays.

#### **2. Eliminate Investigation-to-Resolution Gaps**
- **Automate resolution triggers:**
  - Once investigation is marked as "complete," the system **immediately assigns resolution** to the same agent (if possible).
- **Encourage parallel processing:**
  - Allow agents to **resolve tickets immediately after investigation** if no further input is needed.
- **Set a hard SLA:**
  - **Resolution must start within 2 hours** of investigation completion.

#### **3. Optimize Initial Assignment (Case 104)**
- **Dynamic workload balancing:**
  - Use an **AI-based assignment system** that considers **agent availability, skill level, and ticket urgency**.
- **Prioritize tickets based on business impact:**
  - **High-priority tickets** (e.g., VIP customers) should skip queues.
- **Shift-based staffing adjustments:**
  - If delays occur during peak hours, **schedule more Level-1 agents** during high-traffic periods.

#### **4. Improve Handoffs Between Teams**
- **Standardize handoff documentation:**
  - Require **Level-1 agents to provide a summary** when escalating, reducing back-and-forth.
- **Real-time visibility dashboards:**
  - **Track escalated tickets in real-time** to prevent them from being forgotten.
- **Cross-training:**
  - Train **Level-1 agents to handle simpler Level-2 tasks** to reduce escalation volume.

#### **5. Address Overnight Delays**
- **Extend Level-2 support hours:**
  - If possible, **offer 24/7 Level-2 coverage** for critical escalations.
- **Automated overnight resolution:**
  - For **non-complex escalations**, allow **automated resolution scripts** to run overnight.
