### **Phase 2: Divergent Processing Paths (Standard vs. Custom)**

This is the most distinct part of the process. The workflow branches into two mutually exclusive sub-processes based on complexity.

#### **Path A: The Standard Stream (High Efficiency)**
*   **Trigger:** `If Type = Standard`
*   **Task B1: "Perform Standard Validation":** Automated or rule-based checking (e.g., format, required fields).
