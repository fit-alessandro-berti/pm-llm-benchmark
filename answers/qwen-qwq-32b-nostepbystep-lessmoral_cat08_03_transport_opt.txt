### 2. **Performance Analysis and Bottleneck Identification**  

#### **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**  
1. **On-Time Delivery Rate (OTDR):**  
   - Calculate as (Number of deliveries within time windows) / Total Deliveries.  
2. **Average Time per Delivery Stop:**  
   - Time between "Arrive Customer" and "Depart Customer."  
3. **Travel Time vs. Service Time Ratio:**  
   - Ratio of total travel time (sum of GPS-based travel segments) to total service time (sum of delivery durations).  
4. **Fuel Consumption per Package:**  
   - Estimate via distance (calculated from GPS coordinates) and package count (from dispatch data).  
5. **Vehicle Utilization Rate:**  
   - (Actual package deliveries / vehicle capacity) per shift.  
6. **Traffic Delay Frequency:**  
   - Count of "Low Speed" events exceeding a threshold (e.g., <15 km/h for >10 minutes).  
7. **Failed Delivery Rate:**  
   - Failed deliveries requiring re-runs, calculated as (Failed Deliveries / Total Deliveries).  

#### **Bottleneck Identification**  
- **Process Mining Techniques:**  
  - **Cycle Time Analysis:** Identify long-running processes (e.g., a vehicle taking 3 hours for 10 stops vs. planned 2 hours).  
  - **Bottleneck Detection:**  
    - **Route-Specific:** Use GPS paths to pinpoint traffic hotspots (e.g., recurring "Low Speed" events on Route X).  
    - **Temporal Bottlenecks:** Peak hours (e.g., morning rush causing delays for 80% of drivers.  
    - **Driver- or Vehicle-Specific:** Flag drivers/vehicles with consistently high dwell times or frequent failed deliveries.  
  - **Variant Analysis:** Compare high-performing vs. low-performing routes/drivers using **Cluster Analysis**.
