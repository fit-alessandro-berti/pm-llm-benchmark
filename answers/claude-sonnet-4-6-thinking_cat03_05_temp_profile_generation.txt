# Temporal Profile: Global High-Tech Electronics Supply Chain

## Reasoning & Assumptions

The process follows a canonical linear backbone:
> **SS  OP  RC  QI  CA  PT  PK  WS  DT  AS**

Base durations per adjacent step and their standard deviations are estimated from realistic supply-chain benchmarks (e.g., global electronics lead times, factory testing cycles, warehouse dwell times). Non-adjacent pair averages are obtained by **summing intermediate step averages**; standard deviations grow (via accumulated variance) as more steps are crossed.
