### Activity Naming Logic

Low-level system actions were translated into analyst-friendly process activities:

- `FOCUS` and destination side of `SWITCH` became **Open/Resume Document**, **Open Email Workspace**, **Open/Resume PDF Review**, or **Open/Resume Spreadsheet**.
- `TYPING` was mapped based on context:
  - Word: **Draft Document Content** or **Add Supporting Reference**
  - Excel: **Update Spreadsheet**
  - Email: **Compose Email Reply**
- `SCROLL` became **Review Email** or **Review PDF**.
- `CLICK` actions in email were mapped to **Open Email**, **Start Email Reply**, or **Send Email Reply**.
- `SAVE`, `CLOSE`, and `HIGHLIGHT` were mapped directly to **Save**, **Close**, and **Annotate** activities.

The resulting event log is suitable for import into process mining tools as a CSV-style event log using `Case ID`, `Activity Name`, and `Timestamp` as the core columns.
