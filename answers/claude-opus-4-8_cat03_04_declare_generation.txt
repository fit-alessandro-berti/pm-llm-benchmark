## Explanation of the Modeling Logic

| Constraint Type | Business Rationale |
|-----------------|--------------------|
| **init: IG** | Every product lifecycle begins with idea generation. |
| **existence/exactly_one** | IG, AG, and FL are mandatory milestones occurring once. |
| **response (IGDD, MPFL)** | Captures the natural forward flow of work. |
| **precedence (AGFL, TFCAG)** | A product cannot launch without approval; approval requires technical & cost checks. |
| **coexistence (TFCCE, LTUT)** | Feasibility and cost are evaluated jointly; both testing forms accompany each other. |
| **chainresponse (IGDD, MPFL)** | Tight immediate sequencing where no intermediate steps are allowed. |
| **chainprecedence (AGMP)** | Marketing planning starts directly after approval. |
| **nonsuccession (FLIG, AGTFC)** | Prevents illogical backward flows (e.g., re-checking feasibility after approval). |
| **nonchainsuccession (IGFL)** | Prevents skipping the entire design/test/approve pipeline. |

This model enforces a realistic **gated product-development workflow**: *idea  design  feasibility/cost  prototype  testing  approval  marketing  launch*, while preventing shortcuts and backward transitions.
