Based on the provided interview transcripts, here is the structured textual representation of the Invoice Approval Process. This model is organized to be directly translatable into a BPMN 2.0 diagram.

---

### **Process Model: Invoice Approval Process**

#### **1. Participants (Pools/Lanes)**
*   **Supplier** (External Entity)
*   **Accounts Payable (AP) Clerk** (Mary)
*   **Purchasing Officer** (Dan)
*   **AP Supervisor** (Rita)
*   **Supplier Relationship Manager** (Karen)
*   **Department Manager** (Carlos)
*   **Accounting System** (Automated System/Service)

#### **2. Artifacts (Data Objects)**
*   **Invoice:** The primary document triggering the process.
*   **Purchase Order (PO):** The reference document used for verification.
*   **Confirmation Email:** Communication between Purchasing and AP.
*   **Retrospective PO:** A PO created after the fact for informal contracts.

#### **3. Process Flow**

**Phase 1: Intake and Initial Validation**
*   **Start Event:** Invoice received (typically via email).
*   **Activity (AP Clerk):** Check for valid Purchase Order (PO) number.
*   **Gateway [Decision]: Is PO Number Present?**
    *   **No:** 
        *   **Activity (AP Clerk/SRM):** Request PO details/revised invoice from Supplier. (Note: Karen may assist here).
        *   **Flow:** Return to "Start Event" once the revised invoice is received.
    *   **Yes:** 
        *   **Activity (AP Clerk):** Log invoice into Accounting System.
        *   **Activity (AP Clerk):** Forward invoice to Purchasing Department for receipt confirmation.

**Phase 2: Purchasing Verification**
*   **Activity (Purchasing Officer):** Compare invoice details (quantities, prices, delivery charges) against the PO.
*   **Gateway [Decision]: Do details match PO?**
    *   **No (Discrepancy):**
        *   **Activity (Purchasing Officer/SRM):** Contact supplier for correction or consult contract (Karen).
        *   **Activity (AP Clerk):** Place invoice on hold.
        *   **Flow:** Loop back to "Compare invoice details" once corrected.
    *   **Yes:**
        *   **Activity (Purchasing Officer):** Send confirmation email to AP Clerk.

**Phase 3: Final Matching and Escalation**
*   **Activity (AP Clerk):** Match invoice line items against the PO within the Accounting System.
*   **Gateway [Decision]: Is system match successful?**
    *   **No (Discrepancy/Error):**
        *   **Activity (AP Clerk):** Email Purchasing for clarification.
        *   **Gateway [Decision]: Can it be resolved easily?**
            *   **Yes:** Resolve and proceed to "Match invoice line items."
            *   **No:** **Escalate to AP Supervisor (Rita).**
            *   **Activity (AP Supervisor):** Coordinate between departments or contact supplier.
            *   **Sub-Process (Informal Contract):** If no PO exists due to informal contract $\rightarrow$ Work with Dept Head to create a **Retrospective PO**.
            *   **Flow:** Return to "Match invoice line items" once resolved.
    *   **Yes:**
        *   **System Trigger:** Accounting System sends automatic approval request email to Department Manager.

**Phase 4: Management Approval**
*   **Activity (Dept Manager):** Review invoice, PO, and confirmation in the system.
*   **Gateway [Decision]: Approval Granted?**
    *   **No (Rejected/Suspicious):**
        *   **Activity (Dept Manager):** Reject invoice or request investigation.
        *   **Activity (AP Clerk):** Investigate discrepancy.
        *   **Flow:** Loop back to "Purchasing Verification" or "Final Matching" depending on the issue.
    *   **Yes:**
        *   **Activity (Dept Manager):** Hit "Approve" in the system.

**Phase 5: Payment Execution**
*   **Activity (AP Clerk):** Schedule invoice for payment run/cycle.
*   **End Event:** Invoice paid.

---

### **4. Summary of Exceptions and Variations**

| Exception | Trigger | Resolution Path |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Missing PO** | Initial check by AP Clerk | Supplier is contacted (via Mary or Karen) for a revised invoice. |
| **Line Item Discrepancy** | Matching phase (Mary/Dan) | Collaborative check between Purchasing, SRM, and AP; may involve supplier contact. |
| **Unresolved Conflict** | AP Clerk cannot resolve match | Escalation to AP Supervisor (Rita) for intervention or retrospective PO creation. |
| **Budgetary/Suspicious Item** | Manager review | Rejection by Carlos $\rightarrow$ Investigation by Mary $\rightarrow$ Rework. |
| **Informal Contracts** | No PO exists in system | AP Supervisor creates a retrospective PO to allow the process to continue. |

### **5. Ambiguities & Notes for Modeler**
*   **Overlap in Validation:** Both Mary (AP) and Dan (Purchasing) describe "checking" or "matching" the invoice against the PO. In the model, this is represented as a two-stage verification: first a business verification (Dan), then a system-level line-item match (Mary).
*   **SRM Involvement:** Karen (SRM) acts as a "support role" rather than a sequential step. Her activities are modeled as parallel support to the AP Clerk and Purchasing Officer during discrepancy resolution.
