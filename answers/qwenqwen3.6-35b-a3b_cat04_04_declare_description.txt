4.  **Quality Assurance & Offer Assembly:**

5.  **Authorization:** `Authorize_Contract_Terms` represents the official approval. This step is constrained to occur only after the `Quality_Assurance_Review` (`Precedence`) and after the `Preliminary_Credit_Check` (`Chainprecedence`). 6.  **Disbursement:** Once authorized, `Transfer_Funds` must occur (`Chainresponse` from `Assemble_Loan_Offer_Package`). This links the disbursement directly to the preparation of the offer, ensuring funds are only moved when an offer exists.
