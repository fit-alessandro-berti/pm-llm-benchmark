###  **Explanation of Added Constraints and Rationale**

1. **Coexistence Constraints:**
   - **`FinalDecision` must coexist with `ManualReview`**
     - Ensures that every final decision is reviewed manually, reducing the risk of automated bias.
   - **`Approve_Minority` and `Reject_Minority` must coexist with `ManualReview`**
     - Ensures that any decision involving a minority applicant is manually reviewed, promoting fairness.

2. **Response Constraints:**
   - **`CheckApplicantRace` must be followed by `BiasMitigationCheck`**
     - Prevents immediate decision-making after identifying a sensitive attribute, giving time to apply fairness checks.
   - **`BiasMitigationCheck` must be followed by `Approve` or `Reject`**
     - Ensures that any decision after identifying a sensitive attribute goes through a mitigation step.

3. **Non-Succession Constraints:**
   - **`CheckApplicantRace` must not be immediately followed by `Approve` or `Reject`**
     - Prevents biased decisions from being made immediately after identifying sensitive attributes without review.

###  **Impact on Fairness**

These constraints introduce **safeguards** that:
- Prevent **automated, immediate decisions** based on sensitive attributes.
- Enforce **manual oversight** when decisions involve potentially sensitive cases.
- Ensure that **sensitive attributes do not directly influence outcomes** without mitigation.

By embedding such constraints into the process model, we promote **transparency**, **accountability**, and **fairness** in automated decision-making systems like loan approvals.
