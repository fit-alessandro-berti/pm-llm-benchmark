#### Activity Naming and Standardization:

- **CLICK Events**: activity names were derived from the `Action` attribute (e.g., `Open_Email_about_Annual_Meeting` for opening an email) to add specificity.
- **SCROLL/HIGHLIGHT**: These actions were translated into descriptive terms (e.g., `SCROLL` to `SCROLL` with direction) for clarity.
- **Case-Specific Activities**: Events tied to document transitions (e.g., switching back to Word after working on Excel) were labeled with `FOCUS` or `SWITCH` to reflect the workflow.

#### Key Attributes:
- **Case ID**: Ensures events from different sessions are identifiable.
- **Application/Window**: Contextualizes activities (e.g., `Microsoft Word | Document1.docx`).
- **Timestamp**: Maintains temporal resolution for event sequencing.
- **Additional Attributes**: `Keys` in `TYPING` events highlight content changes, while `Action` text in `CLICK` events adds granularity.
