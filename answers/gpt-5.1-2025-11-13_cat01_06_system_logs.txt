### Mapping / Design Logic

**1. Case Identification**

- Each **file or logical work item** is a case:  
  - `CASE_DOC1`  Word: `Document1.docx`  
  - `CASE_QREPORT`  Word: `Quarterly_Report.docx`  
  - `CASE_EMAIL_MEETING`  Email thread: “Email about Annual Meeting” (in Chrome, Inbox window)  
  - `CASE_PDF_REPORT`  PDF: `Report_Draft.pdf` (Acrobat)  
  - `CASE_EXCEL_BUDGET`  Excel: `Budget_2024.xlsx`  
- `SWITCH` events are assigned to the **destination object** if they represent focusing on a new item, and to the **source object** if they represent leaving it (Excel case). This creates clear start/resume/end points for each case.

**2. Activity Naming (abstraction from low-level actions)**

Raw  Abstracted Activity:
- `FOCUS` on document/spreadsheet  `Open document` / `Open spreadsheet` or `Resume editing` depending on context.
- `TYPING` in Word  `Edit content`  
- `TYPING` in Excel  `Edit data`  
- `SAVE` in Word  `Save document`  
- `SAVE` in Excel  `Save spreadsheet`  
- `CLOSE`  `Close document`  
- `SWITCH` to email or PDF  `Open email thread` / `Open document` / `Resume editing` depending on case and whether this is first or subsequent focus.  
- Email-specific:
  - `CLICK` open email  `Open specific email`
  - `SCROLL` in email  `Review email`
  - `CLICK` reply  `Start reply`
  - `TYPING` reply  `Compose reply`
  - `CLICK` send  `Send email`
- PDF-specific:
  - `SCROLL`  `Review document`
  - `HIGHLIGHT`  `Annotate document`

**3. Narrative / Process Structure**

- **Document1 case**: open  multiple edit–save cycles  switch away  resume editing  save  close.
- **Email case**: open inbox  open specific email  review  reply  compose  send.
- **PDF case**: open  review  annotate.
- **Excel case**: open  edit data  save  switch away.
- **Quarterly report case**: quick initial focus, long gap, then resume, edit, save, close.

This structure yields a clean, analyst-friendly event log that can be directly used to discover models and analyze user work patterns across documents, emails, and supporting files.
