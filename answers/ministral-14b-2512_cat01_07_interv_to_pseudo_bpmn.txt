### **Process Name:** Invoice Approval Process
**Objective:** Ensure accurate, compliant payment of invoices by validating against purchase orders (POs), resolving discrepancies, and securing approvals.

---

### **1. Start Event**
**Trigger:** Invoice received (via email or system upload).
**Input:** Invoice document (PDF/email attachment).

---

### **2. Activities & Sequence Flow**

#### **Task 1: Validate Invoice for PO Reference**
**Performer:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary)
**Description:**
- Check if the invoice includes a **valid purchase order (PO) number**.
- **Decision Gateway (Exclusive):**
  - **Yes (PO exists):**
    - Proceed to **Task 2: Log Invoice in System**.
  - **No (PO missing):**
    - **Action:** Send invoice back to **Supplier** (via email) requesting PO details.
    - **Artifact:** Email with request for PO number.
    - **Loop:** Return to **Start Event** (await revised invoice).
    - *Note:* If supplier cannot provide PO, escalate to **Accounts Payable Supervisor (Rita)** for further action (e.g., retrospective PO or rejection).

#### **Task 2: Log Invoice in System**
**Performer:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary)
**Description:**
- Enter invoice details into the **accounting system** (e.g., SAP, QuickBooks).
- **Output:** System-generated invoice record with PO reference.

#### **Task 3: Request Goods/Service Receipt Confirmation**
**Performer:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary)
**Description:**
- Forward logged invoice to **Purchasing Department (Dan)** for confirmation that goods/services were received.
- **Artifact:** Email with invoice + request for receipt confirmation.
- **Wait:** Await response from Purchasing.

#### **Task 4: Confirm Receipt of Goods/Services**
**Performer:** Purchasing Officer (Dan)
**Description:**
- Compare invoice line items (quantities, prices, charges) against the **original PO**.
- **Decision Gateway (Exclusive):**
  - **Match:** Send **confirmation email** to Accounts Payable.
  - **Discrepancy (e.g., missing items, incorrect pricing):**
    - **Action:**
      - If minor: Request **Supplier (Karen)** to clarify/correct invoice.
      - If major: Escalate to **Accounts Payable Supervisor (Rita)** for resolution.
    - **Artifact:** Email to supplier or supervisor with discrepancy details.
    - **Loop:** Return to **Task 3** (await corrected invoice or resolution).
    - *Note:* Purchasing **never approves** without PO confirmation.

#### **Task 5: Match Invoice to PO**
**Performer:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary)
**Description:**
- Cross-check invoice details (after Purchasing confirmation) against the **PO in the system**.
- **Decision Gateway (Exclusive):**
  - **Match:** Proceed to **Task 6: Request Manager Approval**.
  - **Discrepancy:**
    - **Action:** Email **Purchasing (Dan)** for clarification.
    - **Loop:** Return to **Task 4** (await resolution).
    - *If unresolved:* Escalate to **Accounts Payable Supervisor (Rita)**.

#### **Task 6: Request Manager Approval**
**Performer:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary)
**Description:**
- System **automatically emails** the **Department Manager (Carlos)** with:
  - Invoice details.
  - PO reference.
  - Purchasing confirmation.
- **Artifact:** Approval request email (system-generated).

#### **Task 7: Manager Approval**
**Performer:** Department Manager (Carlos)
**Description:**
- Review invoice for:
  - Budget compliance.
  - Unexpected items/charges.
- **Decision Gateway (Exclusive):**
  - **Approve:**
    - **Action:** Hit "Approve" in the system.
    - Proceed to **Task 8: Schedule for Payment**.
  - **Reject/Investigate:**
    - **Action:**
      - If minor: Request **Accounts Payable (Mary)** to hold and recheck.
      - If major: Escalate to **Accounts Payable Supervisor (Rita)** for investigation.
    - **Loop:** Return to **Task 5** (after resolution).

#### **Task 8: Schedule for Payment**
**Performer:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary)
**Description:**
- Add approved invoice to the **next payment run schedule**.
- **Output:** Payment file sent to **Treasury/Finance** for processing.
- **End Event:** Process complete (invoice paid).

---

### **3. Roles/Participants**
| Role                     | Responsibilities                                                                 |
|--------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Accounts Payable Clerk** (Mary) | Validate PO, log invoices, match details, escalate exceptions, schedule payments. |
| **Purchasing Officer**   (Dan)     | Confirm receipt of goods/services; validate invoice vs. PO.                     |
| **Department Manager**   (Carlos)  | Final approval for budget/compliance.                                           |
| **AP Supervisor**       (Rita)     | Resolve complex exceptions (e.g., no PO, supplier disputes).                  |
| **Supplier**            (Karen)    | Provide missing PO details or corrected invoices.                                |
| **System**              | Automates email notifications, PO/invoice matching, approval workflows.         |

---

### **4. Artifacts/Inputs/Outputs**
| Artifact               | Description                                                                     |
|------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Invoice**            | Input: Supplier’s bill (PDF/email).                                           |
| **Purchase Order (PO)** | Reference document for approved purchases.                                    |
| **Confirmation Email** | Sent by Purchasing to AP after receipt validation.                            |
| **Approval Email**     | System-generated request to Manager.                                          |
| **Payment File**       | Output: Approved invoices sent to Treasury for payment.                       |

---

### **5. Exceptions & Variations**
1. **Missing PO:**
   - Invoice returned to supplier for PO details.
   - If unresolved: Supervisor (Rita) creates retrospective PO or rejects invoice.
2. **Discrepancies:**
   - Minor: Resolved via supplier/Purchasing coordination.
   - Major: Escalated to Rita for investigation (e.g., contract review, budget checks).
3. **Manager Rejection:**
   - Invoice held until issues are resolved (e.g., budget overrun, fraud risk).
4. **Informal Purchases:**
   - Invoices without POs are flagged by Rita; may require department head approval.
5. **Supplier Coordination:**
   - Karen (Supplier Relationship Manager) mediates corrections but does not approve.

### **6. Notes on Ambiguities/Conflicts**
- **PO Handling for Services:**
  - Rita mentions some service invoices lack POs (handled retrospectively). This is not reflected in Mary/Dan’s accounts.
  - *Resolution:* Add a sub-process under **Task 1** for "Informal Invoice Handling" (triggered if no PO exists after supplier response).
- **Payment Scheduling:**
  - Dan assumes AP schedules payments post-approval, but Mary’s transcript implies this is her role. Clarified in the model.
- **Supplier Involvement:**
  - Karen’s role is reactive (only for discrepancies). Explicitly noted in **Task 4**.

### **7. Visualization Guidance for BPMN**
- **Gateways:**
  - Use **Exclusive Gateways ()** for all decision points (PO check, matching, approval).
  - Example: After **Task 1**, split into "PO exists" ( Task 2) and "PO missing" ( Supplier).
- **Loops:**
  - Represent rework (e.g., discrepancies) with **sequence flows returning to earlier tasks**.
- **Roles:**
  - Use **Pools/Lanes** in BPMN to separate departments (AP, Purchasing, Management).
- **Exceptions:**
  - Model as **interrupting events** (e.g., "Escalate to Supervisor") or **sub-processes** (e.g., "Informal Invoice Handling").
