To construct the event log, I have integrated the provided tables based on their relationships (`order_id`, `invoice_id`).

### Assumptions & Methodology:
1.  **Event Aggregation**: Multiple item picks within the same order are listed as individual events to maintain granularity.
2.  **Sorting**: Events are sorted chronologically by timestamp within each `Case ID`.
3.  **Ambiguity**: For records where a specific resource isn't directly linked in the table (e.g., `Order Created`), the resource associated with the entity (e.g., `sales_person_id`) is used.
4.  **Inference**: Delivery events are split into "Dispatched" and "Arrived." Invoice and Payment events are mapped to their respective orders.
