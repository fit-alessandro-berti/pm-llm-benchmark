1. **Verify Documents**

### Step 2: Rename Activities to Reflect Context

Based on the context, we can rename these activities as follows:

- **Verify Documents**: 
  - **Re-verify Documents (Post-Submission)**: Indicates a recheck after additional documents are provided.
  - **Secondary Document Verification (Post-Interaction)**: Indicates verification after a customer interaction (e.g., call).
  - **Physical ID Verification**: For in-branch verification of physical documents.

- **Call Customer**:
  - **Inform Customer of Documentation Need**: Emphasizes the purpose of informing the customer in the first instance in C001.
  - **Clarify Account Discrepancy (Outbound)**: Highlights the purpose of the call in C002 after an issue is identified.
  - **Customer Return Prompt (Branch)**: Indicates the purpose of the call in C003 to have the customer return to the branch.
