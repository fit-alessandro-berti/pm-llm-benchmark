## Summary of Design Decisions

| Constraint Category | Key Rationale |
|---|---|
| **existence** | All 10 activities are mandatory in every product launch trace |
| **exactly_one** | IG, AG, MP, FL are critical milestones that must not be repeated |
| **init** | Every process trace must begin with Idea Generation |
| **response / precedence / succession** | Encode the core sequential pipeline (IG  DD  TFC/CE  PC  LT/UT  AG  MP  FL) |
| **coexistence** | TFCCE, LTUT, AGMP, MPFL are mutually dependent pairs |
| **chainresponse / chainprecedence / chainsuccession** | AGMP and MPFL must happen in immediate succession with no intervening steps |
| **altresponse / altprecedence / altsuccession** | Enforce strict alternating patterns in testing phases |
| **noncoexistence** | FL and IG cannot both appear (no relaunching the same product idea) |
| **nonsuccession** | Prevent backward flows (e.g., returning to prototyping after launch) |
| **nonchainsuccession** | Prevent skipping intermediate mandatory steps between major phases |
