# Textual BPMN Process Model: Invoice Approval & Payment Workflow

## 1. Start Event
*   **Event:** `Start: Invoice Received`
*   **Trigger:** Physical or digital arrival of invoice (typically via email/system notification).
*   **Participants:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary)

## 2. Main Process Flow

### Activity 1: PO Validity Check
*   **Task ID:** `AP-001`
*   **Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary)
*   **Description:** Verify if the invoice contains a valid Purchase Order (PO) number.
*   **Input Artifact:** Invoice Document/Email.
*   **Output Artifact:** PO Number Status.

### Gateway 1: Is Valid PO Present?
*   **Type:** Exclusive Gateway (`XOR`)
*   **Condition:** Yes / No

#### Path A: Valid PO Exists
*   **Flow:** Proceed to `AP-002`.
*   **Note:** If valid, proceed to logging.

#### Path B: Invalid/No PO Exists
*   **Flow:** Proceed to `AP-003` (Request PO).
*   **Task ID:** `AP-003`
    *   **Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary)
    *   **Description:** Request additional details from the supplier.
    *   **Support Role:** Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen) may be looped in to provide contract info if PO is missing due to informal contracting.
*   **Loop Condition:** Repeat until valid PO received.

### Activity 2: Invoice Logging & Forwarding
*   **Task ID:** `AP-002`
*   **Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary)
*   **Description:** Log invoice into accounting system and forward to Purchasing Department for receipt confirmation.
*   **Output Artifact:** Internal System Record / Email Notification to Purchasing.

### Activity 3: Purchasing Verification (Three-Way Match Check)
*   **Task ID:** `PUR-001`
*   **Role:** Purchasing Officer (Dan)
*   **Description:** Open PO and compare invoice details (quantities, prices, delivery charges) against ordered items.
*   **Input Artifact:** Invoice + Purchase Order.
*   **Output Artifact:** Confirmation Email to Accounts Payable.

### Gateway 2: Discrepancy Detected? (Purchasing Step)
*   **Type:** Exclusive Gateway (`XOR`)
*   **Condition:** Yes / No

#### Path A: No Discrepancy
*   **Flow:** Proceed to `AP-004`.
*   **Action:** Dan sends confirmation email back to Accounts Payable.

#### Path B: Discrepancy Detected (Qty, Price, Charge)
*   **Flow:** Proceed to `PUR-002` (Resolution Loop).
*   **Task ID:** `PUR-002`
    *   **Role:** Purchasing Officer (Dan) & Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen)
    *   **Description:** Contact supplier directly or ask AP to hold invoice until corrected version is provided.
    *   **Support Role:** Karen may contact supplier if pricing/contract terms are disputed.
*   **Loop Condition:** Repeat until Discrepancy Resolved and Invoice Confirmed.

### Activity 4: Final Invoice Matching (AP Step)
*   **Task ID:** `AP-004`
*   **Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary)
*   **Description:** Match invoice line items against PO in system *after* receiving Purchasing confirmation.
*   **Output Artifact:** System Ready for Manager Approval.

### Gateway 3: Discrepancy Detected? (AP Step - Internal Review)
*   **Type:** Exclusive Gateway (`XOR`)
*   **Condition:** Yes / No
*   **Note:** Based on Mary's account, she checks for discrepancies here too (e.g., missing items).

#### Path A: No Discrepancy
*   **Flow:** Proceed to `APR-001`.

#### Path B: Discrepancy Detected (Pricing/Items)
*   **Flow:** Proceed to `PUR-003` (Escalation Loop).
*   **Task ID:** `PUR-003`
    *   **Role:** Accounts Payable Supervisor (Rita)
    *   **Description:** Escalate unresolved discrepancies. Contact suppliers, coordinate departments, or decide on retrospective POs for informal contracts.
    *   **Support Role:** Department Manager (Carlos) may be consulted for budget impact.
*   **Loop Condition:** Repeat until issue resolved and invoice can move forward.

### Activity 5: Manager Approval
*   **Task ID:** `MGR-001`
*   **Role:** Department Manager (Carlos)
*   **Description:** Review system notification (Invoice + PO + Purchasing Confirmation). Verify quantities, pricing, and budget alignment.
*   **Input Artifact:** System Notification / Invoice Details.

### Gateway 4: Approval Decision?
*   **Type:** Exclusive Gateway (`XOR`)
*   **Condition:** Approve / Reject

#### Path A: Reject / Suspicious Amount
*   **Flow:** Proceed to `MGR-002`.
*   **Task ID:** `MGR-002`
    *   **Role:** Department Manager (Carlos) or Accounts Payable Supervisor (Rita)
    *   **Description:** Investigate further, request corrections, or hold invoice.
    *   **Loop Condition:** Return to `PUR-001` (if Purchasing needs correction) or `AP-003` (if PO missing).

#### Path B: Approve
*   **Flow:** Proceed to `PAY-001`.

### Activity 6: Payment Scheduling
*   **Task ID:** `PAY-001`
*   **Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary) / System
*   **Description:** Schedule invoice for payment run cycle.
*   **End Event:** `Payment Scheduled`

## 3. Exception Handling & Variations

| Exception Scenario | Trigger Condition | Resolution Path | Roles Involved |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Missing PO** | Invoice arrives without PO number. | Request PO from Supplier (Hold invoice). | Mary, Karen |
| **Price/Qty Mismatch** | Discrepancy found during Purchasing or AP matching. | Contact Supplier for corrected invoice or clarify contract terms. | Dan, Karen, Mary |
| **Unresolved Issue** | Discrepancy cannot be resolved by line staff. | Escalate to Supervisor (Rita) for coordination/retroactive PO decision. | Rita, Carlos |
| **Budget Variance** | Manager sees suspicious amount vs. budget. | Reject or request investigation from AP Clerk. | Carlos, Mary, Rita |
| **Informal Contract** | Invoice lacks PO but relates to service contract. | Create retrospective PO or reject invoice based on policy. | Rita, Karen, Carlos |

## 4. Ambiguities & Notes for Visualization

1.  **Parallel vs. Sequential Verification:** There is slight ambiguity regarding whether Mary performs the "Line Item Match" *before* Dan's "Purchasing Confirmation" (Interview 1) or if Dan's confirmation implies he has already verified the match (Interview 2). The model assumes a sequential flow: AP Logs -> Purchasing Confirms -> AP Final Matches.
2.  **Karen's Visibility:** Karen does not access the system for approvals; she acts as an external support node during discrepancies involving suppliers or missing POs. She is modeled as a supporting task participant rather than a direct process actor in the main flow.
3.  **Escalation Triggers:** While Mary mentions escalation to her supervisor (Rita) if discrepancies cannot be resolved easily, the specific trigger for Rita's involvement is not explicitly defined beyond "unresolved issues." This is modeled as a gateway condition where the resolution loop fails.
4.  **System vs. Email:** Some steps rely on email communication (e.g., Dan to Mary), while others use system notifications (e.g., Manager). In a visual diagram, these are treated as internal transitions unless specific message flow modeling is required.

## 5. Artifacts Summary

*   **Invoice:** Input artifact throughout the process.
*   **Purchase Order (PO):** Critical input for validation; may be missing initially.
*   **Confirmation Email:** Artifact generated by Purchasing Officer (Dan) to signal readiness for AP matching.
*   **System Notification:** Artifact generated after Matching/Verification to trigger Manager Approval.
*   **Corrected Invoice:** Output artifact from Supplier/Supplier Relationship Manager during discrepancy resolution.
