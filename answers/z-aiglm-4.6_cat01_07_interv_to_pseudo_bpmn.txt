### **Process: Invoice Approval and Payment Scheduling**

**Participants (Swimlanes):**
1.  **Accounts Payable (AP) Clerk** (Mary)
2.  **Purchasing Officer** (Dan)
3.  **Department Manager** (Carlos)
4.  **AP Supervisor** (Rita) - *Exception Handler*
5.  **Supplier Relationship Manager** (Karen) - *Exception Handler*

**Key Artifacts / Data Objects:**
*   Invoice (Email)
*   Purchase Order (PO)
*   Accounting System Record
*   Confirmation Email
*   Manager Approval Notification
*   Payment Schedule

---

### **Main Process Flow ("Happy Path")**

**Lane: Accounts Payable (AP) Clerk**

1.  **Start Event:** Invoice Received (via email)
2.  **Task:** Check for Valid PO Number on Invoice
3.  **Exclusive Gateway (XOR): PO Present?**
    *   **No Path:** -> Go to Exception Path E1 (Missing PO)
    *   **Yes Path:** -> Continue to step 4
4.  **Task:** Log Invoice into Accounting System
5.  **Task:** Forward Invoice to Purchasing for Receipt Confirmation
    *   *Sequence Flow:* Passes control to the Purchasing Officer lane.
6.  **Task:** Receive Confirmation from Purchasing
7.  **Task:** Match Invoice Line Items against PO in System
    *   **Note:** This step occurs after receiving confirmation from Purchasing, as per Mary's account.
8.  **Exclusive Gateway (XOR): Invoice Matches PO?**
    *   **No Path:** -> Go to Exception Path E2 (Discrepancy)
    *   **Yes Path:** -> Continue to step 9
9.  **Task:** Send Invoice to Department Manager for Approval
    *   *Sequence Flow:* Passes control to the Department Manager lane.
10. **Task:** Receive Manager's Approval
11. **Task:** Schedule Invoice for Payment
12. **End Event:** Invoice Processed and Scheduled

---

**Lane: Purchasing Officer**

1.  **Task:** Receive Invoice from AP Clerk
2.  **Task:** Compare Invoice Details (quantities, prices, charges) against PO
3.  **Exclusive Gateway (XOR): Invoice Details Correct?**
    *   **No Path:** -> Go to Exception Path E2 (Discrepancy)
    *   **Yes Path:** -> Continue to step 4
4.  **Task:** Send Confirmation Email to AP Clerk
    *   *Sequence Flow:* Returns control to the AP Clerk lane (at step 6).

---

**Lane: Department Manager**

1.  **Task:** Receive Approval Notification (System-generated)
2.  **Task:** Review Invoice, PO, and Purchasing Confirmation
3.  **Exclusive Gateway (XOR): Approve Invoice?**
    *   **No Path:** -> Go to Exception Path E3 (Manager Rejection)
    *   **Yes Path:** -> Continue to step 4
4.  **Task:** Approve Invoice in System
    *   *Sequence Flow:* Returns control to the AP Clerk lane (at step 10).

---

### **Exception and Variation Paths**

**Exception Path E1: Missing PO Number**
*   **Trigger:** AP Clerk's "PO Present?" gateway results in "No".
*   **Lane: AP Clerk**
    *   **Task:** Request PO/Details from Supplier via Email
    *   **Sequence Flow:** This creates a loop. The process waits for a response. Once a PO is provided, the flow returns to the **AP Clerk's "Check for Valid PO Number"** task.

**Exception Path E2: Invoice Discrepancy**
*   **Trigger 1:** AP Clerk's "Invoice Matches PO?" gateway results in "No".
*   **Trigger 2:** Purchasing Officer's "Invoice Details Correct?" gateway results in "No".
*   **Lane: AP Clerk**
    *   **Task:** (From trigger 1) Email Purchasing Team to Clarify Discrepancy.
*   **Lane: Purchasing Officer**
    *   **Task:** (From trigger 2) Contact Supplier for Corrected Invoice OR Ask AP to Hold Invoice.
*   **Decision Point (Implicit):** Can the discrepancy be resolved easily by the AP Clerk or Purchasing Officer?
    *   **If Yes:** The task is completed (e.g., supplier sends corrected invoice), and the process returns to the point of failure (either "Match Invoice" for the AP Clerk or "Compare Invoice" for Purchasing).
    *   **If No / Escalation Needed:** The flow goes to the **AP Supervisor** lane.

**Exception Path E3: Manager Rejection / Investigation Request**
*   **Trigger:** Department Manager's "Approve Invoice?" gateway results in "No".
*   **Lane: Department Manager**
    *   **Task:** Reject Invoice OR Request Investigation from AP Clerk.
*   **Lane: AP Clerk**
    *   **Task:** Receive Investigation Request.
    *   **Sequence Flow:** This may lead to a rework loop or trigger an escalation to the **AP Supervisor** if the issue is complex.

---

### **Exception Handler Lanes**

**Lane: AP Supervisor (Rita)**
*   **Trigger:** Escalation from AP Clerk or Purchasing Officer for an unresolved discrepancy (from Exception Path E2) or a complex investigation request (from E3).
*   **Task:** Investigate Exception (e.g., contact supplier, coordinate with departments).
*   **Exclusive Gateway (XOR): Invoice for Informally Contracted Service?**
    *   **Yes Path:**
        *   **Task:** Work with Department Head to create Retrospective PO.
        *   **OR**
        *   **Task:** Decide to Reject Invoice.
    *   **No Path:**
        *   **Task:** Resolve Issue through direct coordination.
*   **Task:** Return Resolved Invoice to Normal Flow.
    *   **Sequence Flow:** The invoice is handed back to the **AP Clerk**, typically to re-enter the main flow at the "Match Invoice Line Items against PO" step.

**Lane: Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen)**
*   **Trigger:** Looped in by AP or Purchasing for supplier-side issues (e.g., pricing disputes, missing POs).
*   **Task:** Check Contractual Terms with Supplier.
*   **Task:** Contact Supplier to Resolve Issue.
*   **Task:** Provide Resolution Details (e.g., correct price, PO number) to AP/Purchasing.
    *   **Sequence Flow:** This information is used by the AP Clerk or Purchasing Officer to resolve the exception and return the invoice to the main flow. This lane does not directly interact with the system for approval but provides critical input to other roles.
