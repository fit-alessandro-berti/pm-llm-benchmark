This diagram represents a process flow for shipping goods from a Computer Hardware Shop, detailing interactions among the roles: Dispatch of Goods, Secretary, Warehouse, and Logistics.

1. **Starting Point:**
   - The process begins with "Ship goods."

2. **Secretary's Actions:**
   - **Clarify Shipment Method:** The secretary first clarifies the shipment method.
   - **Special Handling Check:** They check if special handling is required.
     - If **no**, they proceed to:
       - **Get 3 Offers from Logistic Companies:** Obtain quotes from three logistics providers.
       - **Select Logistic Company and Place Order:** Choose the best option and place the order.
     - If **yes**, they move to:
       - **Check if Insurance is Required:** Check customer records to see if insurance is necessary.
         - If **no insurance needed**, they proceed to package goods.
         - If **yes insurance needed**, they insure the parcel and then write the package label.
   - After selecting the logistic company, they write the package label.

3. **Package Labeling and Preparation:**
   - The package label is written, and then the goods are packaged.
   - **Package Goods:** Goods are packed within 48 hours.
   - **Fill Out Shipments Paperwork:** Required paperwork is filled out.

4. **Warehouse Actions:**
   - After packaging, the next step is to move the package to the pickup area.

5. **Logistics:**
   - The logistics company takes over from the point of shipment preparation.

6. **Delay Notification:**
   - If there is a delay in shipping (indicated by a timer), the customer is notified.

7. **End Point:**
   - The process ends when the shipment is prepared and ready for dispatch.

**Key Points:**
- The flow involves decision points (e.g., special handling, insurance requirement) that guide the process.
- The secretary and logistics companies interact to ensure the correct shipment method and handling.
- The warehouse ensures goods are packaged and prepared within a specified timeframe.
- Customer notifications are made if there are delays in the shipping process.
