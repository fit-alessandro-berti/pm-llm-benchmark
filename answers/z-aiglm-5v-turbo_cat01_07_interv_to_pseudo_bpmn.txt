Based on the interview transcripts provided, here is a structured, BPMN-like textual process model for the **Invoice Approval Workflow**.

### **Process Overview: Invoice Approval & Payment Scheduling**

**Primary Objective:** To validate incoming supplier invoices against purchase orders (POs), confirm receipt of goods/services, resolve discrepancies, obtain managerial approval, and schedule payment.

---

### **1. Participants (Swimlanes/Roles)**

*   **Accounts Payable (AP) Clerk (Mary):** Responsible for initial intake, logging, matching, and final scheduling.
*   **Purchasing Officer (Dan):** Responsible for confirming receipt of goods/services and validating order details.
*   **Department Manager (Carlos):** Responsible for final budgetary and validity approval.
*   **AP Supervisor (Rita):** Handles complex exceptions, escalations, and non-standard invoices.
*   **Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen):** Ad-hoc support role for resolving contractual disputes or missing data with external suppliers.
*   **Supplier (External):** Submits invoices; provides corrections or missing information.
*   **Accounting System:** Automated entity that triggers emails and stores data.

---

### **2. Process Artifacts (Data Objects)**

*   **Supplier Invoice:** The primary input document (received via email).
*   **Purchase Order (PO):** Internal reference document used for validation.
*   **Goods/Services Receipt Confirmation:** Proof that items were received.
*   **Discrepancy Report/Email:** Communication regarding mismatches or errors.

---

### **3. Detailed Process Flow (BPMN Textual Representation)**

#### **Phase 1: Intake & Validation**
**(Role: AP Clerk - Mary)**

*   **Start Event:** Invoice received (via Email).
*   **Activity 1.1: Validate Invoice Header**
    *   *Action:* Check for valid Purchase Order (PO) Number.
    *   **Gateway (XOR): Valid PO Number?**
        *   **[Path: NO]**
            *   **Activity 1.2:** Request Details/Rejection.
            *   *Action:* Send email to Supplier requesting PO number or more details.
            *   *Flow:* **(Loop back to Start)** *or* **End (if invalid).**
        *   **[Path: YES]**
            *   **Activity 1.3:** Log Invoice.
            *   *Action:* Enter invoice details into the Accounting System.

#### **Phase 2: Operational Verification (The "2-Way" Check)**
**(Handoff: AP Clerk  Purchasing)**

*   **Activity 2.1: Request Receipt Confirmation.**
    *   *Action (AP Clerk):* Forward invoice to Purchasing Department.
*   **Activity 2.2: Verify Goods/Services Receipt.**
    *   *Action (Purchasing Officer - Dan):* Compare invoice details against PO (Quantities, Prices, Delivery).
    *   **Gateway (XOR): Order Details Correct?**
        *   **[Path: NO (Discrepancy Found)]**
            *   **Activity 2.3:** Initiate Discrepancy Resolution.
            *   *Action (Purchasing):* Contact Supplier directly OR instruct AP to hold invoice.
            *   *Sub-flow (Optional):* Involve **Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen)** to verify contract terms/pricing.
            *   *Flow:* Await corrected invoice/details  **(Return to Activity 2.2).**
        *   **[Path: YES]**
            *   **Activity 2.4:** Confirm Receipt.
            *   *Action (Purchasing):* Send confirmation email/update to AP Clerk.

#### **Phase 3: Matching & Exception Handling (The "3-Way" Match)**
**(Role: AP Clerk - Mary)**

*   **Activity 3.1: Perform Line-Item Matching.**
    *   *Action:* Match Invoice line items against PO and Receipt Confirmation in system.
    *   **Gateway (XOR): Match Successful?**
        *   **[Path: YES]**  **Proceed to Phase 4.**
        *   **[Path: NO (Mismatch)]**
            *   **Activity 3.2:** Attempt Clarification.
            *   *Action (AP Clerk):* Email Purchasing team to clarify mismatch.
            *   **Gateway (XOR): Issue Resolved?**
                *   **[Path: YES]**  **Proceed to Phase 4.**
                *   **[Path: NO (Unresolved/Escalation)]**
                    *   **Flow:** Escalate to **AP Supervisor (Rita)**.

#### **Phase 3b: Exception Management (Escalation Path)**
**(Role: AP Supervisor - Rita)**

*   **Activity 3b.1:** Analyze Escalated Invoice.
    *   *Context:* Missing PO, informal contracts, or unresolvable pricing errors.
*   **Gateway (XOR): Resolution Type?**
    *   **[Path A: Retrospective Processing]**
        *   **Activity 3b.2:** Coordinate Retrospective PO creation with Dept Heads.
    *   **[Path B: External Intervention]**
        *   **Activity 3b.3:** Liaise with **SRM (Karen)** or Supplier to correct data.
    *   **[Path C: Rejection]**
        *   **Activity 3b.4:** Reject Invoice.
        *   *Flow:* **End Event (Cancelled).**
*   **Join:** After Paths A or B are complete  **Return to Phase 4 (Manager Approval).**

#### **Phase 4: Authorization**
**(Role: Department Manager - Carlos)**

*   **Intermediate Trigger:** System automatically sends approval request notification.
*   **Activity 4.1: Review & Approve.**
    *   *Input:* View Invoice, PO, and Receipt Confirmation in system.
    *   **Gateway (XOR): Approval Status?**
        *   **[Path: REJECTED / SUSPICIOUS]**
            *   **Activity 4.2:** Reject/Hold Invoice.
            *   *Action:* Flag for investigation by AP Clerk.
            *   *Flow:* **(Return to Phase 3).**
        *   **[Path: APPROVED]**
            *   *Action:* Click "Approve" in system.

#### **Phase 5: Finalization**
**(Role: AP Clerk - Mary)**

*   **Activity 5.1: Schedule Payment.**
    *   *Action:* Add approved invoice to the next payment cycle/run.
*   **End Event:** Invoice Scheduled for Payment.

### **4. Key Variations & Ambiguities Noted**

1.  **Order of Verification:**
    *   *Standard Flow:* Mary (AP) forwards to Dan (Purchasing) first for receipt check, then performs the detailed 3-way match herself upon return.
    *   *Variation:* Dan (Purchasing) indicates he also checks prices/quantities when he receives the invoice. This suggests a pre-matching validation step occurs in the Purchasing lane before official confirmation is sent back to AP.

2.  **Missing PO Scenarios:**
    *   *Strict Path:* If no PO exists at intake (Phase 1), it is returned immediately.
    *   *Exception Path:* Rita (Supervisor) notes that sometimes invoices arrive without POs for "informal services." These are not always rejected outright but are handled via a "Retrospective PO" workflow managed by the Supervisor.

3.  **Discrepancy Ownership:**
    *   Both the AP Clerk and Purchasing Officer contact suppliers regarding discrepancies depending on where the error is caught (Pre-match vs. Post-match). The **Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen)** acts as a floating resource who can be pulled in at any point by either party for contract-specific disputes.
