**Interview Questions to Clarify the Property Onboarding Process**

1. **Initial Contact & Documentation**
   - What specific criteria do you use to evaluate the completeness and validity of the landlord’s documentation (e.g., deeds, compliance certificates, inspection reports) when they first contact your office?

2. **Documentation Gaps**
   - Can you describe a typical situation where a landlord’s documentation is missing or outdated, and how you guide them to provide the necessary updates?

3. **Property Data Entry**
   - Which key property attributes are recorded in the central database, and how do you ensure consistency and accuracy when entering these details?

4. **Manager Assignment**
   - What factors determine which property manager is assigned to a new property (e.g., workload, geographic proximity, property type, experience), and how do you communicate that decision to both the landlord and the manager?

5. **Expectation Alignment**
   - During the introductory meeting with the landlord, what are the most important topics you discuss to set expectations regarding property management, tenant policies, and maintenance responsibilities?

6. **Photography & Inspection Coordination**
   - How do you schedule and coordinate the arrival of the professional photographer and the certified inspector on the property, and what are the typical timelines for these activities?

7. **Inspection Findings**
   - If the inspector identifies issues that need to be resolved before the property can be listed, what is the usual process for communicating those findings to the landlord and arranging for repairs?

8. **Marketing & Pricing**
   - In what ways does the digital marketing team use the inspection report and property photos to shape the online listing, and how do they adjust the pricing model based on market trends?

9. **System Activation**
   - What steps are taken to “activate” the property in the rental portal and internal systems, and how do you verify that the listing is correctly populated with all required information?

10. **Tenant Screening**
    - Can you walk me through the tenant screening workflow, including the types of checks you run (credit, employment, references) and how you handle multiple interested applicants?

11. **Lease Finalization**
    - What key elements are included in the lease agreement you finalize with the tenant, and how do you ensure that any special clauses (e.g., pet policies, custom terms) are documented and agreed upon?

12. **Security Deposit & MoveIn**
    - How is the security deposit collected and recorded in your system, and what procedures are in place for scheduling and confirming the movein date with the tenant?

13. **Quality Checks & Audits**
    - Who conducts the compliance and financial audits after a property is onboarded, and what specific checks do they perform to verify lease adherence and pricing accuracy?

14. **Exception Handling**
    - Could you provide examples of how you manage exceptions such as properties requiring special insurance, landlords with custom lease clauses, or other unique requirements that deviate from the standard process?

15. **Communication & Tracking**
    - How do you track the progress of each property through the various stages of onboarding, and what communication channels (e.g., email, CRM, project management tools) are used to keep all stakeholders informed?

16. **Stakeholder Coordination**
    - Which internal teams (e.g., regional office, compliance, finance, IT) and external partners (e.g., photographers, inspectors, contractors) are involved at each stage, and how do you ensure clear handoffs between them?

17. **Timing & Sequencing**
    - Overall, what is the typical endtoend timeline for onboarding a new property from the landlord’s initial contact to the property being listed and ready for tenant applications?

18. **Continuous Improvement**
    - Based on past experiences, what changes or refinements have you implemented to streamline the onboarding process, and how do you measure the impact of those improvements?
