purchase_to_pay_log_skeleton = {

This model describes the following:

1. **Equivalence**:

2. **Always Before**:

    - 'Goods Received' must be preceded by 'Purchase Order Sent'.
    - 'Invoice Processed' must be preceded by 'Goods Received'.

3. **Always After**:

    - 'Purchase Order Sent' will always be followed by 'Goods Received'.
    - 'Invoice Processed' will always be followed by 'Invoice Received'.

4. **Never Together**:
    - 'Cancelled Order' cannot co-exist with 'Purchase Order Sent' or 'Goods Received' since cancelled orders mean no purchases or goods would have been received.

5. **Activity Occurrences**:
    - 'Requisition Submitted', 'Purchase Order Sent', 'Invoice Received', and 'Invoice Processed' occur exactly once in a case.
    - 'Goods Received' can occur 0 or 1 time (in case of partial fulfillment or no receipt of goods).
    - 'Payment Issued' can occur 0 or 1 time (payment might be blocked in certain situations).
    - 'Cancelled Order' can occur 0 or 1 time.

6. **Directly-Follows**:
    - 'Requisition Submitted' is immediately followed by 'Purchase Order Sent'.
    - 'Purchase Order Sent' is immediately followed by 'Goods Received'.
    - 'Goods Received' is immediately followed by 'Invoice Received'.
    - 'Invoice Received' is directly followed by 'Invoice Processed'.
    - 'Invoice Processed' is directly followed by 'Payment Issued'.
