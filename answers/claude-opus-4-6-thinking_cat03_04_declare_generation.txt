## Rationale Summary

| Constraint Family | Key Modeling Decisions |
|---|---|
| **Existence / Exactly-one / Init** | All 10 activities must appear. *IG*, *AG*, and *FL* are one-time milestones. Every trace starts with *IG*. |
| **Response + Precedence  Succession** | Encodes the main pipeline: `IG  DD  {TFC, CE}  PC  {LT, UT}  AG  {MP, FL}`. Both TFC **and** CE must precede PC (dual gating). Both LT **and** UT must precede AG. |
| **Coexistence** | LTUT (both testing types required together), TFCCE (both evaluations required together), AGFL and MPFL (launch requires both approval and marketing). |
| **Alt / Chain** | *IG* is immediately followed by *DD* (chain-succession). After *AG*, the *Marketing Plan* is the next immediate step (chain-response), and *MP* immediately precedes *FL* (chain-precedence). |
| **Negative constraints** | After *Final Launch* the process cannot loop back to *IG*, *DD*, or *PC* (non-succession). Several "shortcut" direct adjacencies are forbidden (non-chain-succession), e.g., jumping from *IG* straight to *FL*. |
