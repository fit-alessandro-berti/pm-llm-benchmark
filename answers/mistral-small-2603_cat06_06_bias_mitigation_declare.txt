### Explanation of Added Constraints:

1. **New Activities Added**:
   - `ManualReview`: Required for all final decisions to ensure human oversight
   - `BiasMitigationCheck`: Additional fairness check before decisions
   - `Reject_Minority`/`Approve_Minority`: Special cases for decisions involving minority applicants

2. **Bias Mitigation Constraints**:
   - **Coexistence Constraints**:
     - All final decisions must coexist with manual reviews (`FinalDecision`  `ManualReview`)
     - Decisions involving minorities must coexist with manual reviews (`Reject_Minority`  `ManualReview`)

   - **Response/Succession Constraints**:
     - Sensitive attribute checks (`CheckApplicantRace`) must be followed by bias mitigation checks
     - Bias mitigation checks must precede final decisions

   - **Non-succession Constraints**:
     - Prevent direct transitions from sensitive attribute checks to decisions (`CheckApplicantRace`  `Reject/Approve`)
     - This prevents immediate biased outcomes based on sensitive attributes

   - **Absence Constraints**:
     - Limit direct rejections/approvals for minority applicants without proper checks

3. **Chain Constraints**:
   - Enforce strict sequences where sensitive attribute checks must be followed by bias mitigation before decisions

These constraints collectively ensure that:
- Decisions involving sensitive attributes are always subject to additional scrutiny
- There are no direct paths from sensitive attribute disclosure to decisions
- All decisions are preceded by proper review processes
- The model enforces fairness by requiring additional checks for potentially biased cases

The constraints work together to create a process that is more resistant to discrimination while maintaining the original process flow for non-sensitive cases.
