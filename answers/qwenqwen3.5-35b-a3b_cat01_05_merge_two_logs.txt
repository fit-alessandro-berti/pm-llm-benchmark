## 2. Integration Reasoning & Methodology

Events were matched based on a combination of **Order ID**, **Semantic Event Name**, and **Temporal Proximity**.
*   **Order ID:** All events shared `Order#1234`, allowing direct comparison without filtering.
*   **Semantic Mapping:** Event names were normalized to align common meanings:
    *   `OrderReceived` (B) $\leftrightarrow$ `Order Received` (A)
    *   `OrderValidation` (B) $\leftrightarrow$ `Order Validated` (A)
    *   `PaymentCheck` (B) $\leftrightarrow$ `Payment Processed` (A)
    *   `Shipping` (B) $\leftrightarrow$ `Item Shipped` (A)
*   **Temporal Tolerance:** A strict tolerance of **< 2 seconds** was applied for automatic merging. However, semantic consistency was prioritized for the "Payment" event (see below) to avoid data fragmentation.
