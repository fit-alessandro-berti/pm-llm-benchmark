| 4 | **Assembly / Welding** | • Pick up welding tool  <br>• Weld cornerA  <br>• Weld cornerB | The **toolhandling** (pickup) and the **actual welding** are executed consecutively by the **same operator (OperatorB)**. >   2. **Resource affinity** – most groups involve a single operator or a single machine.
