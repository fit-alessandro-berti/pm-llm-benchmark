Here's a DuckDB SQL query that fulfills the specified requirements:

###  **Explanation of the Logic:**

1. **Identify cases with the exact sequence** of three consecutive activities:
   - `'Approve Purchase Requisition'`  `'Create Order'`  `'Confirm Order'`
   - These must appear **in direct succession**, meaning no other event occurs between them in the same case.

2. **Check the time condition**:
   - The time difference between `'Create Order'` and `'Confirm Order'` must be **more than 5 days**.

3. **Exclude** all events from such cases.

4. **Return all events** from remaining cases.

To detect the direct succession, we use `LEAD()` to look ahead two steps within each `case_id`, ordered by timestamp.

---

###  **Final Query:**

```sql
WITH ordered_events AS (
    SELECT 
        case_id,
        activity,
        timestamp,
        LEAD(activity, 1) OVER (PARTITION BY case_id ORDER BY timestamp) AS next_activity,
        LEAD(timestamp, 1) OVER (PARTITION BY case_id ORDER BY timestamp) AS next_timestamp,
        LEAD(activity, 2) OVER (PARTITION BY case_id ORDER BY timestamp) AS next_next_activity,
        LEAD(timestamp, 2) OVER (PARTITION BY case_id ORDER BY timestamp) AS next_next_timestamp
    FROM event_log
),
cases_with_sequence AS (
    SELECT DISTINCT case_id
    FROM ordered_events
    WHERE activity = 'Approve Purchase Requisition'
      AND next_activity = 'Create Order'
      AND next_next_activity = 'Confirm Order'
      AND next_next_timestamp > next_timestamp + INTERVAL 5 DAY
)
SELECT e.*
FROM event_log e
WHERE e.case_id NOT IN (
    SELECT case_id FROM cases_with_sequence
)
ORDER BY e.case_id, e.timestamp;
```

---

###  **How It Works:**

- **`ordered_events` CTE**:
  - Uses `LEAD()` to look at the next two activities and timestamps within each case.
  - Ensures the three specific activities occur in order **with no intervening events**.

- **`cases_with_sequence` CTE**:
  - Filters only those cases where:
    - The sequence matches exactly.
    - The time between `'Create Order'` and `'Confirm Order'` is **more than 5 days**.

- **Final SELECT**:
  - Returns **all events** from `event_log` **except** those from cases identified in `cases_with_sequence`.
  - Results are ordered by `case_id` and `timestamp` for readability.

---

This query ensures correctness by checking both **activity sequence** and **temporal constraints**, then properly excludes matching cases.
