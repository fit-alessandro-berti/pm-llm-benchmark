# BPMN-like Textual Process Model: Invoice Approval Workflow

## 1. Process Overview
*   **Process Name:** Invoice Approval and Payment Scheduling
*   **Goal:** Validate, approve, and schedule incoming invoices for payment based on Purchase Orders (POs).
*   **Primary Trigger:** Receipt of an invoice (typically via email).
*   **Primary End State:** Invoice scheduled for payment run.
*   **Key Stakeholders:** Accounts Payable (AP), Purchasing, Department Manager, AP Supervisor, Supplier Relationship Manager (SRM), Supplier.

## 2. Swimlanes / Participants
*   **Accounts Payable (AP):** Mary (Clerk), Rita (Supervisor).
*   **Purchasing:** Dan (Officer).
*   **Department Management:** Carlos (Manager).
*   **External Support:** Karen (SRM), Supplier.
*   **System:** Accounting/ERP System.

## 3. Process Flow Description

### Phase 1: Intake and Initial Validation
1.  **Start Event:** Invoice Received (Email).
2.  **Task (AP - Mary):** **Check Invoice for PO Number.**
    *   *Input:* Invoice.
    *   *Output:* PO Status (Present/Absent).
3.  **Gateway (Exclusive):** **PO Number Valid?**
    *   **Path A (Yes):** Proceed to **Log Invoice in System**.
    *   **Path B (No):** Proceed to **Handle Missing PO**.
        *   *Note:* Mary states she sends it back to the supplier. Karen (SRM) states she contacts suppliers for missing POs. *Model Decision:* AP initiates contact; SRM supports if contract terms are needed.

### Phase 2: Purchasing Verification
4.  **Task (AP - Mary):** **Log Invoice into Accounting System.**
    *   *Artifact:* Invoice Record created.
5.  **Task (AP - Mary):** **Forward to Purchasing for Receipt Confirmation.**
    *   *Trigger:* System notification sent to Purchasing.
6.  **Task (Purchasing - Dan):** **Verify Goods/Services Received & Details.**
    *   *Action:* Compare Invoice vs. PO (Quantities, Prices, Delivery Charges).
7.  **Gateway (Exclusive):** **Purchase Order Details Correct?**
    *   **Path A (Yes):** Proceed to **Send Confirmation to AP**.
    *   **Path B (No):** Proceed to **Resolve Discrepancy**.
        *   *Action:* Contact Supplier (Directly by Dan or via Karen).
        *   *Action:* Ask AP to hold invoice until corrected.
        *   *Loop:* Return to **Task 6** once corrected.
        *   *Escalation:* If unresolved, escalate to **AP Supervisor (Rita)**.

### Phase 3: Invoice Matching
8.  **Task (AP - Mary):** **Match Invoice Line Items to PO.**
    *   *Condition:* Performed only after Purchasing Confirmation (Task 7).
    *   *Artifact:* Matched Invoice Record.
9.  **Gateway (Exclusive):** **Invoice Matches PO?**
    *   **Path A (Yes):** Proceed to **Request Manager Approval**.
    *   **Path B (No):** Proceed to **Resolve Discrepancy**.
        *   *Action:* Email Purchasing to clarify.
        *   *Escalation:* If discrepancy cannot be resolved easily, escalate to **AP Supervisor (Rita)** or involve **SRM (Karen)** for pricing/contract checks.
        *   *Loop:* Return to **Task 6** or **Task 8** once resolved.

### Phase 4: Manager Approval
10. **Task (System):** **Trigger Manager Approval Notification.**
    *   *Condition:* Invoice matched and Purchasing confirmed.
11. **Task (Manager - Carlos):** **Review and Approve Invoice.**
    *   *Input:* Invoice details, PO, Purchasing Confirmation.
    *   *Check:* Budget compliance, suspicious items.
12. **Gateway (Exclusive):** **Manager Approval Granted?**
    *   **Path A (Yes):** Proceed to **Schedule for Payment**.
    *   **Path B (No):** Proceed to **Handle Rejection**.
        *   *Action:* Reject or Ask AP to investigate/hold.
        *   *Loop:* Return to **Task 8** or **Task 11** (Investigation).

### Phase 5: Payment Execution
13. **Task (AP - Mary):** **Schedule for Payment Run.**
    *   *Input:* Approved Invoice.
    *   *Output:* Payment Transaction.
14. **End Event:** Payment Scheduled.

### Exception Handling (Supervisor & SRM)
*   **AP Supervisor (Rita):**
    *   *Entry Point:* Escalation from Mary or Purchasing regarding unmatched invoices, missing POs, or unresolved discrepancies.
    *   *Task:* Contact suppliers, coordinate departments, create retrospective POs (if informal contracts exist), or authorize rejection.
    *   *Exit:* Return to **Phase 2** or **Phase 3** flow once exception is cleared.
*   **Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen):**
    *   *Entry Point:* Invoked by AP or Purchasing for pricing discrepancies or missing PO terms.
    *   *Task:* Contact supplier, verify contract pricing, clarify terms.
    *   *Exit:* Return resolution data to AP/Purchasing.

## 5. Model Notes & Ambiguities
*   **PO Missing Flow:** Mary states she sends the invoice back to the supplier. Karen (SRM) states she contacts suppliers directly for missing POs.
    *   *Resolution:* The model places the initial responsibility on AP (Mary), but allows SRM (Karen) to intervene if contract terms or supplier relationship issues arise.
*   **Matching Sequence:** Mary states she forwards to Purchasing *to confirm receipt*, then matches. Dan states he checks details (prices/quantities).
    *   *Resolution:* The model sequences Purchasing Verification (Dan) *before* Final Matching (Mary) to ensure data integrity, as Dan confirms the goods were actually received as per Mary's account.
*   **Manager Trigger:** Mary states the system sends an automatic email. Carlos states he gets a notification after matching.
    *   *Resolution:* The model treats this as a System Task that triggers the Manager Task, consistent with both accounts.
*   **Escalation:** Rita (Supervisor) is the primary escalation point for exceptions. Karen (SRM) is a specialized escalation point for supplier-side issues (pricing/contracts).
    *   *Resolution:* Rita manages the process flow exceptions; Karen manages the external relationship exceptions.
*   **Payment Scheduling:** Mary says she schedules it. Rita says it goes back to AP.
    *   *Resolution:* The final task is assigned to the AP role (Mary), as she is the operational executor.

*   **Lane 3 (Manager):** Review -> Approve/Reject.
*   **Lane 4 (Support):** Resolve Exceptions (Rita/Karen).

*   **End:** Payment Scheduled.
