## How activity names were determined
- Low-level actions were **rolled up into analyst-friendly steps**:
  - `TYPING`  “Draft/Compose/Update …”
  - `SAVE`  “Save …”
  - `CLICK` with email intent (“Open email”, “Reply…”, “Send Email”)  “Open/Start Reply/Send …”
  - `HIGHLIGHT`  “Highlight Key Findings in PDF”
  - `CLOSE`  “Close …”
- `FOCUS`/`SWITCH` were not treated as standalone activities except when they clearly indicate **opening a new artifact** (e.g., opening the spreadsheet or returning to a document).
