## Rationale for Added Constraints:

1. **Existence Constraints for ManualReview and BiasMitigationCheck**: Ensure these fairness activities must occur in every trace, making bias mitigation mandatory.

2. **Responded Existence & Coexistence Constraints**: 
   - If sensitive attributes (Race, Age, Gender) are checked, ManualReview must exist in the trace
   - This prevents decisions based solely on sensitive attributes without human oversight

3. **Response Constraints**: 
   - Whenever sensitive attributes are checked, a BiasMitigationCheck must eventually follow
   - Ensures systematic bias evaluation before final decisions

4. **Precedence Constraints**:
   - BiasMitigationCheck and ManualReview must occur before any FinalDecision
   - Prevents decisions without proper fairness verification

5. **Non-succession & Non-chain-succession Constraints**:
   - Prevent direct/immediate succession from checking sensitive attributes to Reject decisions
   - Eliminates the possibility of immediate biased rejections based on demographic factors

These constraints create a robust fairness framework by:
- Mandating human oversight for cases involving sensitive attributes
- Requiring systematic bias checks before final decisions
- Preventing direct causal links between demographic checks and negative outcomes
- Ensuring transparency and accountability through required review steps
