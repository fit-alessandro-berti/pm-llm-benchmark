### Explanation of the Estimation Strategy:
1. **Activity Clusters**: Logistics activities (`OP`, `RC`, `DT`, `AS`) have much higher standard deviations and average times to reflect ocean/air freight variability and consumer behavior. Manufacturing operations (`QI`, `CA`, `PT`, `PK`) have much tighter deviations and shorter elapsed times reflecting controlled factory environments.
2. **Standard Deviation Accumulation**: For `eventually_follows` relationships separated by multiple steps, the total Standard Deviation does not strictly sum together, but generally follows the square root of the sum of the variances $\sqrt{\Sigma \sigma^2}$, plus marginal buffers for handovers between different business domains (e.g. factory floor to shipping logistics).
