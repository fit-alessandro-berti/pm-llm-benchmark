  - **Fraud Prevention:** Mandatory QA reviews (E) at multiple stages reduce bad loans.  
  - **Operational Efficiencies:** *Exactly_One (B)* prevents redundant steps, lowering costs.  
  - **Reputation Management:** Transparent steps (e.g., mandatory notifications) reduce customer disputes.

### **Interplay of Rules for Control**
- **Precedence/Succession:** Enforce a logical flow (e.g., no approval before credit checks), preventing shortcuts.  
- **QA as a Gatekeeper:** QA (*E*) acts as a checkpoint between *Gather_Documents (C)* and *Authorize_Terms (F)*, ensuring only viable applications proceed.  
- **End-to-End Traceability:** Each step’s dependency (e.g., *Assemble_Package (D)*  *QA (E)*  *Authorize (F)*) creates an audit trail for regulators.

This model transforms a loan process into a system where every action is justified, ordered, and validated, balancing efficiency with rigor.
