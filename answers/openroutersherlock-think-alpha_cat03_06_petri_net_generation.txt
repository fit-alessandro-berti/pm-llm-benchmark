## Explanation
This accepting Petri net models the insurance claim process as follows:
- **Main sequence**: start  C  CR  DV  (docs check)  FC  (fraud check)  CA  (approval check)  P  N  CL  end.
- **DV-RDS loop**: From `p_after_DV`, either silent transition to FC (docs OK) or RDS back to before DV (re-submission).
- **FC-II loop**: From `p_after_FC`, either silent transition to CA (fraud OK) or II back to before FC (investigation).
- **Conditional AP**: From `p_after_CA`, either AP or silent transition (no approval needed) to before P.
- Silent transitions (`label=None`) handle choices without observable activities.
- Initial marking: token in `p_start`. Final marking: token in `p_end`.
