## BPMN-like Textual Process Model: Invoice Approval Process

### Participants / Lanes
- **AP Clerk** (Accounts Payable Clerk ñ Mary)
- **Purchasing Officer** (Dan)
- **Department Manager** (Carlos)
- **AP Supervisor** (Rita)
- **Supplier Relationship Manager** (Karen)
- **Supplier** (external)

### Start Event
Invoice arrives (usually by email).

### Flow (Main Path)

1. **AP Clerk** checks if the invoice has a valid purchase order (PO) number.  
   - **Gateway 1** (Exclusive): Has PO?  
     - **If NO**:  
       a. **AP Clerk** sends invoice back to Supplier requesting PO details. (Task: Request PO from Supplier)  
       b. Supplier provides a revised invoice or PO number. (Message / loop back to step 1)  
       *Note: If Supplier fails to respond, escalate to AP Supervisor (see Exceptions).*  
     - **If YES**: Proceed to step 2.

2. **AP Clerk** logs the invoice into the accounting system. (Task: Log Invoice)

3. **AP Clerk** forwards the invoice to **Purchasing Officer** for confirmation that goods/services were received. (Task: Forward to Purchasing)

4. **Purchasing Officer** verifies invoice details against the PO (quantities, prices, delivery charges). (Task: Verify Invoice vs PO)  
   - **Gateway 2** (Exclusive): Are there discrepancies?  
     - **If NO discrepancies**:  
       a. **Purchasing Officer** sends confirmation email to AP Clerk. (Task: Send Confirmation)  
       b. Proceed to step 5.  
     - **If YES (discrepancies exist)**:  
       - **Gateway 2a** (Exclusive): Can the discrepancy be resolved easily?  
         - **If YES** (e.g., contact supplier directly):  
           a. **Purchasing Officer** contacts supplier for correction. (Task: Contact Supplier)  
           b. Supplier sends corrected invoice. (Message)  
           c. Loop back to step 4 (re-verify).  
         - **If NO** (cannot be resolved easily):  
           a. **AP Clerk** escalates to **AP Supervisor**. (Task: Escalate to Supervisor)  
           b. **AP Supervisor** investigates, coordinates with **Supplier Relationship Manager** if needed (e.g., price check, missing PO, retrospective PO creation). (Task: Handle Exception)  
           c. After resolution, invoice returns to step 4 for re-verification.  
   *Alternative sub-case*: If **Purchasing Officer** finds PO number missing (rare after step 1), then **AP Clerk** is informed to request PO from supplier (loop to step 1b).

5. **AP Clerk** (or system) triggers manager approval ñ an automatic email is sent to **Department Manager**. (Task: Request Manager Approval)

6. **Department Manager** reviews invoice details, PO, and confirmation. (Task: Review and Approve)  
   - **Gateway 3** (Exclusive): Approve or reject?  
     - **If APPROVE**:  
       a. **Department Manager** approves in system. (Task: Approve Invoice)  
       b. Proceed to step 7.  
     - **If REJECT** (or suspicious):  
       a. **Department Manager** rejects or asks **AP Clerk** to hold and investigate. (Task: Reject/Hold for Investigation)  
       b. **AP Clerk** investigates (may involve **AP Supervisor** or other departments).  
       c. After resolution, loop back to step 5 for re-submission.

7. **AP Clerk** schedules invoice for payment (adds to next payment cycle). (Task: Schedule Payment)

### End Event
Invoice paid.
