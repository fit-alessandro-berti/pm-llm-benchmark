### **POWL Model for the Manufacturing & Distribution Process**

The POWL model is structured as a **StrictPartialOrder** with the following nodes and execution order:

---

#### **Nodes**
1. **OR** (Order Received)
2. **MS** (Material Sourcing)
3. **QS** (Quality Screening)
4. **\* (AS, IQC)** (Loop: Assembly  Quality Check  [exit or redo])
5. **TST** (Testing)
6. **\* (TST, RT)** (Loop: Testing  Re-Testing  [exit or redo])
7. **PK** (Packaging)
8. **X (LB, )** (Choice: Labeling or skip)
9. **DP** (Distribution Planning)
10. **D** (Dispatch)

---

#### **Partial Order (Execution Constraints)**
```
OR  MS
MS  QS
QS  * (AS, IQC)
* (AS, IQC)  TST
TST  * (TST, RT)
* (TST, RT)  PK
PK  X (LB, )
X (LB, )  DP
DP  D
```

---

#### **Operator Definitions**
1. **Loop \* (AS, IQC)**:
   - Execute **AS** (Assembly).
   - Then, either:
     - **Exit**  Proceed to **TST** (if quality check passes).
     - **Execute IQC followed by AS**  Repeat the loop (if quality check fails).

2. **Loop \* (TST, RT)**:
   - Execute **TST** (Testing).
   - Then, either:
     - **Exit**  Proceed to **PK** (if test passes).
     - **Execute RT followed by TST**  Repeat the loop (if test fails).

3. **Choice X (LB, )**:
   - Exclusive choice between:
     - **LB** (Labeling)  Proceed to **DP**.
     - **** (Silent transition, skip labeling)  Proceed to **DP**.

---

### **Explanation of Key Components**
- **\* (AS, IQC)** captures the repeated assembly-quality check cycle observed in Cases 1, 2, 3, and 5.
- **\* (TST, RT)** models the testing-rework loop seen in Cases 1 and 5.
- **X (LB, )** accounts for the optional labeling step (skipped in Case 2).
- The **StrictPartialOrder** enforces the linear sequence from **OR** to **D**, with concurrency only where explicitly allowed (none in this model).

This structure aligns with all provided event logs while minimizing unnecessary complexity.
