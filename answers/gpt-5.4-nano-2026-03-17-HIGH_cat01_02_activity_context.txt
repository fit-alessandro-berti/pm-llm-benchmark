### Brief justification for the naming changes (contextual clues used)

- **Initiate Request**
  - Renamed to distinguish **channel/context**:
    - *Online Portal Submission* vs *Branch Walk-in* (clue: `WebPortal` vs `BranchReception` and “walk-in customer at branch”).

- **Verify Documents** (appears multiple times in **C001**, and again later in **C003**)
  - Split into variants based on **stage and what changed before the re-check**:
    - *Initial ID Document Check* (C001 09:20)
    - *Re-check After ID Upload* (C001 10:30; clue: “now that ID scan is provided”)
    - *Physical ID Verification* (C003 11:25; clue: branch/physical verification)
    - *Re-verify After Account Adjustment* (C003 12:20; clue: “after account adjustment”)

- **Call Customer** (appears multiple times in **C002**, plus different intent across cases)
  - Renamed to capture the **purpose** of each call:
    - *Request Missing Documents* (C001; clue: “verification incomplete…missing docs”)
    - *Clarify Account Discrepancy* (C002 first call; clue: “clarify account discrepancy”)
    - *Confirm Adjustments Made* (C002 second call; clue: “follow-up…confirm adjustments made”)
    - *Prompt Return After Leaving* (C003; clue: “customer stepped out…calls to have them return”)

- **Adjust Account**
  - Differentiated by **why** the adjustment happens:
    - *Update Details After Call* (C002; clue: “after call”)
    - *Internal Correction (Missing Info)* (C003; clue: “due to missing info”)

- **Approve Request**
  - Kept essentially the same, with a small qualifier (*Supervisor Final Approval*) to make the role explicit (clue: `Supervisor` and “Final approval given”).
