### **Final Recommendations**
1. **Prioritize High-Complexity Claims:**
   - Implement **automated pre-screening** to flag high-complexity claims early.
   - **Assign dedicated adjusters** for high-complexity cases to reduce bottlenecks.

2. **Balance Workload Across Adjusters:**
   - **Redistribute Region B cases** to prevent Adjuster_Lisa from being overloaded.
   - **Hire additional adjusters** for high-volume regions.

3. **Enforce SLAs for Approvals & Payments:**
   - Set **strict time limits** for approvals (e.g., 4 hours for low/medium, 24 hours for high).
   - **Automate payment processing** to ensure prompt closure.

4. **Standardize Regional Processes:**
   - **Investigate why Region B is slower** (e.g., local regulations, resource constraints).
   - **Provide additional training** to Region B teams if needed.

5. **Monitor & Continuously Improve:**
   - **Track case durations by complexity, region, and resource** to identify new bottlenecks.
   - **Use predictive analytics** to forecast delays based on historical patterns.

By addressing these root causes, the insurance claims process can be **streamlined, reducing average case duration and improving customer satisfaction**.
