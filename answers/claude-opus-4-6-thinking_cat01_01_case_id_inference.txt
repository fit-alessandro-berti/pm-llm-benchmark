# Case Grouping Analysis

## Approach

The key discriminators are **patient_age + patient_gender** (acting as a pseudo-identifier) combined with **department context** and the **logical clinical pathway** each patient follows.

---

## Identified Cases

### **Case A — 45-year-old Male (ER, Trauma/Fracture pathway)**

|-------|-----------|----------|
| 1 | 08:05:13 | Register patient (ER, insurance=private) |

| 12 | 08:23:10 | Doctor consultation (diagnosis=hairline_fracture) |
| 15 | 08:30:05 | Patient discharged (followup=outpatient_clinic) |

> **Narrative:** Male presents to ER  triaged as code 3 (urgent)  X-ray of left leg  diagnosed with hairline fracture  discharged with outpatient follow-up.
