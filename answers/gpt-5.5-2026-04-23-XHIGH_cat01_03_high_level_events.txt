## Proposed High-Level Manufacturing Activities

### 1. Material Loading, Identification, and Preheating

**Low-level events included:**

- Retrieve raw metal sheet
- Scan barcode
- Place metal sheet on table
- Align metal sheet
- Preheat metal sheet

**Rationale:**

These events all prepare the raw material for downstream manufacturing. The operator first retrieves and identifies the correct material, then places it in the work area. The robot aligns it, and the heating unit preheats it to the required temperature. Together, these actions form a coherent preparation phase before welding begins.

**Proposed high-level activity name:**

> **Material Preparation**

---

### 2. Welding Setup and Execution

**Low-level events included:**

- Pick up welding tool
- Weld corner A
- Weld corner B

**Rationale:**

These events represent the actual assembly or joining operation. Picking up the welding tool is directly tied to the welding task and is followed immediately by the weld operations. The events are performed by the same resource, Operator B, and together form the core fabrication step.

**Proposed high-level activity name:**

> **Welding / Assembly**

---

### 3. In-Process Weld Quality Check

**Low-level events included:**

- Measure weld integrity

**Rationale:**

This event verifies whether the welding operation was successful before the product moves to finishing. It is performed by a quality sensor and evaluates a specific process outcome, namely weld integrity. Since it occurs immediately after welding and before coating, it is best treated as an in-process inspection rather than a final inspection.

**Proposed high-level activity name:**

> **Weld Quality Inspection**

---

### 4. Surface Finishing and Coating Cure

**Low-level events included:**

- Apply protective coating
- Dry coating

**Rationale:**

These events are part of the finishing stage. The coating unit applies a protective layer, and the heating unit cures or dries that coating. The two actions are logically dependent and together produce the finished surface condition of the item.

**Proposed high-level activity name:**

> **Protective Coating and Drying**

---

### 5. Final Visual Inspection

**Low-level events included:**

- Visual check

**Rationale:**

This is the final human quality check after welding and coating are complete. It verifies the finished product visually and produces a pass/fail-style result. Because it occurs after all production and finishing activities, it should be modeled as a separate final inspection stage.

**Proposed high-level activity name:**

> **Final Inspection**

---

## Structured Mapping of Low-Level Events to High-Level Activities

| High-Level Activity | Low-Level Activities | Main Resources | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Material Preparation** | Retrieve raw metal sheet; Scan barcode; Place metal sheet on table; Align metal sheet; Preheat metal sheet | Operator A, Robot Arm #2, Heating Unit #1 | Prepare, identify, position, and thermally condition the raw material |
| **Welding / Assembly** | Pick up welding tool; Weld corner A; Weld corner B | Operator B | Join or assemble the metal sheet through welding |
| **Weld Quality Inspection** | Measure weld integrity | Quality Sensor #1 | Verify that the weld meets quality requirements before finishing |
| **Protective Coating and Drying** | Apply protective coating; Dry coating | Coating Unit #3, Heating Unit #2 | Apply and cure the protective surface coating |
| **Final Inspection** | Visual check | Operator C | Perform final visual quality verification |

## General Aggregation Rules

A practical rule set for the full event log would be:

```text
IF Activity IN {
    "Retrieve raw metal sheet",
    "Scan barcode",
    "Place metal sheet on table",
    "Align metal sheet",
    "Preheat metal sheet"
}
THEN HighLevelActivity = "Material Preparation"

IF Activity IN {
    "Pick up welding tool",
    "Weld corner A",
    "Weld corner B",
    "Weld corner C",
    "Weld corner D",
    ...
}
THEN HighLevelActivity = "Welding / Assembly"

IF Activity IN {
    "Measure weld integrity",
    "Check weld strength",
    "Validate weld quality"
}
THEN HighLevelActivity = "Weld Quality Inspection"

IF Activity IN {
    "Apply protective coating",
    "Dry coating",
    "Cure coating"
}
THEN HighLevelActivity = "Protective Coating and Drying"

IF Activity IN {
    "Visual check",
    "Final visual inspection",
    "Operator final check"
}
THEN HighLevelActivity = "Final Inspection"
```

## Final High-Level Process Flow

The overall manufacturing workflow can therefore be represented as:

```text
Material Preparation
        
Welding / Assembly
        
Weld Quality Inspection
        
Protective Coating and Drying
        
Final Inspection
```

This abstraction removes unnecessary low-level detail while preserving the meaningful phases of the manufacturing process.
