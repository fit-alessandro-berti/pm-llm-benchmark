### **BPMN-Like Textual Process Model: Invoice Approval Process**

#### **Process Overview**
The invoice approval process begins when an invoice is received (typically via email) and ends when it is scheduled for payment. The process involves multiple roles (Accounts Payable Clerk, Purchasing Officer, Department Manager, Accounts Payable Supervisor, Supplier Relationship Manager) and includes decision points, rework loops, and exception handling.

---

### **Structured Process Description**

#### **1. Start Event: Invoice Received**
- **Trigger:** Invoice arrives (usually via email).
- **Responsible Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary).

#### **2. Activity: Check for Valid PO Number**
- **Task:** Verify if the invoice has a valid Purchase Order (PO) number.
- **Decision Gateway:**
  - **Condition:** Does the invoice have a valid PO?
    - **Yes:** Proceed to **Log Invoice**.
    - **No:** **Send to Supplier for PO Details** (Mary requests missing information).
      - **Subsequent Action:** Supplier provides PO or revised invoice  Return to **Check for Valid PO Number**.

#### **3. Activity: Log Invoice**
- **Task:** Mary logs the invoice into the accounting system.
- **Output:** Invoice is recorded in the system with PO reference.

#### **4. Activity: Forward to Purchasing for Confirmation**
- **Task:** Mary forwards the invoice to the Purchasing Officer (Dan) to confirm receipt of goods/services.
- **Responsible Role:** Purchasing Officer (Dan).

#### **5. Activity: Purchasing Confirmation**
- **Task:** Dan compares invoice details (quantities, prices, delivery charges) against the PO.
- **Decision Gateway:**
  - **Condition:** Does the invoice match the PO?
    - **Yes:** Dan sends confirmation email to Mary  Proceed to **Match Invoice to PO**.
    - **No:**
      - **Sub-Process: Resolve Discrepancy**
        - Dan contacts supplier or asks Mary to hold the invoice.
        - If supplier corrects the invoice  Return to **Check for Valid PO Number**.
        - If discrepancy persists  Escalate to **Accounts Payable Supervisor (Rita)** or **Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen)** for resolution.
          - **Karen’s Role:** Verifies contract terms, requests corrected invoice from supplier.
          - **Rita’s Role:** Coordinates between departments, may create retrospective PO for informal services.
          - **Resolution Path:** Once resolved, return to **Purchasing Confirmation**.

#### **6. Activity: Match Invoice to PO**
- **Task:** Mary matches invoice line items against the PO in the system.
- **Decision Gateway:**
  - **Condition:** Do all line items match?
    - **Yes:** Proceed to **Request Manager Approval**.
    - **No:**
      - Mary emails Purchasing (Dan) to clarify.
      - If unresolved  Escalate to **Rita** (see **Resolve Discrepancy** above).

#### **7. Activity: Request Manager Approval**
- **Trigger:** System sends automatic email to Department Manager (Carlos) after PO match and receipt confirmation.
- **Task:** Carlos reviews invoice, PO, and confirmation details.
- **Decision Gateway:**
  - **Condition:** Does Carlos approve the invoice?
    - **Yes:** Proceed to **Schedule Payment**.
    - **No:**
      - Carlos rejects or requests further investigation.
      - Mary holds the invoice and investigates (may loop back to **Purchasing Confirmation** or **Resolve Discrepancy**).

#### **8. Activity: Schedule Payment**
- **Task:** Mary schedules the invoice for the next payment run.
- **Responsible Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary).
- **End Event:** Invoice is queued for payment.

---

### **Roles and Responsibilities**
| **Role**                     | **Responsibilities**                                                                 |
|------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary) | Receives invoices, checks PO validity, logs invoices, matches to PO, schedules payment. |
| Purchasing Officer (Dan)     | Confirms receipt of goods/services, resolves discrepancies with suppliers.            |
| Department Manager (Carlos)  | Approves/rejects invoices based on budget and correctness.                            |
| AP Supervisor (Rita)        | Handles exceptions, escalates unresolved issues, coordinates retrospective POs.      |
| Supplier Relationship Mgr (Karen) | Resolves supplier-related discrepancies (missing PO, pricing errors).          |

---

### **Artifacts (Data Objects)**
- **Invoice** (Input: Received via email; Output: Approved for payment)
- **Purchase Order (PO)** (Referenced for validation)
- **Confirmation Email** (From Purchasing to AP)
- **System Notifications** (Automatic emails for manager approval)
- **Corrected Invoice** (From supplier in case of discrepancies)

---

### **Exceptions and Variations**
1. **Missing PO:**
   - Invoice is returned to supplier  Supplier provides PO  Process restarts at **Check for Valid PO Number**.
   - If no PO exists (e.g., informal service), Rita may create a retrospective PO.

2. **Discrepancies (Pricing/Quantities):**
   - Dan/Purchasing holds invoice  Supplier contacted for correction  Process resumes at **Purchasing Confirmation**.
   - If unresolved, escalated to Rita/Karen.

3. **Manager Rejection:**
   - Carlos rejects invoice  Mary investigates  May loop back to **Purchasing Confirmation** or **Resolve Discrepancy**.

4. **Informal Invoices:**
   - No PO exists  Rita coordinates with department heads to create retrospective PO or reject.

---

### **Sequence Flow Summary**
1. **Start**  **Check for Valid PO**  [No PO]  **Request PO from Supplier**  **Check for Valid PO** (Loop)
2. [Valid PO]  **Log Invoice**  **Forward to Purchasing**  **Purchasing Confirmation**
3. [Match]  **Match Invoice to PO**  [No Match]  **Resolve Discrepancy** (Loop back to Purchasing or Escalate)
4. [Match]  **Request Manager Approval**  [Reject]  **Investigate** (Loop back)
5. [Approve]  **Schedule Payment**  **End**

---

### **Notes on Ambiguities/Conflicts**
- **Purchasing Confirmation Timing:** Dan’s description suggests he checks the PO *after* receiving the invoice from AP, but Mary implies she logs it first. Model assumes logging happens before Purchasing confirmation.
- **Manager Approval Trigger:** Mary and Carlos agree it’s automatic after PO match + receipt confirmation.
- **Payment Scheduling:** All roles assume AP handles this after approval, but Rita’s involvement in exceptions is explicit.

This model can be directly translated into a BPMN diagram with pools for each role, gateways for decisions, and clear sequence flows.
