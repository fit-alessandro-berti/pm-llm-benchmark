**Key assumptions behind the estimates**

- **Procurement phase SS → OP → RC**: Largest delays and variance. Supplier lead times dominate and are heavily influenced by geography, customs, and part complexity.
- **Manufacturing phase RC → QI → CA → PT**: Hours to several days. Assembly and testing have moderate variance due to batching, rework, and resource contention.
- **Fulfillment phase PK → WS → DT**: Typically 1 to 5 days. Distribution variance is high because it depends on carrier, distance, and service level.
- **After-sales AS**: Anchored far from upstream steps. Standard deviation grows with temporal distance because more independent factors accumulate.
