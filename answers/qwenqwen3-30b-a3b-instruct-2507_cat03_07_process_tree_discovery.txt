We want a process tree that:

- Captures the **loop** correctly (i.e., repeat `RMI  VF` until exit)

This suggests using:
- `->` (Sequence) for the main flow
- `*` (Loop) for the repeatable RMI/VF block
- `X` (XOR) is not needed since the loop is not a choice, but a repetition
- No parallelism (`+`) is observed

- The loop is structured as:
  - `* ( ->(RMI, VF), AD )`
