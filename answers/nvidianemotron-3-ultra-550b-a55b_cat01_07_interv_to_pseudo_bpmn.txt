#### **3. Process Flow Description (Main Flow & Variations)**

**Start Event:** **Invoice Received (Email to AP Inbox)**

---

##### **Phase 1: Intake & PO Validation (AP Clerk)**
1.  **Task: Receive & Initial Screen Invoice** (AP Clerk)
    *   *Input:* Email with Invoice attachment.
    *   *Action:* Open invoice; check for **Purchase Order (PO) Number**.
    *   **Exclusive Gateway: [PO Number Present?]**
        *   **No (Missing PO):**
            *   **Task: Request PO/Details from Supplier** (AP Clerk / *supported by Karen*)
                *   Send email to Supplier requesting PO reference or corrected invoice.
                *   *Variant:* If informal contract suspected  **Escalate to AP Supervisor (Rita)** for Retrospective PO creation (see Exception Path E1).
            *   **Intermediate Catch Event: Wait for Supplier Response** (Timer: e.g., 5-10 business days).
            *   *Loop Back:* Return to **Task: Receive & Initial Screen Invoice** upon reply.
        *   **Yes (PO Present):**
            *   **Task: Log Invoice in Accounting System** (AP Clerk)
                *   Create record; attach scan; status = *"Logged / Awaiting Validation"*.

---

##### **Phase 2: Goods Receipt & PO Matching (Purchasing)**
2.  **Task: Forward to Purchasing for Validation** (AP Clerk  System Notification to Dan)
    *   *System Action:* Auto-email with link to Invoice/PO in ERP.
3.  **Task: Validate Invoice vs. PO & Goods Receipt** (Purchasing Officer - Dan)
    *   *Action:* Compare Invoice Line Items (Qty, Price, Charges) against PO and internal Goods Receipt records.
    *   **Exclusive Gateway: [Details Match & Goods Confirmed?]**
        *   **No (Discrepancy Found):**
            *   **Task: Communicate Discrepancy** (Purchasing Officer  AP Clerk)
                *   Email/System flag detailing issue (e.g., "Billed for 10, received 8", "Price mismatch", "Missing delivery charge").
            *   **Parallel Path / Decision: [Discrepancy Type?]**
                *   *Price/Contract Terms Issue:* **Task: Engage Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen)**  Karen checks contract  Advises correct price  Informs AP/Purchasing.
                *   *Qty/Receipt Issue:* Purchasing contacts Supplier directly or asks AP to **Task: Request Corrected Invoice from Supplier**.
                *   *Complex/Unresolvable:* **Escalate to AP Supervisor (Rita)** (See Exception Path E2).
            *   **Intermediate Catch Event: Wait for Resolution** (Corrected Invoice / Confirmation).
            *   *Loop Back:* Return to **Task: Validate Invoice vs. PO & Goods Receipt** (Re-validation).
        *   **Yes (Match Confirmed):**
            *   **Task: Send Confirmation to AP** (Purchasing Officer  System/Email)
                *   Status Update: *"Goods Confirmed / PO Matched"*.

---

##### **Phase 3: 3-Way Match & Manager Approval (AP Clerk  Manager)**
4.  **Task: Perform 3-Way Match in System** (AP Clerk)
    *   *Action:* System-assisted match: Invoice  PO  Goods Receipt Confirmation.
    *   *Note:* Mary performs final system match after Dan's confirmation.
    *   **Exclusive Gateway: [System Match Successful?]**
        *   **No (System Flag/Exception):**  **Escalate to AP Supervisor (Rita)** (Exception Path E2).
        *   **Yes (Clean Match):**
            *   **Task: Route to Department Manager for Approval** (AP Clerk / System Auto-Trigger)
                *   *Trigger:* System sends automatic email/notification to Manager (Carlos) with Invoice, PO, Match Report, Purchasing Confirmation.
5.  **Task: Review & Approve/Reject Invoice** (Department Manager - Carlos)
    *   *Action:* Review details, budget impact, anomalies.
    *   **Exclusive Gateway: [Manager Decision?]**
        *   **Reject / Investigate:**
            *   **Task: Return to AP Clerk with Comments** (Manager  AP Clerk)
                *   *Action:* AP Clerk investigates (may loop back to Purchasing or Supplier via Karen).
                *   *Loop Back:* Return to **Task: Perform 3-Way Match** or **Phase 2**.
        *   **Approve:**
            *   *System Action:* Status = *"Approved for Payment"*; Audit trail recorded.

---

##### **Phase 4: Payment Scheduling (AP Clerk)**
6.  **Task: Schedule for Payment Run** (AP Clerk)
    *   *Action:* Add to next payment batch; verify payment terms/dates.
7.  **End Event: Invoice Paid / Process Complete**
    *   *Output:* Payment file generated; Invoice status = *"Paid"* in ERP.

#### **4. Exception & Sub-Process Details**

##### **Exception Path E1: Missing PO / Informal Engagement (Handled by AP Supervisor - Rita)**
*   **Trigger:** AP Clerk cannot obtain PO from Supplier; or Supplier claims no PO issued (informal service).
*   **Sub-Process: Retrospective PO / Exception Resolution** (AP Supervisor - Rita)
    1.  **Task: Investigate Engagement Validity** (Rita + Dept Head / Karen)
        *   Check contracts, email trails, budget codes.
    2.  **Exclusive Gateway: [Valid Engagement?]**
        *   **No:** **Task: Reject Invoice & Notify Supplier** (Rita/AP Clerk)  **End Event: Process Terminated (Rejected)**.
        *   **Yes:** **Task: Create Retrospective PO / Cost Center Assignment** (Rita + Purchasing/Finance).
    3.  **Task: Re-inject into Main Flow** (Rita  AP Clerk)
        *   Invoice routed back to **Phase 2 (Purchasing Validation)** with new PO reference.

##### **Exception Path E2: Unresolvable Discrepancy / System Match Failure (Escalation to Rita)**
*   **Trigger:** Purchasing cannot resolve with Supplier; 3-Way Match fails system controls; Manager requests deep dive.
*   **Sub-Process: Deep-Dive Resolution** (AP Supervisor - Rita)
    1.  **Task: Coordinate Stakeholders** (Rita facilitates AP, Purchasing, Manager, Karen, Supplier).
    2.  **Task: Determine Resolution** (Credit Note, Debit Note, Price Adjustment, Write-off).
    3.  **Task: Authorize Exception Payment / Correction** (Rita + Manager approval if amount > threshold).
    4.  **Merge Back:** Route corrected invoice to **Phase 3 (Manager Approval)** or **Phase 4 (Payment)** if Manager already approved pending correction.
