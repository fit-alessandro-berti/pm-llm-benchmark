This DECLARE model captures the logical flow of the product design and launch process:

- **existence**: All activities must occur in every trace
- **init**: Process always starts with Idea Generation
- **responded_existence**: Critical milestones must be responded to
- **coexistence**: Parallel activities like feasibility/cost checks after design
- **response/precedence/succession**: Sequential dependencies (e.g., design  approval  prototype)
- **alt- templates**: Alternative paths (feasibility OR cost before approval)
- **chain- templates**: Multi-step sequences (designfeasibilityapproval)
- **non- templates**: Prevents illogical sequences (no launch before idea generation)
