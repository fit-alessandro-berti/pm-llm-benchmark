This adjustment might reward a member of a certain community, potentially granting an advantage that weaves into the scoring process, thus introducing bias. 2. **Different Outcomes for Non-Resident Cases:**

3. **Bias Might Hindering Judge Merit:**

Situations where creditworthiness might be similar but the **LocalResident** status differs could lead to a discrepancy that wouldn't necessarily exist if judging was tensor-based and unbiased on this attribute.
