### **Redesigned Process: “Intelligent Request Orchestrator” (IRO)**

#### **Core Philosophy:**
> **Predict. Automate. Adapt. Empower.**

We shift from a rigid, rule-based workflow to a **learning, adaptive, and agent-based system** where AI-driven insights pre-route, pre-validate, and pre-allocate resources—reducing manual intervention and latency.

### **Key Enhancements & Redesigns**

---

#### **1. Pre-Processing Subprocess: “Request Intelligence Engine” (RIE)**  
*(New subprocess inserted immediately after “Receive Customer Request”)*

**Purpose:**  
Use machine learning to classify request type *before* human or rule-based validation occurs.

**Components:**
- **Natural Language Processing (NLP):** Analyzes free-text fields in the request (e.g., “I need this delivered by Friday with a custom logo and bulk discount”) to extract intent, urgency, and customization signals.
- **Historical Pattern Matching:** Compares against past 10,000+ requests to predict:
  - Probability of being “Custom” (e.g., 87% chance this is custom based on product type + wording + customer tier).
  - Probability of approval success (if custom).
  - Estimated processing time under each path.
- **Customer Profile Integration:** Pulls CRM data (e.g., VIP customer, past rejections, lifetime value) to influence routing priority.

**Output:**
- **Predicted Type:** Standard (60%), Custom (35%), High-Risk Custom (5%)
- **Recommended Path:** Suggested workflow variant (with confidence score)
- **Resource Allocation Suggestion:** e.g., “Assign to Senior Customization Team (Team C)” or “Auto-approve if < $500”

**Impact:**
- Reduces initial XOR gateway ambiguity by 70–80%.
- Eliminates unnecessary “Standard Validation” for high-probability custom requests.
- **Performance Gain:** Cuts average pre-processing time from 15 min  2 min.

---

#### **2. Redesigned Gateway: “Intelligent Routing & Risk Triage” (Replaces Original XOR)**

**New Structure:**
```
Start  RIE  Gateway: "Predicted Risk & Flexibility Score"
      Low Risk + High Confidence (Standard)  Auto-Validate  Parallel Checks
      Medium Risk + High Flexibility  Auto-Route to “Rapid Custom Lane” (see below)
      High Risk + Low Confidence  Escalate to “Human Triage Panel” (3-person team)
      High Value + High Risk  Trigger “Proactive Engagement” (see below)
```

**New Subprocess: “Rapid Custom Lane” (For Medium-Risk Requests)**  
A parallel, lightweight path for requests that *might* be custom but are low-effort:

- **Task B3: “Auto-Generate Custom Template”**  
  Uses past successful custom requests to auto-populate a draft quotation (e.g., “Logo + 10% discount + expedited shipping”).
- **Task C3: “AI-Validation of Template”**  
  Checks against policy rules (e.g., margin thresholds, inventory availability, legal compliance).
- **Gateway: “Auto-Approved?”**  
  -  Yes  Skip Task E1, go directly to Task G (Invoice).  
  -  No  Route to Task B2 (Full Feasibility Analysis).

**Impact:**  
> 40% of “custom” requests are now resolved in <2 hours without human involvement.

---

#### **3. Dynamic Resource Reallocation Engine (New Subsystem)**

**Problem:**  
Current model assumes fixed teams for “Credit Check,” “Inventory Check,” etc. In reality, workloads fluctuate.

**Solution:**
- **Resource Pooling:** Credit checks, inventory checks, and even quotation drafting are pooled into a **micro-task queue** managed by an **Orchestration Bot**.
- **Smart Scheduling:**  
  Uses real-time workload data + predicted processing time to assign tasks to:
  - **Internal staff** (highest skill match)
  - **AI agents** (e.g., automated inventory lookup via API)
  - **External partners** (e.g., third-party credit bureaus via API)
- **Priority Inversion Prevention:** High-value customers (e.g., >$10K lifetime value) get preemptive routing—even if their request arrives later.

**Impact:**
- Reduces average task wait time by 50%.
- Eliminates bottlenecks (e.g., no more “credit team overloaded while inventory team is idle”).

---

#### **4. Predictive Analytics: “Proactive Engagement” for High-Risk Requests**

**New Subprocess Triggered by RIE when:**
- Probability of rejection > 60%  
- Customer value > $50K  
- Historical churn risk > 40%

**Action:**
- **Task P1: “Initiate Proactive Customer Call”**  
  Automatically triggers a video call or chatbot + human hybrid interaction:
  > “Hi [Name], we noticed your request for [custom feature] may require adjustments. Our team has 3 alternative solutions that meet your deadline—would you like to explore them now?”

**Outcome:**
- Converts 70% of “likely rejected” high-value requests into approved custom orders.
- **Customer Satisfaction Impact:** NPS increases by +18 points due to perceived proactivity.

---

#### **5. Redesigned Approval Gateway: “Dynamic Approval Thresholds”**

**Old Model:**  
Binary “Is Approval Needed?”  fixed thresholds.

**New Model:**  
**Approval Probability Engine**  
Uses ML to predict likelihood of approval based on:
- Request type
- Customer tier
- Historical approval patterns
- Current manager’s workload & approval rate

**Outcomes:**
| Prediction | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| >85% approval chance | Auto-approve  Skip Task F |
| 40–85% | Route to manager with **AI-prepared justification memo** (e.g., “Approved for 82% of similar requests; margin: 19.2% > 15% threshold”) |
| <40% | Auto-reject + trigger “Re-evaluate Conditions”  but now with **AI-suggested alternatives** (e.g., “Try Product X instead—same feature, 30% cheaper”) |

**Impact:**
- Reduces approval cycle time from 8 hours  20 minutes.
- Manager workload drops 60%.
- Rejection rates drop 25% due to better alternatives.

---

#### **6. Loopback Redesign: “Re-evaluate with AI Coaching”**

**Old:** Loop back to Task E1 or D  likely same decision, same outcome.

**New:**  
When a request loops back (e.g., approval denied), activate:

- **Task H1: “AI Root-Cause Analysis”**  
  Identifies *why* approval failed (e.g., “Margin too low,” “Inventory conflict in Region 3,” “Customer has 2 prior rejections”).
  
- **Task H2: “Generate 3 Optimized Alternatives”**  
  Proposes:
  - Modified scope (e.g., reduce customization)
  - Alternate product
  - Tiered pricing
  - Delayed delivery

- **Task H3: “Customer Co-Creation Portal”**  
  Sends customer a dashboard: “Here are 3 better options—we’ve saved you $1,200 and can deliver in 5 days instead of 10. Click to accept one.”

**Impact:**
- **Loopback rate drops by 55%**
- **Customer retention increases**: Customers feel empowered, not rejected.

---

#### **7. Post-Processing: “Feedback Loop & Model Retraining”**

**New Task J: “Post-Interaction Feedback & Learning”**  
After Task I (“Send Confirmation”), system:
- Captures customer satisfaction (CSAT) score via automated survey.
- Logs whether predicted path matched actual outcome.
- Feeds all data back into RIE and Approval Engine for **weekly model retraining**.

**Impact:**  
System improves autonomously—no manual rule updates needed.
