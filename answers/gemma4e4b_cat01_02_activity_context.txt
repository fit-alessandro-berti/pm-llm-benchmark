### 1. Identification of Contextually Ambiguous Activities

The activities that appear multiple times and require renaming are:

1.  **`Verify Documents`**: This activity occurs multiple times for different reasons (initial check vs. re-check) and involves different resources (BackOfficeAgent vs. BranchOfficer).
2.  **`Call Customer`**: This activity is fundamentally an outbound call, but its purpose shifts dramatically (requesting missing items, clarifying discrepancies, following up on adjustments).
3.  **`Initiate Request`**: Although the function is clear (starting the case), the method of initiation changes (web portal vs. physical branch visit), which is important context.

### 3. Justification of Naming Choices

**1. Activity: `Verify Documents`**

*   **New Names Used:**
    *   `Perform Initial Document Verification`: Used for the very first time the identity documents are checked (C001: 09:20; C003: 11:25). This signifies the start of the due diligence process.
    *   `Review Documents After Upload`: Used when the customer provides new information or documents (C001: 10:30). This specifically denotes a re-evaluation based on new input.
    *   `Review Documents After Adjustment`: Used when the underlying data/account has changed, requiring a fresh look at the documents (C003: 12:20). This highlights the dependency on a prior process change.

**2. Activity: `Call Customer`**

*   **New Names Used:**
    *   `Outbound Call: Request Missing Info`: Used when the agent needs the customer to provide documentation or details (C001: 09:45). Focuses on the *action* needed from the customer.
    *   `Outbound Call: Clarifying Account Discrepancy`: Used when the primary goal of the call is to resolve a specific mismatch in the account data (C002: 09:00).
    *   `Outbound Call: Customer Coordination`: Used when the call is for logistical purposes, like arranging for the customer's physical presence (C003: 11:50).
    *   `Outbound Call: Follow-up Confirmation`: Used when the goal is simply to confirm that a previous internal action (like an account adjustment) was completed successfully (C002: 09:50).

**3. Activity: `Initiate Request`**

*   **New Names Used:**
    *   `Start Service Request (Web Portal)`: Used when the interaction begins digitally, emphasizing the electronic source.
    *   `Start Service Request (Branch Reception)`: Used when the interaction begins physically, emphasizing the in-person source.

*(Note: The activity `Adjust Account` was slightly renamed to `Adjust Account Details` for consistency across both cases, although its core meaning remained clear.)*
