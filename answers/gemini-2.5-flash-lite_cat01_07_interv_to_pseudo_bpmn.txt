**Process Name:** Invoice Approval Process

**Version:** 1.0

**Description:** This process outlines the steps involved in approving and processing invoices received by the company, from initial receipt through to scheduling for payment. It incorporates typical paths, decision points, and exception handling scenarios as described by stakeholders.

---

**1. Participants (Roles & Systems):**

*   **AP Clerk:** Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary)
*   **Purchasing Officer:** Purchasing Department Staff (Dan)
*   **Dept. Manager:** Department Manager responsible for budget/approval (Carlos)
*   **AP Supervisor:** Accounts Payable Supervisor (Rita)
*   **SRM:** Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen)
*   **Supplier:** External Vendor providing goods/services.
*   **Accounting System:** The company's financial/accounting software.
*   **Notification System:** Email or internal system for alerts.

---

**2. Artifacts (Data Objects/Documents):**

*   **Invoice:** The primary document received from the supplier.
*   **PO Reference:** Purchase Order number or document.
*   **Goods/Service Confirmation:** Confirmation that goods were received or services rendered.
*   **Corrected Invoice:** An updated invoice from the supplier.
*   **Clarification Request:** Communication seeking details on discrepancies.
*   **System Notification:** Automated alerts (e.g., email for approval).
*   **Contract Details:** Supplier contract information.
*   **Retrospective PO:** A PO created after the fact for informal services.

---

**3. Process Flow:**

**(Start Event)**
*   **1. Invoice Received**
    *   **Source:** Supplier
    *   **Artifact:** Invoice (typically via Email)

**(Activities & Gateways - Accounts Payable Clerk)**
*   **2. Check for Valid PO Number**
    *   **Role:** AP Clerk
    *   **Artifact:** Invoice
    *   **Gateway:** `[Decision: PO Number Present?]`
        *   **Path: NO (PO Number Missing)**
            *   **2a. Request PO Number / Details from Supplier**
                *   **Role:** AP Clerk
                *   **Assisted by:** SRM (if direct contact required for resolution)
                *   **Artifact:** Invoice, Clarification Request
                *   **Gateway:** `[Decision: PO Number/Details Obtained?]`
                    *   **Path: NO**
                        *   **3a. Hold Invoice (or Reject)**
                            *   **Role:** AP Clerk (may involve AP Supervisor for extended holds)
                            *   **Artifact:** On-Hold Invoice
                            *   **(Exception End Event)**
                    *   **Path: YES**
                        *   **Artifact:** Valid PO Reference, Corrected Invoice
                        *   **Transition:** Rejoin flow at Step 4 (after logging).
        *   **Path: YES (PO Number Present)**
            *   **4. Log Invoice into Accounting System**
                *   **Role:** AP Clerk
                *   **Artifact:** Invoice, Accounting System
                *   **Transition:** To Step 5.

*   **5. Forward Invoice to Purchasing for Receipt Confirmation**
    *   **Role:** AP Clerk
    *   **Artifact:** Invoice forwarded.
    *   **Transition:** To Step 6.

**(Activities & Gateways - Purchasing Officer)**
*   **6. Verify Invoice against PO & Confirm Goods/Services Received**
    *   **Role:** Purchasing Officer
    *   **Artifact:** Invoice, PO Details, Goods Received Record
    *   **Gateway:** `[Decision: Invoice Details Match PO & Goods/Services Confirmed Received?]`
        *   **Path: NO (Discrepancy or Issue)**
            *   **6a. Clarify Discrepancy / Missing Information**
                *   **Role:** Purchasing Officer
                *   **Options:**
                    *   Contact Supplier Directly.
                    *   Request AP Clerk to Hold Invoice.
                    *   Inform AP Clerk of Missing PO (if missed earlier).
                    *   **Assisted by:** SRM (for contract/pricing issues, supplier contact).
                *   **Artifact:** Discrepancy Issue, Contract Details.
                *   **Gateway:** `[Decision: Discrepancy Resolved by Purchasing/Supplier Interface?]`
                    *   **Path: NO (Unresolved)**
                        *   **7a. Escalate Issue to AP Supervisor**
                            *   **Role:** Purchasing Officer / AP Clerk
                            *   **Transition:** To Step 8 (Exception Handling).
                    *   **Path: YES (Resolved)**
                        *   **Artifact:** Resolved Invoice, Corrected Information.
                        *   **Transition:** Rejoin flow at Step 6b (after verification).
        *   **Path: YES (Match Confirmed)**
            *   **6b. Send Confirmation of Goods/Services Received to AP**
                *   **Role:** Purchasing Officer
                *   **Artifact:** Confirmation (Email/System Update)
                *   **Transition:** To Step 9.

**(Activities & Gateways - AP Clerk - Post-Confirmation)**
*   **9. Match Invoice Line Items Against PO in System**
    *   **Role:** AP Clerk
    *   **Artifact:** Invoice, PO, Accounting System
    *   **Gateway:** `[Decision: All Invoice Line Items Match PO?]`
        *   **Path: NO (Discrepancy Found by AP Clerk)**
            *   **9a. Email Purchasing Team to Clarify Discrepancy**
                *   **Role:** AP Clerk
                *   **Artifact:** Clarification Request Email.
                *   **Gateway:** `[Decision: Discrepancy Resolved by Purchasing/Supplier Clarification?]`
                    *   **Path: NO (Unresolved)**
                        *   **9b. Escalate Discrepancy to AP Supervisor**
                            *   **Role:** AP Clerk
                            *   **Transition:** To Step 8 (Exception Handling).
                    *   **Path: YES (Resolved)**
                        *   **Artifact:** Resolved Discrepancy.
                        *   **Transition:** Rejoin flow at Step 9 (Retry Matching).
        *   **Path: YES (Match Confirmed)**
            *   **Transition:** To Step 10.

*   **10. Forward Invoice to Department Manager for Approval**
    *   **Role:** AP Clerk
    *   **Artifact:** Invoice, PO, Confirmation Details
    *   **Transition:** To Step 11.

**(Activities & Gateways - Department Manager)**
*   **11. Review Invoice, PO, and Confirmation Details**
    *   **Role:** Dept. Manager
    *   **Artifact:** Invoice, PO, Confirmation, Budget Information (Implied)
    *   **Gateway:** `[Decision: Manager Approves Invoice?]`
        *   **Path: NO (Suspicious, Budget Difference, Unexpected Items)**
            *   **11a. Reject Invoice or Request Further Investigation/Hold**
                *   **Role:** Dept. Manager (Action passed to AP Clerk)
                *   **Artifact:** Rejection/Hold Instruction
                *   **Transition:** To Step 8 (Exception Handling).
        *   **Path: YES (Approved)**
            *   **11b. Approve Invoice in System**
                *   **Role:** Dept. Manager
                *   **Artifact:** Approved Invoice (System Update)
                *   **Transition:** To Step 12.

**(Activities & Gateways - Exception Handling)**
*   **8. Exception Handling & Resolution**
    *   **Source:** Step 7a (Unresolved Purchasing Issue), Step 9b (Unresolved AP Match Issue), Step 11a (Manager Hold/Investigation)
    *   **Role:** AP Supervisor (Primary), AP Clerk, Dept. Heads
    *   **Description:** AP Supervisor coordinates resolution of complex issues. This may involve contacting suppliers, clarifying with departments, or creating retrospective POs for informal services.
    *   **Artifact:** Resolution Plan, Retrospective PO, Corrected Invoice.
    *   **Gateway:** `[Decision: Exception Resolved?]`
        *   **Path: NO**
            *   **8a. Hold Invoice / Further Escalation**
                *   **Role:** AP Supervisor
                *   **Artifact:** On-Hold Invoice
                *   **(Exception End Event)**
        *   **Path: YES**
            *   **Artifact:** Cleared Invoice, Approved for further processing.
            *   **Transition:** Rejoin normal flow at the *next logical step*.
                *   **[Uncertainty/Consolidation]:** If the exception prevented Purchasing confirmation, it may need re-confirmation. If it was after confirmation but before matching, it leads to matching (Step 9). If after matching, it leads to Manager Approval (Step 11). Based on Rita's description ("...goes back into the normal flow: confirmation from Purchasing, approval by the Manager..."), it implies it will proceed through subsequent steps of the *normal* flow, assuming prior steps are implicitly satisfied or re-validated. The model shows it rejoining at Step 9 (AP Clerk matches) as a common re-entry point after issues are resolved.

**(Activities - Accounts Payable Clerk - Payment)**
*   **12. Schedule Invoice for Payment**
    *   **Role:** AP Clerk
    *   **Artifact:** Approved Invoice, Payment Schedule
    *   **Transition:** To Step 13.

**(End Event)**
*   **13. Payment Scheduled**
    *   **Outcome:** Invoice is included in the next payment run.
