## Explanation of Modeling Choices

| Constraint Type | Business Rationale |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| **init / exactly_one (IG)** | Every product lifecycle starts once with a single idea. |
| **existence (DD, AG, FL)** | Design, approval, and launch are mandatory milestones. |
| **succession (AG  FL)** | The Approval Gate strictly controls whether a launch happens. |
| **precedence (DD  PC)** | You cannot build a prototype without a design. |
| **coexistence (LT  UT)** | Lab and user testing are bundled in the validation phase. |
| **chainsuccession (MP  FL)** | The launch immediately follows finalizing the marketing plan. |
| **nonsuccession (FL  DD/PC)** | Once launched, you can't loop back to redesign/prototyping. |

This model captures **ordering**, **mandatory presence**, **mutual dependencies**, and **forbidden sequences**, giving a robust declarative specification of the process.
