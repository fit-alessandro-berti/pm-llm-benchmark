**Rationale for Added Constraints:**

1. **Absence Constraints**: Prevent explicit discriminatory outcomes like "ApproveWithoutReview_Minority" or "RejectWithoutReview_Female" from occurring at all.

2. **Responded Existence & Chain Precedence**: Ensure that any check of sensitive attributes MUST be followed by a BiasMitigationCheck, preventing race/gender/age information from being processed without proper safeguards.

3. **Coexistence Constraints**: Require that checking sensitive attributes must always coexist with a ManualReview, ensuring human oversight when demographic information is accessed.

4. **Response/Precedence/Succession Constraints**: Create mandatory sequences where BiasMitigationCheck must occur before ManualReview, and ManualReview must occur before FinalDecision, ensuring proper review processes.

5. **Non-coexistence/Non-succession Constraints**: Prevent any direct relationship between checking sensitive attributes and rejection decisions, eliminating the possibility of immediate biased rejections based on demographics alone.

These constraints ensure that sensitive demographic information cannot lead directly to negative outcomes, requires mandatory bias mitigation checks, and ensures human oversight (ManualReview) is always present when sensitive attributes are considered in decision-making.
