**Overview**

It is divided into four vertical swimlanes, each representing a specific department: **Sales**, **Production**, **Procurement**, and **Warehouse**. The process flows generally from top to bottom, with interactions between departments indicated by dashed lines (message flows).

*   **Handoff:** The process ends with a message event "Product complete," which is sent to the Warehouse.
