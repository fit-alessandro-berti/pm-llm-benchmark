**Rationale for added constraints:**
- `responded_existence(CheckApplicantRace/Gender/Age, ManualReview)`: If sensitive attributes are inspected, a ManualReview must appear in the trace, preventing automated decisions based solely on protected characteristics.
- `coexistence(Reject/Approve_Minority, ManualReview)`: Any rejection (or minority-specific approval) must be accompanied by a ManualReview, enforcing human oversight for high-risk decisions.
- `response(CheckApplicantRace/Gender, BiasMitigationCheck)`: After viewing sensitive data, a bias-mitigation step is required before proceeding, inserting a fairness checkpoint.
- `nonchainsuccession(CheckApplicantRace/Gender, Reject/Approve)`: Prevents an immediate jump from viewing protected attributes to a final decision, reducing the possibility of snap biased judgments.

These constraints collectively reduce the model's ability to produce discriminatory traces while preserving the original loan-application flow.
