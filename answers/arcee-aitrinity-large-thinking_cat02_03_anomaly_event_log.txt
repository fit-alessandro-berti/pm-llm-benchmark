### Analysis of the Procure-to-Pay (P2P) Event Log

The standard P2P process follows a strict sequence to ensure proper authorization, budget compliance, and accurate payments. Key controls include:  
1. **Approve Purchase Requisition** before **Issue Purchase Order**.  
2. **Issue Purchase Order** before **Receive Goods**.  
3. **Receive Goods** (or service completion) before **Vendor Invoice Received** (for goods) and before **Release Payment**.  
4. **Match Invoice to PO** (threeway match) before **Release Payment**.  

Any deviation from this flow may indicate control failures, fraud risks, or operational inefficiencies.
