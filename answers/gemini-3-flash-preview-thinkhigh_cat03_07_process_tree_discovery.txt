    * (
        tau,

### **Explanation of the Model**
*   **The Outer Loop `* ( tau, ... )`**: The "A" part of the loop is a silent step (`tau`). This allows the process to either exit immediately after **AE** (Case 2) or enter the "redo" part of the loop.
*   **The Redo Sequence `-> ( ..., 'VF' )`**: If the loop is entered, the process must perform the information request(s) and then a re-validation (**VF**).
*   **The Inner Loop `* ( 'RMI', tau )`**: This captures the behavior in Case 3, where **Request Missing Info (RMI)** can occur once or multiple times consecutively before the form is re-validated.
*   **Fitness & Precision**: This model accounts for all traces in the log (including the multiple RMIs in Case 3) while remaining simple and allowing for the generalizations described in the scenario (multiple re-validation cycles).
