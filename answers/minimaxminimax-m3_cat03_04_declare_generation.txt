| `chainresponse` / `chainprecedence` / `chainsuccession` | The post-approval hand-off is rigid: `AG  MP  FL` occur in immediate succession. |
| `nonsuccession` / `nonchainsuccession` | Early-stage activities (IG, DD, TFC, CE) can never be directly (or eventually) followed by late-stage ones (AG, MP, FL). |
| `noncoexistence` / `absence` | No pair of activities is mutually exclusive in this workflow, so both remain empty. |
