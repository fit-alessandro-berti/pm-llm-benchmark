The diagram is a Sankey diagram illustrating the flow of accounts through various stages of a debt recovery process over time. The overall structure consists of three main vertical sections, each representing different stages: "Send Fine," 'Insert Fine Notification,' and 'Send for Credit Collection.' Each section contains multiple nodes, which represent different states or actions within the process. The flows between these nodes are depicted by curved lines, with the width of each line indicating the volume of accounts transitioning between states. The color of the lines varies, with blue lines representing the flow of accounts that remain within the system and red lines indicating accounts that exit the system, typically due to successful collection or other outcomes. The timeline at the bottom of the diagram spans from January 2000 to April 2013, providing a temporal context for the transitions. The diagram effectively conveys the dynamic and interconnected nature of the debt recovery process, highlighting the volume and direction of account flows through different stages and outcomes.
