- **Sequential steps** were wrapped in `Operator.SEQUENCE`.
- **Exclusive choices** (e.g., CE vs FBP, TM vs SPT) were wrapped in `Operator.XOR`.
- **Concurrent activities** (e.g., AI and NC, or the final billing block) were wrapped in `Operator.PARALLEL`.
