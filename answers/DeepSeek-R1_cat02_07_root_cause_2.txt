**Key Observations:**

**3. Explanations and Mitigation Strategies**  
**Root Causes:**  
- **High Complexity Claims**: Require iterative document requests, creating feedback loops that prolong the process.  
- **Resource Constraints**:  
  - Adjusters may lack tools/training to resolve complex claims in fewer iterations.  
  - Manager_Bill’s workload (handling High complexity cases exclusively) introduces approval delays.  

**Mitigation Suggestions:**  
1. **Optimize High Complexity Claim Handling**:  
   - **Pre-emptive Document Checklists**: Use AI or historical data to identify required documents upfront, reducing iterative requests.  
   - **Specialized Teams**: Assign High complexity claims to dedicated adjusters/managers with expertise.  
2. **Reduce Approval Delays**:  
   - Distribute High complexity approvals between multiple managers (e.g., involve Manager_Ann).  
   - Implement automated reminders for approval steps.  
3. **Adjuster Training**:  
   - Train adjusters to identify document gaps earlier, minimizing follow-ups.  
4. **Process Automation**:  
   - Automate document request follow-ups to shorten response times.  

---

**Summary**: High complexity claims with repeated document requests and resource bottlenecks (Adjuster_Lisa, Manager_Bill) are the primary root causes. Addressing these through process improvements, training, and resource allocation will reduce lead times.
