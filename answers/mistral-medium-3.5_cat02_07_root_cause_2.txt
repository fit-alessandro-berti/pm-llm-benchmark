### **3. Root Causes & Mitigation Strategies**
#### **Primary Root Causes:**
1. **High Complexity Claims Require Multiple Document Requests**

   - **Impact:** Extends timeline significantly (2003: +2 requests, 2005: +3 requests).

2. **Slow Approval by Manager_Bill for High Complexity**
   - **Why?** Possibly due to:
     - Overloaded workload.
     - Lack of expertise in High complexity cases.
     - Manual review processes.
   - **Impact:** 2003 (24h delay), 2005 (~19h delay).

3. **Payment Delays for High Complexity (Finance_Carl)**
   - **Why?** Possibly:
     - Additional verification steps.
     - Manual processing for large amounts.
   - **Impact:** 2005 had a **4h payment delay** (vs. 15-45 min in others).

4. **Adjuster_Lisa’s Handling of High Complexity (2005)**
   - **Why?** Possibly:
     - Inefficient document request process.
     - Poor communication with claimants.
   - **Impact:** 3 document requests  **longest case (71h)**.
