- **Impact**:  
  - Unbounded re-evaluation/approval cycles could lead to inefficiencies or infinite loops.  
  - The ideal process expects a single evaluation and approval before closing.  

#### **Anomaly 2: Optional Customer Notification (N)**

- **Impact**:  
  - Customers might not be informed of claim outcomes, leading to dissatisfaction or legal risks.  
  - The ideal process requires `N` before closing.  

#### **Anomaly 3: Premature Closure (C)**  
- **Description**: The partial order allows `C` (close) to occur as early as **after `A` (assign adjuster)**, bypassing critical steps like `E`, `P`, or `N`.  
- **Impact**:  
  - Claims could be closed without proper evaluation, approval, or customer notification.
