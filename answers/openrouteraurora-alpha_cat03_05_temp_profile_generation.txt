*Explanation of the entries*  
- **Direct pairs** reflect the typical handoff time between two consecutive activities.

- **Longrange pairs** span several steps and therefore have larger averages and variances, reflecting the accumulated uncertainty across the chain.  

These values are illustrative estimates that could be refined by analysing actual eventlog timestamps.
