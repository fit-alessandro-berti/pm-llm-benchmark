#### 1. Investigating R to P (Low Standard Deviation / Auto-Approvals)
This query identifies claims where the time between Receive and Approve is suspiciously close to the 25-hour average (between 24 and 26 hours), and correlates them with claim types and amounts to see if a specific segment drives this rigid timing.

```sql
SELECT 
    c.claim_id,
    c.claim_type,
    c.claim_amount,
    EXTRACT(EPOCH FROM (p_event.timestamp - r_event.timestamp)) AS rp_duration_seconds
FROM claim_events r_event
JOIN claim_events p_event 
    ON r_event.claim_id = p_event.claim_id AND p_event.activity = 'P'
JOIN claims c 
    ON r_event.claim_id = c.claim_id
WHERE r_event.activity = 'R'
  AND EXTRACT(EPOCH FROM (p_event.timestamp - r_event.timestamp)) BETWEEN 86400 AND 93600
ORDER BY c.claim_type, c.claim_amount;
```
