### Rationale for Added Constraints

**1. `existence` for fairness activities**  
Added `ManualReview` and `BiasMitigationCheck` as activities that can exist in traces, ensuring the process can include fairness mechanisms without forcing them in every case (support 0.5 reflects conditional presence).

**2. `absence` for solo rejection**  
Prevents `Reject` from appearing alone without prior checks, ensuring no arbitrary rejections occur without process engagement.

**3. `responded_existence` for manual review**  
If a decision tied to a sensitive attribute (e.g., `Approve_Minority`) occurs, `ManualReview` must exist in the same trace, ensuring human oversight for highstakes demographiclinked outcomes.

**4. `coexistence` for manual review**  
Ties `ManualReview` directly to any decision involving sensitive attributes, guaranteeing consistent scrutiny regardless of decision direction.

**5. `response` for bias checks**  
Whenever a sensitive attribute is checked (`CheckApplicantRace`, etc.), a `BiasMitigationCheck` must eventually occur, ensuring bias awareness is not skipped.

**6. `precedence` for rejections**  
Ensures `Reject_Minority`/`Reject_Gender` are always preceded by `ManualReview`, preventing automated or unchecked rejections of sensitive groups.

**7. `succession` linking attribute checks to decisions**  
Connects sensitive attribute checks to both bias mitigation and final decisions, ensuring the process flows through fairness gates.

**8. `chainresponse` & `chainprecedence`**  
Force `BiasMitigationCheck` to be immediately adjacent to `FinalDecision`, preventing other activities from diluting or bypassing the fairness step.

**9. `noncoexistence` & `nonsuccession`**  
Block any trace where a sensitive attribute check coexists with or is immediately followed by a rejection, eliminating direct biased pathways.

**10. `nonchainsuccession`**  
Prevents any immediate sequence from checking sensitive attributes to rejection, adding another layer of protection against snap judgments.

These constraints collectively ensure that decisions affecting applicants from sensitive demographics are never made without appropriate review, bias checks, and procedural safeguards, promoting fairness and consistency in the loan application process.
