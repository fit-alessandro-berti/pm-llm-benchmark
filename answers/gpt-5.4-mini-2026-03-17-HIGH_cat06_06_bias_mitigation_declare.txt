### Rationale
- **`existence` of `ManualReview` and `BiasMitigationCheck`**: makes fairness checks mandatory in every trace.
- **`chainresponse` from sensitive-attribute checks to `BiasMitigationCheck`**: forces an immediate mitigation step after any race/gender/age inspection.
- **`chainresponse` from `BiasMitigationCheck` to `ManualReview`** and **`chainprecedence` from `ManualReview` to `FinalDecision`**: ensures the final decision is always routed through manual review.
- **`coexistence` between `ManualReview` and `FinalDecision`**: keeps manual review tied to the decision stage for consistency.
- **`nonchainsuccession` from sensitive checks to `FinalDecision`**: prevents a direct biased jump from sensitive-attribute inspection to the final decision.
