1. **Remove the Community Group Bonus**

3. **Standardize Manual Reviews**
   - **Train reviewers on unconscious bias** and implement **structured decision-making guidelines**.

   - **Disclose the decision criteria** used by the Rules Engine.
   - **Allow applicants to see why they were approved/rejected** (e.g., "Your score was 715, but the minimum for non-locals is 720").

5. **Conduct a Disparate Impact Analysis**
   - **Test whether the system disproportionately harms protected groups** (e.g., by race, income, or national origin).
   - **Adjust thresholds or weights** if disparities are found.

6. **Implement Alternative Fairness Measures**
   - **Use adversarial debiasing** in the scoring model to **remove proxy variables** for protected attributes.
   - **Adopt fairness-aware machine learning** techniques (e.g., equalized odds, demographic parity).
