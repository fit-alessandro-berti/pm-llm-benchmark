## 1. Activities with context-dependent repeated meanings

The main recurring activities within the same case are:

| Case ID | Original Activity | Contextual Difference | Renamed Activities |
|---|---|---|---|
| C001 | Verify Documents | First verification happens immediately after request initiation. Second verification happens after the customer uploads a missing ID scan. | `Initial Identity Document Verification`; `Re-verification After Additional ID Submission` |
| C002 | Call Customer | First call is to clarify an account discrepancy. Second call is a follow-up to confirm that the account adjustment was made. | `Call Customer to Clarify Account Discrepancy`; `Follow-up Call to Confirm Account Adjustment` |
| C003 | Verify Documents | First verification is an in-branch physical ID check. Second verification happens after an internal account correction. | `Initial In-Branch ID Verification`; `Re-verification After Account Correction` |

In the revised log below, I also slightly rename some non-repeated but generic activities, such as `Initiate Request`, `Submit Additional`, and `Adjust Account`, where the context makes the role more specific.

## 3. Justification of Naming Choices

- **`Verify Documents` in C001** was split into:
  - `Initial Identity Document Verification`, because it occurs directly after the request is initiated.
  - `Re-verification After Additional ID Submission`, because it occurs after the customer submits a missing ID scan.

- **`Call Customer` in C002** was split into:
  - `Call Customer to Clarify Account Discrepancy`, because the first call is about an account discrepancy.
  - `Follow-up Call to Confirm Account Adjustment`, because the second call happens after the account was adjusted and is explicitly described as a confirmation call.

- **`Verify Documents` in C003** was split into:
  - `Initial In-Branch ID Verification`, because the customer is physically present at a branch and the officer verifies the ID.
  - `Re-verification After Account Correction`, because it happens after an internal account correction.

- **Other generic labels were clarified**:
  - `Initiate Request` became either `Online Request Initiation` or `Branch Walk-in Request Initiation`, based on the submission channel.
  - `Submit Additional` became `Customer Submits Additional ID Scan`, because the context specifies that the additional item was a requested ID scan.
  - `Adjust Account` was renamed according to its trigger: either after customer clarification or due to missing information.
  - `Approve Request` became `Final Request Approval`, reflecting that it is the closing approval step in each case.
