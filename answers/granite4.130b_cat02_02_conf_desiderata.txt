- **Hard violations** are confined to **Case2**, where both the required activity order and the postrejection documentation rule were broken.

These do not invalidate the process but highlight areas for operational improvement—e.g., tighter workflow enforcement, faster internal handoffs, or proactive reminders to perform recommended activities.
