Below are **openended, clarifying questions** grouped by the major aspects of the onboarding workflow.  Each question is designed to surface missing details, decision criteria, role ownership, timing/sequencing, and exception handling – without asking for SQL or implementation specifics.

---

## 1. Documentation & Initial Verification  
1. **Can you walk me through the exact set of documents the property coordinator reviews (e.g., deed, compliance certificates, inspection reports) and the checklist you use to confirm each one is current?**  
2. **How do you determine that a document is “outdated” or insufficient, and what steps trigger a request for an update?**  
3. **Who is responsible for flagging missing or stale documents, and how is that status tracked until the landlord provides the required paperwork?**

## 2. PropertyManager Assignment  
4. **What criteria (workload, geographic coverage, propertytype experience, landlordspecific history) are used to select the dedicated property manager, and how are they weighted?**  
5. **How do you handle situations where no manager meets all the criteria (e.g., a manager is at capacity or lacks experience with a particular property type)?**  
6. **Is the assignment made automatically by the system, or does a supervisor manually approve the match?  Who can override the assignment?**

## 3. Introductory Landlord Meeting  
7. **What topics are covered in the introductory call/meeting, and who prepares the agenda?**  
8. **How long is the meeting typically allowed to last, and what documentation (meeting minutes, agreedupon expectations) is produced afterwards?**  
9. **What happens if the landlord cannot attend the scheduled meeting—how is the meeting rescheduled and who is responsible for following up?**

## 4. Scheduling Photographer & Inspector  
10. **What is the typical leadtime for booking a photographer and a certified inspector, and who coordinates those appointments?**  
11. **How do you verify that the photographer and inspector are qualified, insured, and impartial?**  
12. **If the inspector identifies health or safety issues, what is the remediation workflow?  Who decides whether the property can move forward to marketing, and what happens if the landlord disputes the findings or delays repairs?**

## 5. Pricing, Marketing & Listing Creation  
13. **Can you describe the process for setting the rental price?  What data sources (market comparables, internal pricing models, trend reports) are used, and who has the final approval?**  
14. **What is the workflow for creating the online listing, brochure, and other marketing material?  Who writes the copy, approves the visuals, and uploads them to the rental portal?**  
15. **How do you ensure consistency across listings (brand voice, mandatory fields) and how is the listing reviewed before it goes live?**

## 6. Tenant Screening & Application Handling  
16. **What are the key steps in the tenantscreening process (credit check, employment verification, reference calls), and who performs each step?**  
17. **When multiple applicants are interested, how do you prioritize and select the best candidate?  What criteria are used, and who makes the final decision?**  
18. **How do you handle situations where the selected tenant fails the background check or withdraws before signing the lease?**

## 7. Lease Execution & MoveIn  
19. **Who drafts the lease agreement, and how are custom clauses (requested by the landlord) incorporated?  What review/approval process ensures legal compliance?**  
20. **What is the workflow for collecting the security deposit, scheduling the movein date, and conducting the movein inspection?**  
21. **If there are disputes over the lease terms or deposit amounts, what escalation path and resolution steps are in place?**

## 8. Quality Checks, Audits & Compliance  
22. **After lease execution, what qualitycheck and audit activities are performed (e.g., compliance officer review, financial analyst price verification, IT dataintegration check)?**  
23. **Who is accountable for each type of audit, and how are findings documented and resolved?**  
24. **What happens if an audit reveals a discrepancy—how is it remediated and who signs off on the correction?**

## 9. Exception Handling & Special Cases  
25. **How does the process flag and handle “exceptions” such as properties requiring special insurance, unique lease clauses, or nonstandard landlord requests?**  
26. **What approval hierarchy is used for these exceptions, and who has the authority to waive standard procedures?**  
27. **Can you give an example of a recent exception you’ve dealt with and walk me through the steps taken to resolve it?**

## 10. Communication, Tracking & Tooling  
28. **What tools (CRM, projectmanagement software, email threads) are used to track each onboarding step, and how is communication among landlords, managers, and support teams recorded?**  
29. **Is there a single “master” status dashboard for each property, or do different teams maintain separate tracking systems?**  
30. **How do you ensure that all relevant parties are notified of stage transitions (e.g., from inspection to marketing) and that no handoffs are missed?**

## 11. Timing, SLAs & Performance Metrics  
31. **Are there defined Service Level Agreements (SLAs) or target turnaround times for each stage (document verification, inspection, listing activation, etc.)?**  
32. **How do you monitor delays, and what escalation or corrective actions are triggered when a step exceeds its target time?**  
33. **What KPIs (e.g., daystoactivate, leaseconversion rate, tenantsatisfaction score) are tracked for the onboarding process, and how are they used for continuous improvement?**

## 12. Roles, Responsibilities & Training  
34. **Who is the overall owner or process lead for the onboarding workflow, and how are responsibilities distributed among coordinators, managers, and support staff?**  
35. **What training or certification do new hires receive on the onboarding process, and how is the process updated when regulations or best practices change?**  
36. **How do you ensure that staff understand and follow the documented workflow—through audits, coaching, or performance reviews?**

## 13. Variation Across Property Types & Regions  
37. **How does the onboarding workflow differ for residential versus commercial properties, or for luxury, student, or furnished rentals?**  
38. **Do you adjust the process for different cities or municipalities (e.g., varying housingregulation certificates)?  If so, how are those local variations captured and enforced?**

## 14. Data, Integration & System Considerations  
39. **What key data points are captured in the central property database, and how do you ensure data quality (e.g., completeness, accuracy) during entry?**  
40. **How does the onboarding process feed into downstream systems (accounting, maintenance scheduling, tenantportal) and what checks are in place to confirm successful integration?**

**Why these questions?**  
- They probe **missing details** (documents, checklists, data).  
- They uncover **decision criteria** (manager assignment, tenant selection, pricing).  
- They clarify **roles & responsibilities** (who does what, approvals, escalations).  
- They verify **timing & sequencing** (SLAs, lead times, bottlenecks).  
- They explore **exception handling** (special insurance, custom clauses, disputes).
