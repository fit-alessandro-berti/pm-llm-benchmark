The diagram is a swimlane chart illustrating the process of shipping goods from a computer hardware shop. It is divided into three lanes representing different departments: Warehouse, Dispatch of Goods/Secretary, and Logistics. The process begins with the 'Ship goods' event in the Dispatch lane, which leads to 'Clarify shipment method' and a decision point for 'Special handling?' If special handling is required, the process involves checking if insurance is necessary, getting three offers from logistic companies, selecting a company, and insuring the parcel. If no special handling is needed, the process skips directly to writing the package label. The Warehouse lane handles 'Package goods' and notifying the customer about any delays. Once the package is ready, it moves to the Dispatch lane for 'Fill out Shipment paperwork' and then to the Logistics lane for final shipment preparation. The overall concept of the diagram is to outline the sequential steps and decision points involved in preparing and shipping goods, highlighting the roles of different departments in the process.
