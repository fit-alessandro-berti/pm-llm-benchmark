### **Key Assumptions & Notes**
1. **Missing Data Handling**:
   - **Order 1004**: No delivery, invoice, or payment records exist. Assumed as incomplete or pending.
   - **Resource Mapping**: Used `resource_id` from the **Resources Table** to populate `Resource/Actor` (e.g., `SP-001`  "Alice Brown, Sales Person").

2. **Activity Granularity**:
   - **Item Picking**: Split into individual events per `item_id` to preserve granularity.
   - **Deliveries/Invoices**: Multiple records per `order_id` (e.g., partial shipments or installments) are included as separate events.

3. **Timestamp Logic**:
   - Events are ordered chronologically within each `order_id`.
   - For tied timestamps (e.g., same-day picking), ordering is based on the sequence in the source tables.

4. **Optional Attributes**:
   - Added `item_category` from the **Products Table** and `delivery_note`/`payment_method` for context.

5. **Resource Roles**:
   - Carriers (`CR-*`) are treated as automated/resources without further breakdown (e.g., no individual driver names).
