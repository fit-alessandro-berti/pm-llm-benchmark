### Composite sub-models (operators)

1) **Repeatable in-line quality screening (allows multiple consecutive IQC)**
- **IQC_plus = \* ( IQC,  )**  
  Semantics: execute `IQC` at least once; then either stop, or do `` and perform `IQC` again (repeating).  
  (The `` is silent, so this is effectively `IQC+`.)

2) **Assembly with optional/repeatable IQC-driven rework**
- **ASSY_IQC = \* ( AS, IQC_plus )**  
  Semantics: execute `AS`, then either exit assembly, or do one-or-more `IQC` and return to `AS` (repeat as needed).  
  This captures:
  - `AS` only (case 4)
  - `AS, IQC, AS` (cases 1–2)
  - `AS, IQC, AS, IQC, AS` (case 5)
  - `AS, IQC, IQC, AS, IQC, AS` (case 3)

3) **Testing with possible re-testing loop**
- **TEST_LOOP = \* ( TST, RT )**  
  Semantics: execute `TST`, then either exit testing, or do `RT` and return to `TST` (repeat).

4) **Optional labeling**
- **LABEL_OPT = X ( LB,  )**  
  Exclusive choice: perform `LB` or skip it.

### Global process as a Strict Partial Order

**ROOT = PO=(nodes={ OR, MS, QS, ASSY_IQC, TEST_LOOP, PK, LABEL_OPT, DP, D }, order={…})**

With the following ordering constraints (a sequential chain consistent with the log):

- `OR  --> MS`
- `MS  --> QS`
- `QS  --> ASSY_IQC`
- `ASSY_IQC --> TEST_LOOP`
- `TEST_LOOP --> PK`
- `PK  --> LABEL_OPT`
- `LABEL_OPT --> DP`
- `DP  --> D`

So the final POWL is:

\[
\textbf{PO}(
\text{nodes}=\{OR, MS, QS, \*(AS,\*(IQC,\tau)), \*(TST,RT), PK, X(LB,\tau), DP, D\},\ 
\text{order}=\{OR\!\to\!MS,\ MS\!\to\!QS,\ QS\!\to\!\*(AS,\*(IQC,\tau)),\ \*(AS,\*(IQC,\tau))\!\to\!\*(TST,RT),\ \*(TST,RT)\!\to\!PK,\ PK\!\to\!X(LB,\tau),\ X(LB,\tau)\!\to\!DP,\ DP\!\to\!D\}
)
\]

This captures: linear progression, optional labeling, rework in assembly driven by (possibly repeated) IQC, and repeated testing driven by RT.
