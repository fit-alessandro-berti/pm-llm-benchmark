### **Step 4: Loop for Abnormal Results**
If **CE** is chosen and results are abnormal, the patient enters a loop:
- **SC**: Specialist Consultation (child of CE)
- **TM**: Targeted Medication (child of SC)
- **SPT**: Specialized Physical Therapy (child of SC)
- **RDT**: Re-Diagnostic Test (child of SPT or TM)
  - If **RDT** results are abnormal, the loop repeats:
    - **SC** again
    - **TM** or **SPT** again
    - **RDT** again

                  FBP
