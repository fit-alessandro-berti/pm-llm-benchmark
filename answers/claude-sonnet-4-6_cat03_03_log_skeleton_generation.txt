## Analysis

**Process Flow:** RO  CA  RI  PI  QC  PO  GSL  D  IC  RP

**Reasoning through constraints:**

- **Equivalence:** RO, CA, RI, PI, QC, PO, GSL, D, IC all happen exactly once per order. RP depends on customer payment (may or may not occur).
- **Always Before:** Each step requires prior steps to have occurred.
- **Always After:** Each step guarantees subsequent steps will occur (except RP, which is optional).
- **Never Together:** No mutual exclusions in this linear flow.
- **Directly-Follows:** Strict sequential steps in the core pipeline.
