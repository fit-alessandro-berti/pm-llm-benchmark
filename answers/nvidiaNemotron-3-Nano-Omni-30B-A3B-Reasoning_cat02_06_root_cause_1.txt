**Key Bottlenecks Identified:**  
1. **Escalation Delays (Cases 102, 105):**  
  - Primary bottleneck: long wait for Level-2 agent (2–6.5 hours) before investigation can start.  
  - Escalation itself adds 2.5–4 hours before investigation can begin.  
  - Escalation is the dominant bottleneck, not the investigation or resolution steps.  
  - Escalation delays are the main cause of extended cycle times, not investigation or resolution steps.
