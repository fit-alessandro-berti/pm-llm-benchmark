### **3. Proposed Explanations & Mitigation Strategies**
#### **Key Root Causes of Long Durations:**
1. **High Complexity Claims**
   - **Problem:** Require multiple additional document requests, delaying approval.
   - **Solution:**
     - **Pre-validate claims** at submission to ensure all required documents are included.
     - **Automate document requests** (e.g., templates for common missing items).
     - **Assign senior adjusters** to high-complexity cases to reduce back-and-forth.

2. **Region B’s Slower Processing**
   - **Problem:** Longer durations for high-complexity cases (2005 vs. 2003).
   - **Solution:**
     - **Benchmark Region B’s processes** against Region A to identify inefficiencies.
     - **Cross-train adjusters** between regions to balance workload.
     - **Implement SLA (Service Level Agreements)** for document requests and approvals.

3. **Resource-Specific Bottlenecks**
   - **Adjuster_Lisa (Region B):**
     - **Problem:** More document requests  longer delays.
     - **Solution:**
       - **Provide additional training** on efficient claim evaluation.
       - **Implement a peer-review system** to reduce unnecessary requests.
   - **Manager_Bill:**
     - **Problem:** Approval delays in high-complexity cases.
     - **Solution:**
       - **Delegate some approvals** to other managers.
       - **Automate low-risk approvals** (e.g., based on predefined criteria).
   - **Finance_Carl (Region B):**
     - **Problem:** Slower payment processing.
     - **Solution:**
       - **Increase finance staff** in Region B.
       - **Automate payment processing** for approved claims.

4. **Multiple Additional Document Requests**
   - **Problem:** Each request adds significant delay (e.g., 2005 had 3 requests over 3 days).
   - **Solution:**
     - **Batch document requests** (ask for all missing items at once).
     - **Set a limit on document requests** (e.g., max 2 per case).
