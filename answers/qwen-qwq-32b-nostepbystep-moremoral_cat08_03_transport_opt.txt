### 1. Process Discovery and Conformance Checking  
#### Data Preprocessing and Integration  
**Approach:**  
- **Data Alignment:** Combine data from GPS, scanners, dispatch, and maintenance logs using a **case identifier** (e.g., `Case ID = Vehicle-Day`), ensuring all events are grouped by vehicle and day.  
- **Event Standardization:** Map events to a unified vocabulary. For example:  
  - GPS events (e.g., "Low Speed Detected") are linked to traffic delays.  
  - Scanner events (e.g., "Delivery Failed") flag inefficiencies.  
  - Maintenance logs (e.g., "Unscheduled Stop") highlight vehicle reliability issues.  
- **Timestamp Alignment:** Synchronize timestamps across systems to ensure temporal consistency. Use interpolation or imputation for missing timestamps where events overlap (e.g., a "Delivery Success" event missing a timestamp could be inferred from adjacent events).  
- **Attribute Enrichment:** Add derived fields such as "Travel Time Between Stops," "Dwell Time at Customer," and "Fuel Efficiency (km/package)."  

**Challenges:**  
- **Data Quality Issues:** Missing or inconsistent timestamps between systems (e.g., GPS data might lack precise event triggers).  
- **Event Granularity:** GPS data may provide granular speed/speeding events, but scanners only capture milestone events (e.g., "Arrive Customer"). Bridging these granularities requires aggregation or temporal windowing.  
- **Anonymization:** Masking driver or customer PII while retaining operational insights (e.g., anonymizing addresses but retaining traffic zone identifiers).  

#### Process Discovery  
**Methodology:**  
- Use **process discovery algorithms** (e.g., Inductive Miner, Heuristics Miner) to visualize the **actual end-to end process**. The model will show:  
  - **Delivery Cycles:** Depart Depot  Route Execution (with stops, delays, detours)  Return to Depot.  
  - **Deviation Points:** Unplanned stops (e.g., traffic jams, maintenance), extended dwell times, or sequence deviations (e.g., skipping stops).  
- **Visualization:** Heatmaps of frequent detours or recurring delays.  

#### Conformance Checking  
**Approach:**  
- Compare the **discovered process model** with the **planned routes** from the dispatch system. Use **alignment-based conformance checking** to identify:  
  - **Sequence Deviations:** Did the driver deviate from the planned route (e.g., taking a detour to avoid traffic)?  
  - **Timing Deviations:** Significant delays at specific stops or en route. For example, comparing planned vs. actual travel times.  
  - **Activity Deviations:** Unplanned stops (e.g., "Engine Warning" causing unscheduled maintenance).  

#### Key Deviations to Track:  
- **Route Divergences:** Actual vs. planned paths (GPS vs. dispatch routes).  
- **Unexpected Activity Insertions:** E.g., frequent engine warnings indicating maintenance needs.  
- **Time Overruns:** Stops taking longer than planned (e.g., customer interaction delays).

### 4. Data-Driven Optimization Strategies  
#### Strategy 1: Dynamic Routing with Real-Time Traffic Adaptation  
- **Targets:** Traffic-induced delays and fuel inefficiency.  
- **Root Cause:** Static routes not adjusting to real-time traffic.  
- **Process Mining Support:**  
  - Identify traffic hotspots via frequent "Low Speed" GPS events.  
  - Use historical data to train a traffic-aware routing algorithm.  
- **Impact:** Reduces "Travel Time vs. Service Time" ratio and fuel consumption.  

#### Strategy 2: Customer Time Window Optimization  
- **Targets:** Failed deliveries requiring re-deliveries.  
- **Root Cause:** Poor time window accuracy.  
- **Process Mining Support:**  
  - Cluster "Delivery Failed" events by time windows and customer location.  
  - Use predictive analytics to adjust time windows based on delivery success rates.  
- **Impact:** Reduces overtime (from re-deliveries) and improves On-Time Delivery Rate.  

#### Strategy 3: Predictive Maintenance Scheduling  
- **Targets:** Unplanned stops and maintenance downtime.  
- **Root Cause:** Reactive maintenance delaying routes.  
- **Process Mining Support:**  
  - Correlate GPS mileage, engine status, and maintenance logs to predict failure probabilities.  
  - Schedule maintenance during low-demand periods (e.g., after peak delivery hours).  
- **Impact:** Reduces unscheduled stops and downtime, improving Vehicle Utilization Rate.
