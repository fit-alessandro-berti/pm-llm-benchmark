### **Anomaly 1: Case 1003 — "Post-Factum" Transactions**

1.  **Financial Risk:** A payment cannot be validly released before an invoice is received to verify the amount due.

Furthermore, paying for goods that are physically received *after* the payment is released creates a "delinquent vendor" scenario where the company owes money for nothing received yet.

### **Anomaly 2: Case 1002 — Unauthorized Purchase Order Issuance**

*   **Event Sequence:** Issue Purchase Order ($2024-02-01$) $\rightarrow$ Approve Purchase Requisition ($2024-02-03$).

**Why this is problematic:**
*   **Control Failure:** The "Approve" step is a critical control mechanism designed to ensure that a purchase request is justified and within budget before a financial commitment is made.
