### **BPMN-like Textual Process Model: Invoice Approval Process**

#### **1. Process Overview**
- **Process Name:** Invoice Approval Process
- **Trigger:** Receipt of an invoice (typically via email)
- **End Event:** Invoice scheduled for payment
- **Primary Roles:**
  - **Accounts Payable (AP) Clerk** (Mary)
  - **Purchasing Officer** (Dan)
  - **Department Manager** (Carlos)
  - **AP Supervisor** (Rita)
  - **Supplier Relationship Manager** (Karen)

#### **2. Process Flow**

**Start Event:**
- Invoice received (via email or system).

---

**Activity 1: Validate Invoice PO Association**
- **Performer:** AP Clerk (Mary)
- **Description:** Check if the invoice has a valid Purchase Order (PO) number.
  - **Input:** Invoice (email/system)
  - **Output:** Validated invoice (with/without PO)
- **Gateways:**
  - **Condition:** PO exists?
    - **Yes:** Proceed to Activity 2.
    - **No:** Send back to supplier (Activity 1a).

**Activity 1a: Request PO from Supplier**
- **Performer:** AP Clerk (Mary) or Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen)
- **Description:** Request missing PO number from supplier.
  - **Input:** Invoice without PO
  - **Output:** Revised invoice with PO (loop back to Activity 1) or rejection.

---

**Activity 2: Log Invoice in Accounting System**
- **Performer:** AP Clerk (Mary)
- **Description:** Log the invoice into the accounting system.
  - **Input:** Validated invoice (with PO)
  - **Output:** Logged invoice in system

---

**Activity 3: Confirm Goods/Services Received**
- **Performer:** Purchasing Officer (Dan)
- **Description:** Verify that the goods/services were received as per the PO.
  - **Input:** Logged invoice (from Activity 2)
  - **Output:** Confirmation email to AP Clerk (Mary)
- **Gateways:**
  - **Condition:** Goods/services confirmed?
    - **Yes:** Proceed to Activity 4.
    - **No:** Escalate to AP Supervisor (Activity 3a).

**Activity 3a: Escalate Unconfirmed Invoice**
- **Performer:** AP Clerk (Mary)  AP Supervisor (Rita)
- **Description:** AP Clerk escalates unresolved confirmation to AP Supervisor.
  - **Input:** Unconfirmed invoice
  - **Output:** AP Supervisor investigates (Activity 3b).

**Activity 3b: Investigate Unconfirmed Invoice**
- **Performer:** AP Supervisor (Rita)
- **Description:** Coordinate with departments or suppliers to resolve confirmation.
  - **Input:** Unconfirmed invoice
  - **Output:** Resolved invoice (loop back to Activity 3) or rejection.

---

**Activity 4: Match Invoice to PO**
- **Performer:** AP Clerk (Mary)
- **Description:** Match invoice line items against the PO in the system.
  - **Input:** Confirmed invoice (from Activity 3)
  - **Output:** Matched invoice
- **Gateways:**
  - **Condition:** Invoice matches PO?
    - **Yes:** Proceed to Activity 5.
    - **No:** Escalate discrepancy (Activity 4a).

**Activity 4a: Handle PO Discrepancy**
- **Performer:** AP Clerk (Mary) or Purchasing Officer (Dan)
- **Description:** Investigate discrepancy (e.g., incorrect pricing, missing items).
  - **Input:** Mismatched invoice
  - **Output:** Clarified invoice (loop back to Activity 4) or escalation to AP Supervisor (Activity 4b).

**Activity 4b: Escalate Complex Discrepancy**
- **Performer:** AP Clerk (Mary)  AP Supervisor (Rita)
- **Description:** AP Clerk escalates unresolved discrepancies to AP Supervisor.
  - **Input:** Complex discrepancy
  - **Output:** AP Supervisor resolves (loop back to Activity 4) or rejects invoice.

---

**Activity 5: Request Manager Approval**
- **Performer:** AP Clerk (Mary) or System
- **Description:** System sends automatic email requesting manager’s approval.
  - **Input:** Matched invoice
  - **Output:** Approval request to Department Manager (Carlos)

---

**Activity 6: Manager Approval**
- **Performer:** Department Manager (Carlos)
- **Description:** Review invoice details (PO, quantities, pricing) and approve or reject.
  - **Input:** Approval request
  - **Output:** Approved/rejected invoice
- **Gateways:**
  - **Condition:** Invoice approved?
    - **Yes:** Proceed to Activity 7.
    - **No:** Reject or request investigation (Activity 6a).

**Activity 6a: Handle Rejected Invoice**
- **Performer:** Department Manager (Carlos)  AP Clerk (Mary)
- **Description:** Manager rejects invoice or requests further investigation.
  - **Input:** Rejected invoice
  - **Output:** Invoice returned to AP Clerk (loop back to Activity 4 or Activity 1).

---

**Activity 7: Schedule Payment**
- **Performer:** AP Clerk (Mary)
- **Description:** Add invoice to the next payment cycle.
  - **Input:** Approved invoice
  - **Output:** Scheduled payment

---

**End Event:** Invoice scheduled for payment.

---

#### **3. Key Artifacts/Data Objects**
- **Invoice** (input/output)
- **Purchase Order (PO)** (input)
- **Confirmation Email** (from Purchasing to AP)
- **Approval Request** (from system to Manager)
- **Payment Schedule** (output)

---

#### **4. Exceptions/Variations**
1. **Missing PO:**
   - Invoice sent back to supplier (Activity 1a).
   - Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen) may assist in resolving.
2. **Unconfirmed Goods/Services:**
   - Escalated to AP Supervisor (Activity 3a).
   - May require retrospective PO or rejection.
3. **PO Discrepancy:**
   - Resolved via email/clarification (Activity 4a).
   - Complex cases escalated to AP Supervisor (Activity 4b).
4. **Manager Rejection:**
   - Invoice returned for further review (Activity 6a).
5. **Informal Contracts (No PO):**
   - AP Supervisor (Rita) works with department heads to create retrospective PO or reject invoice.

---

- **Sequence Flow:**
  - Some ambiguity exists in Dan’s account about whether Purchasing sends confirmation directly to AP or if it’s implicit. Assumed confirmation is sent to AP (Activity 3).
  - Carlos mentions that after his approval, the invoice "goes back to AP," but the exact path is unclear. Assumed it proceeds to payment scheduling (Activity 7).
- **Roles:**
  - Karen’s role is indirect (supplier liaison) but critical for resolving supplier-related issues.
- **Gateways:**
  - All gateways are based on explicit conditions in the interviews. Additional conditions (e.g., budget checks) may exist but are not mentioned.
