**Assumptions & notes**

**Order Created* – resource is the sales person (`sales_person_id`).  
**Item Picked* – one event per lineitem (different picking dates).  
**Order Dispatched* / *Order Arrived* – resource is the carrier (`carrier_id`).  
**Invoice Issued* – resource is the billing clerk (`billing_person_id`).  
**Payment Received* – resource is the payment method (e.g., “Credit Card”).  
*If an order has no delivery, invoice or payment records, only the existing events are logged.  
*All timestamps are kept in the original UTC ISO8601 format.
