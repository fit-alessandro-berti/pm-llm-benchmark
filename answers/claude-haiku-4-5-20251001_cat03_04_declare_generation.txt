## **Explanation:**

### **Unary Constraints:**

1. **`existence`**: All major activities must occur (except UT which is optional)
2. **`absence`**: User Testing (UT) is optional and absent in ~20% of cases
3. **`init`**: Idea Generation (IG) is always the first activity

### **Binary Constraints:**

1. **`responded_existence`**: If an earlier activity occurs, a later activity must also occur (e.g., if DD occurs, TFC must occur)

2. **`precedence`**: Enforces strict ordering (e.g., IG must precede DD, DD must precede TFC/CE)

3. **`response`**: After an activity completes, a subsequent activity must follow

4. **`succession`**: Combines precedence and response to ensure order and response

5. **`chainresponse`** & **`chainprecedence`**: Enforces immediate succession (e.g., IG directly followed by DD)

6. **`noncoexistence`**: UT and FL shouldn't coexist in the same trace (testing not always with final launch)

7. **`nonchainsuccession`**: CE and LT shouldn't be directly consecutive

The confidence values reflect real-world variability where some paths may deviate (e.g., sometimes UT is skipped for certain products).
