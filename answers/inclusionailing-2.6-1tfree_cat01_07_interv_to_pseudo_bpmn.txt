**BPMN-like Textual Process Model: Invoice Approval (Accounts Payable)**  

---

### **1. Process Overview**  
- **Name:** Invoice Receipt, Validation, Approval, and Payment Scheduling  
- **Primary Trigger:** Invoice received (typically via email)  
- **Primary End:** Invoice scheduled for payment run  

---

### **2. Roles / Participants**  
- **Accounts Payable Clerk** (AP Clerk) ñ Mary  
- **Purchasing Officer** ñ Dan  
- **Department Manager** ñ Carlos  
- **Accounts Payable Supervisor** ñ Rita  
- **Supplier Relationship Manager** ñ Karen (as-needed exception handler)  
- **System:** Accounting system (sends notifications, stores PO/invoice data)  
- **External:** Supplier  

---

### **3. Data Objects / Artifacts**  
- Invoice (email or document)  
- Purchase Order (PO)  
- POñInvoice match record / reconciliation  
- Confirmation emails / system approvals  
- Discrepancy notes / exception logs  

---

### **4. Process Flow (Textual BPMN-like Description)**  

#### **Start Event:**  
- **Start:** Invoice received (usually by email).  

#### **Lane: Accounts Payable (AP Clerk)**  
- **Task 1: Check PO Validity**  
  - Determine if invoice is associated with a valid PO number.  
  - *Gateway (Exclusive):* PO Present?  
    - **Yes**  Task 2.  
    - **No**   
      - **Task 1a: Request PO from Supplier** (email supplier for PO or revised invoice).  
        - *Note (exception path):* If missing PO persists, may escalate to Supervisor (Task 7) or create retrospective PO.  
        - After resolution, return to Task 1 or, if resolved, proceed to Task 2.  

- **Task 2: Log Invoice into Accounting System**  

- **Task 3: Forward to Purchasing for Receipt/Details Confirmation**  
  - Trigger: Automatic assignment/email to Purchasing Officer.  

#### **Lane: Purchasing (Purchasing Officer)**  
- **Task 4: Compare Invoice vs PO**  
  - Validate quantities, prices, delivery charges, and receipt of goods/services.  
  - *Gateway (Exclusive):* Match Acceptable?  
    - **Yes**  Task 5.  
    - **No** (discrepancy)   
      - **Task 4a: Clarify Discrepancy**  
        - Contact supplier directly **or** request Accounts Payable Clerk to place invoice on hold and obtain corrected invoice.  
        - If price discrepancy, may loop in Supplier Relationship Manager (Task 8) to verify contract pricing.  
      - *Possible outcomes:*  
        - Supplier provides corrected invoice  return to Task 4.  
        - Unresolvable discrepancy  escalate to Supervisor (Task 7).  
      - If missing PO number, require AP Clerk to obtain before proceeding.  

- **Task 5: Confirm Receipt and PO Alignment to AP**  
  - Send confirmation email to Accounts Payable Clerk.  

#### **Lane: Accounts Payable (AP Clerk) ñ continued**  
- **Task 6: Line-Item Match in System**  
  - Reconcile invoice line items against PO in system based on Purchasing confirmation.  
  - *Gateway (Exclusive):* Match Successful?  
    - **Yes**  Task 9.  
    - **No**   
      - **Task 6a: Resolve Discrepancy**  
        - Email Purchasing for clarification.  
        - If unresolved, escalate to Supervisor (Task 7).  

#### **Lane: Supervisor / Exception Handling**  
- **Task 7: Exception Resolution**  
  - Triggered by escalation from AP Clerk or Purchasing.  
  - Actions:  
    - Contact supplier / coordinate between departments.  
    - For invoices without PO, work with department heads to create retrospective PO or reject invoice.  
  - *Gateway (Exclusive):* Can Exception Be Resolved?  
    - **Yes**  Return invoice to normal flow at Purchasing confirmation (Task 5) or AP line-item match (Task 6), depending on resolution stage.  
    - **No** (reject or unable to resolve)  End process (invoice rejected).  

#### **Lane: Department Manager**  
- **Task 9: Manager Review and Approval**  
  - Trigger: Automatic system notification after confirmed receipt and PO match.  
  - Manager reviews invoice details, PO, and any notes.  
  - *Gateway (Exclusive):* Approve?  
    - **Yes**  Task 10.  
    - **No** (suspicious/budget mismatch/unexpected items)   
      - **Task 9a: Reject or Hold for Investigation**  
        - Notify AP Clerk to place on hold; may request further investigation.  
        - After investigation/correction, may return to Task 9 for re-review.  

#### **Lane: Accounts Payable (AP Clerk) ñ final steps**  
- **Task 10: Schedule for Payment**  
  - Add invoice to next payment cycle / payment run.  

- **End Event:** Invoice scheduled for payment.  

---

### **5. Exception / Variation Summary**  

| **Exception Type** | **Trigger** | **Handling Path** | **Typical Roles Involved** |  
|--------------------|-------------|-------------------|----------------------------|  
| Missing PO | Invoice lacks PO or PO invalid | Request PO from supplier; if unavailable, escalate to Supervisor for retrospective PO or rejection | AP Clerk  Supervisor; possibly Karen (Supplier Mgr) |  
| Pricing / Quantity Discrepancy | Invoice does not match PO | Purchasing or AP Clerk clarifies with supplier; involve Karen if contract review needed; escalate if unresolvable | Purchasing, AP Clerk, Supervisor, Karen |  
| Receipt Not Confirmed | Purchasing cannot confirm goods/services received | Invoice placed on hold; Purchasing contacts supplier; escalate if no resolution | Purchasing, Supervisor |  
| Manager Rejection | Manager finds suspicious or budget-deviating items | Invoice held; investigation; possible rework and return to review | Manager, AP Clerk |  
| System/Process Ambiguity | Conflicting accounts in interviews | Model allows flexibility: AP Clerk may go directly to payment scheduling after manager approval; Purchasing reconfirmation may loop back to match task | All roles as needed |  

---

### **6. Notes on Ambiguities & Assumptions**  
- **Sequence between Purchasing confirmation and AP line-item match:** Interviews suggest the AP Clerk performs system matching after receiving Purchasing confirmation, but the exact demarcation can vary (Purchasing may also verify line items). Model assumes AP does the formal system reconciliation.  
- **Escalation routing:** Escalations can occur from multiple steps and may return to different points after resolution (e.g., after Supervisor fixes missing PO, process may resume at Purchasing confirmation or AP matching).  
- **Manager approval trigger:** Confirmed as automatic after receipt confirmation and PO match in the system.  
- **Karenís involvement:** Indirect; only looped in to resolve supplier/contract disputes, not for approvals.  
- **Final payment scheduling:** Always performed by AP Clerk after manager approval.
