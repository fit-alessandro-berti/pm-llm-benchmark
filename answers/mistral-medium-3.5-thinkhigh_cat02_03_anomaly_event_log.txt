  - Ensure that `Match Invoice to PO` is a **prerequisite** for `Release Payment` (fixes Cases 1002, 1003).

  - Set system rules so that `Approve Purchase Requisition` must occur **before** `Issue PO` (fixes Case 1002).

- **Automate P2P Workflows:**
