Below are the two **POWL models** representing the hiring process at **GlobalTeq Solutions**, one with potential unfairness (due to the XOR branch for community affiliation) and one without bias (where all applicants undergo the same evaluation).

---

### **1. POWL Model with Potential Unfairness**
This model introduces an **XOR choice** after the skill assessment, where applicants either go through a **standard cultural fit evaluation** or a **CommunityAffiliationCheck** (which may provide an implicit advantage).

```python
from pm4py.objects.powl.obj import StrictPartialOrder, OperatorPOWL, Transition, SilentTransition
from pm4py.objects.process_tree.obj import Operator

# --- Activities ---
ReceiveApplication = Transition(label="ReceiveApplication")
DataCompletenessCheck = Transition(label="DataCompletenessCheck")
RequestMoreInfo = Transition(label="RequestMoreInfo")
SkillAssessment = Transition(label="SkillAssessment")
CulturalFitCheck = Transition(label="CulturalFitCheck")
CommunityAffiliationCheck = Transition(label="CommunityAffiliationCheck")
ManagerialReview = Transition(label="ManagerialReview")
FinalDecision = Transition(label="FinalDecision")

# --- Loop for Data Completeness ---
DataLoop = OperatorPOWL(operator=Operator.LOOP, children=[DataCompletenessCheck, RequestMoreInfo])

# --- XOR Choice: Standard vs. Affiliation-Based Path ---
StandardPath = CulturalFitCheck
AffiliationPath = CommunityAffiliationCheck
CulturalFitXOR = OperatorPOWL(operator=Operator.XOR, children=[StandardPath, AffiliationPath])

# --- Main Workflow (Partial Order) ---
root = StrictPartialOrder(
    nodes=[
        ReceiveApplication,
        DataLoop,
        SkillAssessment,
        CulturalFitXOR,
        ManagerialReview,
        FinalDecision
    ]
)

# --- Ordering Constraints ---
root.order.add_edge(ReceiveApplication, DataLoop)
root.order.add_edge(DataLoop, SkillAssessment)
root.order.add_edge(SkillAssessment, CulturalFitXOR)
root.order.add_edge(CulturalFitXOR, ManagerialReview)
root.order.add_edge(ManagerialReview, FinalDecision)
```

#### **Key Observations:**
- The **XOR choice (`CulturalFitXOR`)** introduces bias because:
  - Some applicants take `CulturalFitCheck` (fair evaluation).
  - Others take `CommunityAffiliationCheck` (potential implicit advantage).
- This violates the principle of **equal treatment** for all candidates.

---

### **2. POWL Model Without Unfairness**
This model ensures **all applicants undergo the same evaluation** by removing the XOR branch and replacing it with a **single `CulturalFitCheck`**.

```python
from pm4py.objects.powl.obj import StrictPartialOrder, OperatorPOWL, Transition, SilentTransition
from pm4py.objects.process_tree.obj import Operator

# --- Activities ---
ReceiveApplication = Transition(label="ReceiveApplication")
DataCompletenessCheck = Transition(label="DataCompletenessCheck")
RequestMoreInfo = Transition(label="RequestMoreInfo")
SkillAssessment = Transition(label="SkillAssessment")
CulturalFitCheck = Transition(label="CulturalFitCheck")  # No affiliation-based path
ManagerialReview = Transition(label="ManagerialReview")
FinalDecision = Transition(label="FinalDecision")

# --- Loop for Data Completeness ---
DataLoop = OperatorPOWL(operator=Operator.LOOP, children=[DataCompletenessCheck, RequestMoreInfo])

# --- Main Workflow (Partial Order) ---
root = StrictPartialOrder(
    nodes=[
        ReceiveApplication,
        DataLoop,
        SkillAssessment,
        CulturalFitCheck,  # No XOR, all applicants go here
        ManagerialReview,
        FinalDecision
    ]
)

# --- Ordering Constraints ---
root.order.add_edge(ReceiveApplication, DataLoop)
root.order.add_edge(DataLoop, SkillAssessment)
root.order.add_edge(SkillAssessment, CulturalFitCheck)
root.order.add_edge(CulturalFitCheck, ManagerialReview)
root.order.add_edge(ManagerialReview, FinalDecision)
```

#### **Key Observations:**
- **No XOR branch**  All applicants follow the same path.
- **Fairness is preserved** because no candidate receives preferential treatment based on affiliation.
- The process remains **transparent and merit-based**.

---

### **Comparison of the Two Models**
| Feature | **Model with Unfairness** | **Model Without Unfairness** |
|---------|--------------------------|-----------------------------|
| **Post-Skill Assessment Path** | XOR choice (Standard vs. Affiliation) | Single `CulturalFitCheck` |
| **Potential Bias** | Yes (affiliation-based advantage) | No (equal evaluation) |
| **Transparency** | Lower (hidden bias) | Higher (uniform process) |
| **Compliance with Fairness** | Violates equal treatment | Aligns with fairness principles |

### **Conclusion**
- The **first model** introduces **subtle bias** due to the XOR branch favoring certain groups.
- The **second model** ensures **fairness** by removing the biased path and enforcing a **uniform evaluation process**.
