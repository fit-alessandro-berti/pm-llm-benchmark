### 3) Brief justification of naming choices

- **Sequence position** drove “initial” vs “recheck” naming (first verification after intake vs second verification after remediation).
- **Preceding event type** clarified purpose:
  - after `Submit Additional`  recheck after new customer evidence.
  - after `Adjust Account`  recheck after internal correction.
- **Call intent** came directly from context text:
  - “clarify discrepancy” (diagnostic),
  - “confirm adjustments made” (confirmation),
  - “have them return” (customer return reminder).
- **Resource/channel** helped disambiguate branch physical verification vs back-office digital verification.
