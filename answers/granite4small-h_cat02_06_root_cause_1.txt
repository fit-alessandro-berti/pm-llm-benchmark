### Step 5: Recommendations

1. **Streamline Escalation Processes**:
   - Develop a more efficient escalation process to minimize delays post-escalation. This could include clearer guidelines for what constitutes an issue that requires escalation and better resources or protocols at higher support levels.

2. **Reduce Waiting Times Between Activities**:
   - Implement systems or processes to reduce idle times between task assignments, investigations, and resolutions. Automation tools can be used to remind agents of pending tasks or automatically escalate based on predefined criteria.

3. **Analyze Investigation Delays**:
   - Investigate why there are long durations during the investigation phase for escalated cases. This could involve additional training for support staff, providing more resources to handle complex issues, or revising how escalations are managed internally.

4. **Standardize Workflow**:

5. **Performance Monitoring**:
   - Regularly monitor ticket resolution times to identify trends or recurring issues early on, allowing for timely interventions before delays become significant performance bottlenecks.

By focusing on these areas, it is possible to reduce the total resolution time of support tickets and improve overall efficiency in handling customer support requests.
