## Rationale for Added Constraints

### 1. **Mandatory Bias Mitigation Activities (existence)**
- Added `BiasMitigationCheck` and `ManualReview` as mandatory activities to ensure every loan application undergoes fairness checks.

### 2. **Coexistence Constraints**
- `CheckSensitiveAttributes` must coexist with both `ManualReview` and `BiasMitigationCheck` to ensure that whenever sensitive data is accessed, proper review mechanisms are triggered.
- `Approve` and `Reject` decisions must coexist with `FairnessValidation` to ensure all decisions are validated for fairness.

### 3. **Response Constraints**
- After checking any sensitive attribute (`CheckApplicantRace`, `CheckApplicantGender`, `CheckApplicantAge`), a `ManualReview` must eventually occur.
- `CheckSensitiveAttributes` must be followed by `BiasMitigationCheck` to ensure bias is actively checked after accessing sensitive data.

### 4. **Precedence Constraints**
- `BiasMitigationCheck` must precede both `Approve` and `Reject` decisions, ensuring bias checks happen before any decision.
- `ManualReview` and `FairnessValidation` must precede `FinalDecision` to ensure human oversight and validation.

### 5. **Non-succession Constraints**
- Prevents direct succession from sensitive attribute checks (`CheckSensitiveAttributes`, `CheckApplicantRace`, etc.) to rejection decisions, avoiding immediate biased outcomes.
- This forces intermediate steps (like bias checks and manual reviews) between accessing sensitive data and making decisions.

### 6. **Non-coexistence Constraints**
- Prevents automatic approval or rejection without review when sensitive attributes are checked, ensuring human oversight in sensitive cases.

These constraints collectively ensure that:
- No decision can be made immediately after checking sensitive attributes
- All decisions involving sensitive data undergo multiple layers of review
- Bias mitigation checks are mandatory before final decisions
- Human review is required for cases involving sensitive attributes
- The process maintains transparency and fairness throughout the loan application review
