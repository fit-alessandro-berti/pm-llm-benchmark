## **3. Justification for Groupings**

### **a) Material Preparation**
- **Events:** Retrieval, scanning, placement, alignment.
- **Why?**
  - These are **initial setup steps** for the raw material.
  - They are **temporally sequential** (occur first in the log).
  - They involve **handling and positioning** the material before processing.
  - Performed by **Operator A and Robot Arm #2** (human-machine collaboration).

### **b) Pre-Processing**
- **Events:** Preheating.
- **Why?**
  - A **distinct phase** where the material is modified (heated) before assembly.
  - Performed by a **dedicated resource (Heating Unit #1)**.
  - Occurs **after alignment but before assembly**.

### **c) Assembly**
- **Events:** Picking up tool, welding.
- **Why?**
  - **Active manufacturing** (joining parts).
  - Performed by the **same operator (Operator B)** with the same tool (W-12).
  - **Temporally grouped** (all welding-related actions occur together).

### **d) Quality Assurance**
- **Events:** Weld integrity measurement, visual check.
- **Why?**
  - **Validation steps** to ensure product quality.
  - Involves **different resources (sensor and operator)** but serves the same purpose.
  - Occurs **after assembly but before finishing**.

### **e) Finishing**
- **Events:** Coating, drying.
- **Why?**
  - **Finalization steps** to protect the product.
  - Performed by **dedicated units (Coating Unit #3, Heating Unit #2)**.
  - Occurs **last in the sequence**.
