### Identification of Bias in the Lending Process

### **Where and How Bias Is Introduced**

This creates a **double-layered advantage** for long-term residents: they benefit both from the residency bonus *and* the likelihood of qualifying for the community-group bonus.

  - The adjustment is **not disclosed to applicants**, eliminating transparency and the opportunity for applicants to understand or contest why their application may be scored lower than expected.

- **Bias Mechanism**:
