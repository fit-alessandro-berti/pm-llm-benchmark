### Procure-to-Pay (P2P) Event Log Analysis

Below is an analysis of the provided event log based on standard Procure-to-Pay best practices. The standard P2P flow typically follows this sequence:
1.  **Create & Approve Requisition:** Demand identification and budget approval.
2.  **Issue Purchase Order (PO):** Formal commitment to the vendor based on the approved requisition.
3.  **Receive Goods:** Physical verification of goods/services against the PO.
4.  **Vendor Invoice Received:** Bill arrives from the vendor.
5.  **Match Invoice to PO:** Verification (Three-Way Match: PO, Goods Receipt, Invoice).
6.  **Release Payment:** Payment is authorized after successful matching.
7.  **Close Case:** Process completion.

**Key Controls:** Approval must precede PO; PO must precede Goods Receipt; Matching must precede Payment; Payment should generally follow Invoice receipt.
