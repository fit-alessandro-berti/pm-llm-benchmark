### How the Log Was Built – Brief Explanation

1. **Case Identification**  
   - A *case* represents a logical work item that the user manipulates: a document, an email, or a PDF.  
   - The raw log was scanned for a change of the **Window** (or *Item*) field. When the window title switched from one document/email/PDF to another, a new case was started.  
   - The resulting cases are:  
     - **C1** – *Document1.docx* (the main report draft).  
     - **C2** – *Email about Annual Meeting* (the email thread).  
     - **C3** – *Report_Draft.pdf* (PDF review).  
     - **C4** – *Budget_2024.xlsx* (budget update).  
     - **C5** – *Quarterly_Report.docx* (executivesummary draft).  

2. **Activity Naming**  
   - Lowlevel actions (FOCUS, TYPING, SAVE, CLICK, SCROLL, HIGHLIGHT, CLOSE) were mapped to higherlevel, analystfriendly activity names:  
     - **Focus Document** – user brings a window to the foreground.  
     - **Write / Write Reference / Compose Email** – any TYPING event.  
     - **Save** – explicit SAVE action.  
     - **Open Email**, **Read Email**, **Reply Email**, **Send Email** – derived from the sequence of CLICK and TYPING events in Chrome.  
     - **Read PDF** – SCROLL in Acrobat.  
     - **Highlight Text** – HIGHLIGHT in Acrobat.  
     - **Update Data** – TYPING in Excel (treated as a dataentry step).  
     - **Close Document** – explicit CLOSE action.  

3. **Event Attributes**  
   - **Case ID** links every event to its logical work item.  
   - **Activity Name** is the standardized step.  
   - **Timestamp** is kept exactly as in the source log.  
   - **Application** and **Item** (document, email, PDF) are retained as auxiliary attributes; they are useful for filtering or visualisation in processmining tools.  

4. **Narrative Coherence**  
   - The resulting event log tells a clear story: the user starts by opening a quarterly report, then edits a draft document, handles an email, reviews a PDF, updates a budget spreadsheet, returns to the draft to insert a reference, and finally finishes the executivesummary draft.  
   - Each case follows a natural starttoend flow (focus  work  save  close), which makes the log easy to analyse for bottlenecks, rework, or handoffs between applications.
