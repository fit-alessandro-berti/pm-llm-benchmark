## Rationale for Added Bias-Mitigating Constraints

1. **existence["ManualReview"] and existence["BiasMitigationCheck"]**: Ensures these fairness-enforcing activities always appear in traces, mandating bias checks in every loan review process.

2. **coexistence["CheckApplicantRace"]["ManualReview"] and coexistence["CheckApplicantGender"]["ManualReview"]**: If sensitive attributes (race/gender) are checked, a ManualReview *must* also occur somewhere in the trace, preventing decisions without human oversight for at-risk applicants.

3. **response["CheckApplicantRace"]["BiasMitigationCheck"] and response["CheckApplicantGender"]["BiasMitigationCheck"]**: After checking sensitive attributes, a BiasMitigationCheck must eventually follow (somewhere later), blocking progression without explicit bias auditing.

4. **response["BiasMitigationCheck"]["ManualReview"]**: Guarantees ManualReview follows bias mitigation, creating a mandatory fairness review chain.

5. **precedence["BiasMitigationCheck"]["FinalDecision"] and precedence["ManualReview"]["FinalDecision"]**: No final decision (Approve/Reject) can occur unless bias mitigation and manual review have already happened, enforcing sequential fairness gates.

6. **nonsuccession/ nonchainsuccession["CheckApplicantRace"]["Reject"] and ["CheckApplicantGender"]["Reject"]**: Prevents direct or chained succession from sensitive attribute checks to Reject decisions, eliminating immediate biased rejections without intervening fairness checks.

## How These Reduce Bias
These constraints create a **mandatory fairness pipeline**: sensitive attribute checks  bias mitigation  manual review  final decision. Direct paths to rejection are blocked, and human oversight is enforced for at-risk cases, significantly reducing discrimination risk in loan approvals while preserving process flexibility.
