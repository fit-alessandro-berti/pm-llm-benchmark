## 1. **Understanding the Standard Hire-to-Retire Process**

The typical **Hire-to-Retire** process should proceed in a linear (or at least sequential with clear branches) manner:

1. **Post Job Ad** (recruitment starts)
2. **Screen Candidates** (filter applications)
3. **Conduct Interviews** (evaluate shortlisted candidates)
4. **Make Hiring Decision** (select candidate)
5. **Onboard New Employee** (integrate into the organization)
6. **Add to Payroll System** (ensure they are paid and benefits are set up)
7. **Close Case** (mark hiring complete)

These steps are usually performed in order, with some minor parallelism (e.g., screening and interviews may overlap for different candidates), but generally a clear direction from recruitment through onboarding to closure.
