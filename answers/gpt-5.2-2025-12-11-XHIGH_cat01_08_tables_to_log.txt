**Assumptions / notes**
- `order_id` is the **Case ID** for all events; payments are linked to cases via `payment.invoice_id  invoice.order_id`.
- Each **order line** creates a separate **“Item Picked”** event (no aggregation to “picking completed”).
- Each **delivery record** creates two events: **“Delivery Dispatched”** and **“Delivery Arrived”**; the **carrier** is used as the resource for these events.
- **Payment** rows do not contain a resource/actor in the provided data, so the resource fields are left blank.
- Order **1004** has no delivery/invoice/payment records in the extract; the case ends at the last available event.
- All timestamps are treated as **UTC** (suffix `Z`).
