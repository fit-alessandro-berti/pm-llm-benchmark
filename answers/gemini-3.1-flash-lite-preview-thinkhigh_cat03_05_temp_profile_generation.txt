### Key Considerations in this Profile:
1.  **Cumulative Variance**: As the time between activities increases, the standard deviation grows. This reflects the "accumulation of uncertainty" inherent in long-running supply chain processes.
2.  **Activity Dependencies**: 
    *   **Procurement (`SS`, `OP`, `RC`)**: Driven by external logistics, hence higher base times and significant standard deviations.
    *   **Manufacturing (`QI`, `CA`, `PT`)**: Driven by internal production schedules, resulting in tighter, more predictable timeframes.
    *   **Logistics (`WS`, `DT`)**: Highly susceptible to transport delays, leading to larger deviations.
    *   **Support (`AS`)**: Reflects a long-term horizon where the timing is highly variable, depending on product failure rates and consumer behavior.
