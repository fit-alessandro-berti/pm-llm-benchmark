## Key Design Decisions:

1. **Existence constraints** ensure every product goes through all required steps
2. **Initialization** must start with Idea Generation (IG)
3. **Sequential dependencies** reflect the typical flow: IG  DD  TFC/CE (parallel)  PC  LT/UT  AG  MP  FL
4. **Parallel activities** (TFC and CE, LT and UT) have coexistence constraints
5. **Chain constraints** enforce immediate succession where appropriate
6. **Non-coexistence/nonsuccession** prevent impossible process flows
7. **Support = 1.0** indicates these constraints must always hold
8. **Confidence values** range from 0.8 to 1.0, with higher values for more critical constraints

This model captures the essential declarative rules governing the product development process while allowing flexibility in parallel activities.
