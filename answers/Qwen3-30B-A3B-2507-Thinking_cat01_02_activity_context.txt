### Key Principles Applied
- **Context-Driven Renaming**: Each new name includes *trigger* (e.g., "After Submission"), *purpose* (e.g., "Request Missing Documents"), or *medium* (e.g., "Physical") to eliminate ambiguity.  
- **Consistency**: "Initial" for first verification, "Re-Verification" for follow-ups, and "After [Event]" for dependency-based actions.  
- **Eliminated Redundancy**: Removed generic terms ("Call Customer") that masked purpose. All new names *explicitly state the activity’s role* in the process flow.  
- **Cross-Case Clarity**: Even though "Verify Documents" appears in all cases, the names now reflect *case-specific context* (e.g., "Physical" for branch, "After Adjustment" for C003), making the log self-explanatory for analysts.  

This approach ensures the log accurately reflects *why* each activity occurs, not just *what* it is—critical for process mining, compliance, and workflow optimization in financial services.
