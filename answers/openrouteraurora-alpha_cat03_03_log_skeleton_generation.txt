**Explanation of the chosen constraints**

| Constraint | Rationale |
|------------|-----------|
| **AlwaysBefore / AlwaysAfter** | Captures the canonical linear flow of the orderfulfilment process (RO  CA  …  RP). |
| **DirectlyFollows** | Enforces the *exact* ordering for the typical “happypath” case; each activity must be immediately followed by its logical successor. |
| **Equivalence** | No activities are required to appear the same number of times (each step is performed once). |
| **NeverTogether** | No pair of activities is mutually exclusive in this scenario. |
| **Activity Frequencies** | All steps except payment are mandatory exactly once. Payment may be omitted when the order is prepaid, hence `{0,1}`. |
