Here’s a list of targeted, open-ended questions designed to deepen your understanding of the property onboarding process:

---

### **Documentation & Initial Setup**
1. **How does the property coordinator determine which documents are "missing" or "outdated"? What is the threshold for proceeding if certain documents are delayed?**
2. **What is the typical timeline for verifying documentation, and how does this vary if a landlord is slow to respond?**
3. **Are there any documents that automatically disqualify a property from onboarding, or are most issues resolvable with follow-up?**

---

### **Property Manager Assignment**
4. **How is the property manager assigned when a landlord has unique requirements (e.g., luxury units, student housing)? Is this a manual or automated process?**
5. **What happens if a property manager’s workload becomes too high after assignment? Is there a system to redistribute properties?**
6. **Can a landlord request a specific property manager, and if so, under what circumstances is that allowed?**

---

### **Introductory Meetings & Expectations**
7. **What are the most common issues or conflicts that arise during the introductory meeting between the property manager and landlord?**
8. **How does the company ensure that special conditions (e.g., pet policies, maintenance responsibilities) are clearly documented and communicated internally?**
9. **If a landlord’s expectations are misaligned with company policies, how is that resolved before moving forward?**

---

### **Inspections, Photography, and Repairs**
10. **How are photographers and inspectors selected? Are there preferred vendors, or is it based on availability?**
11. **What is the process if an inspection fails? Who bears the cost of repairs—the landlord, the company, or shared?**
12. **Is there a standard timeline for completing repairs after an inspection, or does this vary by issue severity?**

---

### **Marketing and Pricing**
13. **How does the marketing team tailor listings for different property types (e.g., furnished vs. unfurnished)?**
14. **What factors go into setting the initial rental price range? How often is this adjusted based on market trends?**
15. **If a property doesn’t lease within a certain timeframe, what steps are taken to reprice or re-market it?**

---

### **Tenant Screening & Selection**
16. **What are the key criteria used to evaluate tenant applications? How do credit scores, income, and references weigh against each other?**
17. **When multiple tenants apply for the same property, how is the decision made? Are there priority systems (e.g., longer lease terms, higher credit score)?**
18. **How does the company handle disputes or disagreements between landlords and selected tenants before lease signing?**

---

### **Lease Finalization & Deposits**
19. **Are lease agreements standardized, or do they include custom clauses for unique requirements (e.g., pet policies, maintenance terms)?**
20. **What is the process for collecting and managing security deposits? Are there state-specific policies or timelines?**
21. **How does the company handle last-minute changes to move-in dates or lease terms?**

---

### **Quality Checks & Audits**
22. **Which department or role typically conducts compliance audits, and how frequently are they performed?**
23. **If an audit identifies a discrepancy (e.g., pricing mismatch, fair housing violation), what is the resolution process?**
24. **How does the IT department verify that property data is correctly integrated into systems like the rental portal or CRM?**

---

### **Exceptions & Special Cases**
25. **How does the company handle properties that require special insurance or unique lease terms?**
26. **What happens if a property fails to attract tenants despite proper marketing and pricing? Are there contingency plans?**
27. **Are there any properties that are rejected outright during onboarding, and if so, what are the reasons?**

---

### **Communication & Stakeholder Coordination**
28. **How are updates communicated to landlords throughout the process (e.g., inspection results, tenant applications)?**
29. **What tools or systems are used to track communication with external stakeholders (e.g., inspectors, photographers, contractors)?**
30. **How are delays or bottlenecks in the process escalated to management or resolved in real time?**

---

### **Metrics & Performance**
31. **What are the key performance indicators (KPIs) for the onboarding process (e.g., time to lease, landlord satisfaction, tenant retention)?**
32. **How does the company measure the success of a property post-onboarding, and what feedback loops exist to improve the process?**

---

These questions are designed to uncover nuances in decision-making, roles, and exceptions, while also clarifying the flow and dependencies between steps. They’ll help you understand not just the "what" but the "why" and "how" behind the process.
