### Normative expectations (from the description)
After **Receive order** the flow must be:

- **Normal (enough inventory):**  
  **Receive  Check inventory  Authorize payment  Package items  Ship order  Close order**
- **Insufficient inventory:**  
  **Receive  Check inventory  Cancel order  Close order**

Key constraints:
- **Authorize payment must not occur before “Check inventory.”**
- **Package items must not occur before “Authorize payment.”**
- **Ship order must only occur after “Package items.”**

- **Close order must be the final activity.**
