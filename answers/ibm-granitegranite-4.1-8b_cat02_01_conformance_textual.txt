### Analysis of the Event Log Against the Normative Process

#### Normative Process Recap:
1. **Receive order**  
2. **Check inventory**  
   - If **sufficient inventory**: 
     - **Authorize payment**  
       - **Package items**  
         - **Ship order**  
           - **Close order**  
   - If **insufficient inventory**: 
     - **Cancel order**  
       - **Close order**

**Key Constraints**:
- **Check inventory** must occur before **Authorize payment** or **Package items**.
- **Authorize payment** must occur before **Package items** (only when inventory is sufficient).
- **Package items** must occur before **Ship order**.
- **Ship order** should never follow **Cancel order**.
- **Close order** is the final activity in all scenarios.

### Summary of Violations

- **Case 2**: Packages items before authorizing payment (violates the payment-first requirement when inventory is sufficient).
- **Case 3**: Authorizes payment before canceling, then ships after cancellation (violates both the rational sequence for cancellation and the prohibition of shipping after cancellation).
- **Case 5**: Skips the mandatory **Package items** step before **Ship order** (violates the packaging prerequisite for shipping).

These deviations indicate processes that do not align with the expected norms as described, whether due to misordered activities or omitted steps.
