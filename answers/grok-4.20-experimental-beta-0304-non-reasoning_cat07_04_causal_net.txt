### Legend (Splits and Joins)

The grey box at the bottom explains the **control flow patterns** used:

- **XOR-split / XOR-join**: Exclusive choice (only one path is taken).
- **AND-split / AND-join**: Parallel execution (all paths are taken).
- **OR-split / OR-join**: Inclusive choice (one or more paths can be taken).

These are standard building blocks in process modeling (BPMN, EPC, Petri nets, etc.) to represent decision points, synchronization, and parallelism.
