### **Clarifying the Overall Process & Scope**

### **Roles & Responsibilities**
3. **Property Coordinator:**

4. **Property Manager Assignment:**
   - You mentioned several factors for assigning a property manager (workload, geography, experience, etc.). How are these weighted? For example, is geographic proximity more important than experience with a specific property type?

   - How do you measure a property manager’s "track record" with landlords? Are there KPIs (e.g., landlord satisfaction scores, retention rates) that influence assignments?

5. **Collaboration Between Roles:**
   - How do property managers and coordinators divide responsibilities during the onboarding process? Are there handoff points where miscommunication commonly occurs?

   - How are disagreements between the property manager and landlord (e.g., over lease terms or maintenance responsibilities) resolved?

### **Inspection & Compliance**
6. **Inspection Process:**
   - What are the most common issues flagged by inspectors, and how frequently do they delay onboarding? Are there "dealbreaker" issues (e.g., structural problems) that automatically disqualify a property?

   - How do you handle properties in jurisdictions with *stricter* or *more lenient* housing regulations than your standard? Are there regional variations in the inspection process?

7. **Compliance & Legal:**
   - You mentioned a compliance officer reviews leases for fair housing adherence. What other regulations (e.g., local tenant-landlord laws, ADA accessibility) are checked during onboarding?

   - What happens if a property is *conditionally* compliant (e.g., "grandfathered" into older regulations)? How is this documented and communicated to tenants?

   - What happens if multiple qualified tenants apply for the same unit? How is the final decision made (e.g., first-come-first-served, "best fit" for the property, landlord preference)?

### **Lease Finalization & Move-In**

11. **Move-In Process:**
