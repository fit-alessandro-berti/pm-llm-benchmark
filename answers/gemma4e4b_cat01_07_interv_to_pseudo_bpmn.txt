## BPMN-like Textual Process Model: Accounts Payable Invoice Processing

This model describes the process of Invoice Approval and Payment Scheduling, consolidating inputs from multiple stakeholders (Accounts Payable, Purchasing, Management, and Supporting Roles).

---

###  Process Overview
**Process Name:** Invoice Approval and Payment Cycle
**Trigger:** Receipt of an Invoice (via email/system upload).
**Goal:** To validate an invoice, obtain necessary approvals, and schedule the payment.

###  Roles / Participants (Pools/Lanes)

1. **Invoice (Artifact):** The document being processed.
2. **Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary):** Initial logging, data matching, and process flow management.
3. **Purchasing Officer (Dan):** Verifying the goods/services receipt and matching details to the PO.
4. **Department Manager (Carlos):** Final business approval.
5. **Accounts Payable Supervisor (Rita):** Handling exceptions and discrepancies.
6. **Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen):** Resolving contractual or supplier-specific discrepancies.
7. **External Supplier:** Provides the initial invoice.
8. **Accounting System:** The platform executing the workflow and notifications.

---

###  Process Flow Description (Sequence & Gateways)

#### **Phase 1: Intake and Initial Validation (Accounts Payable)**

1. **[START]** **Event:** Invoice Received (Input Artifact: Invoice, typically via email).
2. **[Activity]** **Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary):**
    * **Task:** Check for Purchase Order (PO) Number association.
    * **Gateway/Decision:** Is PO Number present on the Invoice?
        * **[NO]:** **(Path A: Missing PO)**
            * **Task:** Return Invoice to Supplier.
            * **Action:** Request necessary PO details from the Supplier. (Involves Supplier/Karen)
            * **-> Loop:** Wait for Supplier correction/update.
        * **[YES]:** **(Path B: Valid PO)**
            * **Task:** Log Invoice details into Accounting System.
            * **Handoff:** Send to Purchasing Officer.

#### **Phase 2: Three-Way Match (Purchasing Department)**

3. **[Activity]** **Purchasing Officer (Dan):**
    * **Input:** Invoice + PO Number (from AP Clerk).
    * **Task:** Execute the "Three-Way Match" (Compare: Invoice vs. PO vs. Confirmation of Goods Receipt).
    * **Gateway/Decision:** Do quantities, prices, and details match the PO/Contract?
        * **[NO - Discrepancy]:** **(Path C: Discrepancy)**
            * **Task:** Investigate discrepancy.
            * **Gateway/Decision:** Can the discrepancy be resolved internally (e.g., missing delivery notes)?
                * **[YES]:** **(Path C.1: Resolution)**
                    * **Task:** Contact Internal/External Parties (e.g., Contact Supplier/Karen).
                    * **Handoff:** Return to AP Clerk or Purchasing to resolve (loop back).
                * **[NO]:** **(Path C.2: Escalation)**
                    * **Task:** Escalate to Accounts Payable Supervisor (Rita).
                    * **-> Handoff:** Proceed to Phase 3 (Escalation Loop).
        * **[YES - Match Confirmed]:** **(Path D: Successful Match)**
            * **Task:** Send Confirmation of Match to Accounts Payable Clerk.
            * **Handoff:** Send to Manager for Approval.

#### **Phase 3: Approval (Management)**

4. **[Activity]** **Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary) / Accounting System:**
    * **System Action:** Automatically notify the Department Manager (Carlos) that the three-way match is confirmed.
5. **[Activity]** **Department Manager (Carlos):**
    * **Input:** Full Invoice/PO details via System Notification.
    * **Task:** Review and approve/reject the invoice based on business judgment (e.g., budget constraints).
    * **Gateway/Decision:** Does the Manager find the invoice acceptable?
        * **[NO - Rejection]:** **(Path E: Manager Rejection)**
            * **Task:** Reject/Hold Invoice and send it back to AP Clerk/Purchasing for root cause investigation.
            * **-> Loop:** Process halts until issue is resolved.
        * **[YES - Approval]:** **(Path F: Manager Approval)**
            * **Handoff:** Send to Accounts Payable Clerk.

#### **Phase 4: Exception Handling (Supervisory Level)**

*(This phase intercepts the flow if Paths C.2 or initial Path A require complex resolution.)*

6. **[Activity]** **Accounts Payable Supervisor (Rita):**
    * **Input:** Escalated invoice (from AP Clerk or Purchasing).
    * **Task:** Determine root cause of the failure (e.g., informal services, missing PO).
    * **Gateway/Decision:** Can the exception be processed (e.g., retrospective PO created)?
        * **[YES]:**
            * **Task:** Coordinate required resolution (e.g., create retrospective PO, contact supplier).
            * **Handoff:** Return the fully remediated invoice to the beginning of the sequence (Phase 2 or 3).
        * **[NO]:**
            * **Task:** Reject Invoice (Final Step).
            * **[END]** Process terminated.

#### **Phase 5: Payment Scheduling (Completion)**

7. **[Activity]** **Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary):**
    * **Input:** Approved and Verified Invoice.
    * **Task:** Schedule the invoice for payment cycle.
    * **[END]** Payment Scheduled.

---

###  Summary of Key Decisions & Variations

| Element | Condition / Gateway | Next Action Path | Responsible Role |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Missing PO** | Invoice lacks a valid PO number. | Send back to Supplier/Use Karen to contact Supplier. | AP Clerk, Karen, Supplier |
| **Match Failure** | Invoice details ($\$$/Qty) do not match PO/Receipt. | Investigate discrepancy. May require escalation. | Purchasing Officer, AP Clerk |
| **Discrepancy** | Discrepancy is complex or unresolvable by standard means. | Escalate to Supervisor. | AP Clerk $\rightarrow$ Rita |
| **Manager Review** | Invoice suspicious (e.g., budget overruns). | Manager rejects and sends back for investigation. | Dept. Manager |
| **Exception** | Core process failure (e.g., no PO exists for services). | Supervisor handles special process steps (e.g., retrospective PO creation). | AP Supervisor, Karen |
