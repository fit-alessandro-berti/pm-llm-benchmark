To create a comprehensive event log, I have integrated the provided ERP tables. 

### Assumptions & Logic:
1.  **Event Aggregation**: To avoid excessive rows for multiple line items in a single order, I have consolidated "Items Picked" events per `order_id` if they occurred on the same day by the same user. If different items were picked by different users, individual events are retained to preserve resource tracking.
2.  **Chronological Ordering**: All events are sorted by their `Timestamp`.
3.  **Resource Mapping**: The "Resource" column draws directly from the provided `Resources Table`.
4.  **Missing Case 1004**: Order 1004 appears in the `Orders` and `Order Lines` tables, but has no corresponding delivery, invoice, or payment entries in the provided data. It remains in the log as a "Process initiated" instance.
