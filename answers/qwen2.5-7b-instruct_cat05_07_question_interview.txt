2. **Property Manager Assignment:**
   - What criteria do you use to select a property manager for a new property? Are there any specific qualities or experiences you look for in a property manager?
   - How do you ensure that the assigned property manager has the necessary workload capacity to handle the new property effectively?

3. **Introduction Meeting:**
   - Can you describe the typical topics that are discussed during the introductory meeting between the property manager and the landlord? Are there any standard templates or checklists you use to guide these discussions?
   - How frequently do you find that landlords request changes to the initial property details after this meeting? How do you handle such requests?

4. **Professional Photography and Inspection:**
   - What is the typical timeline between property verification and the assignment of the photographer and inspector? Are there any circumstances where immediate inspections are required?
   - How do you handle properties that require more extensive repairs or renovations before they can be certified for rental?

5. **Marketing and Activation:**
   - How do you determine which marketing channels are most effective for different types of properties (e.g., luxury units, student housing, etc.)?
   - Are there any specific guidelines or templates you use for creating digital marketing materials and brochures?

6. **Tenant Screening:**
   - What is the typical timeline from property activation to tenant screening completion? Are there any properties that require expedited screening processes?
   - How do you handle situations where multiple tenants are interested in the same property? Are there any negotiation strategies or policies in place?

7. **Leasing and Move-In:**
   - What is the average time from choosing a tenant to the completion of the lease agreement and move-in? Are there any properties that require special handling during these steps?
   - How do you manage the collection of security deposits and any associated fees?

8. **Quality Checks and Internal Audits:**
   - What are the key areas that a compliance officer reviews during the lease agreement? Are there any specific regulations or guidelines they must adhere to?
   - How do you ensure that the IT systems are correctly updated and integrated for each new property?

9. **Exception Handling:**
   - Are there any specific scenarios where you need to deviate from the standard onboarding process? How do you manage these exceptions?
   - How do you handle properties that require special insurance or custom lease clauses?

10. **Communication and Stakeholder Management:**
    - How do you ensure that all stakeholders (landlords, property managers, IT, compliance, etc.) are kept informed and aligned throughout the onboarding process?
    - Are there any tools or methods you use to track and manage the various steps and exceptions in the process?

11. **Feedback and Continuous Improvement:**
    - How do you gather feedback from landlords and tenants after the property has been on-boarded and available for rent? How is this feedback used to improve the onboarding process?
    - Are there any recent changes or updates to the onboarding process that you are planning or have recently implemented?

12. **Scalability and Future Growth:**
    - How do you plan to scale the onboarding process as you continue to grow and take on more properties in new cities or regions?
    - Are there any emerging technologies or processes you are considering to streamline the onboarding of new properties?
