### Key Design Notes:
- **Concurrency**: `StrictPartialOrder` instances with no `.order.add_edge()` calls (like `phase2` and `phase5`) naturally model parallel/concurrent execution per POWL semantics.
- **Loop Semantics**: `OperatorPOWL(operator=Operator.LOOP, children=[BNC, DR])` correctly implements `*(BNC, D&R)`, meaning `BNC` runs first, then the process either exits or runs `D&R` followed by another `BNC` iteration.
- **Exclusive Choice**: `OperatorPOWL(operator=Operator.XOR, children=[CD, MD])` models the mandatory selection of exactly one deployment path.
- **Silent Transitions**: While imported as requested, they are not strictly necessary here since POWL's native operators handle routing and loop exits implicitly. They can be instantiated as `SilentTransition()` and inserted into any node list if future routing requires explicit tau steps.
