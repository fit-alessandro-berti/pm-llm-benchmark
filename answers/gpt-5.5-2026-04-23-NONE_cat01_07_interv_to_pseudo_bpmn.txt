## BPMN-like Textual Process Model: Invoice Approval Process

### 1. Participants / Swimlanes

- **Supplier**
- **Accounts Payable Clerk (AP Clerk / Mary)**
- **Purchasing Officer / Purchasing Department (Dan)**
- **Department Manager (Carlos)**
- **Accounts Payable Supervisor (Rita)**
- **Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen)**
- **Accounting System**

---

## 2. Main Process Flow

### Start Event
**Start:** Invoice received from Supplier  
- Typical channel: email  
- Input artifact: **Supplier Invoice**

---

### Task 1: Receive Invoice
**Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk  
**Description:** AP receives the invoice, usually by email.

**Input:** Supplier invoice  
**Output:** Invoice ready for initial validation

---

### Task 2: Check for Purchase Order Number
**Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk  
**Description:** AP checks whether the invoice contains a valid purchase order number.

**Gateway 1: Is there a valid PO number?**

#### Path A: No PO number or invalid PO number
1. **Task:** Request PO details or revised invoice from Supplier  
   - **Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk  
   - **Possible support:** Supplier Relationship Manager  
   - **Output:** Request sent to Supplier for missing PO number or corrected invoice

2. **Intermediate Event:** Await Supplier response

3. **Gateway 1A: Can supplier provide valid PO information?**

   - **Yes:** Return to **Task 2: Check for Purchase Order Number**
   - **No / unresolved:** Escalate to **Accounts Payable Supervisor**

4. **Task:** Handle missing PO exception  
   - **Role:** Accounts Payable Supervisor  
   - **Description:** Supervisor investigates whether the invoice relates to informally contracted services or another legitimate purchase.

5. **Gateway 1B: Can a retrospective PO be created or invoice otherwise validated?**

   - **Yes:**  
     - **Task:** Create or confirm retrospective PO  
       - **Role:** Accounts Payable Supervisor, possibly with Department Head  
       - **Output:** Valid PO or validated PO reference  
     - Return to **Task 2: Check for Purchase Order Number**

   - **No:**  
     - **Task:** Reject invoice  
       - **Role:** Accounts Payable Supervisor / Accounts Payable Clerk  
       - **Output:** Rejection communicated to Supplier  
     - **End Event:** Invoice rejected

#### Path B: PO number exists and is valid
Proceed to **Task 3: Log Invoice in Accounting System**

---

### Task 3: Log Invoice in Accounting System
**Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk  
**System:** Accounting System  
**Description:** AP records the invoice in the accounting system.

**Input:** Invoice with valid PO number  
**Output:** Logged invoice record

---

### Task 4: Forward Invoice to Purchasing for Receipt / Order Confirmation
**Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk  
**Recipient:** Purchasing Officer / Purchasing Department  
**Description:** AP forwards the invoice to Purchasing to confirm that goods or services were received and that invoice details align with the purchase order.

**Output artifact:** Invoice forwarded to Purchasing

---

### Task 5: Review Invoice Against PO and Receipt
**Role:** Purchasing Officer  
**Description:** Purchasing checks:
- PO reference
- Quantities
- Prices
- Delivery charges
- Whether goods/services were actually received

**Input artifacts:**
- Supplier invoice
- Purchase order
- Receipt / delivery confirmation, if available

**Output:** Purchasing confirmation or discrepancy report

---

### Gateway 2: Do invoice details match PO and receipt confirmation?

#### Path A: Details match
1. **Task:** Send confirmation to Accounts Payable  
   - **Role:** Purchasing Officer  
   - **Output:** Confirmation email / receipt confirmation

2. Proceed to **Task 6: Match Invoice Line Items Against PO in System**

#### Path B: Details do not match
1. **Task:** Clarify discrepancy  
   - **Role:** Purchasing Officer and/or Accounts Payable Clerk  
   - **Description:** Discrepancies may include:
     - Incorrect pricing
     - Extra units billed
     - Missing items
     - Unexpected delivery charges
     - Missing invoice information

2. **Gateway 2A: Who resolves discrepancy?**

   - **Purchasing contacts Supplier directly**
     - **Task:** Contact Supplier for correction or clarification  
       - **Role:** Purchasing Officer  
       - **Output:** Supplier clarification or corrected invoice

   - **AP asks Supplier / holds invoice**
     - **Task:** Place invoice on hold pending supplier correction  
       - **Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk  
       - **Output:** Invoice on hold

   - **Supplier Relationship Manager assists**
     - **Task:** Check contract terms and contact Supplier  
       - **Role:** Supplier Relationship Manager  
       - **Description:** Used especially for pricing discrepancies or key suppliers.
       - **Output:** Correct price or revised invoice information

3. **Gateway 2B: Is discrepancy resolved?**

   - **Yes:**  
     - Return to **Task 5: Review Invoice Against PO and Receipt** or proceed to **Task 6** if Purchasing confirms resolution.

   - **No / cannot be resolved easily:**  
     - Proceed to **Task 5E: Escalate discrepancy to AP Supervisor**

---

### Task 5E: Escalate Discrepancy to AP Supervisor
**Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk  
**Recipient:** Accounts Payable Supervisor  
**Description:** AP escalates unresolved discrepancies, missing information, or unmatched invoice issues to the supervisor.

---

### Task 5F: Coordinate Exception Resolution
**Role:** Accounts Payable Supervisor  
**Supporting roles:** Purchasing Officer, Supplier Relationship Manager, Supplier, Department Heads  
**Description:** Supervisor works to resolve problem invoices by:
- Contacting suppliers
- Coordinating between AP and Purchasing
- Checking whether missing PO issues can be corrected
- Working with department heads to create retrospective POs where appropriate
- Determining whether the invoice should be rejected

---

### Gateway 3: Can the exception be resolved?

#### Path A: Exception resolved
1. **Task:** Update invoice / PO / clarification details  
   - **Role:** Accounts Payable Supervisor or AP Clerk  
   - **Output:** Corrected invoice, updated PO, confirmation, or explanatory note

2. Return to normal flow at:
   - **Task 5: Review Invoice Against PO and Receipt**, if Purchasing must reconfirm; or
   - **Task 6: Match Invoice Line Items Against PO in System**, if Purchasing confirmation is already complete.

#### Path B: Exception not resolved
1. **Task:** Reject invoice  
   - **Role:** AP Supervisor / AP Clerk  
   - **Description:** Invoice is rejected if it cannot be validated or corrected.

2. **Task:** Notify Supplier of rejection  
   - **Role:** AP Clerk or AP Supervisor  
   - **Output:** Rejection notice

3. **End Event:** Invoice rejected

---

## 3. Matching and Manager Approval

### Task 6: Match Invoice Line Items Against PO in Accounting System
**Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk  
**System:** Accounting System  
**Description:** AP matches invoice line items against the PO in the accounting system after Purchasing has confirmed receipt and order accuracy.

**Input artifacts:**
- Logged invoice
- Purchase order
- Purchasing confirmation
- Corrected invoice or clarification, if applicable

---

### Gateway 4: Does AP line-item match succeed?

#### Path A: Match succeeds
Proceed to **Task 7: Trigger Manager Approval Request**

#### Path B: Match fails
1. **Task:** Email Purchasing to clarify mismatch  
   - **Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk  
   - **Recipient:** Purchasing Officer

2. Proceed through discrepancy resolution path:
   - **Task 5F: Coordinate Exception Resolution**, if unresolved or complex
   - Otherwise return to **Task 6: Match Invoice Line Items Against PO in Accounting System**

---

### Task 7: Trigger Manager Approval Request
**Role:** Accounting System  
**Description:** Once receipt is confirmed and the invoice matches the PO, the system automatically sends an approval notification to the Department Manager.

**Input artifacts:**
- Invoice details
- PO details
- Purchasing confirmation
- Matching result

**Output:** Manager approval request notification

---

### Task 8: Review Invoice for Final Approval
**Role:** Department Manager  
**System:** Accounting System  
**Description:** Department Manager reviews:
- Invoice details
- Purchase order
- Purchasing confirmation
- Quantities
- Pricing
- Special notes
- Budget or total amount concerns

---

### Gateway 5: Does Manager approve the invoice?

#### Path A: Manager approves
1. **Task:** Approve invoice in system  
   - **Role:** Department Manager  
   - **Output:** Approved invoice

2. Proceed to **Task 9: Schedule Invoice for Payment**

#### Path B: Manager rejects or requests investigation
1. **Task:** Reject invoice or request hold/investigation  
   - **Role:** Department Manager  
   - **Description:** Used when the manager sees suspicious items, large budget differences, unexpected items, or other concerns.

2. **Gateway 5A: Manager action type**

   - **Reject outright**
     - **Task:** Notify AP of rejection  
       - **Role:** Accounting System / Department Manager
     - **Task:** Notify Supplier if needed  
       - **Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk
     - **End Event:** Invoice rejected

   - **Request hold and investigation**
     - **Task:** Place invoice on hold  
       - **Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk
     - **Task:** Investigate manager concern  
       - **Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk  
       - **Supporting roles:** Purchasing Officer, AP Supervisor, Supplier Relationship Manager, Supplier, Department Head if needed

3. **Gateway 5B: Is manager concern resolved?**

   - **Yes:** Return to **Task 7: Trigger Manager Approval Request** or **Task 8: Review Invoice for Final Approval**
   - **No:** Proceed to **Reject invoice** and end process

---

## 4. Payment Scheduling

### Task 9: Schedule Invoice for Payment
**Role:** Accounts Payable Clerk / Accounts Payable Team  
**System:** Accounting System  
**Description:** After manager approval, AP schedules the invoice for the next payment cycle or payment run.

**Input:** Approved invoice  
**Output:** Invoice scheduled for payment

---

### End Event
**End:** Invoice approved and scheduled for payment

---

# 5. Key Artifacts / Data Objects

| Artifact / Data Object | Description |
|---|---|
| Supplier Invoice | Original invoice received from Supplier |
| Purchase Order | Required reference document for validation |
| PO Number | Must be present and valid before normal processing continues |
| Accounting System Invoice Record | Logged invoice in accounting system |
| Receipt / Goods or Services Confirmation | Confirmation that ordered goods/services were received |
| Purchasing Confirmation Email | Confirmation from Purchasing that invoice aligns with PO and receipt |
| Corrected Invoice | Revised invoice from Supplier if discrepancy exists |
| Contract Terms | Used by Supplier Relationship Manager for pricing discrepancies |
| Manager Approval Notification | Automatic system-generated request for Department Manager approval |
| Approved Invoice | Invoice approved by Department Manager |
| Rejection Notice | Communication to Supplier when invoice is rejected |
| Payment Schedule / Payment Run Entry | Output after AP schedules invoice for payment |

# 6. Exceptions and Variations

## Exception 1: Missing PO Number
- AP requests missing PO information from Supplier.
- Purchasing may refuse to proceed without confirmed PO reference.
- Supplier Relationship Manager may contact Supplier for the PO number or revised invoice.
- If unresolved, AP Supervisor investigates.
- In some cases, a retrospective PO may be created for informally contracted services.
- If no valid PO or retrospective PO can be established, invoice is rejected.

## Exception 2: Invoice Does Not Match PO
Examples:
- Incorrect pricing
- Extra units billed
- Missing items
- Unexpected delivery charges

Resolution options:
- Purchasing contacts Supplier directly.
- AP places invoice on hold pending corrected invoice.
- Supplier Relationship Manager checks contract terms and contacts Supplier.
- AP Supervisor coordinates unresolved or complex cases.

If resolved, the process returns to matching and confirmation.  
If unresolved, invoice is rejected.

## Exception 3: Manager Concern During Approval
Examples:
- Suspicious items
- Large difference from budget
- Unexpected items
- Special notes requiring investigation

Possible outcomes:
- Manager approves after clarification.
- Manager rejects invoice.
- Manager asks AP to hold and investigate.
- If investigation resolves the concern, invoice returns for manager approval.
- If not resolved, invoice is rejected.

## Exception 4: Unclear Ordering Between Purchasing Confirmation and AP Matching
There is a slight ambiguity in the interviews:
- Mary states that Purchasing first confirms goods/services were received, then AP matches invoice line items against the PO.
- Dan describes Purchasing itself comparing invoice details to the PO before confirming back to AP.

A consolidated model treats Purchasing as performing an initial PO/receipt review, followed by AP performing the formal system-based invoice-to-PO match before manager approval.

# 7. Compact BPMN-style Flow

```text
Start: Invoice received from Supplier
  -> AP Clerk: Check invoice for valid PO number
  -> Gateway: Valid PO number?

    No:
      -> AP Clerk: Request PO details / revised invoice from Supplier
      -> Gateway: Valid PO provided?
          Yes -> AP Clerk: Check invoice for valid PO number
          No  -> AP Supervisor: Investigate missing PO / informal service
                -> Gateway: Retrospective PO or validation possible?
                    Yes -> AP Supervisor/Department Head: Create or confirm retrospective PO
                           -> AP Clerk: Check invoice for valid PO number
                    No  -> AP Clerk/AP Supervisor: Reject invoice
                           -> End: Invoice rejected

    Yes:
      -> AP Clerk: Log invoice in accounting system
      -> AP Clerk: Forward invoice to Purchasing
      -> Purchasing: Confirm receipt and compare invoice to PO
      -> Gateway: Invoice matches PO and receipt?

          No:
            -> Purchasing/AP/Supplier Relationship Manager: Resolve discrepancy with Supplier
            -> Gateway: Discrepancy resolved?
                Yes -> Purchasing: Send confirmation to AP
                No  -> AP Clerk: Escalate to AP Supervisor
                      -> AP Supervisor: Coordinate exception resolution
                      -> Gateway: Exception resolved?
                          Yes -> Purchasing: Confirm / AP: Continue matching
                          No  -> AP Clerk/AP Supervisor: Reject invoice
                                 -> End: Invoice rejected

          Yes:
            -> Purchasing: Send confirmation to AP

      -> AP Clerk: Match invoice line items against PO in system
      -> Gateway: AP match successful?

          No:
            -> AP Clerk: Email Purchasing to clarify
            -> Purchasing/AP Supervisor/SRM: Resolve mismatch
            -> Gateway: Mismatch resolved?
                Yes -> AP Clerk: Match invoice line items against PO in system
                No  -> AP Clerk/AP Supervisor: Reject invoice
                       -> End: Invoice rejected

          Yes:
            -> Accounting System: Send manager approval notification
            -> Department Manager: Review invoice, PO, confirmation, and details
            -> Gateway: Manager approves?

                No:
                  -> Gateway: Reject or investigate?
                      Reject:
                        -> Department Manager/System: Reject invoice
                        -> AP Clerk: Notify Supplier if required
                        -> End: Invoice rejected
                      Investigate:
                        -> AP Clerk: Place invoice on hold
                        -> AP/Purchasing/AP Supervisor/SRM: Investigate concern
                        -> Gateway: Concern resolved?
                            Yes -> Accounting System: Send manager approval notification
                            No  -> AP Clerk/AP Supervisor: Reject invoice
                                   -> End: Invoice rejected

                Yes:
                  -> Department Manager: Approve invoice in system
                  -> AP Clerk/AP Team: Schedule invoice for payment
                  -> End: Invoice approved and scheduled for payment
```
