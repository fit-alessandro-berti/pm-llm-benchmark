# Comprehensive Strategy for Optimizing E-Commerce Fulfillment Operations

## 1. Identifying Instance-Spanning Constraints and Their Impact

### Shared Packing Stations (Cold-Packing)
- **Analysis Method:** Use resource profiles from process mining to analyze the utilization of each packing station, with a focus on cold-packing stations.
- **Impact Metric:** Waiting time due to resource contention at cold-packing stations.
- **Data Utilization:** Filter event log for "Packing" activities where "Requires Cold Packing" is TRUE. Calculate the difference between the start time of the activity and the previous activity's completion time to determine waiting time.

### Shipping Batches
- **Analysis Method:** Analyze the time between when an order is ready for shipping label generation and when the batch is processed.
- **Impact Metric:** Batch wait time, calculated as the difference between the completion time of the last activity before shipping label generation and the actual start time of shipping label generation.
- **Data Utilization:** Group orders by "Destination Region" and track the time each order waits for batch completion.

### Priority Handling for Express Orders
- **Analysis Method:** Analyze the sequence of activities to identify where standard orders get paused when express orders come in.
- **Impact Metric:** Delay caused to standard orders by express orders, measured by the difference in processing times when express orders are present versus absent.
- **Data Utilization:** Compare the processing times of standard orders when express orders are being processed simultaneously versus when they are not.

### Regulatory Limits on Hazardous Materials
- **Analysis Method:** Track the number of hazardous orders in packing or quality check at any given time to identify bottlenecks when the limit of 10 is reached.
- **Impact Metric:** Throughput reduction due to hazardous material limits, measured by the number of orders delayed when the limit is reached.
- **Data Utilization:** Monitor the number of orders with "Hazardous Material" flagged as TRUE in the "Packing" and "Quality Check" activities.

### Differentiating Waiting Time
- **Within-Instance Factors:** Track the time between the completion of one activity and the start of the next for the same order. If the resource is free but the order still waits, it might be due to within-instance factors.
- **Between-Instance Factors:** If there's a gap and the resource is busy with another order, that's between-instance.

### Batching and Hazardous Materials
- **Interaction:** If multiple hazardous orders are going to the same region, batching them might hit the hazardous material limit, causing delays.
- **Impact:** Delays in processing hazardous orders due to regulatory limits, affecting overall throughput.
