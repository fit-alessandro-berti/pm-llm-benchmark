### Rationale for Added Constraints

The updated `declare_model` introduces specific activities (`CheckApplicantRace`, `BiasMitigationCheck`, `ManualReview`) to explicitly model the handling of sensitive attributes within the loan application process. The new constraints serve the following purpose:

1.  **Non-succession Constraint:** The entry in `"nonsuccession"` ensures that a decision (`FinalDecision`), such as an automatic Reject, cannot immediately follow a check for sensitive attributes (`CheckApplicantRace`). This prevents the model from making decisions based solely on race or other protected characteristics without intervention.
2.  **Coexistence Constraint:** The entry in `"coexistence"` mandates that if `CheckApplicantRace` occurs, `ManualReview` must also occur. This ensures that any potential bias identified by the race check is subject to human oversight before a final decision is made.
3.  **Precedence and Succession Constraints:** By linking `BiasMitigationCheck` to `FinalDecision`, we enforce a flow where mitigation checks happen before decisions are finalized, ensuring fairness consistency across all applicant demographics.

These constraints collectively limit the process's bias by enforcing a mandatory review step whenever sensitive data is accessed, preventing direct automated decision-making based on protected attributes.
