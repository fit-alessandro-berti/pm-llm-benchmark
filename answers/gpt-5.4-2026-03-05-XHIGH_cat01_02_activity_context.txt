## 3) Brief justification of naming choices

- **Verify Documents**
  - Renamed to **Initial Identity Verification** when it is the first verification step after request initiation.
  - Renamed to **Re-verification ...** when it happens after a corrective action:
    - after **Submit Additional** in C001
    - after **Adjust Account** in C003
  - In C003, I included **Physical ID** because the resource/context clearly indicates in-person branch verification.

- **Call Customer**
  - Renamed by purpose, inferred from the surrounding events and context notes:
    - **Request Missing Documents** in C001 because it is followed by customer document upload.
    - **Clarify Account Discrepancy** in C002 because it precedes account correction.
    - **Confirm Account Adjustment** in C002 because it occurs after the adjustment.
    - **Ask to Return to Branch** in C003 because the customer had stepped out.

- **Other activities left unchanged**
  - I kept labels like **Initiate Request**, **Adjust Account**, **Submit Additional**, and **Approve Request** unchanged in the main revision because the strongest ambiguity was in the repeated within-case activities.
  - If desired, these could also be further specialized, e.g.:
    - `Initiate Request - Online Portal`
    - `Initiate Request - Branch Walk-In`
    - `Adjust Account - Discrepancy Correction`
    - `Adjust Account - Missing-Info Correction`
