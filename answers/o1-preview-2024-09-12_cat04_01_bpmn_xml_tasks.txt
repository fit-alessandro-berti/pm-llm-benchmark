Yes, the tasks in the provided BPMN 2.0 process model are:

1. **decide**
2. **pay compensation**
3. **check ticket**
4. **examine casually**
5. **register request**
6. **reject request**
7. **examine thoroughly**
8. **reinitiate request**

These tasks are represented by the `<bpmn:task>` elements within the BPMN XML definition and are the activities performed in the process.
