#### **Case 1002**

1. **Issue Purchase Order Before Approval of Purchase Requisition**  
   - *Observed:* PO issued at 2024-02-01 08:40, but PR approval occurs at 2024-02-03 08:00 (after PO issuance and even after payment).

2. **Release Payment Before Goods Receipt and Matching**  
   - *Observed:* Payment released on 2024-02-03 07:15, but goods are received on 2024-02-04 and invoice matching occurs on 2024-02-05.  
   - *Issue:*  
     - Paying before goods receipt risks financial loss if goods are never delivered or are substandard.

3. **Invoice Received Before Goods Receipt**  
   - *Observed:* Invoice received on 2024-02-03 07:00, goods received on 2024-02-04.  
   - *Issue:* While not always problematic, receiving an invoice before goods receipt may indicate a lack of three-way matching (PO, goods receipt, invoice), increasing the risk of erroneous payments.

4. **Late Approval of Purchase Requisition**  
   - *Observed:* PR approval occurs after PO issuance, invoice receipt, and payment.  
   - *Issue:* Retroactive approval defeats the purpose of control mechanisms and may indicate process breakdowns or fraudulent behavior.
