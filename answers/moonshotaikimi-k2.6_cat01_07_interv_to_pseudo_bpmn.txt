### **2. Process Flow (Sequence & Gateways)**

#### **Start Event**
- **[Start]** Invoice received by AP Clerk *(typically via email)*

---

#### **Phase 1: PO Validation & Logging**
- **[User Task]** AP Clerk: Check invoice for valid Purchase Order (PO) number
  - **[XOR Gateway]** PO present and valid?
    - **No**  **[User Task]** AP Clerk: Send invoice back to Supplier requesting PO details / missing information  
       *(Message flow to Supplier)*  
       **[Intermediate Event]** Await revised invoice or PO details from Supplier  
       *(Loop back to: Check invoice for valid PO number)*  
      - *Exception variation:* If the invoice lacks a PO because the service was contracted informally, AP Clerk escalates to AP Supervisor instead of returning to Supplier *(see Exception Handler A below)*.
    - **Yes**  **[User Task]** AP Clerk: Log invoice into accounting system  
       **[User Task]** AP Clerk: Forward invoice to Purchasing for receipt confirmation

---

#### **Phase 2: Purchasing Confirmation**
- **[User Task]** Purchasing Officer: Confirm goods/services received *(compare invoice line items to PO: quantities, prices, delivery charges)*
  - **[XOR Gateway]** Invoice details correct and aligned with PO?
    - **No (Discrepancy)**   
      - **[User Task]** Purchasing Officer: Contact Supplier directly **OR** request AP Clerk to hold invoice until corrected version is received  
        *(Ambiguity: Interview accounts differ on whether Purchasing contacts the Supplier directly, asks AP to hold, or both; treated as alternative branches)*  
      - **[User Task]** *(Optional, if looped in)* Supplier Relationship Manager: Verify contract terms and facilitate supplier correction  
       *(Message flow to Supplier)*  
       **[Intermediate Event]** Await corrected invoice or clarification  
       *(Loop back to: Purchasing Officer confirms goods/services received)*
    - **Yes**  **[User Task]** Purchasing Officer: Send confirmation email to AP Clerk

---

#### **Phase 3: Matching & Exception Handling**
- **[User Task]** AP Clerk: Match invoice line items against PO in accounting system
  - **[XOR Gateway]** Match successful?
    - **No (Discrepancy)**  **[User Task]** AP Clerk: Email Purchasing to clarify discrepancy
      - **[XOR Gateway]** Can discrepancy be resolved easily?
        - **Yes**  *(Loop back to: AP Clerk matches invoice line items after clarification)*
        - **No**  **[User Task]** AP Clerk: Escalate to AP Supervisor  
           *(Sequence flow to Exception Handler B)*
    - **Yes**  **[Service Task]** System: Send automatic approval request email to Department Manager  
       *(Data object attached: Invoice, PO, Purchasing confirmation)*

---

#### **Phase 4: Managerial Approval**
- **[User Task]** Department Manager: Review invoice details, PO, and Purchasing confirmation
  - **[XOR Gateway]** Approve?
    - **No (Suspicious / Over budget / Unexpected items)**  **[User Task]** Department Manager: Reject invoice or request hold and further investigation  
       **[User Task]** AP Clerk: Hold invoice and investigate further  
       *(Loop back to an earlier step; exact re-entry point ambiguous—likely returns to AP Matching or AP Supervisor escalation depending on the issue found)*
    - **Yes**  **[User Task]** Department Manager: Approve in system  
       *(Sequence flow returns to AP Clerk)*

---

#### **Phase 5: Payment Scheduling**
- **[User Task]** AP Clerk: Schedule invoice for payment *(add to next payment cycle / payment run)*
- **[End Event]** Payment scheduled

### **3. Exception Handlers (Sub-processes / Off-page Connectors)**

#### **Exception Handler A: Missing PO / Informal Services**
- *Triggered by:* AP Supervisor (when AP Clerk escalates or when no PO exists for informally contracted services)
- **[User Task]** AP Supervisor: Coordinate with department head to create retrospective PO **OR** reject invoice
  - **[XOR Gateway]** Resolution path?
    - **Create retrospective PO**  Invoice re-enters normal flow *(likely at Purchasing confirmation or AP Matching; exact re-entry point not specified)*
    - **Reject invoice**  Process terminates for this invoice *(or returns to Supplier)*
- **[User Task]** *(If involved)* Supplier Relationship Manager: Check contract and communicate correct terms to AP / Purchasing

#### **Exception Handler B: Unresolvable Discrepancy**
- *Triggered by:* AP Clerk escalation after matching failure
- **[User Task]** AP Supervisor: Resolve exception *(contact suppliers, coordinate between AP and Purchasing)*
- **[User Task]** *(If involved)* Supplier Relationship Manager: Verify contract pricing and advise on correct terms
- Once resolved  Invoice returns to normal flow at **[AP Clerk: Match invoice line items]** or **[Purchasing Officer: Confirm goods/services received]** *(Ambiguity: exact re-entry point varies by case)*
