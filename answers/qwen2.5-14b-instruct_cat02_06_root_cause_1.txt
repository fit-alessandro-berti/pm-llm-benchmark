#### 2. Potential Root Causes of Performance Issues

- **Escalations:**  
  - Cases 102 and 105 both required escalations to Level-2 Agents, which takes additional time and resources.
  
- **Long Waiting Times Between Activities:**  
  - There are notable gaps between activities in all cases, but particularly in Cases 102 and 105. For example, in Case 102, the gap between "Escalate to Level-2 Agent" and "Investigate Issue" is 6 hours, and in Case 105, it is 24 hours. These gaps suggest inefficiencies or bottlenecks in the workflow.
  
- **Unnecessary Delays Before Investigation and Resolution Steps:**  
  - Cases 102 and 105 had delays right after triaging and assigning, which may indicate inefficiencies in the initial stages of the ticket handling process or potential misrouting of tickets.

#### 3. Insights and Recommendations to Address Performance Bottlenecks

- **Automate Escalation Processes:**  
  - Automate the process of escalating tickets to Level-2 Agents based on predefined criteria to minimize delays. This could involve setting up rules that trigger escalations automatically when Level-1 Agents cannot resolve issues within a certain timeframe.

- **Implement Real-Time Monitoring:**  
  - Use real-time monitoring tools to track the progress of tickets and identify delays promptly. This allows for timely intervention and can help in reallocating resources when necessary.

- **Streamline Initial Handling and Assignment:**  
  - Streamline the initial triage and assignment process to ensure that tickets are directed to the appropriate agents without unnecessary delays. Implementing a more efficient initial assessment protocol can help in routing tickets to the correct level of support more quickly.

- **Increase Level-2 Support Capacity:**  
  - If the primary cause of delays is an overload of Level-2 Agents, consider increasing the team size or outsourcing tasks to specialized support centers. This can help in reducing the time taken to resolve escalated tickets.

- **Enhance Communication Between Teams:**  
  - Improve communication and coordination between Level-1 and Level-2 support teams. Clearer and more effective communication can help in reducing misunderstandings and delays during the handover process.

By addressing these areas, the overall efficiency and responsiveness of the customer support ticket resolution process can be significantly improved, leading to better customer satisfaction and reduced resolution times.
