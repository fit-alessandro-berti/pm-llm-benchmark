### Rationale for the added constraints

- **`existence` for `ManualReview` and `BiasMitigationCheck`**: ensures fairness-related checks are part of the modeled process.
- **`responded_existence` from sensitive attribute checks to `ManualReview`**: whenever a sensitive attribute is considered, a manual review must also occur.
- **`coexistence` between sensitive checks and `ManualReview`**: guarantees that traces involving sensitive attribute inspection also include human oversight.
- **`response` and `succession` from sensitive checks to `BiasMitigationCheck`**: require a bias mitigation step after sensitive attributes are checked.
- **`coexistence` and `response` involving `BiasMitigationCheck` and `FinalDecision`/`ManualReview`**: ensure the final decision is made only after mitigation and review.
- **`precedence` for `FinalDecision` requiring `BiasMitigationCheck`**: prevents a final decision from being made without fairness review first.
- **`noncoexistence`, `nonsuccession`, and `nonchainsuccession` from sensitive checks to `Reject`**: reduce the risk of immediate or direct rejection following sensitive-attribute inspection.

These constraints do not “prove” fairness, but they do enforce process structure that makes biased direct decision paths harder to encode or mine from the data.
