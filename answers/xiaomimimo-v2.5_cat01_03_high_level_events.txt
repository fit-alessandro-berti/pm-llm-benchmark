### **Justification Summary**
The groupings follow a **natural manufacturing workflow progression**:
1.  **Prepare** the raw component (`Material Preparation`).
2.  **Perform** the core fabrication (`Assembly`).
3.  **Treat** the assembled part for durability/aesthetics (`Post-Assembly Treatment`).
4.  **Verify** the critical technical specification (`In-Line Quality Verification`).
5.  **Approve** the final product (`Final Approval`).

This structure moves from input preparation, through transformation and finishing, to multiple stages of quality assurance (one automated/technical, one holistic/human). It separates activities by **objective** (setup vs. build vs. finish vs. check) and **resource type** (operators for prep/assembly, machines for treatment, sensors for data, different operators for final QC).
