### 4. Value of Aggregation

Moving from the low-level log to these high-level steps offers the following benefits for process management:

1.  **Process Mining Efficiency:** Analyzing 4 steps is significantly faster and less noisy than analyzing 14 granular events per case.
2.  **Bottleneck Identification:** If delays occur, management can now pinpoint exactly which *stage* is slow (e.g., is "Surface Finishing" taking too long?) rather than hunting for specific timestamps in a raw log.
3.  **Standardization:** If a new operator or machine is introduced, as long as they perform the same high-level activities (e.g., "Welding"), the process flow remains consistent in the model.
4.  **KPI Calculation:** It becomes easier to calculate cycle times per stage (e.g., "Average Time in Material Preparation" vs. "Average Time in Welding").
