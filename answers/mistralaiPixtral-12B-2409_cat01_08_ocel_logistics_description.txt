   - **Create Package** -> **Send Package**: Packages are created and subsequently sent.
   - **Send Package** -> **Package Delivered**: Sent packages are delivered.

2. **Stock Management**:
   - **Pick Item** -> **Item Out of Stock**: If items are picked and found to be out of stock, a reorder process is initiated.
   - **Item Out of Stock** -> **Reorder Item**: Items are reordered.
   - **Reorder Item** -> **Pick Item**: Reordered items are picked again.

3. **Payment**:
   - **Confirm Order** -> **Pay Order**: Orders are confirmed and then paid for.
   - **Package Delivered** -> **Pay Order**: Payment is required after the package is delivered.
   - **Pay Order** -> **Pick Item**: Payment may also trigger the picking of items.

   - **Package Delivered** -> **Create Package**: Sometimes, packages are created again after delivery.
