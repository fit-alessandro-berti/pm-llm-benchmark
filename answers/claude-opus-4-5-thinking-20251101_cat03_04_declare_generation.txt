## Key Constraint Explanations:

| Constraint Type | Rule | Business Rationale |
|----------------|------|-------------------|
| **init** | IG starts process | Every product begins with ideation |
| **existence** | Core activities required | Complete process needs all steps |
| **exactly_one** | IG, AG, FL once | Single idea, approval, and launch per product |
| **precedence** | TFCPC, CEAG, LT/UTAG | Can't prototype without feasibility; can't approve without cost/test results |
| **chainsuccession** | MPFL | Marketing plan directly precedes launch |
| **nonsuccession** | FLIG forbidden | Process ends at launch |
| **coexistence** | LTUT | Both testing types must occur together |
