#### 2. **Welding and Assembly**

#### 3. **Quality Assurance (QA)**
- **Events Included:**
  - Measure weld integrity
  - Apply protective coating
  - Dry coating
- **Justification:**

  - The measurement of weld integrity is a quality check, while the coating and drying steps are part of the finishing process.

#### 4. **Final Inspection**
- **Events Included:**

- **Justification:**
