### How These Constraints Reduce Bias:
- **Prevent Direct Bias**: The `nonsuccession` constraints eliminate immediate transitions from sensitive data checks to decisions, closing a major pathway for discrimination.
- **Enforce Fairness Checks**: The `response` and `precedence` constraints guarantee that all sensitive cases undergo review, while the `chainsuccession` constraints ensure these reviews are timely and unavoidable.
- **Process Transparency**: By mandating structured sequences (e.g., `CheckRace  ManualReview  Decision`), the model becomes auditable for fairness, aligning with ethical AI principles in lending.

These additions collectively create a "bias firewall" in the process, where sensitive data cannot influence decisions without explicit, documented review.
