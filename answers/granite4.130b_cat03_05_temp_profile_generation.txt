### How to interpret the numbers

| Pair                | Avg. Time (s) |  Duration | StdDev (s) |  Variability |
|---------------------|---------------|------------|-------------|----------------|
| `('SS','OP')`       | 3600          | ~1h       | 1800        | ±30min |

- **Average time** reflects the *mean* elapsed seconds between the first occurrence of the first activity and the first occurrence of the second activity in any trace where they appear in order.
- **Standard deviation** captures typical fluctuations (e.g., supplier delays, inspection bottlenecks).
