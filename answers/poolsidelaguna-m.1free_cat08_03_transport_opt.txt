Cost: Fuel consumption, time duration, distance

## 2. Performance Analysis and Bottleneck Identification

### Key Performance Indicators

**Operational Efficiency KPIs:**
1. **On-Time Delivery Rate:** % of deliveries completed within customer time windows
2. **Service Time Variability:** Standard deviation of time spent per delivery stop
3. **Travel/Service Ratio:** Travel time ÷ Service time (target < 2.0)
4. **Vehicle Utilization:** Working hours ÷ Available hours per vehicle
5. **Failed Delivery Rate:** Failed attempts ÷ Total delivery attempts
6. **Fuel Efficiency:** Liters per 100km or per successful delivery

**Cost-Related KPIs:**
- **Maintenance Impact Hours:** Hours lost to breakdowns per 1000km
- **Overtime Percentage:** Hours beyond scheduled shift ÷ Total hours
- **Idle Time Ratio:** Non-productive time ÷ Total working time

**Calculation Methods:**
```sql
-- Example KPI Calculations from Event Log:
-- Travel Time per Stop
SELECT CASE_ID, 
       SUM(CASE WHEN Activity IN ('Travel') THEN Duration END) / 
       COUNT(DISTINCT Package_ID) AS Avg_Travel_Time_Per_Stop
FROM Events 
GROUP BY CASE_ID

-- On-Time Rate
SELECT COUNT(CASE WHEN Actual_Delivery_Time BETWEEN Window_Start AND Window_End THEN 1 END) * 100.0 / 
       COUNT(*) AS Ontime_Rate
FROM (SELECT e.*, c.Time_Window_Start, c.Time_Window_End
      FROM Events e JOIN Customers c ON e.Location = c.Address)
```

### Bottleneck Identification Techniques

**Statistical Analysis Methods:**
- **Control Flow Analysis:** Identify activities with longest waiting times or highest frequency of re-execution
- **Resource Performance Analysis:** Compare driver efficiency, vehicle reliability
- **Time-Based Pattern Analysis:** Peak delay periods, rush hour impacts

**Bottleneck Classification:**
1. **Systemic Bottlenecks:** Recurring delays at specific locations or times
2. **Resource Bottlenecks:** Specific drivers or vehicles consistently underperforming
3. **Process Bottlenecks:** Activities taking disproportionately long compared to expectation

**Impact Quantification:**
- **Delay Propagation Analysis:** How one late delivery affects subsequent stops
- **Cost Attribution:** Fuel waste, overtime costs, re-delivery expenses per bottleneck type
- **Capacity Utilization:** Vehicles/idle drivers during bottleneck periods
