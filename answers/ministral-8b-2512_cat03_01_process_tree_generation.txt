### Explanation of the Process Tree:
1. **Root (`Operator.SEQUENCE`)**:
   - `A` (Arrival)  `RG` (Registration)  `IA` (Intake Assessment)  `TI` (Triage Interview)  `TC` (Triage Classification).
   - Exclusive choice for initial diagnostic test (`CE` or `FBP`).
   - Loop for abnormal results (`SC`  `(TM or SPT)`  `RDT`).
   - Advanced steps (`AI` and `NC` in concurrency).
   - Final sequence (`FAS`  `DDI`).
   - Final concurrency (`BL`, `ICP`, `MRF`).

2. **Loop (`Operator.LOOP`)**:
   - The loop body (`SC`  `(TM or SPT)`  `RDT`) repeats until results are normal.
   - The `RDT` itself is an exclusive choice (`FCE` or `FBA`).

3. **Concurrency (`Operator.PARALLEL`)**:
   - `AI` and `NC` can be done in any order or simultaneously.
   - `BL`, `ICP`, and `MRF` are done in true concurrency.

### Key Notes:
- The `parent` attribute must be explicitly set for all nodes (except the root).
- The `children` list must be populated after setting the `parent` of each child.
- The loop (`Operator.LOOP`) is constructed such that the loop body (`loop_body`) is executed repeatedly until the loop exits (implicitly when results are normal).
