# **Process Model: Invoice Approval & Payment Scheduling Workflow**

## **1. Participants & Roles**
| Role | Primary Responsibilities |
|------|--------------------------|
| **Accounts Payable (AP) Clerk** | Receives invoices, validates PO presence, logs into system, routes to Purchasing, performs line-item matching, triggers manager approval, schedules payment. |
| **Purchasing Officer** | Verifies receipt of goods/services, cross-checks invoice against PO quantities/pricing, confirms accuracy or flags discrepancies, communicates with suppliers/AP. |
| **Department Manager** | Reviews matched invoice/PO data, validates against budget/contract terms, grants final approval or rejects/holds for investigation. |
| **AP Supervisor** | Handles escalations for unresolvable discrepancies, coordinates cross-departmental/supplier resolutions, authorizes retrospective POs or rejections. |
| **Supplier Relationship Manager** | Advisory/support role; verifies contractual terms, assists in supplier communication during pricing/PO disputes. |
| **External Supplier** | Provides original/corrected invoices, supplies missing PO numbers or pricing clarifications. |

---

## **2. Data Objects & Artifacts**
- **Invoice** (Initial email, corrected/revised versions)
- **Purchase Order (PO)** (System record, may be created retrospectively for informal contracts)
- **Accounting/ERP System** (Logs invoices, stores PO data, hosts matching & approval modules)
- **Notifications/Emails** (System auto-alerts for manager approval, confirmation emails, discrepancy/escalation messages)
- **Approval/Rejection Records** (System logs of manager sign-off or holds)

---

## **3. Process Flow (BPMN-Style Sequence)**
1. **[Start Event]** Invoice Received (via email)
2. **[Task]** Verify PO Association  *Role: AP Clerk*
3. **[Exclusive Gateway]** Valid PO Number Present?
   - **No**  `[Task]` Request PO/Details from Supplier  *Roles: AP Clerk / Supplier RM*  `[Task]` Receive Corrected Invoice  `[Sequence Flow]` Loop back to Step 2.
   - **Yes**  `[Task]` Log Invoice into Accounting System  *Role: AP Clerk*  `[Sequence Flow]` Proceed to Step 4.
4. **[Task]** Forward to Purchasing for Receipt Verification  *Role: AP Clerk*
5. **[Task]** Cross-Check Invoice against PO & Physical/Service Receipt  *Role: Purchasing Officer*
6. **[Exclusive Gateway]** Invoice Details Match PO & Receipt?
   - **No**  `[Task]` Flag Discrepancy & Contact Supplier/Request Correction  *Role: Purchasing Officer*  `[Task]` Hold Invoice Pending Correction  *Role: AP Clerk*  `[Sequence Flow]` Loop back to Step 5 upon receipt of corrected invoice.
   - **Yes**  `[Task]` Send Confirmation Email to AP  *Role: Purchasing Officer*  `[Sequence Flow]` Proceed to Step 7.
7. **[Task]** Systematic Line-Item Matching against PO  *Role: AP Clerk*
8. **[Exclusive Gateway]** Line Items Match System Records?
   - **No**  `[Task]` Request Clarification from Purchasing  *Role: AP Clerk*
     - `[Decision]` Resolved Easily?
       - **Yes**  `[Sequence Flow]` Loop back to Step 7.
       - **No**  `[Task]` Escalate to AP Supervisor  *Role: AP Clerk*  `[Task]` Investigate & Resolve Exception  *Role: AP Supervisor* (may involve Supplier RM or Dept Heads for retrospective PO or rejection)  `[Sequence Flow]` Resume flow at Step 5 or Step 7 depending on resolution.
   - **Yes**  `[Task]` Trigger Automatic Manager Approval Request  *System*  `[Sequence Flow]` Proceed to Step 9.
9. **[Task]** Review Invoice, PO, & Confirmation Details  *Role: Department Manager*
10. **[Exclusive Gateway]** Manager Approval Decision?
    - **Reject/Hold**  `[Task]` Flag for Investigation & Return to AP  *Role: Department Manager*  `[Sequence Flow]` Loop back to Step 7 or Step 4 for rework.
    - **Approve**  `[Task]` Record Approval in System  *Role: Department Manager*  `[Sequence Flow]` Proceed to Step 11.
11. **[Task]** Schedule Invoice for Payment Cycle  *Role: AP Clerk*
12. **[End Event]** Invoice Cleared for Payment

## **4. Gateways & Decision Logic**
| Gateway | Trigger Condition | Outgoing Paths |
|---------|-------------------|----------------|
| **G1: PO Validation** | AP Clerk checks for valid PO number | `No`  Supplier request loop<br>`Yes`  System logging & handoff to Purchasing |
| **G2: Receipt/PO Match** | Purchasing Officer compares invoice to PO & delivery | `No`  Discrepancy flag  Supplier correction loop<br>`Yes`  Confirmation to AP |
| **G3: System Line-Item Match** | AP Clerk matches invoice data to system PO | `No`  Clarification request  Easy resolution loop OR Supervisor escalation<br>`Yes`  Auto-trigger manager approval |
| **G4: Manager Approval** | Department Manager reviews matched data & budget alignment | `Reject/Hold`  Return to AP for investigation<br>`Approve`  Payment scheduling |

---

## **5. Exceptions & Alternative Paths**
- **Missing PO Number:** Routed to supplier for details; may require Supplier Relationship Manager intervention to expedite.
- **Pricing/Quantity Discrepancy:** Purchasing contacts supplier directly or instructs AP to hold invoice. Corrected invoice re-enters verification loop.
- **Unresolvable Matching Issues:** Escalated to AP Supervisor. Supervisor coordinates with departments/suppliers. May result in retrospective PO creation (for informal contracts) or invoice rejection.
- **Manager Rejection:** Invoice returned to AP. AP determines whether to re-route to Purchasing for re-verification or to Supplier for correction.
- **Informal/Uncontracted Services:** Handled exclusively via Supervisor escalation path before normal flow can resume.
