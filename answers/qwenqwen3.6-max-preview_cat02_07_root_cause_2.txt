### 3. Explanations & Mitigation Strategies

####  Why These Attributes Cause Delays
1. **Reactive Document Collection:** The process evaluates claims first, then requests missing documents one batch at a time. High-complexity claims inherently require more documentation, but the current workflow handles this iteratively rather than proactively.
2. **Customer Response Latency:** Each `Request Additional Documents` event introduces a waiting period (likely 19–30 hours per cycle). Multiple cycles multiply this delay.
3. **Lack of Complexity-Based Routing/Checklists:** All claims appear to enter the same evaluation funnel. High-complexity claims aren't flagged upfront for enhanced validation or specialized handling, causing adjusters to discover missing documents sequentially.

####  Recommended Mitigations
| Root Cause | Mitigation Strategy | Expected Impact |
|------------|---------------------|-----------------|
| Iterative document requests | **Implement complexity-driven submission checklists.** Require mandatory document uploads at submission based on claim type/complexity. Use automated validation to reject incomplete submissions instantly. | Eliminates 1–2 request cycles per high-complexity case. |
| Sequential waiting periods | **Batch all missing documents in a single request.** Train adjusters to perform a comprehensive initial review and send one consolidated request instead of multiple piecemeal requests. | Cuts external wait time by 50–70%. |
| Customer response latency | **Deploy a self-service customer portal with real-time upload & validation.** Add automated SMS/email reminders and SLA countdowns for pending documents. | Reduces average response time from ~24h to <6h. |
| Lack of specialized handling | **Create a "Complex Claims Fast-Track" team.** Route High-complexity claims to senior adjusters with dedicated capacity, proactive follow-up protocols, and authority to request documents concurrently. | Prevents queue congestion and reduces internal handoff delays. |
| Process visibility gaps | **Implement process mining dashboards with alerts.** Flag cases with >1 document request or >24h in "waiting for documents" status for managerial intervention. | Enables early escalation and continuous improvement tracking. |
