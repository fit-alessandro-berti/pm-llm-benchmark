->(
    RA,

        VF,
        ->(
            AE,

**Explanation of the structure:**

1. **Main sequence** (`->` operator):
   - `RA` (Receive Application) always happens first
   - Then `VF` (Validate Form)
   - Then `AE` (Assess Eligibility)
   - Finally `SC` (Send Confirmation) and `AC` (Archive Case) in sequence

2. **Optional loop for missing information** (`X` operator after AE):
   - **Option 1**: Directly proceed to `AD` (Approval Decision) - as in case 2
   - **Option 2**: Enter the missing information handling:
     - Loop (`*` operator) for `RMI` (Request Missing Info) with `tau` as exit
     - After exiting the loop, execute `VF` again (re-validation)
     - Then proceed to `AD`

This model explains all three cases:
- **Case 1**: RA  VF  AE  [RMI  VF]  AD  SC  AC  
  (Enters loop once, takes one RMI, exits, then VF and AD)
- **Case 2**: RA  VF  AE  AD  SC  AC  
  (Takes the direct option, no RMI)
- **Case 3**: RA  VF  AE  [RMI  RMI  VF]  AD  SC  AC  
  (Enters loop twice, takes two RMIs, exits, then VF and AD)

The model balances fitness (replays all traces), precision (doesn't allow behavior not seen in the log), and simplicity (uses minimal operators). The loop structure captures that RMI can repeat zero or more times, with VF always following the last RMI.
