1. **Documentation Review:**
   - Can you walk me through the specific types of documentation that are required from the landlord at the initial stage?
   - How do you handle cases where the landlord is unable to provide certain documents, such as outdated inspection reports?

2. **Property Details Recording:**
   - What specific details about the property are recorded in the central property database, and how is this information used later in the process?
   - Are there any automated tools or systems that assist in recording and verifying property details?

3. **Property Manager Assignment:**
   - What criteria are used to determine the best property manager for a new property?
   - How do you balance the workload among property managers to ensure efficient assignment?

4. **Introductory Meeting:**
   - What are the key topics discussed during the introductory call or meeting between the property manager and the landlord?
   - How do you ensure that both parties are aligned on expectations and special conditions?

5. **Property Inspection and Photography:**
   - What are the common issues that inspectors typically find, and how are these issues addressed?
   - How do you coordinate the scheduling of photographers and inspectors to ensure timely completion?

6. **Marketing and Listing:**
   - Can you describe the process of creating online listings and marketing materials for the property?
   - How do you determine the pricing strategy for the property based on market trends?

7. **Tenant Screening:**
   - What are the key criteria used in the tenant screening process?
   - How do you handle situations where multiple tenants are interested in the same property?

8. **Lease Finalization:**
   - What steps are involved in finalizing the lease agreement with the chosen tenant?
   - How do you ensure that the lease agreement complies with fair housing regulations?

9. **Quality Checks and Audits:**
   - What specific quality checks and internal audits are conducted after the property is activated?
   - How do you ensure that the property’s online listing and data entries are correctly integrated into the system?

10. **Exception Handling:**
    - Can you provide examples of exceptions that have occurred during the onboarding process and how they were handled?
    - How do you manage communication and coordination among regional offices, landlords, property managers, and support teams?

11. **Timing and Sequencing:**
    - What is the typical timeline for onboarding a new property from initial contact to activation?
    - Are there any critical milestones or deadlines that must be met during the onboarding process?

12. **Roles and Responsibilities:**
    - Can you clarify the roles and responsibilities of the property coordinator, property manager, compliance officer, and financial analyst in the onboarding process?
    - How do these roles interact and collaborate to ensure a smooth onboarding process?

13. **Communication and Tracking:**
    - What tools and methods do you use to track communication and progress throughout the onboarding process?
    - How do you ensure that all stakeholders are kept informed and up-to-date on the status of the onboarding process?

14. **Feedback and Improvement:**
    - How do you gather feedback from landlords and tenants about the onboarding process?
    - What steps do you take to continuously improve the onboarding process based on feedback and lessons learned?

These questions aim to provide a comprehensive understanding of the property onboarding process, including the roles, responsibilities, decision criteria, timing, and handling of exceptions.
