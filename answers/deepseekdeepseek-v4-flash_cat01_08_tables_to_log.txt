- The case-level attributes (customer ID, type, priority) are repeated in the details for each event to allow direct filtering; they could be stored as separate columns if desired.
- The "Additional Details" column aggregates relevant identifiers and descriptors. For a stricter column-based format, these could be split into multiple columns (e.g., Item ID, Quantity, Delivery ID, etc.), but the current format is concise and self-contained.
