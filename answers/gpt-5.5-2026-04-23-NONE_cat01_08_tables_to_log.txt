## Assumptions

- **Case ID** is `order_id`.
- Each **order line** becomes a separate **ōItem Pickedö** event because item-level timestamps and pickers are available.
- Each delivery record creates two events:
  - **ōOrder Dispatchedö**
  - **ōOrder Arrivedö**
- Each invoice creates one **ōInvoice Issuedö** event.
- Each payment creates one **ōPayment Receivedö** event, linked back to the order through `invoice_id`.
- Resource names and roles are enriched from the Resources table where available.
- Product descriptions and categories are added for item-picking events.
- Order `1004` has no delivery, invoice, or payment data in the provided tables, so its trace ends after item picking.
- Payment events do not have an explicit resource/actor in the Payments table, so the resource is left blank.
