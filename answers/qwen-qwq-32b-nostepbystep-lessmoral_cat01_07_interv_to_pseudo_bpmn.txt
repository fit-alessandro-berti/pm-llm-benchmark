**Artifacts/Inputs/Outputs:**  
- Invoice, Purchase Order (PO), mandatory),  
- Confirmation of Goods Receipt,  
- Matched PO/Invoice Data,  
- Manager’s Approval Decision,  
- Payment Scheduling

- **Roles:** AP Clerk (Mary)  

---

- Roles: AP Clerk  
- Description: Verify invoice includes valid PO number.

- **Yes  Proceed**.  
- **No ** *Task 2a: Request PO Information from Supplier*  
   - Roles: AP Clerk, Supplier  
   - Description: Send email to supplier requesting missing PO number.  
   - **End Event (until supplier resubmits invoice with valid PO).*  

---

- Roles: AP Clerk

- Roles: AP Clerk  Purchasing Officer (Dan)

- Roles: Purchasing Officer (Dan)  
- Description: Verify goods/services were received and align with PO.

- **No  Proceed.**  
- **Yes ** *Task 4a: Resolve Receipt/PO Discrepancies*  
   - Roles: Purchasing Officer, AP Clerk  
   - Description: Collaborate to resolve discrepancies (e.g., missing items). If unresolved:  
     - *Task 4b: Escalate to AP Supervisor (Rita)*  
       - Roles: AP Clerk  AP Supervisor (Rita)  
       - Rita coordinates with Supplier Manager (Karen) to contact supplier for corrections.  
     - *Task 4c: Recheck After Resolution*  Loop back to *Confirm Receipt* (Task 4).  

---

**Task 5:** *Match Invoice Line Items to PO*  
- Roles: AP Clerk  

**Decision 3:** *Line Items Match PO?*

- Roles: AP Clerk  Department Manager  

**Decision 4:** *Approved?*  
- **Yes  Proceed.**  
- **No ** *Task 6a: Address Manager’s Rejection*  
   - Roles: AP Clerk  
   - Description: Revise invoice details and reprocess from the step where discrepancy originated (e.g., re-match items or contact supplier).  

---

**Task 7:** *Schedule Payment*  
- Roles: AP Clerk  

**End Event:** Payment Processed

**Gateways/Decision Points:**  
- **Decision 1 (PO Check):** Mandatory for process initiation.  
- **Decision 2 (Receipt Confirmation):** Validates goods match PO.  
- **Decision 3 (Line Item Match):** Ensures invoice and PO align.  
- **Decision 4 (Manager’s Approval):** Final authorization point.

- **AP Clerk (Mary):** Primary executor for logging, matching, and coordination.
