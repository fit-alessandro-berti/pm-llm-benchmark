Here is a list of targeted, open-ended questions designed to clarify the property onboarding process, categorized by the specific area of the workflow they address.

### Initiation & Documentation Verification
1.  **Intake Channels:** You mentioned the landlord contacts the regional office to request services. Could you describe the specific mechanisms for this intake (e.g., web form, phone call, email), and is there a standardized triage process to prioritize certain leads over others?
2.  **Document Validity:** Regarding the compliance certificates and inspection reports, how do you determine the "expiration" or validity period of these documents? Is there a specific threshold for how "recent" a report must be to be accepted without re-inspection?
3.  **Data Entry Ownership:** While the coordinator records property details into the central database, is there a secondary verification step to ensure the accuracy of critical data points like square footage or unit count before they are used for marketing?

### Property Manager Assignment
4.  **Assignment Logic:** You listed several factors for assigning a property manager (workload, geography, experience). Is this assignment process manual, relying on a regional director's discretion, or is there a scoring system used to match managers to properties objectively?
5.  **Capacity Management:** How is a property manager's "current workload" defined and tracked? Is there a cap on the number of units or landlords a single manager can handle before they are considered at capacity?
6.  **Continuity:** If the assigned property manager leaves the company or goes on extended leave during the onboarding phase, what is the protocol for transitioning the landlord relationship to a new manager without restarting the process?

### Inspection, Repairs, & Marketing
7.  **Repair Authority:** When an inspector finds issues, you mentioned the manager works with the landlord to address them. Is there a predefined threshold of critical safety issues that *must* be fixed before onboarding can continue, versus cosmetic issues that can be addressed later?
8.  **Pricing Strategy:** You noted that the digital marketing team updates internal pricing models based on market trends. Who has the final sign-off authority on the listed rental price—the landlord, the property manager, or the financial analyst?
9.  **Marketing Timing:** Does the marketing material creation (photos, brochures) begin strictly after the inspection passes, or can some of this work run in parallel to save time?

### Tenant Screening & Lease Finalization
10. **Screening Criteria:** Are the tenant screening criteria (credit score, employment verification) standardized across the company, or does the property manager have the autonomy to adjust these requirements based on the landlord's preferences?
11. **Multiple Applicants:** In scenarios where multiple qualified tenants are interested, what is the hierarchy of decision-making? Does the landlord have the final say in tenant selection, or does the property manager make the recommendation based on company policy?
12. **Lease Customization:** You mentioned landlords sometimes insist on custom lease clauses. Is there a legal review process for these custom clauses, and do they require approval from the compliance officer before the lease is finalized?

### Audits, Exceptions, & Process Flow
13. **Audit Failure:** Regarding the final quality checks (compliance, financial, IT), what happens if a property fails one of these audits after it has already been "activated" in the portal? Is the listing immediately deactivated, or is there a grace period for remediation?
14. **Exception Handling:** Can you walk me through a specific example of a "special insurance" exception? How does the presence of special insurance requirements alter the standard onboarding workflow?
15. **Process Visibility:** With communication tracked across emails, CRMs, and project management tools, how do stakeholders currently gain a holistic view of where a specific property is in the onboarding pipeline? Is there a single source of truth for the status?
16. **Timeline Expectations:** What is the expected end-to-end turnaround time for a standard onboarding process, and what are the most common bottlenecks that cause delays beyond this timeframe?
