### BPMN-like Textual Process Model — **Invoice Approval & Payment Scheduling**

#### 1) Participants / Lanes
- **Supplier** (external)
- **Accounts Payable Clerk (AP Clerk / Mary)**
- **Purchasing Officer (Dan)**
- **Department Manager (Carlos)**
- **AP Supervisor (Rita)** (exception handling)
- **Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen)** (optional support in exceptions)
- **Accounting System**
- **Department Head(s)** (only when retrospective PO is needed)

---

#### 2) Key Data Objects / Artifacts
- **D1:** Supplier Invoice (initial)
- **D2:** Purchase Order (PO)
- **D3:** Goods/Services Receipt Confirmation
- **D4:** Purchasing Confirmation Email
- **D5:** Corrected/Revised Invoice
- **D6:** Supplier Contract Terms (used by Karen)
- **D7:** Retrospective PO (exception case)
- **D8:** Manager Approval/Rejection Record
- **D9:** Payment Run Schedule Entry

---

#### 3) Sequence Flow (BPMN-like)

**S1 (Start Event):** Invoice received by AP (usually email from Supplier, D1)  
**T1 (AP Clerk):** Check invoice for valid PO and required details  
**G1 (XOR):** PO present and valid?

- **If YES **
  - **T2 (AP Clerk):** Log invoice in accounting system
  - **T3 (AP Clerk):** Forward invoice to Purchasing
  - **T4 (Purchasing):** Verify goods/services receipt + compare invoice vs PO (qty/price/delivery)
  - **G2 (XOR):** Purchasing verification successful?

    - **If YES **
      - **T5 (Purchasing):** Send confirmation email to AP (D4)
      - **T6 (AP Clerk):** Perform AP-side line-item match vs PO in system
      - **G3 (XOR):** AP match successful?

        - **If YES **
          - **T7 (System):** Auto-send manager approval request (triggered after confirmation + match)
          - **T8 (Manager):** Review invoice + PO + confirmation (+ budget/context)
          - **G4 (XOR):** Manager decision?

            - **Approve **
              - **T9 (Manager):** Approve in system (D8)
              - **T10 (AP Clerk):** Schedule invoice for next payment cycle (D9)
              - **E1 (End Event):** Invoice approved and scheduled for payment

            - **Reject / Hold for investigation **
              - **T11 (AP Clerk + Purchasing; optional Karen/Rita):** Investigate manager concern
              - **G5 (XOR):** Issue resolved?

                - **Resolved ** return to **T7** (resubmit for manager sign-off)  
                - **Not resolved / final reject  E2**

        - **If NO ** go to discrepancy resolution path (**T12**)

    - **If NO ** go to discrepancy resolution path (**T12**)

- **If NO (from G1) **
  - **T13 (AP Clerk):** Request missing PO/details from Supplier; set invoice to hold
  - *(Optional)* **T14 (Karen):** Contact supplier to obtain PO/revised invoice
  - **G6 (XOR):** Missing info obtained?

    - **Yes ** loop back to **T1**
    - **No / unresolved **
      - **T15 (AP Clerk):** Escalate to AP Supervisor
      - **T16 (Rita + Dept Head(s)):** Resolve exception (supplier coordination; may create retrospective PO D7)
      - **G7 (XOR):** Exception outcome?

        - **Resolved (PO/revised invoice available) ** loop back to **T1**
        - **Reject invoice  E2**

---

#### 4) Shared Exception Subprocess (Discrepancy Path)
**T12 (AP/Purchasing):** Clarify discrepancy (pricing, qty, unexpected items, missing data); hold invoice  
- Purchasing may contact supplier directly **or** ask AP to hold until corrected invoice  
- Karen may be involved for contract/term validation (D6)  
- If unresolved, escalate to Rita for cross-functional resolution  
**G8 (XOR):** Resolved?

- **Yes ** return to appropriate re-check step:
  - usually **T4** (Purchasing re-verification) and then **T6** (AP re-match)
- **No  E2 (End):** Invoice rejected/closed, no payment

---

#### 5) End States
- **E1:** Approved + scheduled for payment run  
- **E2:** Rejected/closed (cannot proceed)

---

#### 6) Noted Ambiguities (captured explicitly)
1. **Who does detailed matching first:** Dan describes detailed check in Purchasing; Mary also describes AP line-item matching after Purchasing confirmation. Model includes **both checks**.
2. **Who contacts supplier during issues:** Could be Purchasing, AP, Supervisor, or Karen (relationship role). Modeled as variable/optional tasks in exception path.
3. **Missing PO handling:** Usually request supplier correction; for informal services, Rita may arrange **retrospective PO** or reject.
4. **Manager negative decision:** May be “hold and investigate” (loop) or direct rejection (end).
