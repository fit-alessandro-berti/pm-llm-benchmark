- **Automate approval workflows:** Implement rule-based approvals for routine high complexity cases to reduce manager involvement.

### **B. Improve Regional/Process Consistency**

### **C. Monitor and Analyze by Resource/Region/Complexity**
- **Track KPIs by these dimensions:** Use dashboards to monitor case duration by resource, region, and complexity in real time.
- **Root cause analysis:** When delays are detected, perform a focused review on the corresponding attributes (e.g., all high-complexity cases handled by CSR_Paul in Region B).
