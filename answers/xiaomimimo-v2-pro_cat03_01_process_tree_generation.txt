**Key design decisions:**

1. **Sequencing**: The main flow uses `->` to connect the 7 major phases
2. **Conditional testing**: `X` captures the exclusive choice between cardiac (CE) and general (FBP) diagnostics
3. **Loop structure**: `*` implements the repeat-until-normal loop with:
   - Body: Specialist consultation  treatment choice  retest choice
   - Redo: Silent step (tau) representing "check results and possibly repeat"
4. **Concurrency**: `+` for parallel execution of:
   - AI and NC (advanced services)
   - BL, ICP, and MRF (final administrative processes)
5. **Nested parallelism**: ICP and MRF are in their own parallel block within the larger final parallel block

This model captures all possible traces through the hospital process, including:
- Direct path (if initial tests are normal)
- Multiple iterations through the specialist loop
- Various combinations of parallel activities
- All possible sequences within concurrent blocks
