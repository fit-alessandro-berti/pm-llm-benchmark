*   **Impact:** This replaces the initial `XOR Gateway` with a more nuanced, predictive router. Standard requests can be fast-tracked. Likely-custom requests are immediately flagged for senior review or routed to a specialist team, eliminating a waiting step.

For requests identified as standard, we can automate and parallelize aggressively.
