### Rationale (what was added and why)

1. **`coexistence` + `response` (with `ManualReview`)**
   - Added: whenever **`CheckApplicantAge` / `CheckApplicantGender` / `CheckApplicantRace`** occurs, **`ManualReview`** must also occur (and eventually occurs).
   - Effect: reduces the chance that decisions are made solely based on sensitive attributes by requiring additional scrutiny.

2. **`precedence` (ManualReview before decisions)**
   - Added: **`ManualReview` must precede** key decision/outcome activities (**`Approve`, `Reject`, and `RequestAdditionalInfo`**).
   - Effect: prevents decision steps from occurring without the bias-mitigation gate being executed first.

3. **`nonsuccession` (block immediate transitions from sensitive checks to decisions)**
   - Added: disallow direct consecutive transitions:
     - `CheckApplicantAge`  `Approve/Reject`
     - `CheckApplicantGender`  `Approve/Reject`
     - `CheckApplicantRace`  `Approve/Reject`
   - Effect: blocks the most direct form of discrimination—instant decisioning immediately after sensitive attribute revelation—forcing at least some intervening process behavior (e.g., `ManualReview`).
