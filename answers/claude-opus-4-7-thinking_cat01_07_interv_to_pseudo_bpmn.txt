# BPMN-like Process Model: Invoice Approval Process

## 1. Participants (Pools/Lanes)

- **Accounts Payable (AP) Clerk** — Mary
- **Purchasing Officer** — Dan
- **Department Manager** — Carlos
- **AP Supervisor** — Rita (exception handler)
- **Supplier Relationship Manager** — Karen (exception handler, external liaison)
- **Supplier** (external participant)
- **Accounting / Approval System** (automated actor)

---

## 2. Artifacts / Data Objects

- **Invoice** (email attachment  system record)
- **Purchase Order (PO)**
- **Goods/Services Receipt Confirmation**
- **Approval Notification** (system-generated email)
- **Corrected/Revised Invoice** (supplier-issued)
- **Retrospective PO** (created for informal/contracted services)
- **Payment Schedule Entry**

---

## 3. Process Flow (Textual BPMN)

### Start Event
- **Trigger:** Invoice received via email from supplier.

### Sequence Flow

**T1. Receive Invoice** *(AP Clerk)*
- Input: Invoice (email)

**G1. Gateway (XOR) — "Is a valid PO number present?"**
- **No ** T2. Request PO / Details from Supplier *(AP Clerk, optionally via Supplier Relationship Manager)*
  - Loop: Await corrected invoice from supplier  return to T1.
- **Yes ** Continue to T3.

**T3. Log Invoice in Accounting System** *(AP Clerk)*
- Output: Invoice record in system

**T4. Forward Invoice to Purchasing for Receipt & Match Check** *(AP Clerk  Purchasing Officer)*

**T5. Verify Receipt of Goods/Services & Match Invoice to PO** *(Purchasing Officer)*
- Compares quantities, pricing, delivery charges against PO.

**G2. Gateway (XOR) — "Does invoice match the PO?"**
- **Match (Yes) ** T6. Send Confirmation to AP *(Purchasing Officer)*  proceed to T9.
- **Discrepancy (No) ** proceed to T7.

**T7. Resolve Discrepancy** *(Purchasing Officer  AP Clerk)*
- Contact supplier OR ask AP to hold invoice pending corrected version.
- Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen) may be looped in for:
  - Missing PO info
  - Pricing mismatch vs. contract

**G3. Gateway (XOR) — "Can the discrepancy be resolved at the clerk/purchasing level?"**
- **Yes ** Supplier issues corrected invoice / data clarified  return to T5 (rework loop).
- **No ** T8. Escalate to AP Supervisor.

**T8. Exception Handling by AP Supervisor** *(Rita)*
- Coordinates between AP, Purchasing, Supplier Relationship Manager.
- Sub-variant: If invoice has **no matching PO** (e.g., informal service contract):
  - **G3a. Gateway:** "Create retrospective PO or reject invoice?"
    - **Create Retrospective PO ** with Department Head  return to T5.
    - **Reject Invoice ** End Event: *Invoice Rejected*.
- Once resolved  re-enter normal flow at T5.

**T9. System Notifies Department Manager for Approval** *(System)*
- Automated email to manager with invoice + PO + confirmation.

**T10. Review and Decide on Invoice** *(Department Manager)*

**G4. Gateway (XOR) — "Manager's decision?"**
- **Approve ** T11.
- **Hold / Investigate ** return to T8 (AP Supervisor escalation) or loop back to AP Clerk for further review.
- **Reject ** End Event: *Invoice Rejected*.

**T11. Schedule Invoice for Payment Run** *(AP Clerk)*
- Output: Payment Schedule Entry.

### End Event
- **Invoice Approved & Scheduled for Payment** (happy path)
- **Invoice Rejected** (alternate terminating event)

---

## 4. Decision Points Summary

| Gateway | Condition | Outcomes |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| G1 | Valid PO number present? | Yes  log; No  return to supplier |
| G2 | Invoice matches PO? | Yes  forward for approval; No  resolve discrepancy |
| G3 | Discrepancy resolvable at AP/Purchasing level? | Yes  rework; No  escalate to Supervisor |
| G3a | (Exception) Create retrospective PO or reject? | Create PO  resume; Reject  end |
| G4 | Manager decision? | Approve  payment; Hold  re-investigate; Reject  end |

---

## 5. Exceptions and Rework Loops

1. **Missing PO number loop:** AP Clerk (or Karen) requests info from supplier  supplier resubmits  restart validation.
2. **Pricing/quantity discrepancy loop:** Purchasing + AP coordinate; corrected invoice triggers re-match (back to T5).
3. **Escalation path:** Persistent issues routed to AP Supervisor (Rita); she may engage department heads or create retrospective POs.
4. **Supplier contact loop:** Karen (Supplier Relationship Manager) handles supplier-facing communication when contract terms or pricing need verification.
5. **Manager-initiated hold:** Manager (Carlos) can push an invoice back into the investigation subprocess (~once every couple of months).
6. **No-PO / informal service invoices:** Handled via retrospective PO creation workflow by the AP Supervisor.

---

## 6. Noted Ambiguities

- **Routing after Purchasing confirmation:** Dan "assumes" Mary forwards it to the manager, but Mary's and Carlos's accounts suggest the **system** auto-notifies the manager. Modeled here as a **system-triggered** step (T9), consistent with Mary and Carlos.
- **Manager rejection outcome:** Carlos describes both "reject" and "ask Mary to hold and investigate." Modeled as two separate branches of G4.
- **Karen's (Supplier Relationship Manager) entry point:** Not formalized — she can be triggered from either T2 (missing PO) or T7 (discrepancy). Modeled as an ad-hoc supporting participant rather than a fixed sequence step.
- **Double-check after retrospective PO creation:** Rita implies the invoice re-enters at "confirmation from Purchasing" (T5); this is assumed but not explicitly confirmed by Dan.

---

## 7. Simplified Happy-Path Summary

```
Start  Receive Invoice  [PO valid?]  Log in System  Forward to Purchasing 
       Verify Receipt & Match  [Match OK?]  Confirm to AP 
       System Notifies Manager  Manager Approves 
       Schedule Payment  End
```

Deviations from this happy path route through supplier clarification, AP Supervisor escalation, or supplier relationship liaison as described above.
