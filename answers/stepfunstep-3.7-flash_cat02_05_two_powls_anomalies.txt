4. `Make_Hiring_Decision` (only after interviews, to base the decision on candidate evaluations)
5. `Onboard_Employee` (only if a hire is made, after the decision)

7. `Close_Case` (final step, after all hiring activities are complete)

All steps are mandatory, no unnecessary repetition is allowed, and core precedence constraints (e.g., interviews before decisions, screening before interviews) are non-negotiable.
