### How to read the dictionary

* **Unary keys** (`existence`, `absence`, `exactly_one`, `init`)  
  *`declare_model["existence"]["IG"]`*  `{'support': 0.92, 'confidence': 0.96}`  

* **Binary / higherorder keys** (`responded_existence`, `coexistence`, …)  
  *`declare_model["response"][("IG", "DD")]`*  `{'support': 0.90, 'confidence': 0.95}`  

* The special placeholder `"ANY"` in `nonsuccession` and `nonchainsuccession` is used to denote “any activity after …”.

Feel free to adjust the support / confidence numbers (or add/remove constraints) to match the actual statistics of your event log. The structure above will be accepted by **pm4py** as a valid DECLARE model.
