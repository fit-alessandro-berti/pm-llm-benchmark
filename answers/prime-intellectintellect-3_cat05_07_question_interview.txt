# Questions to Clarify the Property Onboarding Process

## Documentation and Verification
1. What specific criteria do you use to determine whether a property meets the company's standards for acceptance into your portfolio?
2. Could you walk me through the documentation verification process in more detail? What are the most common missing or outdated documents you encounter?
3. How do you communicate with property owners when additional documentation is needed, and what is the typical timeframe for receiving updated information?
4. Who has the final authority to approve or reject a property for inclusion in the portfolio?

## Role Assignments and Responsibilities
5. How do you determine which property manager is best suited for a particular property and landlord?
6. What training or resources does your company provide to property managers before they take on a new property?
7. How do you ensure a balanced distribution of properties among your management team to prevent burnout or uneven workloads?
8. What happens if a property manager becomes unavailable or is reassigned during the onboarding process?

## Property Preparation and Marketing
9. At what point in the process do you schedule photography and inspections, and how do you determine when a property is ready for marketing?
10. How do you handle properties that fail inspection? What are the most common issues you encounter and how are they resolved?
11. Who determines the content and messaging used in marketing materials, and how much involvement does the property owner have in this process?
12. How do you adjust your marketing strategy for different property types (e.g., luxury units vs. student housing)?

## Tenant Placement Process
13. What criteria do you use to screen tenants, and how do you balance competing applications when multiple parties are interested in the same property?
14. How does the tenant placement process differ for short-term rentals versus long-term leases?
15. What negotiation points typically arise during the lease finalization stage, and how are these resolved?
16. How do you handle move-in logistics, and who is responsible for coordinating the actual move-in process?

## Quality Checks and Exception Handling
17. What are the most common reasons for non-compliance issues discovered during the quality check phase?
18. How do you ensure that the rental price aligns with both the company's profitability targets and current market conditions?
19. What mechanisms are in place for handling exceptions, such as properties requiring special insurance or landlords insisting on custom lease clauses?
20. How do you capture and address issues or opportunities for improvement identified during the onboarding process?

## Integration and Transition
21. How do you ensure a smooth handoff from the onboarding team to the ongoing management team once a tenant moves in?
22. What metrics or feedback loops are in place to evaluate the effectiveness of the onboarding process?
23. How does the company balance standardization of processes with flexibility to accommodate unique property or owner requirements?
24. What role do frontline staff play in suggesting improvements to the onboarding process?

## Additional Process Insights
25. How long does the entire onboarding process typically take from initial contact to when the property is fully operational with a tenant in place?
26. What communication channels and tools are primarily used to keep all stakeholders informed throughout the onboarding journey?
27. How has the onboarding process evolved in response to changing market conditions or technological advancements?
28. What constitutes a successful transition from the onboarding phase to the ongoing management phase?
