Here is a list of targeted, open-ended questions categorized by operational themes:

### 1. Decision Criteria & Rules
*   **Property Manager Assignment:** You mentioned that assigning a Property Manager (PM) depends on workload, geography, and experience. Is this matching process guided by a formalized scoring system/algorithm, or is it a manual decision made by a regional director? What specific threshold defines a PM's "maximum workload"?
*   **Pricing Reconciliation:** How do you reconcile discrepancies between the landlord's expected rental price, the PM's local market assessment, and the financial analyst's pricing model? Who has the final authority to set the listing price?
*   **Tenant Screening Standards:** What are the objective thresholds for tenant screening (e.g., minimum credit score, rent-to-income ratio)? If an applicant falls just short of a threshold but has excellent references, does the PM have the discretion to approve them, or is there a formal waiver process?

### 2. Timing, Sequencing, & Parallelism
*   **Audit Timing and Risks:** The description places compliance audits, financial analysis, and IT checks *after* the tenant is chosen and the lease finalized. What happens if a compliance officer or financial analyst flags an issue *after* the lease has already been signed? Are there any plans or gates to move these checks earlier in the process?
*   **Document Delays:** While waiting for a landlord to submit missing or outdated compliance documentation during the initial intake, is the onboarding process paused, or do you proceed with PM assignment and scheduling vendors in parallel? Is there an expiration timeframe after which an inactive onboarding request is canceled?

### 3. Roles, Responsibilities, & Authority Boundaries
*   **Maintenance & Repair Authority:** If an inspector identifies issues that require repairs, what is the PM's level of financial authority? Can they automatically dispatch a preferred contractor up to a certain dollar amount, or must the landlord approve every quote regardless of cost?
*   **Custom Negotiations:** When multiple tenants are interested and PMs negotiate lease terms (e.g., offering short-term discounts or pet policy exceptions), do these terms require approval from the landlord or a regional manager, or is the PM fully empowered to negotiate independently?

### 4. Exception Handling & "Happy Path" Off-Ramps
*   **Inspection Failures & Off-Ramping:** What is the protocol if a property repeatedly fails inspection, or if a landlord refuses to perform the safety repairs recommended by the inspector? At what point, and how, does the company off-board or reject a property?
*   **Custom Lease Clauses:** For landlords who insist on custom lease clauses, what is the review process? Is there an internal legal team that must draft or approve these variations, or do PMs use a library of pre-approved addenda?
*   **Special Insurance Verification:** How and when in the process is the need for "special insurance" flagged, and who is responsible for verifying that the landlord has secured this coverage before marketing the property?

### 5. Information Flow & Systems Integration
*   **Single Source of Truth:** Because communication is spread across email, CRMs, and project management tools, how do team members ensure they have the most up-to-date status of a property? If a landlord calls for an update, which system is considered the "single source of truth"?
*   **Handoff Protocols:** When a property transitions from the "Property Coordinator" (intake) to the "Property Manager" (execution), how is the knowledge about landlord preferences and documentation history transferred to avoid the landlord having to repeat information?
