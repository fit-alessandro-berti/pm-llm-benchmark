    - **Task:** *Send Confirmation to AP* (Dan  AP Clerk).
    - **Next:** **[4. Match Invoice Line Items]**.
  - ** No (Discrepancy Detected):**
    - ** Sub-Gateway: Can Issue Be Resolved with Supplier?**
      - ** Yes:**
        - **Task:** *Contact Supplier* (Dan **or** Karen).
          - **Action:** Request corrected invoice or missing PO.
          - **Output:** Supplier sends revised invoice.
          - **Loop:** Return to *Start Event* (reprocess corrected invoice).
      - ** No:**
        - **Task:** *Ask AP to Hold Invoice* (Dan  AP Clerk).
        - **Task:** *Escalate to AP Supervisor* (AP Clerk  Rita).
          - **Subprocess:** *Resolve Discrepancy* (see **Exception Paths**).

#### **4. Line Item Matching (AP Clerk)**
- **Task:** *Match Invoice Line Items to PO in System* (Mary).
  - **Action:** Verify all line items (quantities, prices) match the PO.
- ** Exclusive Gateway: All Line Items Match?**
  - ** Yes:**
    - **Next:** **[5. Request Manager Approval]**.
  - ** No:**
    - **Task:** *Email Purchasing for Clarification* (Mary  Dan).
    - ** Sub-Gateway: Discrepancy Resolved?**
      - ** Yes:** Proceed to **[5. Request Manager Approval]**.
      - ** No:**
        - **Task:** *Escalate to AP Supervisor* (Mary  Rita).
          - **Subprocess:** *Resolve Discrepancy* (see **Exception Paths**).

#### **5. Manager Approval**
- **Task:** *Request Manager Approval* (System):
  - **Action:** Automatic email sent to Department Manager (Carlos) with:
    - Invoice details.
    - PO.
    - Purchasing confirmation.
- **Task:** *Review and Approve/Reject* (Carlos):
  - **Actions:**
    - Review for anomalies (e.g., budget overruns, unexpected items).
    - Approve, reject, or request hold for investigation.
- ** Exclusive Gateway: Manager Approval Decision**
  - ** Approve:**
    - **Next:** **[6. Schedule Payment]**.
  - ** Reject/Hold:**
    - **Task:** *Investigate Further* (Carlos  AP Clerk/Rita).
      - **Loop:** Return to relevant step (e.g., *Validate Invoice Against PO* or *Match Line Items*).
      - **Resolution:** Once issues are addressed, resubmit for approval.

  - **Action:** Add invoice to the next payment run.
- **End Event:** *Invoice Approved and Scheduled for Payment*.

## ** Exception Paths & Subprocesses**

### **1. Handle Missing PO**
- **Trigger:** Invoice lacks a valid PO number.
- **Steps:**
  1. **AP Supervisor (Rita) Assesses Invoice:**
     - **Gateway:** *Can a retrospective PO be created?*
       - ** Yes:**
         - **Task:** *Create Retrospective PO* (Rita + Department Heads).
         - **Loop:** Return to **[1. Check for Valid PO]**.
       - ** No:**
         - **Task:** *Reject Invoice* (Rita).
         - **End Event:** *Invoice Rejected*.

### **2. Resolve Discrepancy**
- **Trigger:** Discrepancy found during Purchasing validation or AP matching.
- **Steps:**
  1. **AP Supervisor (Rita) Coordinates:**
     - **Task:** *Contact Supplier* (may involve Karen).
     - **Task:** *Liaise with Purchasing/AP* to clarify issues.
  2. **Gateway:** *Discrepancy Resolved?*
     - ** Yes:** Return to the step where discrepancy was detected (e.g., *Match Line Items*).
     - ** No:**
       - **Task:** *Reject Invoice* (Rita).
       - **End Event:** *Invoice Rejected*.

### **3. Manager Rejection Handling**
- **Trigger:** Manager rejects or holds invoice.
- **Steps:**
  1. **Investigation:**
     - **Task:** *AP Clerk/Rita Investigates* (e.g., recheck PO, contact supplier).
     - May involve **Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen)** for contract/price verification.
  2. **Gateway:** *Issues Resolved?*
     - ** Yes:** Resubmit to **[5. Request Manager Approval]**.
     - ** No:**
       - **Task:** *Reject Invoice* (Rita).
       - **End Event:** *Invoice Rejected*.

## ** Loops & Iterations**
1. **Supplier Correction Loop:**
   - If invoice is missing PO or has discrepancies, supplier may resend corrected invoice  **Loop back to *Start Event***.
2. **Discrepancy Resolution Loop:**
   - Unresolved discrepancies may require multiple iterations between AP, Purchasing, and Supplier  **Loop back to relevant validation step**.
3. **Manager Rejection Loop:**
   - Rejected invoices may re-enter the flow after issues are resolved  **Loop back to *Request Manager Approval***.

## ** Notes on Ambiguities & Consolidations**
1. **Overlapping Validation Steps:**
   - *Purchasing* validates invoice against PO (quantities, prices, receipt).
   - *AP Clerk* matches line items against PO in the system.
   - **Interpretation:** Purchasing confirms **receipt and high-level accuracy**, while AP performs **detailed financial matching**.

2. **Automatic Notifications:**
   - The system sends an automatic email to the manager **after** Purchasing confirmation and AP matching are complete (per Mary’s account).

3. **Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen):**
   - Involved **on-demand** when:
     - PO is missing (to request it from supplier).
     - Pricing/contract discrepancies arise (to verify correct terms).
   - **Not a mandatory step** in the happy path; only engaged for exceptions.

4. **Retrospective POs:**
   - Some invoices (e.g., for informally contracted services) lack POs initially.
   - **Rita** coordinates with department heads to create a PO retroactively or reject the invoice.

5. **Escalation Hierarchy:**
   - **AP Clerk (Mary)**  **AP Supervisor (Rita)**  (if needed) **Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen)**.
