**BPMN-like Textual Process Model: Invoice Approval Process**

---

### **Participants / Roles**
- **Accounts Payable Clerk (AP Clerk)** – Executes initial invoice validation, logging, matching, and forwarding.
- **Purchasing Officer (Purchasing)** – Verifies invoice against Purchase Order (PO) and confirms receipt of goods/services.
- **Department Manager** – Provides final approval based on financial and operational review.
- **Accounts Payable Supervisor (AP Supervisor)** – Handles escalations and exceptions that cannot be resolved at lower levels.
- **Supplier Relationship Manager (Supplier RM)** – Assists in resolving supplier-related discrepancies (e.g., missing PO, pricing).
- **System** – Automates notifications (e.g., manager approval request) and maintains records.

---

### **Artifacts / Data Objects**
- **Invoice** – Primary document submitted by supplier.
- **Purchase Order (PO)** – Reference document for ordered goods/services.
- **Confirmation Email** – From Purchasing to AP Clerk verifying invoice accuracy.
- **System Notification** – Automated request for manager approval.

---

### **Process Flow Description**

#### **1. Start Event: Invoice Received**
- **Source:** Supplier (typically via email).
- **Artifact:** Invoice arrives at Accounts Payable.

#### **2. Activity: Check for Valid PO Number**
- **Role:** AP Clerk
- **Description:** Examine invoice for a valid, legible Purchase Order number.
- **Gateway (XOR):**  
  - **Path A (No PO):**  
    - **Action:** Return invoice to supplier requesting PO number/details.  
    - **Role:** AP Clerk (may involve Supplier RM if supplier is unresponsive).  
    - **Flow:** Back to *Start Event* (supplier resubmits corrected invoice).  
  - **Path B (Has PO):** Proceed to next activity.

#### **3. Activity: Log Invoice into Accounting System**
- **Role:** AP Clerk
- **Description:** Enter invoice details (PO number, amount, vendor) into the system.
- **Output:** Invoice record created in system.

#### **4. Activity: Forward to Purchasing for Receipt Confirmation**
- **Role:** AP Clerk
- **Description:** Send invoice and PO to Purchasing Officer to verify that goods/services were received.
- **Gateway (XOR) – Based on Purchasing’s Review:**  
  - **Path A (Confirmation OK):**  
    - **Action:** Purchasing sends confirmation email to AP Clerk.  
    - **Flow:** Proceed to *Invoice Matching* (Step 5).  
  - **Path B (Discrepancy/Issue):**  
    - **Action:** Purchasing notifies AP Clerk of issue (e.g., missing items, pricing mismatch).  
    - **Flow:** Go to *Clarify/Resolve Discrepancy* (Step 4a).

#### **4a. Activity: Clarify/Resolve Discrepancy (if needed)**
- **Roles:** AP Clerk, Purchasing, Supplier RM (if supplier-related)
- **Description:**  
  - AP Clerk contacts Purchasing to understand issue.  
  - If supplier error: Supplier RM may contact supplier for corrected invoice or clarification.  
  - If internal error: Coordinate with relevant department.  
- **Gateway (XOR):**  
  - **Path A (Resolved):** Issue clarified; proceed to *Invoice Matching* (Step 5).  
  - **Path B (Unresolved / Complex):** Escalate to AP Supervisor (Step 4b).

#### **4b. Activity: Escalate to AP Supervisor**
- **Role:** AP Clerk  AP Supervisor
- **Description:** AP Supervisor reviews escalated case, contacts supplier or department head, may create retrospective PO, or decide to reject invoice.
- **Output:** Issue resolved or decision made.
- **Flow:** Back to *Clarify/Resolve Discrepancy* (Step 4a) if resolved; or if rejected, process ends (invoice returned/cancelled).

#### **5. Activity: Match Invoice Against PO**
- **Role:** AP Clerk
- **Description:** Compare invoice line items (quantities, prices, terms) against PO in the system.
- **Gateway (XOR):**  
  - **Path A (Match OK):** Proceed to *Send to Manager for Approval* (Step 6).  
  - **Path B (Mismatch):**  
    - **Action:** AP Clerk emails Purchasing to clarify discrepancy.  
    - **Flow:** Back to *Clarify/Resolve Discrepancy* (Step 4a) – may involve Purchasing again.

#### **6. Activity: Send to Department Manager for Approval**
- **Role:** System (automated) / AP Clerk (manual if system not used)
- **Description:** System sends notification to Manager with invoice, PO, and Purchasing confirmation. Manager reviews.
- **Gateway (XOR) – Manager Decision:**  
  - **Path A (Approve):**  
    - **Action:** Manager clicks approve in system.  
    - **Flow:** Proceed to *Schedule for Payment* (Step 7).  
  - **Path B (Reject / Request Investigation):**  
    - **Action:** Manager rejects invoice or asks AP Clerk to hold and investigate (e.g., budget mismatch).  
    - **Flow:** Go to *Investigate/Resolve* (Step 6a).

#### **6a. Activity: Investigate/Resolve (Manager Rejection)**
- **Roles:** AP Clerk, Department Manager, possibly AP Supervisor or Supplier RM
- **Description:** AP Clerk investigates reason for rejection (e.g., budget issue, unexpected item). May involve department head or supplier.
- **Gateway (XOR):**  
  - **Path A (Resolved):** Issue clarified; invoice may return to *Manager Approval* (Step 6) or be cancelled.  
  - **Path B (Requires Escalation):** Escalate to AP Supervisor (Step 4b) if systemic issue.

#### **7. Activity: Schedule for Payment**
- **Role:** AP Clerk
- **Description:** Once approved, add invoice to next payment run cycle in accounting system.
- **Output:** Invoice queued for payment.

#### **8. End Event: Payment Processed**
- **Description:** Invoice paid via scheduled payment run. Process ends.

---

### **Key Exceptions & Variations Noted**
- **Missing PO:** Invoice returned to supplier; loop back to start.
- **Discrepancy at Purchasing:** Clarified internally or with supplier; may escalate.
- **Discrepancy at Matching:** Clarified with Purchasing; may escalate.
- **Manager Rejection:** Investigated; may loop back or escalate.
- **Complex Exceptions (e.g., no PO, contract pricing):** Escalated to AP Supervisor; may involve retrospective PO or rejection.
- **Supplier Communication:** Supplier RM assists with supplier-related issues but does not approve.

---

### **Notes on Ambiguities / Consolidations**
- **Sequence of Confirmation vs. Matching:** Interviews indicate Purchasing confirms *before* AP Clerk matches. Model reflects this order.
- **System Automation:** Carlos mentions automatic notification; Mary implies manual forwarding. Model includes both possibilities (system-automated or clerk-mediated).
- **Escalation Path:** Rita states she handles exceptions when Mary cannot resolve them. Model shows escalation from AP Clerk to AP Supervisor.
- **Supplier RM Role:** Karen helps resolve issues but does not touch the system. Model shows her involvement only in supplier-related clarifications.
- **Payment Timing:** Not explicitly detailed; assumed to be after approval and scheduling.
