### Rationale for Added Constraints

1.  **`existence` (New Activities):**
    *   **Added Activities:** `CheckApplicantRace`, `BiasMitigationCheck`, `ManualReview`, `Reject`.
    *   **Rationale:** To enforce fairness, the model must explicitly track sensitive attribute checks (`CheckApplicantRace`) and mitigation steps (`BiasMitigationCheck`, `ManualReview`). `Reject` is added as a specific decision activity to allow for granular constraints on negative outcomes.

2.  **`coexistence` (CheckApplicantRace <-> ManualReview):**
    *   **Constraint:** If `CheckApplicantRace` occurs, `ManualReview` must also occur in the trace, and vice versa.
    *   **Rationale:** This ensures that whenever sensitive demographic data is reviewed, a human oversight step (`ManualReview`) is guaranteed to happen. This prevents automated or biased decisions from being made without human intervention when sensitive data is involved.

3.  **`precedence` (BiasMitigationCheck -> Reject):**
    *   **Constraint:** `BiasMitigationCheck` must happen before `Reject`.
    *   **Rationale:** This forces a specific fairness check to occur prior to a rejection. It ensures that a decision to reject cannot be made without first running a bias mitigation protocol, reducing the likelihood of discriminatory outcomes.

4.  **`nonsuccession` (CheckApplicantRace -> Reject):**
    *   **Constraint:** `CheckApplicantRace` cannot immediately precede `Reject`.
    *   **Rationale:** This prevents the process from jumping directly from identifying a sensitive attribute (e.g., Race) to a negative decision (`Reject`). It enforces an intervening step (like `BiasMitigationCheck` or `ManualReview`), breaking the direct causal link that could lead to immediate bias.

5.  **`response` (Reject -> ManualReview):**
    *   **Constraint:** If `Reject` occurs, `ManualReview` must eventually occur.
    *   **Rationale:** This acts as a post-decision audit. If a rejection happens, it mandates a review, ensuring accountability and providing an opportunity to correct potential bias after the fact.

6.  **`precedence` (ManualReview -> FinalDecision):**
    *   **Constraint:** `ManualReview` must precede `FinalDecision`.
    *   **Rationale:** This elevates the importance of human oversight, ensuring that a final decision is not reached without a manual verification step, further safeguarding against algorithmic or procedural bias.
