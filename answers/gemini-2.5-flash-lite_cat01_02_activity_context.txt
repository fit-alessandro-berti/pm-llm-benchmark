### Justification of Naming Choices

The following activities recurred within cases and have been renamed to clarify their contextual meaning:

**1. Original Activity: `Verify Documents`**

*   **Occurrences & Contexts:**
    *   **First Instance in a Case (C001, C002, C003):** The initial check of documents submitted at the start of the request.
        *   **New Name:** `Initial Document Verification`
        *   **Justification:** This name clearly distinguishes the first verification step from any subsequent checks, indicating it pertains to the foundational documents provided.

    *   **Second Instance in Case C001:** Context: "Agent re-checks documents now that ID scan is provided." This happens after the customer has uploaded requested information.
        *   **New Name:** `Follow-up Document Verification`
        *   **Justification:** This name reflects that the verification is a follow-up action taken *after* the customer has supplied previously requested documentation.

    *   **Second Instance in Case C003:** Context: "Re-verify docs after account adjustment." This happens after an internal adjustment has been made to the account.
        *   **New Name:** `Post-Adjustment Document Verification`
        *   **Justification:** This name highlights that the re-verification is specifically triggered by or follows an internal account adjustment, indicating a change in the state of the case that necessitates re-checking.

**2. Original Activity: `Call Customer`**

*   **Occurrences & Contexts:**
    *   **Instance in Case C001:** Context: "Verification incomplete, agent calls to request missing docs." The call's purpose is to obtain information that was not provided or was insufficient initially.
        *   **New Name:** `Request Missing Information Call`
        *   **Justification:** This name directly states the purpose of the call: to acquire specific information or documents that were identified as missing during the verification process.

    *   **First Instance in Case C002:** Context: "Outbound call to clarify account discrepancy." The call is to resolve an issue or ambiguity in the customer's account details.
        *   **New Name:** `Account Clarification Call`
        *   **Justification:** This name clearly indicates that the call is focused on resolving discrepancies or seeking clarification regarding the customer's account information.

    *   **Instance in Case C003:** Context: "Customer stepped out. Officer calls to have them return." The call is to get the customer back to the service point.
        *   **New Name:** `Customer Return Call`
        *   **Justification:** This name specifies that the call is made to prompt the customer to return to complete their interaction or process, highlighting a logistical need.

    *   **Second Instance in Case C002:** Context: "Follow-up call to confirm adjustments made." This call is made after an action has been completed, to ensure it's confirmed.
        *   **New Name:** `Confirmation Call`
        *   **Justification:** This name denotes that the call serves as a follow-up action to confirm the completion and understanding of a previously performed task, such as an account adjustment.
