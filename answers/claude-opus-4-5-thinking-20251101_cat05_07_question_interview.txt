# Clarifying Questions for Property Onboarding Process

## Process Initiation & Documentation

1. When a landlord first contacts the regional office, what channels do they typically use, and is there a preferred or required method for initiating this request?

2. You mentioned the coordinator checks for missing or outdated documents—what specifically qualifies a document as "outdated," and who determines those thresholds?

3. What happens if a landlord is unable or unwilling to provide certain required documentation? Is there a point where the process is terminated, and who makes that call?

4. How long does a landlord typically have to submit missing documents before the request is considered inactive or closed?

## Property Manager Assignment

5. You mentioned workload as a factor in assigning property managers—how is workload measured or defined within your organization?

6. Who has the authority to make the final decision on property manager assignment, and is there any input from the landlord in this selection?

7. What happens if no property manager meets all the ideal criteria for a particular property? How are trade-offs handled?

8. Once a property manager is assigned, under what circumstances might they be reassigned, and what does that transition look like?

---

## Introductory Meeting & Expectations

9. You described an introductory call or meeting between the manager and landlord—is there a standard agenda or checklist that guides this conversation?

10. What happens if the landlord and property manager cannot reach agreement on policies like pet allowances or maintenance responsibilities? Is there an escalation path?

11. How are the outcomes of this meeting documented, and who else in the organization has visibility into what was agreed upon?

## Inspection & Photography

12. How do you select or approve the professional photographers and certified inspectors—are they internal staff, preferred vendors, or can landlords suggest their own?

13. When the inspector identifies issues, how do you categorize the severity, and are there different remediation timelines based on issue type?

14. What happens if a landlord disputes the inspector's findings or refuses to make required repairs?

15. Is there a maximum number of re-inspections allowed, or a point at which a property is declined from the portfolio?

16. Can photography and inspection happen simultaneously, or must one be completed before the other?

## Marketing & Pricing

18. What happens if there's a disagreement between the landlord's expected rental price and the company's pricing model recommendation?

19. How frequently are pricing models updated to reflect market trends, and can a property's price be adjusted after it's been activated?

## Activation & Availability
