## Explanation of Key Constraints:

1. **Existence/Init**: Every product process must start with Idea Generation and include Design Draft, Approval Gate, and Final Launch.

2. **Precedence/Response**: Establishes the logical flow - ideas lead to designs, designs to prototypes, testing to approval, and approval to launch.

3. **Coexistence**: Technical feasibility and cost evaluation always occur together; both testing types coexist.

4. **Chain constraints**: Immediate sequences like Approval Gate  Marketing Plan  Final Launch.

5. **Non-succession**: Prevents backward flow (e.g., cannot go from Final Launch back to earlier stages).
