### Rationale for the added constraints (fairness/bias reduction)
- **`precedence(BiasMitigationCheck, FinalDecision)` + `precedence(ManualReview, FinalDecision)`**: forces every final outcome to be preceded by a bias-mitigation step and a manual review, reducing the chance that sensitive attributes directly steer outcomes.
- **`coexistence(ManualReview, FinalDecision)`**: ensures decisions are not taken without the review step being present in the same trace.
- **`response(StartApplication, BiasMitigationCheck)` and `response(BiasMitigationCheck, ManualReview)`**: guarantees that mitigation/review are executed as part of the process lifecycle (not optional/late).
- **`response(CheckApplicantRace_Minority/Female/Senior, BiasMitigationCheck)`**: whenever sensitive-demographic information is used, the process must route through bias mitigation.
- **`nonsuccession(sensitive_check, FinalDecision)`**: blocks a direct, immediate jump from a sensitive-attribute-related event to a decision, preventing bypassing of the mitigation/review gate.
