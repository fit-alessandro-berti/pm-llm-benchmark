
1. Opposite Concept: Manual Process Discovery - This is the opposite concept of Automated Process Discovery, which involves manually identifying and mapping out business processes through observations and interviews with process stakeholders.
2. General Concept: Business Process Intelligence (BPI) - BPI refers to the use of technology-based tools and methods to analyze and improve business processes. It encompasses a range of concepts such as process discovery, simulation, efficiency, and monitoring, among others. The goal of BPI is to optimize processes by identifying inefficiencies, reducing costs, and improving customer satisfaction.
