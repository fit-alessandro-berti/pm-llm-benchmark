2. **Precedence Constraints:** `CheckApplicantRace` must be followed by `BiasMitigationCheck`, not directly by `FinalDecision` or `Reject`. This prevents immediate biased decisions based on race or other sensitive attributes.

3. **Succession Constraints:** `BiasMitigationCheck` must precede any `FinalDecision`, ensuring that a fairness check occurs before finalizing the outcome.

4. **Non-succession Constraints:** Prevent direct succession from `CheckApplicantRace` to `Reject` or `Approve`, ensuring that a decision is not made immediately after checking sensitive attributes without a fairness check.

5. **Nonchainsuccession Constraints:** Prevent long chains from `CheckApplicantRace` to `FinalDecision` or `Reject`, ensuring that even if multiple steps occur, a fairness check intervenes before a biased decision is made.

These constraints collectively enforce that sensitive attributes influence decisions only after appropriate fairness checks, promoting equitable treatment of all applicants regardless of demographic characteristics.
