### Rationale for Added Constraints:

1. **Existence of `ManualReview` and `BiasMitigationCheck`:**
   - Ensures these fairness checks are part of the process, reducing the risk of biased decisions.

2. **Coexistence Constraints:**
   - `"CheckApplicantRace": {"ManualReview": ...}`: Forces a manual review whenever sensitive attributes like race are checked.
   - `"Reject_Minority": {"ManualReview": ...}`: Ensures manual review is part of the trace if a minority applicant is rejected.

3. **Response Constraint:**
   - `"CheckApplicantRace": {"BiasMitigationCheck": ...}`: Requires a bias mitigation check immediately after checking sensitive attributes.

4. **Precedence and Succession Constraints:**
   - `"BiasMitigationCheck": {"FinalDecision": ...}`: Ensures bias mitigation happens before final decisions for sensitive cases.

5. **Non-succession Constraints:**
   - `"CheckApplicantRace": {"Reject": ...}` and `"CheckApplicantGender": {"Reject": ...}`: Prevents direct transitions from checking sensitive attributes to rejection, forcing intermediate fairness checks.

These constraints collectively ensure that:
- Sensitive attributes cannot lead to immediate biased outcomes.
- Fairness checks (`ManualReview`, `BiasMitigationCheck`) are mandatory in critical paths.
- The process is more transparent and less prone to discrimination.
