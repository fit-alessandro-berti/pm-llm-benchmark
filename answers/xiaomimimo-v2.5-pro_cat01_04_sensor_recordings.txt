## Key Diagnostic Signatures

| Activity | Distinguishing Sensor Pattern |
|---|---|
| **Cutting** | Rising temp + rising vibration + steady material flow + tool advancing |
| **Assembly** | High pressure + low vibration + tool steady at advanced position |
| **Welding** | **Extreme** temp spike + **maximum** vibration + **maximum** energy + zero flow + fixed tool |
| **Inspection** | Cooling temp + very low vibration + slight tool advance + low energy |

The most reliable single indicator for distinguishing phases is the **vibration–pressure relationship**: cutting is *high-vibration / moderate-pressure*, assembly inverts to *low-vibration / high-pressure*, and welding maximizes *both* simultaneously while eliminating material flow entirely.
