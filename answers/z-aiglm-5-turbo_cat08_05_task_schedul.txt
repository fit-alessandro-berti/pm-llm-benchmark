*   **Process Mining Linkage:** The weights $K_1$ and $K_2$, as well as the average processing time ($\bar{p}$) and average setup time ($\bar{s}$), are directly parameterized using the distributions mined from the event logs. The $s_{ij}$ variable is dynamically pulled from the Setup Time Transition Matrix.
*   **Pathology Addressed:** Resolves poor prioritization and ignores setup families.
*   **Expected KPI Impact:** 15-25% reduction in mean tardiness; 10-15% reduction in average setup times.

#### Strategy 3: Setup-Optimized Bottleneck Scheduling (Dynamic Job Batching)
