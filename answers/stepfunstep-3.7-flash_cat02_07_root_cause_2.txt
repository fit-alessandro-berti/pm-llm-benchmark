### Step 3: Mitigation Suggestions
#### Explanations for Performance Issues
- High-complexity claims require more document requests because incomplete initial submissions are common for high-risk, high-value claims. Each request cycle relies on slow external (claimant) response times, which are amplified if adjusters do not follow up proactively.
- Regional performance gaps stem from uneven work distribution: overloaded adjusters in Region B cannot prioritize follow-ups or conduct thorough initial reviews, leading to more requests and longer gaps between processing steps.
- Resource overload reduces adjuster capacity to focus on complex cases, increasing the risk of missed required documents in initial requests and delayed follow-ups.

1. **Reduce document request cycles for high-complexity claims**:

   - Align documentation requirements for identical complexity levels across regions to avoid unnecessary follow-up requests.
