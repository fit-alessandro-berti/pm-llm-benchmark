```python
        ('GSL', 'D'),
        ('RO', 'IC')

        ('PO', 'GSL'),
        ('GSL', 'D')

```

### Explanation:
- **equivalence**: Empty, as no activities are interchangeable.
- **always_before**: Captures the strict sequencing (e.g., CA must always precede RO in time).
- **always_after**: Enforces that fulfillment steps must follow their predecessors.
- **never_together**: Empty, as all activities can theoretically co-exist in the same case.
- **activ_freq**: Core process activities (ROD, IC) occur exactly once; RP is optional (0 or 1) since payment may not occur in the observed trace.
- **directly_follows**: Defines the direct succession in the main fulfillment flow (ROCARIPIQCPOGSLD). IC and RP are not directly-followed due to potential asynchrony with payment processing.
