The provided diagram is a flowchart that outlines the process of dispatching goods from a computer hardware shop, covering stages from order receipt to shipment preparation. Here is a detailed summary:

### Swimlanes and Departments:
1. **Dispatch of Goods (Computer Hardware Shop/Secretary)**
2. **Logistics**
3. **Warehouse**

### Process Flow:

1. **Start (Ship Goods):**
   - The process begins with the initiation of shipping goods.

2. **Clarify Shipment Method:**
   - The secretary clarifies the shipment method based on order details.

3. **Decision Point - Special Handling?:**
   - **If yes (special handling required):**
     - **Check if Insurance is Required:**
       - If insurance is necessary, the customer information is used to insure the parcel.
     - Proceed to get logistic offers and place an order with the selected logistics company.
   - **If no (no special handling):**
     - Directly proceed to get offers from logistic companies.

4. **Get 3 Offers from Logistic Companies:**
   - The secretary obtains three different logistic offers.

5. **Select Logistic Company and Place Order:**
   - The secretary selects a logistics company and places the shipment order.

6. **Write Package Label:**
   - The package label is written, irrespective of the insurance requirement.

7. **Merge Point (Post-Insurance and Labeling):**
   - After handling insurance (if necessary) and writing the package label, the process converges.

8. **Package Goods:**
   - The warehouse packages the goods, which should take no longer than 48 hours.

9. **Decision Point - Delay?:**
   - **If there is a delay:**
     - Notify the customer about the delay.
   - **If no delay:**
     - Proceed to fill out the shipment paperwork.

10. **Fill Out Shipment Paperwork:**
    - The warehouse fills out the necessary shipment paperwork.

11. **Move Package to Pickup Area:**
    - The package is moved to the pickup area for the logistics company to collect.

12. **End (Shipment Prepared):**
    - The process concludes with the shipment prepared for dispatch.

### Additional Notes:
- **Insurance Process:**
  - If insurance is required, the parcel is insured before proceeding to logistic company selection.
- **Customer Notification:**
  - If there is any delay in packaging, the customer is notified.

This flowchart ensures a structured and clear process for handling the dispatch of goods, including considerations for special handling, insurance, and potential delays.
