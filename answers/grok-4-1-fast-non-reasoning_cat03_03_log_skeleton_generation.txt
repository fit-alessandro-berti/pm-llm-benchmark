### Explanation of the Log Skeleton:

**Key Business Logic Captured:**
- **Sequential fulfillment process**: RO  CA  RI  PI  QC  PO  GSL  D (enforced via `always_before`, `always_after`, and `directly_follows`)
- **Order initiation requirement**: RO must occur exactly once to start any case (`activ_freq: {1}`)
- **Independent invoicing**: IC can happen after RO but is parallel to fulfillment (`always_before/after` with RO/IC/RP)
- **Payment is optional**: RP may or may not occur (`activ_freq: {0,1}`)
- **Strict ordering**: Each fulfillment step requires the previous one (`always_before`)
- **Direct succession**: Core fulfillment steps happen immediately after each other (`directly_follows`)

**Why these specific constraints:**
1. **No equivalence**: No activities are interchangeable
2. **No never_together**: All activities can potentially co-exist in a case
3. **RP is optional**: Payment might not be received immediately or at all
4. **Invoicing independence**: IC can happen anytime after RO, not tied to physical fulfillment
