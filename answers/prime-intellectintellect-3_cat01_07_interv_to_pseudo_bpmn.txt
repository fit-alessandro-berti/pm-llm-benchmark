# BPMN-like Process Model: Invoice Approval Process

## Process: Invoice Approval Process

### Main Process Flow:
1. **Invoice Receipt** - Organization receives invoice (typically via email)
2. **PO Validation** - Check if invoice contains valid Purchase Order (PO) number
   - *Decision Point*: PO Present Gateway
3. **Logging** - Log invoice into accounting system
4. **Forward for Confirmation** - Forward invoice to Purchasing Department for confirmation of goods/services receipt
5. **Confirm Receipt** - Purchasing Officer confirms receipt of goods/services and verifies invoice details against PO
   - *Decision Point*: PO Match Gateway
6. **Match PO** - Accounts Payable Clerk matches invoice line items against PO
7. **Manager Approval** - Department Manager approves invoice (triggered by system notification)
   - *Decision Point*: Manager Approval Gateway
8. **Schedule Payment** - Accounts Payable Clerk schedules approved invoice for payment

### Decision Points & Branches:
1. **PO Present Gateway** (after Invoice Receipt)
   - *If NO PO number*: Send invoice back to supplier requesting clarification (returns to Invoice Receipt step after resolution)
   - *If PO present*: Continue to Logging

2. **PO Match Gateway** (after Confirm Receipt)
   - *If PO doesn't match*: Email Purchasing Team to clarify (discrepancy handling path)
   - *If PO matches*: Continue to Manager Approval

3. **Manager Approval Gateway** (after Match PO)
   - *If rejected*: Initiate investigation (exception handling path)
   - *If approved*: Continue to Schedule Payment

### Actives/Roles:
- **Accounts Payable Clerk** (Mary): 
  - Performs invoice receipt, PO validation, logging, matching, payment scheduling, and rework
  
- **Purchasing Officer** (Dan):
  - Performs confirmation of goods received and PO matching
  
- **Department Manager** (Carlos):
  - Performs final approval based on invoice details, PO, and confirmation
  
- **Accounts Payable Supervisor** (Rita):
  - Handles exception resolution when issues can't be resolved by clerk
  
- **Supplier Relationship Manager** (Karen):
  - Helps resolve supplier-related issues when contacted

### Artifacts:
- **Invoice** (received via email)
- **Purchase Order (PO)** (referenced for validation and matching)
- **Confirmation Email** (sent by system for manager approval)
- **Payment Schedule** (added to next payment cycle after approval)

### Exception Paths:
1. **Missing PO Number**:
   - Send back to supplier  Receive clarification  Return to PO Validation
   
2. **PO Mismatch**:
   - Initiate clarification  Receive corrected invoice  Return to Match PO step
   
3. **Manager Rejection**:
   - Initiate investigation  Resolve issues  Return to Manager Approval step
   
4. **Unresolved Invoices**:
   - Supervisor intervenes  Create retrospective PO or reject invoice

### Subprocesses:
1. **Clarification**:
   - Email communication with supplier
   - Rework on invoice details
   
2. **Exception Handling**:
   - Supervisor resolution
   - Possible creation of retrospective PO
   - Invoice rejection if needed

### Process Uncertainties:
- In some cases, if the PO number is missing, Mary needs to obtain the information from the supplier before proceeding
- If a discrepancy can't be resolved easily, escalation to the supervisor occurs
- For invoices without PO, a retrospective PO may be created after supervisor intervention
