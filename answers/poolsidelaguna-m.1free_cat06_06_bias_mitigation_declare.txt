## Rationale for Added Constraints

### 1. **Response/Chainresponse Constraints for Bias Mitigation**
These ensure that whenever sensitive attributes (race, gender, age) are checked, a `BiasMitigationCheck` activity must be performed before any decision:
- `CheckApplicantRace  BiasMitigationCheck`
- `CheckApplicantGender  BiasMitigationCheck`
- `CheckApplicantAge  BiasMitigationCheck`

This creates a mandatory "cooling off" period where the decision-maker must actively consider fairness.

### 2. **Non-succession/Nonchainsuccession Constraints**
These directly prevent biased outcomes by forbidding certain activity sequences:
- Prevent `CheckApplicantRace  Reject` (immediate negative bias)
- Prevent `CheckApplicantGender  Reject` (gender-based discrimination)
- Prevent `CheckApplicantAge  Reject` (age discrimination)

### 3. **Precedence Constraints**
Ensure proper process flow:
- `ManualReview` must precede `FinalDecision` for sensitive cases
- `BiasMitigationCheck` must precede `FinalDecision` for all cases

### 4. **Coexistence Constraints**
Require fair treatment mechanisms to be present:
- `ManualReview` must coexist with `FinalDecision` for sensitive applicants
- `BiasMitigationCheck` must coexist with decisions involving protected groups

These constraints work together to create a fairer loan application process where sensitive attributes don't directly influence adverse decisions without proper oversight and mitigation procedures.
