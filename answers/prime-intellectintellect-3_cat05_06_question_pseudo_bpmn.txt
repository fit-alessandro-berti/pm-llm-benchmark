6. **Decision Logic**

7. **Logistics Efficiency**  
   Why prioritize air freight for high-demand markets over cost-effective sea freight? How is demand volatility factored into this decision?  

8. **Customs Risk**  
   How does the process anticipate and mitigate delays in customs clearance for regulated markets (e.g., EU’s CE marking or US FCC compliance)?  

9. **Inventory Management**

11. **Assembly Scalability**

13. **E-Waste Reduction**
