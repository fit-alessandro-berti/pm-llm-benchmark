# Integrated Event Log for Process Mining

## Approach & Assumptions

1. **Case ID** = `order_id`.
2. **Activities derived**: `Order Created`, `Item Picked` (one per order line, since each has its own timestamp and picker), `Order Dispatched` / `Order Arrived` (one pair per delivery record — split shipments yield multiple events), `Invoice Issued` (one per invoice), `Payment Received` (one per payment, linked to the order via `invoice_id`).
3. **Resources** enriched from the Resources table; items enriched from the Products table.
4. **Assumption — Order 1004**: No delivery, invoice, or payment records exist. The case is treated as **in progress / incomplete** (ends after picking). No events were invented.
5. **Assumption — Payment resource**: Payments have no internal actor; the customer is treated as the initiating party, with the payment method recorded as an attribute.
6. Events are sorted chronologically **within each case**.
