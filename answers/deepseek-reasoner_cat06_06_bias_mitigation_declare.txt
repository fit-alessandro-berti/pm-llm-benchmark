**Rationale for Added Constraints:**

1. **Coexistence Constraints**:  
   - `Approve_Minority`, `Reject_Minority`, etc., must coexist with `ManualReview`. This ensures that decisions involving sensitive demographics undergo manual scrutiny, reducing automated bias.

2. **Response Constraints**:  
   - `CheckApplicantRace`, `CheckApplicantAge`, and `CheckApplicantGender` must be followed by `BiasMitigationCheck`. This guarantees that sensitive attribute checks trigger fairness validation before proceeding.

3. **Precedence Constraints**:  
   - `BiasMitigationCheck` must precede any decision (`Approve`, `Reject`, `RequestAdditionalInfo`). This ensures decisions are only made after bias checks, enforcing a fair process.

4. **Nonchainsuccession Constraints**:  
   - Direct transitions from sensitive checks (e.g., `CheckApplicantRace`) to decisions are blocked. This prevents immediate biased outcomes without intermediate checks.
