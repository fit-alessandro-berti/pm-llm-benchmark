## What the visualization represents in plain language
In simple terms:

- Every Monday morning, Sales reviews customer orders.
- Sales verifies the customer exists in the database, adding them if needed.
- Sales sends order details to Production and Procurement.
- Procurement orders/checks materials and may return faulty materials or cancel the order if necessary.
- Production plans and manufactures the product.
- Once the product is complete, Warehouse ships it.
- After shipment is confirmed, Sales drafts the invoice.
