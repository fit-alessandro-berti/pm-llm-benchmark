### **Process Overview**
**Process Name:** Invoice Approval and Payment Scheduling  
**Start Event:** Invoice Received (via email from Supplier)  
**End Event:** Invoice Scheduled for Payment OR Invoice Rejected

---

### **Participant Lanes & Swimlanes**

#### **Lane 1: Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary)**
1. **Task:** Check Invoice for Valid PO Number
   - **Exclusive Gateway (GW1):** PO Number Present and Valid?
     - **NO:**  
       - Task: Send Invoice Back to Supplier (request PO details)  
       - *Sequence Flow:* Return to Start Event (await revised invoice)
     - **YES:** Continue to next task

2. **Task:** Log Invoice into Accounting System

3. **Task:** Forward Invoice to Purchasing Officer for Receipt Confirmation

4. **Intermediate Event:** Wait for Purchasing Confirmation (Message Catch: "Confirmation Email")

5. **Task:** Match Invoice Line Items Against PO in System
   - **Exclusive Gateway (GW2):** Invoice Matches PO Exactly?
     - **NO:**  
       - Task: Email Purchasing Team for Clarification  
       - **Exclusive Gateway (GW3):** Discrepancy Resolved Within 48 Hours?
         - **YES:** Loop back to "Match Invoice Line Items Against PO"
         - **NO:**  
           - Task: Escalate to AP Supervisor  
           - *Sequence Flow:* Move to **Lane 4: Accounts Payable Supervisor (Exception Flow)**
     - **YES:** Continue to next task

6. **Task:** Initiate Manager Approval Request (System-Triggered)
   - *System automatically sends email notification to Department Manager*

7. **Intermediate Event:** Wait for Manager Decision (Message Catch: "Approval/ Rejection")

8. **Exclusive Gateway (GW4):** Manager Decision Received?
   - **APPROVED:**  
     - Task: Schedule Invoice for Payment  
     - **End Event:** Payment Scheduled
   - **REJECTED/INVESTIGATE:**  
     - Task: Hold Invoice for Investigation  
     - *Sequence Flow:* Loop back to manual resolution (discretionary)

---

#### **Lane 2: Purchasing Officer (Dan)**
1. **Intermediate Event:** Receive Invoice from AP (Message Start: "Invoice for Verification")

2. **Task:** Verify Invoice Against PO (Compare quantities, prices, delivery charges)
   - **Exclusive Gateway (GW5):** Invoice Details Correct?
     - **NO:**  
       - **Parallel Gateway (GW6):** Choose Resolution Path
         - Path A: Task: Contact Supplier Directly  
         - Path B: Task: Request AP Clerk to Hold Invoice  
       - Task: Request Corrected Invoice from Supplier  
       - **Intermediate Event:** Wait for Corrected Invoice (Message Catch)  
       - *Sequence Flow:* Loop back to "Verify Invoice Against PO"
     - **YES:** Continue to next task

3. **Task:** Send Confirmation Email to Accounts Payable Clerk
   - *Sequence Flow:* Triggers AP Clerk's "Wait for Purchasing Confirmation" event*

---

#### **Lane 3: Department Manager (Carlos)**
1. **Intermediate Event:** Receive Approval Notification (System-Generated Message)

2. **Task:** Review Invoice, PO, and Purchasing Confirmation
   - **Exclusive Gateway (GW7):** Approve Invoice?
     - **APPROVE:**  
       - Task: Approve in System (one-click)  
       - *Sequence Flow:* Triggers AP Clerk's "Wait for Manager Decision"*
     - **REJECT/INVESTIGATE:**  
       - Task: Reject/Hold Invoice in System  
       - Task: Notify AP Clerk to Investigate Further  
       - *Sequence Flow:* Returns to AP Clerk's "Hold Invoice for Investigation"*

---

#### **Lane 4: Accounts Payable Supervisor (Rita) - Exception Flow**
*Triggered by escalation from AP Clerk OR direct involvement for complex exceptions*

1. **Intermediate Event:** Escalation Received (Message Start: "Exception Case")

2. **Task:** Review Escalated Invoice and Exception Details

3. **Exclusive Gateway (GW8):** Exception Type?
   - **MISSING/INVALID PO:**  
     - Task: Coordinate with Supplier to Provide Missing PO  
   - **PRICING/DELIVERY DISCREPANCY:**  
     - Task: Contact Supplier to Resolve Dispute  
   - **NO PO MATCH (Informal Services):**  
     - Task: Work with Department Head to Create Retrospective PO  
       - **Exclusive Gateway (GW9):** Retrospective PO Approved?
         - **YES:** Continue to normal flow
         - **NO:** Task: Reject Invoice Permanently  **End Event:** Invoice Rejected
   - **OTHER:** Task: Coordinate Cross-Department Resolution

4. **Task:** Document Resolution and Return to Normal Flow
   - *Sequence Flow:* Returns to appropriate step in **Lane 1** (typically "Match Invoice Line Items" or "Verify Invoice")*

#### **Lane 5: Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen) - Support Role**
*Invoked ad-hoc via informal request from AP Clerk or Purchasing Officer*

1. **Intermediate Event:** Issue Resolution Support Requested (Message Catch)

2. **Task:** Contact Supplier (Leverage Relationship)

     - Verify Invoice Against Active Contract Terms
     - Request Revised Invoice with Correct PO/Pricing
     - Expedite Response

3. **Task:** Provide Clarification to AP/Purchasing
   - *Sequence Flow:* Information feeds back into whichever lane originated the request*

#### **Lane 6: Supplier (External Participant)**
*Not a direct system user but triggers external events*
- **Event:** Send Original Invoice (triggers Start Event)
- **Event:** Send Corrected Invoice (triggers Intermediate Events in Lanes 1 & 2)
- **Event:** Provide PO Clarification (triggers resolution paths)

#### **Lane 7: Accounting System (Automated Service)**
