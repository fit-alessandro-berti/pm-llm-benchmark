# Clarifying Questions for the Property Onboarding Process

## Initial Intake & Documentation
1. When a landlord first contacts the regional office, is there a standard intake form or checklist used, or does it vary by coordinator and region?
2. What specific criteria determine whether a document is considered "outdated"𡟙s there a defined expiration window (e.g., inspection reports older than 12 months)?
3. How long does the company typically wait for landlords to provide updated documents before pausing or canceling the onboarding?
4. Are there scenarios where a property is rejected outright at the documentation stage? If so, what are the most common reasons?
5. Who has authority to make exceptions for missing or non-standard documentation?

## Property Details & Database Entry
6. Who is responsible for verifying the accuracy of the data entered into the central property database𡟙s it self-audited or double-checked by another party?
7. How is the "expected rental price range" initially determined before the financial analyst formally reviews it?
8. Are there standardized categories for amenities and property types, or is this free-text entry?

## Property Manager Assignment
9. How are the assignment factors (workload, specialization, experience, track record) weighted against one another when they conflict?
10. Is the assignment made by a human decision-maker, an algorithm, or a combination of both?
11. What happens if no available property manager fits the criteria珳oes the property wait, or is a less-ideal manager assigned?
12. Can a landlord request a specific property manager, and how is that handled?
13. How often is a property manager reassigned later in the process, and what triggers that?

## Introductory Meeting & Expectations
14. Is the introductory call/meeting mandatory, or can it be skipped for repeat landlords?
15. What happens if the landlord and property manager disagree on special conditions during this meeting?
16. Are the outcomes of this meeting formally documented, and if so, where?

## Photography & Inspection
17. Are the photographer and inspector internal employees or external vendors, and how are they selected?
18. What is the typical turnaround time between scheduling and receiving the inspection report?
19. What defines a "pass" versus a "fail" on inspection㻡re there tiered severity levels for issues?
20. Who bears the cost of repairs鍟he landlord, the company, or is it negotiated case-by-case?
21. What happens if a landlord refuses to address inspection issues?

## Marketing & Pricing
22. How long does the digital marketing team typically take to prepare listings and brochures?
23. What market data sources feed into the internal pricing models, and how often are they refreshed?
24. If the financial analyst later finds the rental price misaligned with strategy, does the listing get pulled and repriced?
25. Who approves the final listing before it goes live?

## Property Activation
26. What does "activation" involve technically and operationally㻡re there separate systems that need to be synced?
27. Is there a delay between when marketing finishes and when the property goes live, or is activation immediate?
28. Who is notified when a property is activated?

## Tenant Screening & Lease Negotiation
29. What are the explicit criteria for approving or rejecting a tenant application?
30. When there are multiple interested tenants, who decides which to prioritize鍟he property manager alone, or with landlord input?
31. How are promotional discounts approved, and are there limits on the property manager's discretion?
32. How is the decision to offer a longer lease weighed against a higher monthly rate?
33. What happens if no suitable tenant applies within a certain timeframe珳oes the price or strategy get revisited?

## Lease Finalization & Move-In
34. Are lease templates standardized, and how are custom clauses requested by landlords reviewed and approved?
35. Who collects and holds the security deposit, and how is it tracked?
36. Is there a formal move-in walkthrough, and who is responsible for it?

## Quality Checks & Audits
37. Are the compliance, financial, and IT reviews done sequentially or in parallel?
38. Do these checks occur before tenant move-in, after, or both?
39. What happens if the compliance officer finds an issue in the lease after it's already signed?
40. Are these audits performed on every property, or only on a sample?

## Exceptions & Communication
41. How is a property requiring special insurance routed differently through the process?
42. What is the escalation path when a landlord insists on clauses the legal team wouldn't normally approve?
43. Across the CRM, email, and project management tools, is there a single source of truth for a property's onboarding status?
44. How do handoffs between teams (coordinator  manager  marketing  leasing  audit) get confirmed and tracked?
45. Are there defined SLAs for each stage, and how is slippage detected and addressed?

## Overall Process
46. From first contact to tenant move-in, what is the typical end-to-end duration, and what are the most common bottlenecks?
47. Are there metrics or KPIs used to measure the success of the onboarding process itself?
48. How do you handle onboarding several properties from the same landlord simultaneously珳oes the process get streamlined?
49. Has the process changed recently, and are there parts currently under review for improvement?
50. Who owns the overall process end-to-end𡟙s there a single process owner, or is accountability distributed?
