Here’s a list of targeted, open-ended questions to clarify the property onboarding process:

---

### **Documentation & Compliance**  
1. Could you walk me through the criteria the property coordinator uses to determine whether a landlord’s documentation is "missing" or "outdated"? Are there specific deadlines for providing updated documents, or is this handled on a case-by-case basis?  
2. How does the team escalate or document cases where a landlord refuses to provide required documents or complete repairs identified during inspection?  

---

### **Property Manager Assignment**  
3. When assigning a property manager, which criteria take priority if there’s a conflict—for example, if the most geographically specialized manager has an excessively high workload?  
4. How do you ensure continuity of knowledge when a property manager leaves mid-onboarding, or if a conflict arises between the landlord and the assigned manager?  

---

### **Inspection & Repairs**  
5. What happens if the inspector identifies safety hazards that the landlord resists addressing? Are there predefined thresholds for when the company declines to onboard the property?  
6. Are there standardized timelines for landlords to complete repairs, and what steps follow if deadlines aren’t met?  

---

### **Pricing & Marketing**  
7. How do market trends influence the final pricing decision, and how is this balanced with the landlord’s requested price range? Is there a formal process for negotiating discrepancies?  
8. Are there instances where the marketing team’s suggested price differs from the landlord’s expectations? How is this resolved?  

---

### **Tenant Screening & Leasing**  
9. What are the non-negotiable criteria in tenant screening, and how do you handle edge cases where a tenant meets most criteria but has a minor red flag (e.g., a short employment history)?  
10. When multiple qualified tenants express interest, how do you decide between them? Are there specific priorities (e.g., lease length, credit score thresholds)?  

---

### **Exceptions & Custom Requests**  
11. How do you handle landlords who request non-standard lease clauses (e.g., pet deposits exceeding company policy) or insist on clauses that conflict with fair housing laws?  
12. Are there common types of properties requiring special insurance, and how does this affect the onboarding timeline?  

---

### **Roles & Collaboration**  
13. How does the compliance officer engage with the property manager during the lease review? Are there specific points of conflict that often arise here?  
14. When the financial analyst reviews pricing, what metrics do they prioritize (e.g., profitability, market competitiveness), and how do disagreements between the manager and analyst get resolved?  

---

### **Timing & Sequencing**  
15. What are the key milestones and deadlines in the onboarding timeline, and which steps are most prone to delays? How does the team manage cascading delays?  
16. Are there any parallel processes (e.g., marketing preparation while repairs are ongoing), or is the process strictly sequential?  

---

### **Communication & Handoffs**  
17. When a property moves from the coordinator to the property manager, what handoff documentation or briefing takes place to ensure alignment?  
18. How does the team track and resolve miscommunications between the landlord, property manager, and support teams during the process?  

---

### **Quality Checks & Audits**  
19. What are the most critical checks the compliance officer performs on lease agreements, and what actions are taken if violations are found?  
20. How do IT teams verify data accuracy in the central database? Are there automated systems or manual processes for this?  

---

### **Decision-Making & Prioritization**  
21. When negotiating lease terms with a potential tenant, how does the property manager balance the landlord’s preferences with the company’s standard policies?  
22. How do you prioritize which properties to onboard first when there’s a high volume of requests?  

---

### **Special Cases & Edge Scenarios**  
23. Can you describe a scenario where a property was almost rejected due to compliance issues but was salvaged through compromise? What steps were taken?  
24. How does the process adapt for properties with unique challenges (e.g., historic buildings, multi-family complexes with mixed unit types)?  

---

### **Process Improvement & Learning**  
25. What metrics or feedback loops exist to identify inefficiencies in the onboarding process, and how are they addressed?  
26. Have there been instances where a property had to be paused or rolled back mid-onboarding? How was this handled, and what was learned?  

---

### **Technology & Tools**  
27. How do the CRM and project management tools track dependencies between steps (e.g., marketing can’t start until the inspection is cleared)?  
28. Are there tools or protocols to ensure consistency in communication across stakeholders (e.g., avoiding conflicting timelines for repairs and marketing launches)?  

---

### **Policy Alignment**  
29. What’s the process for updating the onboarding process when new housing regulations or insurance requirements emerge?  
30. How do you ensure that all parties (landlords, tenants, staff) are aware of policy changes that affect their roles in the process?
