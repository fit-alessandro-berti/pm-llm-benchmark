A valid-but-incorrect trace is:

**`Receive_Order  Check_Inventory  Ship_Order  Package_Items  Authorize_Payment  Close_Order`**

### Why the model accepts it
The model puts **Authorize_Payment**, **Package_Items**, and **Ship_Order** inside a **parallel** block, so it allows them to occur in **any order**.

### Why this is wrong in the real process
In a normal Order-to-Cash process, the logical order should be:

**Authorize_Payment  Package_Items  Ship_Order**

Shipping the order before payment is authorized, or before the items are packaged, violates the expected business rules. So this trace fits the overly permissive model, but it should not be allowed from a domain perspective.

If you want, I can also point out the other obvious modeling issue: the **cancel** branch allows an extra `Close_Order`, leading to a trace with `Close_Order` twice.
