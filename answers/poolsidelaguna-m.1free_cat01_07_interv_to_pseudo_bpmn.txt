# Invoice Approval Process Model

## Process Overview
**Process Name:** Three-Way Match Invoice Approval Process  
**Primary Goal:** Process vendor invoices for payment through systematic validation and approval

## Participants/Roles
- **Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary)** - Initial invoice processing
- **Purchasing Officer (Dan)** - PO and receipt verification
- **Department Manager (Carlos)** - Final approval authority
- **Accounts Payable Supervisor (Rita)** - Exception handling/escalation
- **Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen)** - External coordination for discrepancies
- **Supplier** - External party providing goods/services

## Artifacts/Documents
- Invoice (from supplier)
- Purchase Order (PO)
- Goods Receipt Confirmation
- Approved Invoice
- Payment Schedule
- Exception Report

---

## Main Process Flow

### START: Invoice Receipt
**Activity:** Receive Invoice  
**Performed by:** Accounts Payable Clerk  
**Trigger:** Email from supplier  
**Output:** Invoice document

### Gateway 1: PO Number Check
**Decision Point:** Does invoice contain valid PO number?

**Path A: No Valid PO**
- **Activity:** Request Missing PO Information  
  **Performed by:** Accounts Payable Clerk  
  **Output:** Email to supplier requesting PO details
- **Sequence Flow:** Wait for supplier response  Return to Gateway 1

**Path B: Valid PO Present**
- **Activity:** Log Invoice to System  
  **Performed by:** Accounts Payable Clerk  
  **System:** Accounting system  
  **Output:** Logged invoice record

### Activity: Forward to Purchasing
**Activity:** Send Invoice for Receipt Confirmation  
**Performed by:** Accounts Payable Clerk  
**Output:** Invoice forwarded to Purchasing department

---

### Parallel Activity: PO and Receipt Verification
**Activity:** Verify PO and Receipt Details  
**Performed by:** Purchasing Officer  
**Input:** Invoice, PO records, delivery documentation  
**Output:** Verification confirmation or discrepancy identification

**Gateway 2: Match Check**
**Decision Point:** Do invoice details match PO and receipt?

**Path A: Complete Match**
- **Activity:** Send Confirmation to Accounts Payable  
  **Performed by:** Purchasing Officer  
  **Output:** Confirmation email/document

**Path B: Discrepancy Found**
- **Activity:** Clarify Discrepancy with Supplier  
  **Performed by:** Purchasing Officer or Supplier Relationship Manager  
  **Output:** Resolution communication
  
  **Gateway 3: Resolution Successful?**
  - **Yes:** Return to Gateway 2
  - **No:** **Activity:** Escalate to Supervisor  
    **Performed by:** Accounts Payable Clerk  Accounts Payable Supervisor

---

### Activity: Manager Approval Request
**Activity:** Request Manager Approval  
**Performed by:** Accounts Payable Clerk (automated system email)  
**Trigger:** Receipt of confirmation from Purchasing  
**Output:** Approval request notification

### Activity: Manager Review
**Activity:** Review and Approve Invoice  
**Performed by:** Department Manager  
**Input:** Invoice, PO, confirmation documents  
**System:** Approval workflow system

**Gateway 4: Manager Decision**
**Decision Point:** Is invoice acceptable?

**Path A: Approved**
- **Activity:** Schedule for Payment  
  **Performed by:** Accounts Payable Clerk  
  **Output:** Invoice added to payment cycle

**Path B: Rejected/Requires Investigation**
- **Activity:** Hold for Investigation  
  **Performed by:** Accounts Payable Clerk  
  **Follow-up:** Escalate to Accounts Payable Supervisor
- **Activity:** Investigate Issues  
  **Performed by:** Accounts Payable Supervisor  
  **Involves:** Coordination with Purchasing, potentially retrospective PO creation

---

## Exception Sub-Process: Retrospective PO Creation
**Trigger:** No valid PO and no informal arrangement record

**Activity:** Coordinate Retrospective PO  
**Performed by:** Accounts Payable Supervisor with Department Head  
**Outcome Options:**
1. Create retrospective PO  Return to main flow
2. Reject invoice  **Activity:** Notify Supplier of Rejection

---

## Exception Sub-Process: Pricing Discrepancies
**Trigger:** Invoice pricing doesn't match contract terms

**Activity:** Resolve Pricing Discrepancy  
**Performed by:** Supplier Relationship Manager  
**Actions:**
1. Check contract terms
2. Communicate with supplier
3. Provide correct pricing to Accounts Payable/Purchasing
4. Receive corrected invoice or resolve discrepancy

---

## End Events

### Standard Completion
**Event:** Invoice Scheduled for Payment  
**Result:** Process complete, payment will be issued

### Exception Completion
**Event:** Invoice Rejected  
**Result:** Process terminated, supplier notified

---

## Key Process Metrics/Benchmarks
- Manager approval trigger: Requires both PO confirmation AND receipt verification
- Exception rate: Approximately monthly (Carlos reports issues "once every couple of months")
- Escalation pathway: AP Clerk  AP Supervisor when discrepancies cannot be resolved
