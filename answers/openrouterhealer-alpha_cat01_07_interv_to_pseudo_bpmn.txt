# BPMN-Like Textual Process Model: Invoice Approval Process

## Process Overview
**Process Name:** Standard Invoice Approval Process  
**Primary Pool:** Finance Department  
**Key Roles (Lanes):**  
1. Accounts Payable Clerk (Mary)  
2. Purchasing Officer (Dan)  
3. Department Manager (Carlos)  
4. Accounts Payable Supervisor (Rita)  
5. Supplier Relationship Manager (Karen)  
6. Supplier (External)

---

## Main Process Flow

### Start Event
**Trigger:** Invoice received (typically by email)

### Activities & Sequence Flow

1. **[AP Clerk] Check PO Number**  
   *Action:* Verify if invoice contains a valid purchase order number  
   *Artifact:* Incoming Invoice

2. **Gateway 1: PO Number Present?**  
   - **Yes Path:** Proceed to "Log Invoice in System"  
   - **No Path:** 
     - Send invoice back to supplier requesting PO details  
     - Wait for corrected invoice with PO number (loops back to Step 1)

3. **[AP Clerk] Log Invoice in Accounting System**  
   *Action:* Enter invoice details into system  
   *Artifact:* System-recorded invoice

4. **[AP Clerk] Forward to Purchasing for Receipt Confirmation**  
   *Action:* Send invoice to Purchasing department  
   *Artifact:* Invoice forwarded (digital or email)

5. **[Purchasing Officer] Confirm Goods/Services Received**  
   *Action:* Verify invoice line items against PO (quantities, prices, delivery charges)  
   *Artifact:* PO details reviewed

6. **Gateway 2: Invoice Details Match PO?**  
   - **Yes Path:** Send confirmation email to Accounts Payable  
   - **No Path:** 
     - Contact supplier directly OR
     - Request Accounts Payable to hold invoice until corrected version received

7. **[AP Clerk] Match Invoice to PO in System**  
   *Action:* Compare invoice line items against PO in accounting system  
   *Artifact:* Matched invoice record

8. **Gateway 3: Match Successful?**  
   - **Yes Path:** Proceed to "Send for Manager Approval"  
   - **No Path:** 
     - Email Purchasing team for clarification
     - If unresolved  Escalate to Supervisor (Rita)

9. **[AP Clerk] Send for Manager Approval**  
   *Action:* Trigger system notification to Department Manager  
   *Artifact:* Approval request with invoice, PO, and confirmation details

10. **[Department Manager] Review and Approve Invoice**  
    *Action:* Examine invoice details, PO, and confirmation  
    *Decision:* Approve or Reject

11. **Gateway 4: Manager Decision?**  
    - **Approved Path:** Proceed to "Schedule Payment"  
    - **Rejected Path:** 
      - Hold invoice and investigate
      - May require rework (loops back to Step 7 or Step 5)

12. **[AP Clerk] Schedule Payment**  
    *Action:* Add approved invoice to next payment cycle  
    *Artifact:* Payment scheduled in system

### End Event
**Result:** Invoice approved and scheduled for payment

---

## Exception Handling Flow

### Exception Path A: Missing PO Number
- **Trigger:** Invoice lacks PO number (detected at Gateway 1)
- **Resolution Path:** 
  1. [AP Clerk] Request PO from supplier
  2. [Supplier] Provides PO number or revised invoice
  3. Process resumes at Step 1

### Exception Path B: Supplier Relationship Issues
- **Trigger:** Pricing discrepancy, missing information, or contract issues
- **Resolution Path:**
  1. [AP Clerk/Purchasing] Loop in Supplier Relationship Manager
  2. [SRM] Check contract terms, contact supplier, provide correct pricing
  3. [SRM] Inform AP or Purchasing of resolution
  4. Process resumes at appropriate step

### Exception Path C: Unresolvable Discrepancies
- **Trigger:** Invoice cannot be matched; issues persist after clarification attempts
- **Resolution Path:**
  1. [AP Clerk] Escalate to AP Supervisor
  2. [Supervisor] Contact supplier, coordinate between departments
  3. For informal service invoices: Work with department heads to:
     - Create retrospective PO, OR
     - Reject invoice
  4. After resolution, process resumes at "Confirm Goods/Services Received" (Step 5)

### Exception Path D: Manager Rejection
- **Trigger:** Manager rejects invoice due to suspicious discrepancies or budget concerns
- **Resolution Path:**
  1. [AP Clerk] Hold invoice and investigate
  2. May involve:
     - Re-matching with PO
     - Contacting Purchasing for clarification
     - Escalating to Supervisor if needed
  3. After investigation, process may resume at:
     - "Match Invoice to PO" (Step 7) if data correction needed
     - "Send for Manager Approval" (Step 9) if approved after investigation

| **Department Manager (Carlos)** | Final approval decision | Steps 10, 11 |
| **AP Supervisor (Rita)** | Exception handling, escalation management | Exception Paths B, C |

## Process Characteristics

- **Process Type:** Sequential with multiple exception paths
- **Primary Complexity:** Handling discrepancies and exceptions
- **Key Decision Points:** 4 major gateways (PO presence, detail matching, match success, manager decision)
- **Collaboration Intensity:** High銄equires coordination between 4 internal roles and external supplier
- **System Integration:** Accounting system used for logging, matching, and notifications
