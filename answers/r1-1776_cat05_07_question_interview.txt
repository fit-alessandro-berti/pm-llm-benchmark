Here’s a structured list of open-ended questions designed to clarify and deepen understanding of the property onboarding process:

### **Process Flow & Timing**
1. Could you walk me through how steps in this process overlap or depend on one another? For example, does documentation verification ever happen concurrently with assigning a property manager?
2. How long does each phase typically take (e.g., document review vs. inspection), and what factors most commonly cause delays?
3. Are there formal handoffs between teams (e.g., coordinator to property manager) and how is accountability ensured during transitions?

### **Decision Criteria & Roles**
4. When assigning a property manager, how is "workload capacity" quantified (e.g., number of properties vs. complexity)? What happens if all managers in a region are at capacity?
5. For geographic specialization: Is this defined by city districts, neighborhoods, or other boundaries? How do managers gain expertise in niche property types (e.g., student housing)?
6. Who approves exceptions when a landlord’s requested rental price deviates significantly from the company’s pricing model?

### **Documentation & Compliance**
7. If a landlord cannot provide required documents (e.g., outdated certificates), are there alternative pathways to proceed (e.g., provisional approvals)?
8. How does the compliance officer ensure alignment with fair housing laws during lease reviews? Are there recurring issues they flag?

### **Exceptions & Edge Cases**
9. What happens if a property fails inspection repeatedly due to landlord reluctance to fund repairs? Is there an escalation process?
10. For landlords insisting on custom lease clauses (e.g., no-pet policies), how does the company balance flexibility with standardization?
11. How are "special insurance" requirements identified and managed (e.g., historic properties requiring additional coverage)?

### **Stakeholder Collaboration**
12. How do property managers coordinate with external vendors (e.g., photographers/inspectors) to prioritize urgent requests?
13. If disagreements arise between internal teams (e.g., marketing’s pricing vs. finance’s targets), how are these resolved?

### **Tenant Screening & Negotiation**
14. Beyond credit checks and references, what qualitative factors influence tenant selection when multiple applicants qualify?
15. Who has authority to approve promotional discounts or lease term adjustments during tenant negotiations?

### **Quality Assurance & Tools**
16. How does the IT department validate system integration (e.g., automated checks vs. manual audits)? What common errors do they encounter?
17. Are there metrics or KPIs used to evaluate the success of onboarding (e.g., time-to-rental, landlord satisfaction)?

### **Communication & Tracking**
18. How does the company ensure consistency when tracking communications across email, CRM, and project tools? Are there pain points in maintaining visibility?
19. For landlords with unique requirements (e.g., furnished units), how are these preferences documented and communicated across teams post-onboarding?

### **Continuous Improvement**
20. Have there been instances where this process failed unexpectedly? What changes were made as a result?

These questions aim to uncover gaps in dependencies, decision-making authority, exception handling, and cross-team collaboration while avoiding technical implementation details.
